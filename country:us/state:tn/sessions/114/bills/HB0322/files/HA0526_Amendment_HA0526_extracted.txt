AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 392 House Bill No. 322*

SECTION 3. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 39-13-314(a), is amended by adding
the following as a new subdivision:
( ) "Organization" means any of the following, whether foreign or domestic:
(A) Limited liability companies;
(B) Corporations;
(C) Business trusts;
(D) Estates;
(E) General partnerships;
(F) Limited partnerships;
(G) Registered or unregistered limited liability partnerships;
(H) Trusts; or
(I) Joint ventures;
