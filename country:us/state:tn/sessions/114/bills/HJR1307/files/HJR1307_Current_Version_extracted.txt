HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1307
By Warner
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Jerry Williams for his
distinguished service to Marshall County and to
the State of Tennessee.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should specially
recognize those estimable individuals who, through their extraordinary efforts, have
distinguished themselves as community leaders of whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such noteworthy person is Jerry Williams, who has faithfully served the
citizens of Marshall County as Road Superintendent since 2002, demonstrating unwavering
dedication, integrity, and leadership throughout his tenure; and
WHEREAS, during his many years of public service, Mr. Williams has earned the
respect and admiration of colleagues and constituents alike through his dependability, practical
wisdom, and steadfast commitment to maintaining and improving the county's infrastructure;
and
WHEREAS, the Marshall County Highway Department has operated with efficiency and
purpose, under the leadership of Mr. Williams, guided by his philosophy of stewardship and
resourcefulness, often expressed in his own words as running operations "like a broke farmer";
and
WHEREAS, prior to his public service, Jerry Williams devoted decades to farming
tobacco, hay, and cattle, instilling in his family and community a strong work ethic and
contributing to the agricultural heritage of Marshall County; and
WHEREAS, beyond his professional accomplishments, Mr. Williams has quietly and
consistently served his community through acts of kindness, generosity, and neighborly support,
never seeking recognition but always willing to lend a helping hand, earning him the nickname
"Top Shelf"—referencing the precious rarity of his extraordinary friendship; and

WHEREAS, he has been a devoted husband to his wife, Vickie, since April 12, 1972; a
loving father to his children, Stephanie, Mikey, and Anthony; and a proud grandfather and great-
grandfather, serving as a role model of character, faith, and family values; and
WHEREAS, Jerry Williams's lifetime of service, humility, and dedication to others
exemplifies the very best of public service and community leadership; Mr. Williams epitomizes
the spirit and commitment that are characteristic of a true Tennessean; and
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that the members of this General Assembly acknowledge
this esteemed gentleman; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Jerry Williams for his lifetime of contributions to
his community and the State of Tennessee, his exemplary service to Marshall County, and his
enduring contributions to its people and infrastructure. We extend to him our best wishes for
much continued success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
