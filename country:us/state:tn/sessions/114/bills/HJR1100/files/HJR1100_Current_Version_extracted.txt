HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1100
By Terry
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate the Providence
Christian Academy girls' basketball team,
TSSAA Division II-A state champions.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly are pleased to specially honor and
commend those successful high school teams that, through their magnificent exploits, bring
honor to Tennessee and serve as exceptional ambassadors for this State; and
WHEREAS, the Providence Christian Academy (PCA) girls' basketball team achieved an
extraordinary level of success during the 2025-2026 season, winning the TSSAA Division II-A
state championship; and
WHEREAS, the PCA Lady Lions claimed the Division II-A state championship with an
impressive victory in the title game on March 7, 2026, at F&M Bank Arena in Clarksville,
finishing the season with an exceptional record and demonstrating outstanding skill, teamwork,
and determination throughout the tournament; and
WHEREAS, the 2025-2026 PCA Lady Lions were led by Head Coach Tara James, who
instilled in these exceptional young women a commitment to excellence in all that they do and
encouraged maximum effort on the basketball court; and
WHEREAS, state championships are achieved only through hard work, dedication, and
the ability to work together as a team and excel with a cooperative spirit; the Lady Lions
exhibited each of these rare qualities during their championship season; and
WHEREAS, the young women of the PCA Lady Lions realized an extraordinary
achievement on the court but also represented their school in a professional manner off the
court; and

WHEREAS, the dedication, determination, work ethic, and talent of these exceptional
young athletes steered them to a TSSAA Division II-A state championship, bringing great pride
to their school and community; and
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to acknowledge and applaud the members of the
Providence Christian Academy Lady Lions basketball team for serving as exemplars of the high
quality of the student athletes of Tennessee; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate the players, coaching staff, team support staff,
administrators, cheerleaders, and fans of the Providence Christian Academy girls' basketball
team on winning the TSSAA Division II-A state basketball championship during the 2025-2026
season.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
