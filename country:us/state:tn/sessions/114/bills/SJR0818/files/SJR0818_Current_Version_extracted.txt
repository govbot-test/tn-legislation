SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 818
By Stevens
A RESOLUTION to congratulate Vaughn Bounds of Dresden High
School on winning the 2025 TSSAA Class A
Boys' Cross Country Individual Championship.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly are pleased to specially honor and
commend those successful high school athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring
honor to Tennessee and serve as exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable athlete is Vaughn Bounds of Dresden High School, who
won the 2025 TSSAA Class A Boys' Individual State Cross Country Championship held at
Shelby Farms Park in Memphis on November 6-7, 2025; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Bounds's winning time of 16:18:79 secured the championship and
demonstrated his exceptional endurance, discipline, and competitive spirit; and
WHEREAS, a sophomore at Dresden High School, Vaughn Bounds is noted for being
part of the first-ever duo (along with Hudson McDaniel) to win state titles for the school in the
same year; and
WHEREAS, a standout athlete, Mr. Bounds also earned All-State honors in 2025 and is
a top contender in track and field, placing third in state-championship competition in the 1,600-
meter event and fourth in the 800-meter event; and
WHEREAS, Vaughn Bounds's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride
for his family, his school, and his community; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Bounds's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of our respect,
admiration, and commendation; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Vaughn Bounds of

Dresden High School on winning the 2025 TSSAA Class A Boys' Cross Country Individual
Championship and extend to him our best wishes for much continued success in his future
