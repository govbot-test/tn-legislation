HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1403
By Hicks G
A RESOLUTION to honor William Lewis Jenkins on his receipt of
the Hawkins County Republican Party Lifetime
Achievement Award.
WHEREAS, William Lewis Jenkins, born on November 29, 1936, has devoted a lifetime
of service to the State of Tennessee and the United States of America, exemplifying integrity,
leadership, and dedication to public service; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Jenkins is a proud seventh-generation Tennessean with deep roots in
Rogersville and Hawkins County, reflecting a lifelong commitment to his community and its
values; and
WHEREAS, he honorably served in the United States Army, demonstrating his
dedication to the defense and security of our nation; and
WHEREAS, he pursued higher education at Tennessee Technological University and
the University of Tennessee College of Law, preparing himself for a distinguished career in law,
public service, and leadership; and
WHEREAS, his public service began in the Tennessee General Assembly as a member
of the House of Representatives, where he served from 1962 to 1971, and rose to the esteemed
position of Speaker of the House, earning the respect of his colleagues and constituents alike;
and
WHEREAS, he continued his service as a member of the Board of Directors of the
Tennessee Valley Authority and later as a circuit court judge, upholding the rule of law with
fairness and integrity; and
WHEREAS, following the retirement of Congressman Jimmy Quillen, Mr. Jenkins
answered the call to serve in the United States House of Representatives, representing
Tennessee's First Congressional District from 1997 to 2007; and

WHEREAS, during his five terms in Congress, he maintained a steadfast commitment to
conservative principles and represented his constituents with dedication, humility, and
effectiveness; and
WHEREAS, his distinguished career followed in the legacy of respected leaders such as
B. Carroll Reece, contributing to a long tradition of principled representation in Northeast
Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, upon announcing his retirement in 2006, Mr. Jenkins demonstrated his
enduring commitment to family values, choosing to dedicate more time to his loved ones after
decades of public service; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate William Lewis Jenkins upon his receipt of the
Lifetime Achievement Award from the Hawkins County Republican Party in recognition of his
exemplary service, unwavering leadership, and lasting contributions to Hawkins County, the
State of Tennessee, and the United States of America.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
