SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1098
By Roberts
A RESOLUTION to congratulate H.G. Hill Pleasant View upon
winning a National Award for Merchandising at
the NGA Show Creative Choice Awards.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should pay tribute to those consummate businesses that
serve as supportive community partners, enhancing the economic life of Tennessee and its
citizens; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable business is H.G. Hill Pleasant View, which recently won
a National Award for Merchandising at the National Grocers Association Show Creative Choice
Awards; and
WHEREAS, H.G. Hill Pleasant View garnered the national award for its summer-long
celebration with The Peach Truck peaches; and
WHEREAS, from the bakery to the meat department and beyond, this award-winning
campaign was rooted in local flavor, community connection, and a whole lot of love; and
WHEREAS, H.G. Hill Pleasant View works every day to serve its neighbors and friends
and create a space that nourishes the well-being of everyone who crosses its threshold; and
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that we specially recognize this extraordinary business on
this special occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we congratulate H.G. Hill Pleasant View upon
winning a National Award for Merchandising at the NGA Show Creative Choice Awards,
extending our best wishes for every continued success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
