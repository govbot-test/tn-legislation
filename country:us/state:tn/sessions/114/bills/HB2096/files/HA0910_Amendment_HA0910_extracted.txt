Section 2:
SECTION __. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-13-111(h), is amended by
deleting the subsection and substituting:
(h) The meetings of the governing body of a public charter school are deemed
public business and must be held in compliance with title 8, chapter 44, part 1. All
information providing notice of public meetings as required under § 8-44-103 must be
kept current by a public charter school on the public charter school's website.
Notwithstanding title 8, chapter 44, part 1 to the contrary, a governing body of a public
charter school may conduct a scheduled board meeting by electronic means in the same
manner local boards of education may conduct, and local board of education members
may participate in, board meetings by electronic means pursuant to § 49-2-203(c).
Notwithstanding this subsection (h) to the contrary, a member of a governing body of a
public charter school who does not reside in this state may participate in all scheduled
board meetings via electronic means.
