Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-2-203(c)(2), is amended by
deleting the subdivision and substituting:
(2)
(A) Except as provided in subdivision (c)(2)(B), a board meeting must not
be conducted with electronic participation unless a quorum of members is
physically present at the location of the meeting.
(B) A board meeting may be conducted with electronic participation
without a quorum of members being physically present at the location of the
meeting when inclement weather or a natural disaster makes the physical
presence of a quorum of members at the location of the meeting impractical or
unsafe; provided, that a board meeting must not be conducted with electronic
participation without a quorum of members being physically present at the
location of the meeting more than three (3) times per year.
(C) All members participating in a board meeting conducted with
electronic participation must be visually identified in the meeting.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
