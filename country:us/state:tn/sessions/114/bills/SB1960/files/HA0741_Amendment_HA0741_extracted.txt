Section 1:
Amendment No. 1 to HB1857
Jones J
Signature of Sponsor
by adding the following as a new subsection in the amendatory language of Section 1:
(h) Notwithstanding this section to the contrary, each private school that enrolls
recipients of an education freedom scholarship through the program created in part 35 of
this chapter, and each participating school, as that term is defined in § 49-6-2602, shall
annually designate for the private school or participating school one (1) instructional day
each school year to observe the Fourth of July by providing students enrolled in any of
the grades kindergarten through twelve (K-12) with the instruction described in
subsection (b) in accordance with the guidance, objectives, and requirement in
subsections (c), (d), (f), and (g). Each director or chief academic officer or administrator
for the private school or participating school shall comply with the requirements in
subsection (e) for their private school or participating school, as applicable.
