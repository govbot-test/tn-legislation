SENATE BILL 1685
By Walley
HOUSE BILL 1657
By Capley
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 13,
Chapter 7, relative to zoning.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 13-7-208(d)(1), is amended by
deleting the language "necessary to the conduct of such industry or business".
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 13-7-208(i), is amended by deleting
the subsection.
SECTION 3. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
