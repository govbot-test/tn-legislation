HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 730
By Vital
A RESOLUTION to honor the memory of Tony Bryan Hixson of
Decatur.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly were greatly saddened to learn of
the passing of Tony Bryan Hixson; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Hixson was an exemplary public servant and consummate professional
who worked assiduously to improve the quality of life for his fellow citizens in numerous
capacities; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Hixson dedicated his career to carpentry, serving as a valued member
of the Carpenters Local Union 74, crafting with his skilled hands both buildings and cherished
memories for family and friends; and
WHEREAS, Tony Hixson was a devoted member of Maple Grove Baptist Church, which
he served faithfully for many years; and
WHEREAS, a man of many passions, Mr. Hixson enjoyed woodworking, repairing old
cars, fishing, exploring historical sites, boating, and indulging in his passion for racing; and
WHEREAS, Tony Hixson was also deeply devoted to his family, and he always
endeavored to remain true to family values of the highest order; he found peace in creating
countless memories with his loved ones, who found his enthusiasm to be infectious; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Hixson is survived by his mother, Geraldine Tanner; brother, Dale
Hixson; beloved wife, Susan Hixson; children, Cynthia (Whitney) Brown, Benjamin (Cathleen)
Hixson, Catherine (Michael) Carlberg, Chris O'Rourke, and Steven O'Rourke; grandchildren,
Jaxson Brown, Elise Hixson, Christian Carlberg, Chloe Carlberg, Cheyenne Carlberg, and
Carlson Carlberg; great-grandson, Oakland Carlberg; and several nieces, nephews, and
extended family; and
HJR0730
011122
- 1 -
WHEREAS, he was preceded in death by his father, Founty Hixson; his stepfather,
Donald Tanner; the mother of his children, Nancy Gold; and his sister-in-law, Velma Hixson; and
WHEREAS, Tony Hixson leaves behind an indelible legacy of integrity and probity in
public life, compassion and loyalty in private life, and diligence and dedication in all his chosen
endeavors; and
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should pay tribute to the bountiful life of this exceptional
public servant and human being; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor the memory of Tony Bryan Hixson, reflecting fondly upon his
impeccable character and his stalwart commitment to living the examined life with courage and
conviction.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that we extend our sympathy and condolences to the
family and friends of Mr. Hixson.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
