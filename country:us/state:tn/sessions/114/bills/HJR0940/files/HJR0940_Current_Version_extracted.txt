HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 940
By Martin G
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Pastor Jeff Myers and
First Lady Rachel Myers on the occasion of their
fiftieth anniversary in ministry.
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that we should honor those individuals who have answered
a divine call to the ministry and who have served with dedication, vigor, and enthusiasm to
perform God's work for their congregations and their communities; and
WHEREAS, Pastor Jeff Myers and First Lady Rachel Myers have devoted fifty years to
preaching and pastoring in the gospel ministry; and
WHEREAS, Pastor Jeff Myers's enduring youthful spirit, genuine warmth, and love of
humor are consistently reflected in his actions, inspiring joy and fellowship within the
congregation at Union Fork Baptist Church, and his steadfast commitment to the spiritual growth
and well-being of his parishioners has strengthened his church community; and
WHEREAS, Pastor Jeff Myers possesses a particularly remarkable ability to bridge
generational gaps, effectively communicating meaningful messages and spiritual guidance in
ways that are accessible, relatable, and impactful to children and young people while fostering
mutual understanding and appreciation across all ages; and
WHEREAS, he faithfully provides guidance and counsel to his parishioners in matters of
both faith and daily life, including spiritual growth, understanding and applying scripture,
stewardship of resources, marriage and family life, and other essential aspects of Christian
living; and
WHEREAS, Pastor Jeff Myers's keen intellect and business acumen have guided
numerous churches, including Union Fork Baptist Church, through the financial challenges that
have affected many faith-based institutions, providing wise counsel and strategic leadership that

have strengthened the church's stability and enabled Union Fork Baptist Church to become
completely debt-free; and
WHEREAS, Pastor Myers demonstrates visionary leadership, fostering growth not only
in numbers but in programs and in the development of the church facility, consistently thinking
creatively and embracing innovative ideas, while showing a heartfelt interest in other pastors
and their ministries, always seeking ways to encourage, inspire, and uplift the broader local

WHEREAS, Pastor Jeff Myers and First Lady Rachel Myers devote themselves each

Gospel and uplifting others wherever they go; and
WHEREAS, Rachel Myers's deep love for her church and unwavering dedication to its
congregation, particularly its youngest members, is evident in every aspect of her ministry, from
nurturing young hearts in faith and encouraging their spiritual growth, to modeling the love of
Christ through her compassion, guidance, and service, continuously fostering a Christ-centered
environment for the next generation of congregants; and
WHEREAS, Rachel Myers has faithfully supported her husband's ministry while
nurturing her own calling, serving as a steadfast guiding and grounding presence in his ministry,
within the church, and in their personal lives, exemplifying devotion, wisdom, and
compassionate leadership throughout the congregation and the broader Christian community;
and
WHEREAS, Pastor Jeff Myers and First Lady Rachel Myers not only devote themselves
wholeheartedly to the local church community, but they have also traveled the globe ministering
to growing churches and serving in the mission field, faithfully helping to establish churches in

regions where God's Word is seldom heard and touching the lives of countless individuals by
guiding them to know, embrace, and share His teachings; and
WHEREAS, the Myerses' commitment to God, their spiritual leadership, and their love

and admiration; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Pastor Jeff Myers and First Lady Rachel Myers
upon the momentous occasion of their fiftieth anniversary in ministry, extending our deepest
gratitude for their lifetime of faithful service and offering them our best wishes for every
continued success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
