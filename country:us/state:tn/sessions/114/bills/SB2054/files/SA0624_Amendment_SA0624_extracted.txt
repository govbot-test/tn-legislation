Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 67-5-212(n), is amended by deleting
the period (.) at the end of the first sentence and adding the following:
; provided, however, that all structures and site improvements associated with and held
by a nonprofit entity, and its wholly owned subsidiaries, owning or operating a premier
type tourist resort under § 57-4-102(30)(LLLLLL) are exempt from property tax on real
and personal property regardless of use and that the maximum exemption for
undeveloped land owned by the premier type tourist resort is four hundred (400) acres.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
