HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 713
By Powers
A RESOLUTION to celebrate the 160th anniversary of First Baptist
Church of Jacksboro.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly are pleased to recognize those
historic churches and their congregations who have dedicated themselves and their resources
to spreading the good news of the Gospel in their communities, thereby lifting up society with
God's Word; and
WHEREAS, one such dynamic and historic community of faith is First Baptist Church of
Jacksboro, whose members are celebrating the church's 160th anniversary on February 8, 2026,
a most commendable milestone for this venerable institution; and
WHEREAS, founded on February 3, 1866, First Baptist Church of Jacksboro has
continued to serve generations of Tennesseans through community outreach, missions, and
faith-based service; and
WHEREAS, a pillar of Campbell County, First Baptist Church gives back to the
community through contributions to veterans, senior citizens, and community sports events, in
addition to pastoral community engagement and support of both local and international
missions; and
WHEREAS, First Baptist Church of Jacksboro has stood on the rock-solid foundation of
Christian belief, service, and love for 160 years, serving as a bastion of faith and outreach to the
people of its community; and
WHEREAS, thanks to the church's faith, leadership, and strength of its members, the
church has persevered and continues to grow; it is truly one of the great religious institutions
and catalysts for positive social change in our State and nation, and the members of this
