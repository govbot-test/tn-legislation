SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1063
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor Kalli Crowder of Cocke County 4-H for
earning top honors at the Dixie Tennessee
Valley Poultry Show.
WHEREAS, we take great pleasure in recognizing those outstanding young scholars
who have distinguished themselves as emerging leaders in Tennessee's agricultural industry;
and
WHEREAS, Kalli Crowder of Cocke County is one such individual, who, as a member of
Cocke County 4-H, recently received top honors for her 4-H poultry project at the Dixie
Tennessee Valley Poultry Show in Knoxville; and
WHEREAS, the 4-H poultry project can take months or years of dedication, during which
time 4-H members learn about raising quality chickens, including learning about daily feeding,
cleaning, proper breeding, health care, and show preparation; these projects give young people
hands-on opportunities to learn about agriculture while building confidence, leadership, and
strong work ethic; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Crowder's hard work and dedication were evident during this year's
competition; she earned Best of Variety, Best of Breed, and Reserve Champion Feather Leg in
the junior category, all of which honors reflect the quality of her birds and her personal
investment of time and effort; and
WHEREAS, in addition to winning top honors, Kalli Crowder has learned and developed
valuable skills in responsibility, patience, and animal husbandry; and
WHEREAS, an outstanding young Tennessean, Ms. Crowder has reached the very
acme of agricultural competition, and she is most deserving of our approbation; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
SJR1063
016983
- 1 -
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Kalli Crowder of Cocke
County 4-H for earning top honors at the Dixie Tennessee Valley Poultry Show, salute her
expertise, dedication, and composure under pressure, and extend to her our best wishes for
much continued success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
