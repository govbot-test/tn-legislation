SENATE RESOLUTION 318
By Kyle
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate the Evangelical
Christian School girls' soccer team on finishing
as runner-up in the 2025 TSSAA Division II-A
state championship.
WHEREAS, we are pleased to specially honor and commend those successful young
athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring honor to Tennessee and serve as
exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, the Evangelical Christian School (ECS) girls' soccer team of Cordova
achieved an extraordinary level of success during the 2025 season, reaching the finals and
finishing as runner-up in the TSSAA Division II-A state championship tournament; and
WHEREAS, entering the state tourney with an excellent 13-5 record, Evangelical
Christian School blasted DCA 4-0 to advance to the championship game; and
WHEREAS, displaying great sportsmanship and outstanding character, both individually
and collectively, the players and coaches of the Evangelical Christian School Eagles conducted
themselves in a manner befitting the State of Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, the determination, commitment, talent, and teamwork required to reach this
level of success made possible an outstanding season, and the members of the Evangelical
Christian School girls' soccer team are some of the finest young women the Volunteer State has
to offer; and
WHEREAS, the members of the Evangelical Christian School girls' soccer team
displayed uncommon talent, determination, and perseverance in their quest for athletic
excellence, and it is wholly fitting that they should be commended for attaining their lofty goals;
now, therefore,
SR0318
013634
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and congratulate the
Evangelical Christian School girls' soccer team on reaching the finals and finishing as runner-up
in the 2025 TSSAA Division II-A state championship and extend our best wishes for continued
success in their future endeavors as athletes, scholars, and citizens.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
