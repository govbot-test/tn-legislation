SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 837
By Pody
A RESOLUTION to honor Letha May Beatrice Dedman Hallums
on the occasion of her ninety-fifth birthday.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should pay tribute to those citizens who are celebrating
special occasions in their estimable lives; and
WHEREAS, Letha May Beatrice Dedman Hallums celebrated her ninety-fifth birthday on
September 1, 2025, a milestone that was commemorated as yet another precious souvenir of
life's rich pageant; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Hallums has lived an amazing life and has experienced firsthand the
breadth and span of American history. One of ten children, she was born in Lebanon on
September 1, 1930, to Sydney Roy Dedman, Sr., and Lula Mae Andrews Dedman; and
WHEREAS, on December 16, 1946, Letha May Beatrice Dedman was joined in
marriage with the love of her life, William Edward Hallums, and the couple enjoyed fifty-eight
years of marriage; their blessed union produced four children, eight grandchildren, and ten
great-grandchildren; and
WHEREAS, a woman of devout Christian faith and a prayer warrior, Ms. Hallums is a
member of Highland Heights Church of Christ, where she served in numerous capacities
throughout the years; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Hallums is vibrant and dynamic and finds immense delight in the rich
splendor of life. She enjoys visiting with family and friends, gardening, and reading her Bible
daily; and
WHEREAS, throughout her extraordinary life, she has created an estimable legacy for
her children, Larry Edward Hallums, Garry William Hallums, Terresia Hallums Williams, and
Bruce Alan Hallums; her grandchildren, Tracey Michelle Hallums, Bradford William Hallums,
