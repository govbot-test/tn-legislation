HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1395
By Barrett
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Jim Bates upon his
retirement as Hickman County Mayor.
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that the members of this General Assembly should honor
those citizens who have performed with uncommon devotion and enthusiasm throughout their
careers in public service; and
WHEREAS, Jim Bates is one such estimable individual, who, after serving with
distinction as Hickman County Mayor since 2022, is taking a well-deserved retirement; and
WHEREAS, Mayor Bates began his career in public service as a firefighter and
paramedic, dedicating thirty years to Ocala Fire Rescue in Florida before retiring from that
profession in 2017; and
WHEREAS, after relocating to Tennessee, he continued his commitment to public safety
with the Dickson County Fire Department and later with Hickman County Emergency Medical
Services, where he rose to the position of Assistant Director; and
WHEREAS, Jim Bates continued to serve his community in various capacities, including
work within the Hickman County School System and Southwest Airlines, before entering public
office; and
WHEREAS, in 2022, he was elected Hickman County Mayor and has since served with
diligence and integrity in that role; and
WHEREAS, during his tenure as county mayor, Mayor Bates has led and supported
numerous initiatives to improve the quality of life for Hickman County residents, including efforts
to protect the Duck River, the consolidation of county offices to improve efficiency and reduce
costs, and the development of a new county animal shelter; and
WHEREAS, Jim Bates has also worked to strengthen cooperation between municipal
and county governments, fostering a spirit of collaboration for the benefit of all citizens; and

WHEREAS, actively involved in his community, Mayor Bates has contributed his time
and talents to youth athletics programs and civic organizations; and
WHEREAS, Jim Bates is most appreciative of the love and support he shares with his
wife, Tonya; children, David, Andrew, and Montana; and granddaughter, Rowyn James Clark;
and
WHEREAS, throughout his distinguished career, Mayor Bates has demonstrated the
utmost professionalism, ability, and integrity, winning the unqualified respect and admiration of
his colleagues and the citizens he has served; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we extend to Hickman County Mayor Jim Bates our heartfelt wishes for a
happy and fulfilling retirement and every continued success in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
