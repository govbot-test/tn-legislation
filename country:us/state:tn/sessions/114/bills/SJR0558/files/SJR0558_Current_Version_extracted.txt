SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 558
By Johnson
A RESOLUTION to commend Gap26 and the Great American
Prayer Revival.
WHEREAS, Gap26, initiated in July 2024 at Brentwood Baptist Church, has grown into
the Great American Prayer Revival, a nonprofit ministry dedicated to fostering spiritual renewal
across the United States; and
WHEREAS, the mission of the Great American Prayer Revival is to call for national
spiritual revival meetings in all fifty states from March 1-4, 2026, to promote a great awakening,
encouraging Americans to embrace the nation's heritage rooted in Jesus Christ, the Bible, and
the Christian faith; and
WHEREAS, this noble effort is led by a distinguished board, including Chairman Hope
Hines, former Alabama State Senator Hank Erwin, Dr. Charles McGowen, Reverend Keith
Miller, John Mckennon, Business Executive Julie Nimmons, Attorney Joel Bunkowske, Business
Executive Richard Bonar, Reverend Isaiah Hughes, Mr. Bobby Williams, Mr. Cliff Wittman, and
Mr. Paul Sparrow; and
WHEREAS, the Great American Prayer Revival seeks to unite Americans in prayer and
reflection, fostering a renewed commitment to faith and shared values that have shaped the
nation's history; and
WHEREAS, we recognize the importance of spiritual revival in strengthening
communities and inspiring hope for future generations; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we commend Gap26 and the Great American

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
