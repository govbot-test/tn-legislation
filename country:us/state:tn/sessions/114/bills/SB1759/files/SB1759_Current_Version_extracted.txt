Section 1:
By Hurt
SENATE BILL 1759
By Briggs
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 41,
relative to inmates.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 41-1-126, is amended by adding a
new subsection (e):
(e) The department shall keep a copy of each validated risk and needs
assessment of each inmate under its supervision and control for the duration of the
inmate's period of incarceration.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
