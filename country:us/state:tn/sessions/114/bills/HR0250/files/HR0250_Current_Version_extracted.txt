HOUSE RESOLUTION 250
By Chism
A RESOLUTION to commend Jordyn Jones for exemplary service
as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, we are pleased to recognize certain persons who
have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment of the legislative
process; and
WHEREAS, Jordyn Jones is one such individual, who has distinguished herself in her
role as a legislative intern for Representative Jesse Chism; Ms. Jones's work has resulted in
innumerable benefits for the legislators she assisted and the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, Jordyn Jones is a senior at Tennessee State University (TSU), where she is
highly regarded by her peers; and
WHEREAS, she has achieved academic success while majoring in political science with
a concentration in public policy and being a member of the Honors College; she has showcased
her academic success through being listed on the Dean's List and earning the Breaking the
Glass Award; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize herself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further her studies, Ms. Jones brought her many abilities
and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2026 session of the One Hundred Fourteenth
General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, throughout the 2026 legislative session, Jordyn Jones has surpassed all
criteria that define a devoted and conscientious legislative intern; she is committed to
reimagining how organizations operate by developing innovative solutions that are both effective
and sustainable; and
HR0250
018293
- 1 -
WHEREAS, Ms. Jones has performed her many duties with extraordinary insight and
rare decorum; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby
commend Jordyn Jones for outstanding service as a legislative intern for Representative Jesse
Chism and extend to her our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
