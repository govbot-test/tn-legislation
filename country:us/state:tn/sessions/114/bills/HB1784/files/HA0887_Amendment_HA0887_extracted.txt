Section 1:
Amendment No. 2 to HB1784
Pearson
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting all language after the caption and substituting:
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 15, Chapter 2, is amended by adding
the following as a new section:
January 22 of each year is observed as "Protect Women's Rights Day" to honor
the ten thousand twenty (10,020) residents of this state that travelled out of state to
obtain an abortion in 2024, a right given to them by the United States' founding
document, which recognizes that persons are endowed by the Creator with certain
unalienable rights and that among these rights are life, liberty, and the pursuit of
happiness.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
