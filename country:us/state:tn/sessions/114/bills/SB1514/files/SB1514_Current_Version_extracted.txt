HOUSE BILL 1573
By Lafferty
SENATE BILL 1514
By Jackson
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 4,
Chapter 29 and Title 4, Chapter 3, relative to the
department of disability and aging.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 4-29-247(a), is amended by deleting
subdivision (19).
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 4-29-249(a), is amended by inserting
the following as a new subdivision:
( ) Department of disability and aging, created by Section 4-3-101 and Section 4-
3-2701;
SECTION 3. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
