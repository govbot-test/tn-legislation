HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1289
By Darby
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate the Bethel University
Action Shooting Team for winning the 2026
Scholastic Action Shooting Program College
National Championship.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly are pleased to specially honor and
commend those successful student athletes who, through dedication, discipline, and teamwork,
achieve excellence and bring honor to this State; and
WHEREAS, the Bethel University Action Shooting Team has distinguished itself through
an extraordinary performance at the 2026 Scholastic Action Shooting Program (SASP) College
National Championship, held March 13-15, 2026, at the CMP Talladega Marksmanship Park in
Talladega, Alabama; and
WHEREAS, competing against top collegiate programs from across the nation, including
teams from Arizona, Florida State University, Virginia Tech, and Texas A&M University, Bethel
University achieved an unprecedented milestone by capturing all eleven discipline titles,
becoming the first team in SASP history to sweep every division of competition; and
WHEREAS, in addition to securing all eleven discipline titles, the Bethel University
Action Shooting Team claimed all three traveling trophies awarded for overall program
excellence, thereby completing a total championship sweep and affirming its status as the
nation's premier collegiate action shooting program; and
WHEREAS, the team's success was further underscored by exceptional individual
performances, as Bethel student athletes earned a combined thirty-seven High Overall honors
across the championship, demonstrating remarkable skill, consistency, and competitive
excellence; and

WHEREAS, among these outstanding competitors, Olivia Albertson and Kaleb Klein
delivered especially noteworthy performances, earning multiple first-place High Overall finishes
and contributing significantly to the team's historic achievement; and
WHEREAS, Brice Jones contributed four runner-up finishes and four third-place
rankings across nearly every category, while Alyson Battreall provided steady scoring depth
with two second-place finishes and four third-place podiums; and
WHEREAS, Colin Davis secured a runner-up finish in Centerfire Revolver, adding
another key piece to the team's overall success; and
WHEREAS, the team's unprecedented sweep of all eleven disciplines at the 2026 SASP
College National Championship is a testament to the hard work, discipline, and unity of purpose
demonstrated by the Bethel University Action Shooting Team, as well as the guidance and
leadership of its coaches; and
WHEREAS, it is wholly fitting that we specially recognize the members of the Bethel
University Action Shooting Team for their remarkable achievement; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate the Bethel University Action Shooting Team for
winning the 2026 Scholastic Action Shooting Program College National Championship,
applauding its historic accomplishment and extending our best wishes for every continued
success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
