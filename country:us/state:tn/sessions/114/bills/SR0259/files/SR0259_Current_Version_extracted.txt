SENATE RESOLUTION 259
By Walley
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Kady Johnson, Hayley
Sims, Brooklynn McCoy, and Natilee Wilkerson
on being named to the 2025-2026 All-West
Tennessee Soccer Teams.
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that we honor those Tennessee students who, through their
athletic prowess, have achieved the pinnacle of success and serve as exceptional ambassadors
of this State; and
WHEREAS, such estimable individuals are Adamsville High School Lady Cardinal
Soccer Team members Kady Johnson, Hayley Sims, Brooklynn McCoy, and Natilee Wilkerson,
who were each named to the 2025-2026 All-West Tennessee Soccer Teams; and
WHEREAS, Kady Johnson received First-Team honors, while Hayley Sims, Brooklynn
McCoy, and Natilee Wilkerson received Second-Team honors; and
WHEREAS, being selected for the All-West Tennessee Soccer Teams is a supreme
accolade, as only the strongest players in the region are admitted each year; and
WHEREAS, prior to the 2025 season, only twelve Lady Cardinal soccer players had
been selected for this honor in the entirety of the program's history; and
WHEREAS, the noteworthy achievement of Kady Johnson, Hayley Sims, Brooklynn
McCoy, and Natilee Wilkerson is a tremendous source of pride for their families, their
classmates, and their entire community; and
WHEREAS, these four soccer players epitomize all that is good in today's student
athletes, as they have achieved a high level of success in sports while evincing great character
both on and off the field; and
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to acknowledge and applaud these standout student
athletes for serving as exemplars of the high quality of the youth of Tennessee; now, therefore,
