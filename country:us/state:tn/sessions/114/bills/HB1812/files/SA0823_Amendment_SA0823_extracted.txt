Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 39-13-113, is amended by deleting
subdivision (i)(1) and substituting:
(1) It is an offense to knowingly violate a no contact order issued:
(A) Prior to a defendant's release on bond following the defendant's
arrest for a criminal offense;
(B) As part of a defendant's sentence or conditions of probation or parole
following conviction for a criminal offense; or
(C) As part of an agreed disposition of a criminal offense not resulting in
a conviction.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 39-13-113(i)(2), is amended by
deleting "arrested" and substituting "arrested or convicted, if applicable".

Section 4:
SECTION 3. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.
