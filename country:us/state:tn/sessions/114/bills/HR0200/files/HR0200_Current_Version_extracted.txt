HOUSE RESOLUTION 200
By White
A RESOLUTION to commend Jasmine McCutcheon for
exemplary service as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this body is pleased to recognize certain persons
who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment of the legislative
process; and
WHEREAS, Jasmine McCutcheon is one such individual, who has distinguished herself
in her role as a legislative intern for Representative Mark White; Ms. McCutcheon's work has
resulted in innumerable benefits for the legislators she assisted and the General Assembly as a
whole; and
WHEREAS, raised in Nashville, Jasmine McCutcheon is a 2012 graduate of Martin
Luther King, Jr., Academic High School; and
WHEREAS, a recent graduate of LeMoyne-Owen College, where she was highly
regarded by her peers, Ms. McCutcheon achieved academic success and earned a Bachelor of
Social Work degree; she participated in numerous extracurricular activities, including serving as
Senior Class President, Student Ambassador, and President of the Delta Chi chapter of Iota Phi
Lambda Sorority, Inc.; and
WHEREAS, during her collegiate career, Jasmine McCutcheon developed an aspiration
to serve high school students through mentorship, youth advocacy, and creating supportive
spaces for students to be heard; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize herself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further her studies, Ms. McCutcheon brought her many
abilities and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2026 session of the One Hundred
Fourteenth General Assembly; and

WHEREAS, throughout the 2026 legislative session, Jasmine McCutcheon has
surpassed all criteria that define a devoted and conscientious legislative intern; and

BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby
commend Jasmine McCutcheon for outstanding service as a legislative intern for
Representative Mark White and extend to her our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
