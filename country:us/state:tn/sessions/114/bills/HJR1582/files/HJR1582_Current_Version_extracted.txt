HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1582
By Russell
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Dustin Frank, Brian
Fernandez, Anthony Blackwell, and Terry
McConkey for providing aid during an incident at
Kefauver Park.
WHEREAS, the Volunteer State is especially proud to be the home of so many valiant
individuals who perform above and beyond the call of duty in an emergency situation; and
WHEREAS, on April 14, 2026, a Madisonville city employee, Tyler Copeland, was
trapped under an overturned mower in a pond at Kefauver Park while performing routine
maintenance duties for the Madisonville Parks and Recreation Department; and
WHEREAS, taking swift and life-saving action, Madisonville Parks and Recreation
employee Dustin Frank, bystander Terry McConkey, and Brian Fernandez and Anthony
Blackwell, two inmates assigned to the Monroe County Sheriff's Office work release program,
raced into the water and pulled the victim from underneath the lawnmower; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Blackwell grabbed the victim and pulled him to the embankment while
Mr. Fernandez helped lift him out of the pond and onto dry ground, where Mr. Frank began
performing CPR; and
WHEREAS, initially, Mr. Copeland had no pulse and was not breathing, but the group
continued to perform CPR and attempted to clear water from his airway and stomach; when
EMS arrived, he had regained consciousness and was able to breathe and communicate; and
WHEREAS, upon their arrival, Monroe County EMS and the Madisonville Fire
Department assumed medical care of the patient who was transported to the hospital for further
treatment; and

WHEREAS, no doubt, the quick response and coordinated efforts of bystanders, city
personnel, and emergency responders played a critical role in saving the life of Mr. Copeland;
and
WHEREAS, it is wholly fitting that we recognize these exemplary gentlemen for their
exceptional act of heroism; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and commend Dustin Frank, Brian Fernandez, Anthony
Blackwell, and Terry McConkey for their lifesaving response to the accident at Kefauver Park on
April 14, 2026.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
