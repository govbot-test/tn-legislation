HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1404
By Cochran
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend the Fairview Christian
Academy Lady Falcons girls' basketball team
upon winning the 2026 TAACS Division 2 state
championship.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that this General Assembly should salute those student athletes
who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as true champions of
whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such exemplary group of lasses is the Fairview Christian Academy
Lady Falcons girls' basketball team, whose members have proven themselves to be the fiercest
of competitors and truly worthy of admiration by winning the 2026 TAACS Division 2 state
championship; and
WHEREAS, in the championship game on February 28, 2026, the Lady Falcons flew
high and defeated Hendersonville by a score of 51-41 to claim the title of state champions; and
WHEREAS, the Fairview Lady Falcons relied heavily upon the talent, competitive spirit,
and leadership of the tournament's Most Valuable Player, Maggie Pedde, and All-Tournament
teammates Samantha Dodge and Brooke Lakomiak, and the contributions of Laney Wood,
Bryleigh Carver, Kenzie Norton, Candace Mauricio, Carli Cooper, and Claire Pedde; and
WHEREAS, the Lady Falcons' superb performance would not have been possible
without the stellar leadership of Head Coach Katie Pedde and her assistants, Connor Roberts
and Benjamin Johnson, and the unfailing support of their families, their fans, and their
community; and
WHEREAS, the members of the Fairview Christian Academy Lady Falcons basketball
team epitomize all that is good in today's student athletes, as they have achieved a premier

2 state championship and extend to them our best wishes for every continued success in all
their future endeavors.
