HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1033
By Hemmer
A RESOLUTION to recognize and congratulate Jeremy Jones and
Allen Huggins, 2025 President's Cup champions.
WHEREAS, from time to time, we recognize those individuals whose dedication,
perseverance, and commitment in the pursuit of their goals make them exemplars of the
qualities valued by the citizenry of this State; and
WHEREAS, Jeremy Jones and Allen Huggins recently proved themselves supremely
skillful competitors at the game of golf by winning Hillwood Country Club's 2025 President's
Cup; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Jones and Mr. Huggins prevailed in the sixty-team field and outlasted
eleven other teams in the championship shootout, including the runner-up team, Fred Anderson
and Mark Lawrence, with a par on the final sudden-death playoff hole; and
WHEREAS, despite stiff competition, these gentlemen exhibited peak performance
under pressure, relying on skills developed during countless hours of practice and play; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Jones and Mr. Huggins are an inspiration to all who know them, and we
are pleased to congratulate them on their noteworthy win; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we recognize and congratulate Jeremy Jones and Allen Huggins, the 2025
Hillwood Country Club's President's Cup champions, extending our best wishes for every
continued success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
