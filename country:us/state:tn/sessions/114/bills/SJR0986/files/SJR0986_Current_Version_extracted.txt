SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 986
By Reeves
A RESOLUTION to commend Alaya Fuqua for exemplary service
as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this General Assembly is pleased to recognize
certain persons who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment
of the legislative process; and
WHEREAS, Alaya Fuqua is one such individual, who has distinguished herself in her
role as a legislative intern for Senator Shane Reeves; Ms. Fuqua's work has resulted in
innumerable benefits for the legislators she assisted and the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, a native of Winchester, Alaya Fuqua is a junior at Middle Tennessee State
University (MTSU), where she is highly regarded by her peers; she has achieved academic
success while pursuing a degree in accounting; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize herself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further her studies, Alaya Fuqua brought her many abilities
and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2026 session of the One Hundred Fourteenth
General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, throughout the 2026 legislative session, Ms. Fuqua has surpassed all
criteria that define a devoted and conscientious legislative intern; and
WHEREAS, Alaya Fuqua has performed her many duties with extraordinary insight and
rare decorum; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby commend Alaya Fuqua for outstanding
