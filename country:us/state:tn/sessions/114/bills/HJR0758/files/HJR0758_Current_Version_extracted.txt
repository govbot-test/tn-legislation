HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 758
By Bricken
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Mary Gilbert on being
named a 2025–2026 Tennessee Principal of the
Year finalist.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should specially
recognize those outstanding citizens who have devoted their careers to providing the highest-
quality education and learning environments for Tennessee's students; and
WHEREAS, Mary Gilbert is one such estimable individual, who has distinguished herself
as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, the principal at Tullahoma City Schools' Robert E. Lee Elementary School,
Ms. Gilbert was recently named a 2025–2026 Tennessee Principal of the Year finalist by the
Tennessee Department of Education, an honor most befitting her outstanding tenure as a
principal, as it recognizes the positive influence she has had on so many young lives; and
WHEREAS, the Tennessee Principal of the Year award is granted annually to a school
leader for outstanding service in education and exceptional leadership that drives overall
improvements in their school; and
WHEREAS, Mary Gilbert is an extremely talented and compassionate administrator who
has worked assiduously throughout her career in education to motivate her students, teachers,
and staff to reach their full potential as scholars, professionals, and citizens; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Gilbert truly serves as an inspiration to the faculty and staff at Robert E.
Lee Elementary School, and her friendship and counsel are highly valued by those who are
fortunate to know her; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Gilbert epitomizes the dedicated principal who is wholly committed to
the highest ideals of public service and quality education for all, and she should be specially
recognized; now, therefore,

BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Mary Gilbert on being named a 2025–2026
Tennessee Principal of the Year finalist, commend her exceptional service to the students of
Tennessee, salute her professional expertise and personal excellence, and wish her all the best
in her future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
