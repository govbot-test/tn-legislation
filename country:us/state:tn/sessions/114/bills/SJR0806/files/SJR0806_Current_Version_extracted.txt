SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 806
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Kylee Renner on
being named to the TSWA Class A All-State golf
team.
WHEREAS, we are pleased to recognize and commend those successful high school
athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring honor to Tennessee and serve as
exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable individual is Kylee Renner of Greeneville High School,
an athlete of extraordinary talent, skill, and dedication, who has proven herself a formidable
competitor on the golf course; and
WHEREAS, the Tennessee Sports Writers Association (TSWA) recently named Kylee
Renner to the 2025 Class A All-State golf team in recognition of her excellence and her display
of drive and determination; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Renner epitomizes all that is good in today's student athlete, as she has
achieved a high level of success in sports while evincing great character both on and off the golf
course; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Renner's notable achievement is a source of great pride for her family,
her school, and her community; and
WHEREAS, Kylee Renner is a winner both on and off the field of play, and her
dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect, admiration, and commendation of
this General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Kylee Renner of
