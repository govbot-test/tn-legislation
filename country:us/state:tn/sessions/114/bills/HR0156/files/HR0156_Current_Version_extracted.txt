HOUSE RESOLUTION 156
By Lafferty
A RESOLUTION to recognize and commend LuLu's Tea Room on
the occasion of its twentieth anniversary.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should recognize those
small businesses that are beloved mainstays in their communities and that, through dedicated
service and hospitality, contribute to the character and charm of our great State; and
WHEREAS, LuLu's Tea Room, located in Powell, is one such cherished establishment,
known throughout the area for its outstanding service, family-friendly atmosphere, and a
thoughtfully curated menu of family recipes prepared with fresh ingredients; and
WHEREAS, LuLu's Tea Room is proudly owned and operated by Deana "LuLu" Hurd,
whose warmth and vision have made the restaurant a local favorite and a gathering place for
friends, families, and neighbors alike; and
WHEREAS, a true staple of the Powell community, LuLu's Tea Room recently
celebrated twenty years of continuous operation, marking two decades of joyfully welcoming
guests and serving up not only delicious meals but also comfort, tradition, and Tennessee
hospitality; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we recognize
and commend LuLu's Tea Room on the occasion of its twentieth anniversary, extending to
Deana "LuLu" Hurd our sincerest appreciation for the meaningful role her establishment plays in
its community and our best wishes for many more successful years to come.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
