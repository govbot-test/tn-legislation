Section 1:
By Massey
HOUSE BILL 2129
By Alexander
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 9 and
Title 49, relative to pre-kindergarten.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-6-108, is amended by deleting
subdivision (6) and substituting:
(6) Once every three (3) years, provide a report to the governor and general
assembly on the status of pre-kindergarten programs that includes, at a minimum, the
number, location, and types of providers of pre-kindergarten classrooms and the number
of at-risk children served. The report must be published on the department of education,
office of early learning's website.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
