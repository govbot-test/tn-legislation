HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1386
By Vital
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend LifeSpring Community
Health on the occasion of its twentieth
anniversary.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should specially recognize those premier healthcare
providers that have dedicated their professional excellence to improving the health and well-
being of the communities they serve; and
WHEREAS, one such exceptional healthcare provider celebrating its twentieth
anniversary this year is LifeSpring Community Health in Chattanooga, which provides
exemplary pediatric, community, and developmental-behavioral health care to the families it
serves; and
WHEREAS, founded in 2006 by Dr. Michele Pickett, MD, and Jill Fikkert, MSN RN,
LifeSpring Community Health began as a small after-school clinic in a neighborhood center to
meet the critical need for accessible pediatric care in the community; and
WHEREAS, LifeSpring became a 501(c)(3) organization in 2010 and added community
health workers in 2017, moving beyond the clinic walls and out into the neighborhoods; and
WHEREAS, during the COVID-19 pandemic, LifeSpring served those most affected by
providing testing and vaccines, and in 2023, it added behavioral health and subsequently
developmental-behavioral health services, bringing long-overdue specialized services directly to
families in the community; and
WHEREAS, today, LifeSpring Community Health offers a full-spectrum of pediatric
services, including well-care visits, sick visits, immunizations, and screenings for children from
birth through age twenty-one; community health and advocacy; and bilingual mental and
behavioral health care tailored to children and adolescents, focusing on early identification and
compassionate support; and
HJR1386

WHEREAS, LifeSpring Community Health serves as a pediatric practice, providing
integrated care for thousands of children in Chattanooga, particularly those in underserved
neighborhoods, ensuring that circumstances do not dictate the quality of care a child receives;
and
WHEREAS, it is most appropriate that we commend LifeSpring Community Health and
its staff for their unwavering dedication to their patients and their community; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and recognize LifeSpring Community Health on the occasion of
its twentieth anniversary and extend our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
