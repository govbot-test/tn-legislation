HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1014
By Mitchell
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate the Nashville Christian
School football team, 2025 TSSAA Division II-A
state champions.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly are pleased to specially honor and
commend those successful high school teams that, through their magnificent exploits, bring
honor to Tennessee and serve as exceptional ambassadors for this State; and
WHEREAS, the Nashville Christian School football team achieved an extraordinary level
of success during the 2025 season, winning the coveted Tennessee Secondary School Athletic
Association (TSSAA) Division II-A state football championship; and
WHEREAS, the Nashville Christian School Eagles crushed every opponent on their way
to the BlueCross Bowl championship game on December 4, 2025, at Finley Stadium in
Chattanooga, where they played with heart and determination to resoundingly defeat the
University School of Jackson by a score of 59-7 and claim the title of state champions; and
WHEREAS, the 2025 state championship marks Nashville Christian School's second
consecutive state championship and its third title in the last eleven years; and
WHEREAS, Nashville Christian School finished its season with a perfect record of 13-0,
averaging 52 points per game with an average margin of victory of 38 points; and
WHEREAS, state championships are achieved only through hard work, dedication, and
the ability to work together as a team and excel with a cooperative spirit; the Eagles exhibited
each of these rare qualities during their championship season; and
WHEREAS, the football players of Nashville Christian School epitomize all that is good
in today's student athletes, as they have achieved the highest level of success on the field while
showing the highest degree of character both on and off the field; and

WHEREAS, the dedication, determination, work ethic, and talent of this group of young
men not only made possible a championship football season but also surely bode well for their
success in every future endeavor; and
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to acknowledge and applaud the football players of
Nashville Christian School for serving as exemplars of the high quality of the youth of
Tennessee; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate the players, coaching staff, team support staff,
administrators, cheerleaders, and fans of the Nashville Christian School football team on
winning the 2025 TSSAA Division II-A state football championship.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
