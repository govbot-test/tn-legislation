Amendment No. 2 to SB2247
Johnson
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 2247* House Bill No. 2532
by deleting subdivision (i)(1)(C) in Section 3 and substituting:
(C) The sum total of any additional funds received by an LEA pursuant to this
subdivision (i)(1) must not exceed the difference between the LEA's TISA allocation for
the current year and the LEA's TISA allocation for the 2025-2026 school year.
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting "the reason each non-virtual student disenrolled from the
LEA" in subdivision (i)(1)(B) in Section 3 substituting "the reason provided by each non-virtual
student who disenrolled from the LEA".
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting subdivision (i)(2)(B) in Section 3 and substituting:
(B) The sum total of any additional funds received by an LEA pursuant to this
subdivision (i)(2) must not exceed the difference between the LEA's TISA allocation for
the current year and the LEA's TISA allocation for the 2025-2026 school year.
