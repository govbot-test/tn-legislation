SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 844
By Crowe
A RESOLUTION to commemorate National Health Facility
Surveyor Week, August 9–15, 2026.
WHEREAS, the theme for this year's National Health Facility Surveyor Week, "Bigger
Impact. Bolder Leadership. Safer Health Care.", reflects the growing importance of surveyors'
leadership, innovation, and commitment to excellence in an evolving healthcare landscape; and
WHEREAS, health facility surveyors play a vital role in safeguarding the quality, safety,
and integrity of healthcare delivery across our nation; and
WHEREAS, these dedicated professionals work tirelessly to ensure that hospitals,
clinics, long-term care facilities, and other healthcare organizations meet the highest standards
of care; and
WHEREAS, surveyors serve as champions of public trust, using their expertise to
identify risks, strengthen compliance, and promote continuous improvement in healthcare
environments; and
WHEREAS, their work directly contributes to the protection of patients, families, and
communities by upholding essential regulations and advancing a culture of safety; and
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly recognize and honor
the contributions of health facility surveyors whose diligence and professionalism support a
safer, more accountable healthcare system for all; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we commemorate National Health Facility Surveyor
Week, August 9–15, 2026, encouraging all individuals, healthcare organizations, and
communities to join in celebrating the essential work of health facility surveyors.
