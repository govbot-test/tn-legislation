SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 546
By Pody
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Tim Rausch on the
occasion of his retirement from the Tennessee
Valley Authority.
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that we should honor those citizens who have performed
with uncommon devotion and enthusiasm throughout their years of industry; and
WHEREAS, Tim Rausch is one such estimable person, who served with distinction for
more than forty years in the nuclear industry and recently took his much-deserved retirement
from the Tennessee Valley Authority (TVA); and
WHEREAS, in his capacity as Executive Vice President and Chief Nuclear Officer of the
Tennessee Valley Authority, Mr. Rausch exhibited a stalwart commitment to safety, operational
excellence, and continuous improvement to help the agency's nuclear fleet to achieve top
quartile performance in the nation; and
WHEREAS, under his leadership, TVA completed a multi-year 465 megawatt uprate on
the three units at Browns Ferry Nuclear Plant, which provided enough additional generation to
power 280,000 homes from the current plant footprint; and
WHEREAS, additionally, all four steam generators were replaced at Watts Bar under Mr.
Rausch's leadership, demonstrating TVA's commitment to invest in safe, reliable, and carbon-
free nuclear energy to help meet the power demands of today and the future; and
WHEREAS, Tim Rausch exemplifies the spirit and allegiance to family and community
that are characteristic of a true Tennessean; and
WHEREAS, throughout his estimable career, Tim Rausch demonstrated the utmost
professionalism, ability, and integrity, winning the unbridled respect and admiration of his
colleagues; now, therefore,
