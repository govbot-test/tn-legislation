HOUSE RESOLUTION 240
By Pearson
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Memphis Rox for its
transformative impact on community, equity, and
opportunity in Memphis and across the State of
Tennessee.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body recognize those
institutions that uplift communities, restore hope, and create pathways to opportunity for all
people; and
WHEREAS, Memphis Rox, located in the Soulsville community of South Memphis,
stands as the largest nonprofit climbing gym in the world but, more importantly, as a beacon of
healing, belonging, and human connection; and
WHEREAS, Memphis Rox was founded on the belief that access to wellness, recreation,
and community should never be determined by one's ability to pay, operating under a "pay-
what-you-can" model that ensures no person is turned away; and
WHEREAS, through its innovative and compassionate approach, Memphis Rox uses
climbing not merely as sport but as a powerful metaphor for overcoming life's obstacles and
fostering resilience, confidence, and unity among people of all backgrounds; and
WHEREAS, the organization provides far more than a climbing facility, offering holistic
programming including youth mentorship, job training, after-school enrichment, fitness and
wellness classes, and community service initiatives that directly invest in the lives of
underserved youth and families; and
WHEREAS, Memphis Rox actively cultivates a diverse and inclusive environment,
intentionally breaking down racial, cultural, and socioeconomic barriers while creating a safe
and welcoming space where every individual is valued and empowered; and

WHEREAS, the organization's impact extends beyond recreation, addressing food
insecurity through community meals and gardens, supporting neighborhood revitalization
efforts, and fostering meaningful relationships that strengthen the social fabric of Memphis; and
WHEREAS, through volunteerism and community engagement, Memphis Rox has
inspired thousands of acts of service, demonstrating that collective action and shared
responsibility can transform entire communities; and
WHEREAS, Memphis Rox embodies the spirit of Tennessee by lifting up those most
often left behind and proving that when barriers are removed, communities can rise together in
strength, dignity, and hope; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and
commend Memphis Rox for its outstanding commitment to community empowerment, equity,
and holistic well-being.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
