HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 961
By Boyd
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Everett Solomon
Lawson on becoming an Eagle Scout.
WHEREAS, at a time when this nation's youth face difficult challenges, the Boy Scouts
of America is one of our finest assets, providing young people with an educational program that
contributes significantly to their character development, citizenship training, and improved
mental and physical fitness; and
WHEREAS, the highest rank a Scout can attain in the Boy Scouts of America Scouts
BSA program is that of Eagle Scout, and Everett Solomon Lawson, after considerable effort and
diligent preparation, has joined the prestigious Eagle Court of Honor; and
WHEREAS, a student at Green Hill High School in Mt. Juliet, Mr. Lawson maintains a
4.0 grade point average while participating in various extracurricular activities, including
wrestling, track and field, and JROTC; and
WHEREAS, Everett Lawson has a long history in Scouting, having earned the ranks of
Tiger, Wolf, Bear, Webelos, Scout, Tenderfoot, Second Class, First Class, Star, and Life, as
well as achieving Arrow of Light status; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Lawson has demonstrated considerable ability as a leader among his
peers, serving as Troop Guide, Assistant Senior Patrol Leader, and Senior Patrol Leader, the
highest position in his local troop; and
WHEREAS, his notable record of achievement within his Boy Scout troop includes
numerous merit badges, such as those for Archery, Astronomy, Emergency Preparedness, First
Aid, Kayaking, Leatherwork, Public Speaking, Swimming, Wilderness Survival, and Wood
Carving, among many others; and

WHEREAS, Mr. Lawson has been selected for membership in advanced Scouting
organizations—Venture Crew and the Order of the Arrow; and
WHEREAS, Everett Lawson's Eagle project greatly contributed to the betterment of his
community and reflected the true spirit of volunteerism; for his project, he constructed a plant-
based erosion control area at The Hermitage, home of President Andrew Jackson; and
WHEREAS, his project not only beautified the historic grounds, but it also served a
functional purpose and will continue to bring enjoyment to visitors; and
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that we should recognize Everett Lawson as one of the
most outstanding young people in our State; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Everett Solomon Lawson on becoming an
Eagle Scout and extend to him our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
