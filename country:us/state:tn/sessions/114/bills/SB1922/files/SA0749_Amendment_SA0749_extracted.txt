Senate State and Local Government 1
Amendment No. 1 to SB1922
Briggs
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 1922 House Bill No. 1705*
by deleting subsection (b) in SECTION 1 and substituting:
(b)
(1) Except as provided in subdivision (b)(2), an employer shall not:
(A) Appoint or hire a prospective employee on or after July 1,
2026, without verifying the work authorization status of such prospective
employee by using the E-Verify program; or
(B) Adopt an ordinance, resolution, regulation, rule, policy, or
guideline contrary to the requirements of this section.
(2) An employer who currently uses a third-party vendor for Form I-9
verification for prospective employees may continue to use such vendor until
January 1, 2027, at which time the employer must begin using the E-Verify
program.
