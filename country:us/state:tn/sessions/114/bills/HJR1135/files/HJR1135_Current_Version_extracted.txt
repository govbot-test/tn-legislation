HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1135
By White
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend the Coca-Cola
Company's support of America250.
WHEREAS, the United States of America will commemorate its 250th anniversary with
America250, a national celebration honoring the founding of our nation and the enduring legacy
of freedom and democracy; and
WHEREAS, the Coca-Cola Company, founded in the late 19th century, has grown to
become an iconic American brand, symbolizing innovation, unity, and the spirit of our country;
and
WHEREAS, Coca-Cola has demonstrated unwavering support for America250, helping
to promote national pride and the shared values that unite citizens across Tennessee and the
nation; and
WHEREAS, the Coca-Cola Company has played a significant role in American culture,
society, and industry, enriching our communities through economic investment, philanthropy,
and a commitment to service; and
WHEREAS, the Tennessee Coca-Cola bottlers, including Coca-Cola Consolidated,
Coca-Cola United, Corinth Coca-Cola, Reyes Coca-Cola, Union City Coca-Cola, Middlesboro
Coca-Cola, and Pulaski Coca-Cola, have made significant contributions to communities across
Tennessee, including job creation, support for local initiatives, and engagement in historic
celebrations that foster a sense of belonging and purpose; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee's legacy with Coca-Cola bottling dates back to the early 1900s,
when the first bottling operations began, marking the State as an important hub in the growth of
Coca-Cola's nationwide distribution network; and

WHEREAS, the enduring presence of Coca-Cola in American life reflects a tradition of
excellence and an ongoing dedication to the well-being of our people; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend the Coca-Cola Company and Tennessee's Coca-
Cola bottlers for their generous support of America250, recognize their historic and positive
impact on Tennessee and the United States, and extend our gratitude for their continued
commitment to building a brighter future for all Americans.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
