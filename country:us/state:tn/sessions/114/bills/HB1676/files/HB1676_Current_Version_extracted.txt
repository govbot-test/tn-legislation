SENATE BILL 1739
By Seal
HOUSE BILL 1676
By Eldridge
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 7,
Chapter 56, Part 2 and Title 64, Chapter 2, relative
to railroad authorities.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 64-2-101, is amended by deleting the
section and substituting instead:
(a) There is created the North East Tennessee railroad authority in the counties
of Anderson, Campbell, Scott, Claiborne, Union, and Grainger.
(b) This authority is intended to secure economic benefits to the counties of
Anderson, Campbell, Scott, Claiborne, Union, and Grainger by providing for the
continuation of railroad service in Anderson, Campbell, Scott, Claiborne, Union, and
Grainger counties.
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 64-2-102, is amended by deleting
subdivision (1) and substituting instead:
(1) Four (4) or more of the counties of Anderson, Campbell, Scott, Claiborne,
Union, and Grainger are authorized, by a vote of two-thirds (2/3) of their respective
governing bodies, to become members of the authority;
SECTION 3. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 64-2-110, is amended by deleting the
section and substituting instead:
All contracts of the authority must be entered into and executed in such manner
as may be prescribed by statutes, regulations, and procedures governing contracting by
county governments; provided, that a contract or acquisition by purchase of equipment,
apparatus, materials, or supplies involving more than the applicable maximum threshold
