Section 1:
By Hurt
SENATE BILL 2256
By Walley
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 44,
relative to animals used in agriculture.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 44-14-115, is amended by
designating the existing language as subsection (a) and adding the following as a new
subsection:
(b) An association may submit the annual report required by subsection (a) in
electronic form.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
