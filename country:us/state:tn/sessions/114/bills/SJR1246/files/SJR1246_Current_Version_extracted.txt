SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1246
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Rens Verhaar on his
selection as All-South Atlantic Conference in
men's tennis.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should recognize those incredibly successful student
athletes who have distinguished themselves as inspirational leaders among their peers; and
WHEREAS, Rens Verhaar of Tusculum University has been named third team All-South
Atlantic Conference (SAC) in men's tennis singles in honor of his outstanding performance this
season; and
WHEREAS, Rens Verhaar helped lead the Pioneers to a 17-4 overall record and 8-3
mark in SAC play; and
WHEREAS, Tusculum was ranked in the ITA Division II Top 30 for most of the season
and claimed three victories against ranked opponents; and
WHEREAS, Rens Verhaar compiled an 11-8 record in singles play this season for
Tusculum, posting an 8-7 mark in spring dual matches and a 4-3 record in SAC competition;
and
WHEREAS, a native of Amsterdam, The Netherlands, Rens Verhaar earned the
attention of the collegiate tennis world by winning 24 consecutive games across three matches;
and
WHEREAS, Rens Verhaar exemplifies the spirit, determination, and perseverance for
which Tennesseans are notable, and he is richly deserving of our approbation on this special
occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Rens Verhaar of

Tusculum University on his selection as third team All-South Atlantic Conference in men's tennis
singles and wish him much continued success both on and off the court.
