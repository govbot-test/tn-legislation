SENATE RESOLUTION 284
By Yager
A RESOLUTION to confirm the appointment of Tammy White to
the Tennessee Ethics Commission.
WHEREAS, pursuant to Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 3-6-103, Speaker of the
Senate Randy McNally has selected Tammy White of Knox County from a list of candidates
submitted by the Republican Caucus of the Senate as his appointee to the Tennessee Ethics
Commission; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 3-6-103, requires the Senate to
confirm such appointment by a two-thirds (2/3) vote; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that the Senate hereby confirms the
appointment of Tammy White to a four-year term on the Tennessee Ethics Commission, such
term to expire on January 1, 2030.
