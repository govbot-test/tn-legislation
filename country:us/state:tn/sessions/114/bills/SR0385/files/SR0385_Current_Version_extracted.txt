SENATE RESOLUTION 385
By Rose
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Neylan Walker, 2026
Valedictorian of Tipton Christian Academy.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should pay tribute to
those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to academic
excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Neylan Walker is one such exceptional student, who was recently named
Valedictorian of the 2026 graduating class at Tipton Christian Academy; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Mr. Walker has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in his remarkable high school career, Neylan Walker has earned an
exemplary grade point average while participating in numerous extracurricular activities; and
WHEREAS, his display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well for his
future success; and
WHEREAS, Neylan Walker's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride
for his family, his school, and his community; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Walker's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of the members of this legislative body; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we commend and honor the
2026 Tipton Christian Academy Valedictorian, Neylan Walker, on his impressive academic
achievement and extend to him our best wishes for a happy, successful future.
