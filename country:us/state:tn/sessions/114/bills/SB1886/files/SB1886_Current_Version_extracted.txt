Section 1:
By Lamberth
SENATE BILL 1886
By Johnson
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 4,
Chapter 3, Part 54, relative to the Tennessee
Sports Hall of Fame Act of 2019.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 4-3-5404(d)(2), is amended by
deleting the subdivision and substituting:
(2)
(A) An executive committee composed of six (6) members, including the:
(i) Commissioner;
(ii) Chair of the board;
(iii) Vice chair of the board; and
(iv) One (1) member representing each grand division of this
state.
(B) The commissioner shall serve as chair of the executive committee.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 4-3-5404(i), is amended by deleting
the last sentence of the subsection and substituting:
The hall of fame's personnel are subject to an annual performance review by the
commissioner or a direct supervisor of such personnel. Upon completion of the review,
findings shall be presented to the board.

Section 4:
SECTION 3. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 4-3-5405(a), is amended by adding
the following as a new subdivision:
Direct the mission of the hall of fame, including setting strategic direction and
overseeing financial stability and resources;

Section 5:
SECTION 4. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 4-3-5405(b)(1), is amended by
deleting the first sentence in the subdivision and substituting:
At least ninety (90) days prior to the beginning of each calendar year, the board
shall submit a plan for operation to the commissioner for review and approval.

Section 6:
SECTION 5. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
