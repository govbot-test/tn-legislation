HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1154
By Terry
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Lelan Statom for his
outstanding career in broadcast meteorology
and his dedicated service to the people of
Tennessee.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly take great pleasure in recognizing
those estimable citizens who, through their personal excellence and community commitment,
have distinguished themselves and brought honor to the State of Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, one such individual is Lelan Statom, who has served the citizens of Middle
Tennessee and beyond with distinction as a trusted broadcast meteorologist, providing accurate
weather forecasting and vital public safety information for more than three decades; and
WHEREAS, as Chief Meteorologist at WTVF-TV in Nashville, Mr. Statom has earned the
confidence and respect of viewers across Tennessee through his professionalism, integrity, and
dedication to keeping communities informed during severe weather events, including tornado
outbreaks, flooding, and winter storms; and
WHEREAS, throughout his distinguished career, Mr. Statom has played a critical role in
protecting the public's safety by delivering timely and reliable weather information, helping
Tennesseans prepare for and respond to dangerous conditions; and
WHEREAS, a highly respected leader in his profession, he has received numerous
honors and accolades, including Emmy Awards and recognition from national meteorological
organizations, reflecting his excellence in broadcast journalism and meteorology; and
WHEREAS, Lelan Statom has become a cherished and familiar figure to generations of
Tennessee families, and countless children eagerly have watched his forecasts in hopeful
anticipation of winter weather and the possibility of a snow day, making him a memorable and
trusted part of their childhood experience; and

WHEREAS, throughout his career, Lelan Statom has demonstrated the utmost
professionalism, ability, and integrity, winning the unbridled respect and admiration of his
colleagues and generations of the viewing public; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Lelan Statom for his exemplary career in
broadcast meteorology and his faithful service to the people of Tennessee, extending to him our
sincere appreciation for his many contributions to public safety and community life.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
