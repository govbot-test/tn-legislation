HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1112
By Capley
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Isabella Grace
Robinson, 2026 Top Ten Senior of Spring Hill
High School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Isabella Grace Robinson is one such exceptional student, who was recently
named a Top Ten Senior of the 2026 graduating class at Spring Hill High School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Ms. Robinson has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in her remarkable high school career, Isabella Robinson has earned an
exemplary grade point average while participating in numerous extracurricular activities,
including Concert Band and Art Club; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Robinson also volunteers with her church to help local schools at
Christmas time; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Robinson's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes
well for her future success as a student at Columbia State Community College and in her career
as a radiology mammography technologist; and
WHEREAS, Isabella Robinson's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of
pride for her parents, Suzanne Robinson and Roscar Robinson, her school, and her community;
and

WHEREAS, Ms. Robinson's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of the members of this General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor Isabella Grace Robinson on being named
a 2026 Spring Hill High School Top Ten Senior, salute her impressive academic achievement,
and extend to her our best wishes for a happy, successful future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
