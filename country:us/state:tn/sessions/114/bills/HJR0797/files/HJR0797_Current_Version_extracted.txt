HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 797
By Hakeem
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend the Chattanooga Gas
Company.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
recognize those Tennessee companies that have a long-standing commitment to providing
exceptional service to their customers; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable entity is the Chattanooga Gas Company (CGC), which
has proudly served its community and generations of Tennesseans for many years, providing
safe, reliable, and efficient services to the residents of Chattanooga and surrounding areas; and
WHEREAS, in 2024, the Chattanooga Gas Company contributed $35,000 in support of
the Low-Income Home Energy Assistance Program (LIHEAP) through United Way of the Ocoee
Region, La Paz, and The Caring Place; in 2025, CGC invested $35,000 in LIHEAP, which went
to the United Way of Chattanooga and The Caring Place; and
WHEREAS, additionally, CGC invested $125,000 in 2025 in support of the deserving
nonprofit organizations named, as well as the Chattanooga Area Foodbank; and
WHEREAS, the Chattanooga Gas Company invested an additional $64,000 in 2025 for
community organizations, such as Forgotten Child, Orange Grove, City Fields, and the
Community Kitchen, among others; and
WHEREAS, Chattanooga Gas Company volunteers committed 215 service hours in
2024 to the Tennessee River Gorge Trust, Orange Grove, and CSAS Career Fair, and 219
hours in 2025 to Orange Grove, United Way of Ocoee, and Hardy Elementary School, among
several others; and
WHEREAS, the Chattanooga Gas Company is intentional in its efforts to strengthen the
communities and customers it so ably serves; and
HJR0797
011859
- 1 -
WHEREAS, the Chattanooga Gas Company and its employees exemplify a unique
excellence that brings honor to their community, and it is wholly fitting that this business be
specially recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend the Chattanooga Gas Company on its legacy of
providing quality service to its customers and its community, wishing the iconic Tennessee
business much continued success in the future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
