HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 746
By Freeman
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate the Montgomery Bell
Academy Mock Trial team on winning the 2025
National High School Mock Trial Championship.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those successful high school students who, through their natural talents and
unflagging commitment to excellence, have distinguished themselves in national competition;
and
WHEREAS, such exemplary students are William Guo, Russell Howard, George Ma,
Kavneer Majhail, Nikhil Misra, Jasmaih Oberoi, Jack Rogers, Kenneth Royer, and Sammy
Thompson, who recently claimed the team's first national title at the 2025 National High School
Mock Trial Championship; and
WHEREAS, the Montgomery Bell Academy Mock Trial team traveled to Phoenix,
Arizona and competed in the historic Maricopa County Old Courthouse against forty-seven
additional state champions for the top prize; and
WHEREAS, members of the Montgomery Bell Academy Mock Trial championship team
received support from noncompeting team members Henry Bone, Witt Combs, and Alex Kaegi,
as well as coaches Wade Cowan, Tracy Hancock, and Marisa Combs Smith; and
WHEREAS, Montgomery Bell Academy senior Kavneer Majhail was named an
outstanding attorney; and
WHEREAS, the perseverance, determination, and work ethic of these young students
not only made possible their first national championship but also surely bode well for their
success in every future endeavor; and
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to acknowledge and applaud the members of the
Montgomery Bell Academy Mock Trial team for their outstanding success in national

competition and for serving as examples of the exceptional quality of the youth of Tennessee;
now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate the members and coaches of the Montgomery
Bell Academy Mock Trial team on winning the 2025 National High School Mock Trial

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
