SENATE RESOLUTION 256
By Walley
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Cassidy Dickson on
her selection as Lawrence County's 911
Dispatcher of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should pause to specially recognize those outstanding
public servants who have devoted their careers to providing exceptional service to Tennessee
residents; and
WHEREAS, Cassidy Dickson is one such estimable employee, who has distinguished
herself as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, a 911 dispatcher for Lawrence County E-911, Ms. Dickson was recently
named the 2025 911 Dispatcher of the Year, a most befitting honor that recognizes the positive
influence she has on so many lives; and
WHEREAS, Cassidy Dickson is an extremely compassionate and empathetic
professional who works assiduously to provide a calm and steady presence with every call she
answers; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Dickson serves as an inspiration for her unwavering commitment to
serving others; and
WHEREAS, Cassidy Dickson truly epitomizes the dedicated professional who is wholly
committed to the highest ideals of public service, and she should be specially recognized; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby honor and
congratulate Cassidy Dickson on being named Lawrence County's 911 Dispatcher of the Year,
commend her exceptional service to the residents of her community, salute her professional
expertise and personal excellence, and wish her all the best in her future endeavors.
