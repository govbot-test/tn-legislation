HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1472
By Reeves
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Friends of Franklin Parks
on the occasion of its fifteenth anniversary.
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that the members of this General Assembly should honor
those organizations that, through their dedication and civic engagement, contribute to the
preservation and enhancement of Tennessee's natural and community resources; and
WHEREAS, Friends of Franklin Parks is one such estimable organization, which is
celebrating its fifteenth anniversary, marking a decade and a half of service to the citizens of
Franklin and Williamson County; and
WHEREAS, founded in 2011, Friends of Franklin Parks has played an important role in
fostering a spirit of stewardship and conservation through public-private partnership, supporting
the improvement of local parks and green spaces; and
WHEREAS, a Tennessee 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization for the past ten years, Friends
of Franklin Parks was established to bridge the gap between government and community
funding; and
WHEREAS, working closely with the City of Franklin and its Parks Department, the
organization is dedicated to identifying needs within the city's park system, casting a vision for
future enhancements, and fostering connectivity among existing public open spaces; and
WHEREAS, among its notable contributions, it has been instrumental in the preservation
and continued development of the Park at Harlinsdale Farm, where the organization maintains
its offices, one of Franklin's most treasured historic and recreational assets; and
WHEREAS, Friends of Franklin Parks has also supported the creation of inclusive
recreational opportunities, including the development of Franklin's first all-inclusive playground,
Ellie G's Dream World, at the future Pearlene M. Bransford Complex Park, ensuring that

important work.
