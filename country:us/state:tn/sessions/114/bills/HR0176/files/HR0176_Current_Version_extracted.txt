HOUSE RESOLUTION 176
By Shaw
A RESOLUTION to commend Kennedy Havard for exemplary
service as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, the members of this legislative body are pleased to
recognize certain persons who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the
betterment of the legislative process; and
WHEREAS, Kennedy Havard is one such individual, who has distinguished herself in
her role as a legislative intern for Representative Johnny Shaw; Ms. Havard's work has resulted
in innumerable benefits for the legislators she assisted and the General Assembly as a whole;
and
WHEREAS, a native of Chicago, Illinois, Kennedy Havard is the daughter of Shiquesha
Downey and Tyrone Downey and was instilled with the passion for leadership, excellence, and
public service; and
WHEREAS, throughout high school, Ms. Havard was actively involved in student
government, served as cheer captain, and was a proud member of the Honor Society, all of
which helped shape her confidence, discipline, and dedication to serving others; and
WHEREAS, a senior at Tennessee State University (TSU), where she is highly regarded
by her peers, Kennedy Havard has achieved academic success while pursuing a degree in
political science and urban studies; she has a strong interest in education policy, social justice,
and community development; and
WHEREAS, during her collegiate career, Ms. Havard has gained experience working in
educational and childcare settings, further strengthening her passion for youth development and
learning; she currently serves as a Resident Assistant in Wilson Hall, where she mentors and
supports first-year students as they transition into college life; and

WHEREAS, Kennedy Havard's leadership extends beyond the residence hall as she
serves as the Undergraduate Member-at-Large for Zeta Phi Beta Sorority, Inc., for the State of
Tennessee, as well as a university ambassador; she is a member of Phi Alpha Delta Law
Fraternity and participates in the OSA NPHC branch; and
WHEREAS, following graduation, Ms. Havard plans to continue her education at TSU
and pursue a master's degree in education with a concentration in instructional leadership,
further demonstrating her commitment to serving the public and advocating for equitable
opportunities for all students; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize herself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further her studies, Kennedy Havard brought her many
abilities and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2026 session of the One Hundred
Fourteenth General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, throughout the 2026 legislative session, Ms. Havard has surpassed all
criteria that define a devoted and conscientious legislative intern; and
WHEREAS, Kennedy Havard has performed her many duties with extraordinary insight
and rare decorum; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we commend
Kennedy Havard for outstanding service as a legislative intern for Representative Johnny Shaw
and extend to her our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
