Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 68, Chapter 102, Part 1, is amended by
adding the following as a new section:
(a) The volunteer firefighter vehicle grant program is established to annually
provide grants to select volunteer fire departments to assist with local match
requirements for federal grants to purchase vehicles. The commissioner of commerce
and insurance shall establish the amount the state contributes. However, the grant
program must not fund the total local match portion in any year.
(b) The commissioner shall strive to distribute grant program funding
proportionally based on the number of recognized volunteer fire departments serving
each of the three (3) grand divisions of this state.
(c) The commissioner shall decide which grants to award and shall disburse the
grants to the selected volunteer fire departments. The commissioner shall strive to
expend all funds appropriated to the grant program each year, and any funds remaining
must remain available for expenditure for the program created by subsection (a) and the
volunteer firefighter equipment and training grant program created in § 68-102-154(b)(1).
Any funds remaining must not revert to the general fund but remain available for
expenditure in subsequent fiscal years for either grant program.
(d) It is the legislative intent to fund the grant program created by subsection (a)
by providing an initial appropriation of one million dollars ($1,000,000) in the
appropriation act for fiscal year 2026-2027.

(e) The commissioner shall promulgate rules to establish guidelines for
evaluating grant requests and determining which volunteer fire departments receive
grants pursuant to this section. The rules must be promulgated in accordance with the
Uniform Administrative Procedures Act, compiled in title 4, chapter 5.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. For the purpose of promulgating rules, this act takes effect upon becoming
a law, the public welfare requiring it. For all other purposes, this act takes effect at 12:01 a.m.
on July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.
