Senate Commerce and Labor 1
Amendment No. 1 to SB2338
Bailey
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 2338* House Bill No. 2446
by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 68-102-154(e)(1), is amended by
deleting ", other than fire trucks or vehicles".
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 68-102-154(a)(1), is amended by
deleting the subdivision and substituting:
(1) "Firefighting equipment" means the equipment used by a firefighter to contain
or extinguish fires and to protect the life of the firefighter;
SECTION 3.
(a) Sections 1 and 3 of this act take effect upon becoming a law, the public
welfare requiring it, and apply to grants awarded after June 30, 2026.
(b) Section 2 of this act is subject to Senate Bill 1865 / House Bill 2531 of the
114th General Assembly becoming a law and, upon such occurrence, takes effect at
12:01 a.m. on the effective date of that act, the public welfare requiring it.
