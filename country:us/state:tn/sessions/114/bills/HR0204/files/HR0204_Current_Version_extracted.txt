HOUSE RESOLUTION 204
By Barrett
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend the Creek Wood High
School Red Hawks upon winning the TSSAA
Division I, Region 6 boys' bowling championship.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should salute those
student athletes who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as true
champions of whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such exemplary group of individuals is the Creek Wood High School
boys' bowling team, whose members have proven themselves to be the fiercest of competitors
and truly worthy of admiration by winning the TSSAA Division I, Region 6 boys' bowling
championship held at Tenn-Pin Alley in Columbia; and
WHEREAS, this season's stellar finish comes after the Red Hawks garnered the TSSAA
Division I boys' bowling state championship in 2025; and
WHEREAS, in this year's regional finals, Creek Wood built an early lead behind strong
performances from seniors Jayden Morgan and Ethan King, but, ultimately, the match came
down to the final frame; and
WHEREAS, in the tenth frame, Creek Wood anchor bowler Abe Monosmith needed at
least eight pins with a spare to secure the championship; he delivered, lifting the Red Hawks to
the Region 6 title; and
WHEREAS, the Red Hawks' superb performance would not have been possible without
the stellar leadership of their coaches and the unfailing support of their families, their fans, and
their community; and
WHEREAS, the members of the Creek Wood High School boys' bowling team epitomize
all that is good in today's student athletes, as they have achieved a premier level of success in
competition while exhibiting the highest degree of character in all aspects of life; and

WHEREAS, the perseverance, determination, work ethic, and talent of this group of
young men not only made possible an outstanding season but also surely bode well for their
future success; and
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to acknowledge and applaud the Creek Wood High
School Red Hawks for their championship season on the lanes; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and
commend the members and coaches of the Creek Wood High School Red Hawks upon winning
the TSSAA Division I, Region 6 boys' bowling championship and extend to them our best
wishes for every continued success in all their future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
