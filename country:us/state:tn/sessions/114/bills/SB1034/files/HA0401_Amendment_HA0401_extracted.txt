AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 1034* House Bill No. 1071
by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 4-1-412(a)(7), is amended by adding
a new subdivision (C):
(C) An official governmental seal of a city or county government that contains
imagery representative of any historic conflict, historic entity, historic event, historic
figure, or historic organization; and
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
