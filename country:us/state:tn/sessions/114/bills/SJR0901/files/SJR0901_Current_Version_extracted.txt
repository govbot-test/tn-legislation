SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 901
By Powers
A RESOLUTION to commend Alexandria Tetterton for exemplary
service as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this legislative body is pleased to recognize certain
persons who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment of the
legislative process; and
WHEREAS, Alexandria Tetterton is one such individual, who has distinguished herself in
her role as a legislative intern for Senator Bill Powers; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Tetterton's work, her exceptionally personable demeanor, and her
professionalism have resulted in innumerable benefits for the legislators she assisted, the
constituents of Senate District 22, and the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, a student at Austin Peay State University, where she is highly regarded by
her peers, Alexandria Tetterton has achieved academic success while pursuing a Bachelor of
Science degree in Political Science; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Tetterton holds several roles on campus and has achieved a number of
accomplishments during her academic career; she serves as Secretary of the Foreign Policy
Club, Chief Justice of the Student Government Association, President of the Pre-Law Society,
and member of the President's Emerging Leaders Program; and
WHEREAS, Alexandria Tetterton is a compassionate volunteer who has served with
Blood Assurance and YAIPAK; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize herself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further her studies, Alexandria Tetterton brought her many
abilities and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2026 session of the One Hundred
Fourteenth General Assembly; and

WHEREAS, Ms. Tetterton has performed her many duties with extraordinary insight and
