SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 852
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Bobby Broyles on his
induction into the TSSAA Hall of Fame.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should specially
recognize those individuals who have performed their duties with uncommon devotion and
enthusiasm and who, through their magnificent exploits, have brought honor to this State; and
WHEREAS, one such individual is Bobby Broyles, who has been a celebrated figure of
Chuckey-Doak High School for decades as a coach and role model; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Broyles is one of only eight individuals being inducted into the
Tennessee Secondary School Athletic Association (TSSAA) Hall of Fame on April 13, 2026, an
honor reserved for those who have demonstrated exceptional dedication, leadership, and
impact in the field of Tennessee high school athletics; and
WHEREAS, Bobby Broyles's remarkable journey as a coach began in 1955 at Chuckey
High School when he himself had not been out of high school very long; in 1959, when Chuckey
High School and Doak High School consolidated, he led the charge to establish Chuckey-Doak
athletic programs and is credited with giving the school the Black Knights mascot; and
WHEREAS, during his tenure at Chuckey and Chuckey-Doak, Mr. Broyles amassed 542
career wins as coach of the boys' and girls' basketball teams; he helped lead the Black Knights
to 407 wins with 277 losses, two district championships, three district runner-up titles, and
eleven region tournament appearances; and
WHEREAS, he led the Lady Black Knights to a record of 135-73, three district runner-up
finishes, and five region tournament appearances; and

WHEREAS, for a number of years, Mr. Broyles coached sports in Greene County,
including several trips to the state tournament as an assistant for South Greene's basketball
program; and
WHEREAS, he also spent several years sharing the bench with his son, Sam, who is the
current boys' basketball coach at Chuckey-Doak High School; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Broyles's positive impact on the lives of students, athletes, coaches,
and administrators will continue to resound for many years to come, and it is appropriate that we
acknowledge his contributions to high school athletics in Tennessee; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Bobby Broyles upon his
induction into the TSSAA Hall of Fame, where he will be immortalized alongside his peers in the
annals of Tennessee athletic history.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
