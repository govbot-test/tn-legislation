Section 1:
By Haile
HOUSE BILL 2326
By Williams
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 9-
4-603, relative to earnings of the state pooled
investment fund.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 9-4-603, is amended by adding the
following as a new subsection:
(j) Notwithstanding this section or another law to the contrary, on or before June
30 of each year, the treasurer shall allocate and deposit ten percent (10%) of the
investment income earned by the pooled investment fund in that fiscal year to the state
highway fund.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
