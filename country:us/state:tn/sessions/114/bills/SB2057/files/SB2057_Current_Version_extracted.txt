HOUSE BILL 2385
By Vaughan
SENATE BILL 2057
By Taylor
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 7,
Chapter 35; Title 7, Chapter 82 and Title 68,
Chapter 221, relative to sewer systems.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-82-710(a)(1), is amended by
deleting the subdivision and substituting:
Has an existing gravity sewer line located on or adjacent to the owner's property;
and
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
