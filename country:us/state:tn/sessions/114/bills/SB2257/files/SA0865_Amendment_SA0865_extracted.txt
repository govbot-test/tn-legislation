by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 5, Chapter 1, Part 1, is amended by
adding the following as a new section:
(a) Before January 1, 2027, each county shall create an ethics commission for
the county to aid in protecting the integrity of the processes of government and
preventing abuse.
(b) No later than January 1, 2027, unless the governing body of a county has
constituted a fully functioning local ethics board of at least five (5) voting members and
one (1) nonvoting ethics officer, ethics oversight of such county reverts to the state
ethics commission until and unless the state ethics commission determines, by a formal
written decision, that the local ethics board is in full compliance with all state ethics
requirements for counties.
(c) This section applies to any county having a population of more than nine
hundred thousand (900,000), according to the 2020 federal census or any subsequent
federal census.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
