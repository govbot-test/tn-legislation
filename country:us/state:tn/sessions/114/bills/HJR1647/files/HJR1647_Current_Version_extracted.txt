HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1647
By Salinas
A RESOLUTION to honor Luis Alexander Plascencia for his many
contributions to the Town of Cordova and its
social media presence.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should specially
recognize those outstanding citizens who have dedicated themselves to improving the quality of
life in their communities; and
WHEREAS, Luis Alexander Plascencia is a proud resident of the Town of Cordova and
serves as his community's representative and advocate in the realm of social media; and
WHEREAS, Luis Alexander Plascencia is the founder and organizer of the Cordova
community's social media page, which features the inspiring motto: "Farms, Flowers, and
Fellowship"; and
WHEREAS, a true wunderkind, Mr. Plascencia started the Town of Cordova page in
2024, when he was only nineteen years old; and
WHEREAS, in just two years, Mr. Plascencia has created and enhanced a social media
page that helps foster community unity and engagement, support small businesses, organize
neighborhood events, and promote communication and connection among neighbors; and
WHEREAS, through hard work and dedication, Luis Alexander Plascencia has helped
uplift every corner of Cordova to the best of his ability, and his neighbors are most appreciative
of his efforts; and
WHEREAS, equally as impressive as his success as a community organizer is the fact
that he accomplished so much at such an early age; he only recently celebrated his twenty-first
birthday; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Plascencia has evidenced the greatest integrity and humility in service
to the Town of Cordova and its social media presence; and

WHEREAS, Luis Alexander Plascencia has proven himself to be wholly committed to the
noble precepts of public service that have earned Tennessee recognition as the Volunteer
State, and he is most deserving of our approbation; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor Luis Alexander Plascencia for founding, organizing, and
moderating the Town of Cordova's community page on social media, praise his work in
motivating civic engagement and connecting his neighbors, and extend to him our best wishes
for much continued success and happiness in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
