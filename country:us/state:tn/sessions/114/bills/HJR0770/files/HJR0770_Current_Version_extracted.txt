HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 770
By Farmer
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Brooke White on her
selection as the 2025-2026 Sevier County
School System Grades 5-8 Teacher of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
specially recognize those outstanding teachers who have devoted their careers to providing the
highest-quality education for Tennessee's students; and
WHEREAS, Brooke White is one such estimable educator, who has distinguished
herself as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, Ms. White has been selected as the 2025-2026 Sevier County School
System Grades 5-8 Teacher of the Year, a most befitting honor that recognizes the positive
influence she has on so many young lives; and
WHEREAS, a math and science elementary teacher, Brooke White has taught for more
than ten years; she is an extremely talented and compassionate educator who works
assiduously to motivate her students to reach their full potential, both as scholars and as
citizens; and
WHEREAS, throughout her career, Ms. White has represented the Sevier County
School Community by participating in the 2023 Dancing With the Stars competition for United
Way, which earned over $40,000 to support seventeen local nonprofits; implemented Rock Your
School at Catlettsburg Elementary, an initiative to increase student engagement and embrace
innovation among students and teachers; and collaborated with fellow educators to create a
hybrid learning curriculum for fifth graders during the 2020 COVID-19 pandemic; and
WHEREAS, a model servant leader, Brooke White is always willing to sacrifice her
personal time to give extra assistance to a struggling student; she has dedicated herself to

addressing the whole personhood of her students, rather than attempting to isolate their
academic value; and
WHEREAS, Ms. White has represented two schools as Teacher of the Year, completed
her doctorate degree, earned Level 5 distinctions on TVAAS reports, and exceeded benchmark
assessment averages; and
WHEREAS, Brooke White serves as an inspiration to her colleagues and students alike,
and her friendship and counsel are highly valued by those who are fortunate to know her; and
WHEREAS, Ms. White truly epitomizes the dedicated teacher who is wholly committed
to the highest ideals of public service and quality education for all, and she should be specially
recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and congratulate Brooke White on being chosen as the
2025-2026 Sevier County School System Grades 5-8 Teacher of the Year, commend her
exceptional service to the children of Tennessee, salute her professional expertise and personal
excellence, and wish her all the best in her future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
