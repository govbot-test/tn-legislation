HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1540
By Littleton
A RESOLUTION to honor Vice President Anna Beth Thompson
and the University of Tennessee, Knoxville's
Student Government Association on the
occasion of the organization's 105th anniversary.
WHEREAS, it is the privilege of the members of this General Assembly to honor those
students and student-led organizations that greatly contribute to the institutions of higher
learning of this State; and
WHEREAS, this year marks the 105th anniversary of the Student Government
Association (SGA) on the campus of the University of Tennessee, Knoxville (UTK); and
WHEREAS, Anna Beth Thompson of Dickson County was recently elected to serve as
vice president of the SGA at UTK for the 2026-2027 academic year, a point of pride for her
community and her family, most especially her parents, Angie and Andrew Thompson; and
WHEREAS, with Ms. Thompson's ascension to the SGA's second-highest office, the
organization marks a historic milestone—for the first time in its more than a century history, two
women hold the offices of president and vice president; and
WHEREAS, this year also marked the second-largest turnout in UTK's SGA election
history, with 7,254 student voters taking to the polls; Ms. Thompson won with 4,414 votes,
which accounted for 63.8 percent of all votes cast; and
WHEREAS, throughout her campaign, Anna Beth Thompson pushed to strengthen the
connection between SGA and the student body; she hopes to increase student engagement on
social media and to refine operational effectiveness of student government; and
WHEREAS, it is most fitting that we honor the Student Government Association at the
University of Tennessee, Knoxville, and its elected leadership during this historic year; now,
therefore,
HJR1540
018231
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor Vice President Anna Beth Thompson and the University of
Tennessee, Knoxville's Student Government Association on the occasion of the organization's
105th anniversary.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
- 2 - 018231
