AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 683 House Bill No. 382*
by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 49, Chapter 1, Part 4, is amended by
adding the following as a new section:
An LEA may implement the law enforcement against drugs and violence
(L.E.A.D.) program in schools operated by the LEA. An LEA's implementation of the
L.E.A.D. program may be in lieu of the DARE program.
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
