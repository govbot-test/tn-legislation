HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1283
By Bulso
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Tristiano Alfano on
being named the 2025 WILLCO Awards Boys'
Soccer Player of the Year.
WHEREAS, we are pleased to recognize and commend those successful high school
athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring honor to Tennessee and serve as
exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable individual is Tristiano Alfano of Franklin High School, an
athlete of extraordinary talent, skill, and dedication, who has proven himself a formidable
competitor in soccer; and
WHEREAS, in recognition of his success on the field throughout the school year,
Tristiano Alfano was named Boys' Soccer Player of the Year at the 2025 WILLCO Awards
celebration; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Alfano scored a team-best sixteen goals and had six assists for the
Admirals, who advanced to the Class AAA State tournament; and
WHEREAS, Tristiano Alfano epitomizes all that is good in today's student athlete, as he
has achieved a high level of success in sports while evincing great character; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Alfano's notable achievements are a source of great pride for his family,
his community, and his school, and it is appropriate that we recognize this accomplished
athlete; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Tristiano Alfano on being named the 2025
WILLCO Awards Boys' Soccer Player of the Year, wishing him much success and happiness in
his future endeavors.
