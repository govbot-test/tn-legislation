HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1184
By Reneau
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Sergeant Jasin Holmes
of the Chattanooga Police Department upon his
receipt of the Chief's Award.
WHEREAS, our nation was conceived by individuals who were willing to sacrifice their
personal concerns to ensure the public safety and welfare, and the Volunteer State is especially
proud to be the home of so many valiant individuals who have performed above and beyond the
call of duty in service of public safety; and
WHEREAS, one such individual is Sergeant Jasin Holmes of the Chattanooga Police
Department, who was recently honored with the Chattanooga Police Department Chief's Award,
in recognition of his exemplary service and dedication; and
WHEREAS, Sergeant Holmes has distinguished himself through his unwavering
commitment to the community he serves, demonstrating that effective policing extends beyond
enforcement to include building relationships and investing in people; and
WHEREAS, most notably, Sergeant Jasin Holmes has devoted his personal time to
mentoring at-risk youth in the community, providing guidance, encouragement, and support to
young people and helping to shape their futures through his consistent presence and
leadership; and
WHEREAS, the efforts of Sergeant Holmes have made a lasting impact on the
Chattanooga Police Department and the community it serves; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Sergeant Jasin Holmes of the Chattanooga Police
Department upon his receipt of the Chief's Award.
HJR1184
016023
- 1 -
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
