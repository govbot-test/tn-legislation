SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1257
By Akbari
A RESOLUTION to congratulate John Patrick and Susan Marie
Hallett upon the occasion of their forty-fifth
wedding anniversary.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly take great pleasure in paying tribute
to those successful marriages that have stood the test of time to serve as exemplars for present
and future generations; and
WHEREAS, on September 11, 1981, in Assumption, Ohio, John Patrick Hallett and
Susan Marie Beroske were joined in holy matrimony, and since that fateful day, this outstanding
couple has faced the world as one, basking in the glow of their love for each other during both
life's joys and sorrows; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Hallett is the son of Lester Sherman Hallett and Thelma Netta Mann
Hallett; Mrs. Hallett is the daughter of Ronald Francis Beroske and Janet Marie LaVigne
Beroske; and
WHEREAS, the pair met through mutual friends at a party in 1976, when Susan Hallett
was a junior in high school; she went on to earn her art degree from Bowling Green State
University, all the while Mr. Hallett made his intention of marrying her known. She accepted his
third proposal, just before traveling to Brazil to work on illustrations for a sign language book
that was published in 1981; and
WHEREAS, John Patrick Hallett worked in agricultural sales for more than thirty years,
most of which was with AGCO Corp; he helped build local soccer teams, including coaching
soccer for kindergarten students and co-founding a local girls' team; and
WHEREAS, Susan Marie Hallett is an artist who spent years painting beautiful murals in
homes, as well as detailed and personalized Christmas ornaments and bricks; she has assisted

WHEREAS, the Halletts have a deep love of cooking, music, travel, and sailing; they

WHEREAS, for forty-five years, this blessed union has grown and prospered as an

WHEREAS, marriages such as the Halletts' truly represent the best and brightest of our
society, as they remind us of the intrinsic value of continuity and commitment in this ever-
changing world; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we congratulate John Patrick and Susan Marie
Hallett on the occasion of their forty-fifth wedding anniversary and extend to them our warmest
wishes for continued happiness.
