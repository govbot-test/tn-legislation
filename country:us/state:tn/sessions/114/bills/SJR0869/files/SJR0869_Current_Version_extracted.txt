SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 869
By Seal
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend the members and
coaches of the Sevier County High School
Smoky Bears football team.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that this General Assembly should salute those student athletes
who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as true champions of
whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such exemplary group of young athletes is the Sevier County High
School Smoky Bears football team, whose members have all proven themselves to be the
fiercest of competitors and truly worthy of admiration by finishing runner-up in the 2025
Tennessee Secondary School Athletic Association (TSSAA) Division I, Class 5A state
championship; and
WHEREAS, a high school football powerhouse, the Sevier County Smoky Bears
reached the state championship game after defeating Knoxville West 34-9, Walker Valley 42-7,
Lincoln County 28-24, and Knoxville Halls 49-13 to make their fourth championship game
appearance; and
WHEREAS, the Smoky Bears enjoyed the stellar leadership and extensive football
knowledge of Head Coach Todd Loveday and his staff and the tremendous support of their
families, their fans, and their community; and
WHEREAS, the members of the Sevier County High School Smoky Bears football team
epitomize all that is good in today's student athletes, as they have achieved a premier level of
success in competition while exhibiting the highest degree of character in all aspects of life; and
WHEREAS, the perseverance, determination, work ethic, and talent of this group of
young men not only made possible a special season but also surely bode well for their future
success; and

WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly find it appropriate to pause in their
deliberations to acknowledge and applaud the members of the Sevier County High School
Smoky Bears football team for their outstanding performance this season; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and commend the members and
coaches of the Sevier County High School Smoky Bears football team upon their runner-up
finish in the 2025 TSSAA Division I, Class 5A State championship and extend to them our best
wishes for every continued success in all their future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
