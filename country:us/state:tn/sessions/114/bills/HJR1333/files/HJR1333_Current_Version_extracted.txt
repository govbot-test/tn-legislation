HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1333
By Travis
A RESOLUTION to honor Jim Reed on the occasion of his
retirement.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
specially recognize those estimable public servants whose lives exemplify dedicated service,
integrity, and commitment to the people of Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, Jim Reed has rendered exemplary service to the citizens of Rhea County,
serving as a county commissioner for fifteen years, during which time he demonstrated
steadfast leadership, sound judgment, and an unwavering commitment to the public good; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Reed has further distinguished himself through his service as chairman
of the Rhea County Commission for nine years, providing capable leadership and guidance and
exhibiting a keen ability to address the needs of his community; and
WHEREAS, after years of devoted public service, Jim Reed will retire at the conclusion
of his present term, bringing to a close a tenure marked by integrity, diligence, and commitment
to the Rhea County community; and
WHEREAS, through his leadership in the Spring City Lions Club, including service as
president, Mr. Reed helped champion legislation that enabled the distribution of donated
eyeglasses to individuals in need, thereby improving access to vision care for underserved
Tennesseans; and
WHEREAS, as a cofounder of the Rhea Alliance, Jim Reed played a key role in
advancing community initiatives, including the establishment of the Scholarship Bass
Tournament, reflecting his commitment to expanding opportunities for local students and
families; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Reed has further contributed to his community through service on
numerous boards and organizations, including the Rhea County E-911 Board, the Rhea Medical

Center Board, the Rhea County Planning Commission, the Rhea County Fire Department
Association, the Rhea County Fire Authority Board, and the Tennessee County Commissioners

WHEREAS, in addition to his community involvement, Jim Reed is the co-owner and

WHEREAS, Mr. Reed is wholly committed to the noble precepts of public service that

BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Jim Reed for his fifteen
years of distinguished service to the citizens of Rhea County as county commissioner,
extending our sincere appreciation for his many contributions and our best wishes for success
and happiness in all his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
