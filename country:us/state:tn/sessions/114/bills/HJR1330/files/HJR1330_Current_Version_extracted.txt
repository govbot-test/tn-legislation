HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1330
By Reedy
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend William "Billy" Bunce for
exemplary service as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this General Assembly is pleased to recognize
certain persons who have committed themselves, through their exceptional service, to the
betterment of the legislative process; and
WHEREAS, William Bunce is one such individual, who has distinguished himself in his
role as a legislative intern for Representative Jay Reedy; his work has resulted in innumerable
benefits for the legislators he assisted and the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, a student at Vanderbilt University, where he is highly regarded by his peers,
Billy Bunce is in the Master of Public Health program, focusing on health policy and
management; and
WHEREAS, he previously served as a Health and Vaccine Policy intern with Avalere
and a research assistant with the Vanderbilt University Department of Health Policy, as well as
a research technician and lab intern at the University of North Carolina Kidney Center; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Bunce holds a Bachelor of Arts degree in Political Science and
Government from William Peace University; he has earned national and international
certifications from the Regulatory Affairs Processionals Society and attained the rank of Eagle
Scout with the Boys Scouts of America, also serving as assistant leader and quartermaster; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize himself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further his studies, Billy Bunce brought his many abilities
and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2026 session of the One Hundred Fourteenth
General Assembly; and
HJR1330
017279
- 1 -
WHEREAS, throughout the 2026 legislative session, Mr. Bunce has demonstrated a
strong work ethic, attention to detail, and a commitment to public service, gaining valuable
insight into the workings of State government while representing Vanderbilt University and the
broader student community with distinction; and
WHEREAS, Billy Bunce has performed his many duties with extraordinary insight and
rare decorum; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby commend William "Billy" Bunce for outstanding service as a
legislative intern for Representative Jay Reedy and extend to him our best wishes for every
future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
