HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 934
By Powers
A RESOLUTION to honor Donnie Poston of Campbell County
upon his receipt of the Elbert K. Fretwell
Outstanding Educator Award.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should salute those citizens who, through their
extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as community leaders of whom we can all
be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such noteworthy person is Donnie Poston, who was recently honored
with the Elbert K. Fretwell Outstanding Educator Award by the Great Smoky Mountain Council
of the Boy Scouts of America (BSA); and
WHEREAS, the Elbert K. Fretwell Outstanding Educator Award is presented to those
who work in education and follow the BSA's mission "to prepare young people to make ethical
and moral choices over their lifetimes by instilling in them the values of the Scout Oath and
Law"; and
WHEREAS, a lifelong resident of Campbell County, Mr. Poston has enjoyed a fifty-four-
year career as an educator and currently serves as the director of Campbell County Christian
Academy after having served as Director of Schools for Campbell County for four years,
elementary instructional supervisor for the Campbell County Department of Education for eight
years, nineteen years as principal of Valley View Elementary School, and three years as a
teacher at Pinecrest Elementary School; and
WHEREAS, a graduate of LaFollette High School, Mr. Poston holds Bachelor of Science
and Master of Arts degrees in Elementary Education, a Master of Arts +45 degree in
Educational Administration and Supervision, a Ph.D. degree in Professional Counseling, and

professional endorsements in Elementary Grades 1-9, Superintendent/Director, Administrative
Supervisor, Professional Administration PreK-12, and Career Level I Supervisor; and
WHEREAS, immediately prior to joining Campbell County Christian Academy for the
second time, Mr. Poston served as chief operating officer and prevention coordinator/educator
for Generation Rx and Generation Hope at Community Health of East Tennessee, Inc.; and
WHEREAS, an active part of his community, he has rendered sterling service as a
member of the LaFollette Medical Center Advisory Board, CASA Board, Campbell County
Commission, Campbell County Election Commission, Lincoln Memorial Alumni Association, and
the Campbell County Relief Fund; and
WHEREAS, a man of abiding faith, Donnie Poston has been an active member of Forks
Grove Baptist Church for sixty-five years, where he has taught Sunday school for fifty-seven
years; and
WHEREAS, devoted to his family, Donnie Poston enjoys the love and support of his wife
of forty-eight years, Cathy Heatherly Poston, and their child, Corey, and their three
grandchildren, Caden, Cara, and Nova Grace; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Poston epitomizes the spirit and commitment to family and community
that are characteristic of a true Tennessean; and
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to acknowledge and applaud Donnie Poston for his
dedication to the State of Tennessee and its citizens; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we congratulate Donnie Poston upon his receipt of the Boy Scouts of
America's Elbert K. Fretwell Outstanding Educator Award, thank him for his meritorious service
to the students of Tennessee, and extend to him our best wishes for every future success.
