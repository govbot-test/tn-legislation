SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 826
By Walley
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Rushil Patel, 2026
Salutatorian of Chester County High School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Rushil Patel is one such exceptional student, who was recently named
Salutatorian of the 2026 graduating class at Chester County High School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Mr. Patel has achieved outstanding academic success
and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country; and
WHEREAS, in his remarkable high school career, Rushil Patel has earned an exemplary
grade point average of 99.319 while participating in numerous extracurricular activities; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Patel's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well for
his future successes as a student at the University of North Alabama, where he will major in
biology on the pre-med track; and
WHEREAS, Rushil Patel's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for
his parents, Jignesh and Tejal Patel; his brother, Dhanin Patel; his school; and his community;
and
WHEREAS, Rushil Patel attributes his success to his parents pushing him to succeed
and his own desires to succeed and compete with his friends; and
WHEREAS, in his free time, Mr. Patel especially enjoys playing baseball and going to
the gym; in the summer, he plans to work with his parents and take some college classes; and
SJR0826
013449
- 1 -
WHEREAS, Mr. Patel's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of the members of this General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor the 2026 Chester
County High School Salutatorian, Rushil Patel, on his impressive academic achievement and
extend to him our best wishes for a happy, successful future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
