Section 1:
By White
HOUSE BILL 2014
By Littleton
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 39;
Title 40 and Title 55, relative to vehicular
homicide.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 39-13-213(b)(2), is amended by
adding the following as a new subdivision:
(H) If a person convicted of a violation of subdivision (a)(2) is, as a result of the
same course of conduct, also convicted of a violation of § 55-10-101, then the person
shall be punished for the violation of subdivision (a)(2) as a Range II offender; however,
the sentence imposed upon the person may, if appropriate, be within Range III but in no
case shall the sentence be lower than Range II.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.
