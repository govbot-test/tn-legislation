SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 849
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Elijah Spence of
Gatlinburg-Pittman High School upon being
named All-County in football.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly are pleased to recognize and
commend those successful high school athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring
honor to Tennessee and serve as exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable individual is Elijah Spence of Gatlinburg-Pittman High
School in Sevier County, an athlete of extraordinary talent, skill, and dedication, who has proven
himself a formidable competitor on the football field; and
WHEREAS, Elijah Spence was recently named to the All-County team in recognition of
his excellence on the gridiron for Gatlinburg-Pittman High School; and
WHEREAS, a senior three-way player, Mr. Spence was also named to the Tennessee
Sports Writers Association All-State and Tennessee Football Coaches Association All-State
teams and the All-Region 1-3A first team; and
WHEREAS, as a wide receiver, he recorded 57 receptions for 1,036 yards and 14
touchdowns and averaged 18.2 yards per reception for the 2025 season; and
WHEREAS, on defense, Elijah Spence recorded 53 tackles, 10 passes broken up, and 5
interceptions; and
WHEREAS, on special teams, Mr. Spence returned one kickoff for a touchdown and had
7 punt returns for 189 yards; and
WHEREAS, Elijah Spence epitomizes all that is good in today's student athlete, as he
has achieved a high level of success in sports while evincing great character both on and off the
field; and
SJR0849
013249
- 1 -
WHEREAS, Mr. Spence's notable achievement is a source of great pride for his family,
his community, and his school, and it is appropriate that we recognize this accomplished
athlete; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Elijah Spence of
Gatlinburg-Pittman High School on being named to the All-County football team, wishing him
much success and happiness in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
