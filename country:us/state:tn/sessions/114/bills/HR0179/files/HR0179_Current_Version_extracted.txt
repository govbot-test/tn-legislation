HOUSE RESOLUTION 179
By Davis
A RESOLUTION to commend Amelia Margaret "Millie" Haymaker
for exemplary service as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, we are pleased to recognize certain persons who
have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment of the legislative
process; and
WHEREAS, Amelia Margaret "Millie" Haymaker is one such individual, who has
distinguished herself in her role as a legislative intern for Representative Elaine Davis and
Representative Dave Wright; Ms. Haymaker's work has resulted in innumerable benefits for the
legislators she assisted and the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, originally from Hendersonville, Millie Haymaker is a junior at the University
of Tennessee, Knoxville, where she is highly regarded by her peers; and
WHEREAS, a recipient of the Baker School Scholarship and the Tennessee Hope
Scholarship, she has achieved academic success while majoring in political science and
participating in numerous extracurricular activities, including the Women in Politics Club, Phi
Mu, the Eighteen Twenty Prayer Club, and Christian Students of UTK; and
WHEREAS, outside of school, Ms. Haymaker is an active member of her community;
she works with the East Tennessee Children's Hospital, Mobile Meals, and Forward Market
Knoxville; and
WHEREAS, Millie Haymaker plans to attend law school and become a juvenile criminal
defense attorney who advocates for incarcerated minors; and
WHEREAS, her favorite pastimes include playing golf and tennis, singing, songwriting,
and traveling; she has won numerous singing competitions, including the legislative intern talent
show; and

WHEREAS, eager to familiarize herself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further her studies, Ms. Haymaker brought her many
abilities and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2026 session of the One Hundred
Fourteenth General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, during the legislative session, Millie Haymaker was an active participant in
the Community Service Committee and Bible study; and
WHEREAS, throughout the 2026 legislative session, Ms. Haymaker has surpassed all
criteria that define a devoted and conscientious legislative intern; and
WHEREAS, Millie Haymaker has performed her many duties with extraordinary insight
and rare decorum; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby
commend Amelia Margaret "Millie" Haymaker for outstanding service as a legislative intern for
Representative Elaine Davis and Representative Dave Wright and extend to her our best
wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
