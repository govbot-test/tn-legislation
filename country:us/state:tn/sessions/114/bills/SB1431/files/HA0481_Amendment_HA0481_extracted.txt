AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 1431* House Bill No. 1409
by adding the following as a new, appropriately designated section immediately preceding the
antepenultimate section and redesignating the subsequent sections accordingly:
SECTION __. From funds available to the Department of Education for the
Education Freedom Scholarship Program, there is earmarked a sum sufficient for the
sole purpose of making a grant in such amount to the Department of Veterans Services,
to be used to provide grants for disabled and homeless veterans in this state.
