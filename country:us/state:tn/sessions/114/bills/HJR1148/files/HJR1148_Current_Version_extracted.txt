HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1148
By Doggett
A RESOLUTION to recognize Major Wayne Sellers on the
occasion of his retirement.
WHEREAS, our nation was conceived by individuals who were willing to sacrifice their
personal concerns to ensure the public safety and welfare, and the Volunteer State is especially
proud to be the home of so many valiant individuals; and
WHEREAS, one such individual is Major Wayne Sellers of the Tennessee Highway
Patrol (THP), who, having rendered sterling service to the people of the State of Tennessee for
thirty-seven years, is taking his much-deserved retirement this year; and
WHEREAS, Major Sellers began his esteemed career with the THP in 1989 as a road
trooper in Giles County; he was promoted in 1997 to sergeant and again in 2002 to troop
lieutenant, in which role he oversaw field operations for Giles, Lincoln, and Lawrence counties;
and
WHEREAS, Major Wayne Sellers continued to rise through the ranks to Lawrenceburg
district captain in 2014, managing field operations for eleven counties in the region; he was
promoted to major on February 5, 2026, from which position he is retiring; and
WHEREAS, Major Sellers is a graduate of the Northwestern School of Police Staff and
Command and the Tennessee Law Enforcement Executive Development Seminar; and
WHEREAS, his many professional accomplishments aside, Major Sellers is most
grateful for the love and support of his wife, Brenda, and his children, Chelsea and Brad; and
WHEREAS, throughout his law enforcement career, Major Sellers has carried out his
duties and responsibilities with the highest degree of integrity and pride, earning the utmost
respect and admiration of his colleagues and community alike for the superior quality of his
work; and

WHEREAS, Major Sellers epitomizes the spirit and commitment that are characteristic of
a true Tennessean, and his professionalism and dedication to serving his community are
evident to all who have the good fortune of knowing him; and
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to recognize and applaud Major Wayne Sellers for his
dedication to the State of Tennessee and its citizens; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we recognize Major Wayne Sellers of the Tennessee Highway Patrol on
the occasion of his retirement, thank him for thirty-seven years of meritorious service, and
extend to him our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
