HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1250
By Doggett
A RESOLUTION to honor Coach Chad Hall on earning his 500th
career victory in high school girls' basketball.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly take great pleasure in recognizing
those distinguished coaches who, through dedication and leadership, have attained significant
career milestones; and
WHEREAS, Coach Chad Hall, head coach of the Richland High School Lady Raiders
girls' basketball team, recently notched his 500th career victory when his team defeated Moore
County High School on March 2, 2026, in the Region 5A semifinal game at Eagleville High
School; and
WHEREAS, this milestone victory stands as a testament to Coach Hall's many years of
commitment, discipline, and excellence in the coaching profession, as well as his ability to
develop student athletes both on and off the court; and
WHEREAS, under his leadership, the Richland Lady Raiders have achieved sustained
success and become one of the premier programs in their classification; and
WHEREAS, notably, Coach Chad Hall led the Lady Raiders to the 2026 TSSAA Division
I, Class A state championship, marking the first girls' basketball state title in Richland High
School history and a historic achievement for the program and community; and
WHEREAS, through hard work and commitment, Coach Chad Hall has achieved
success that most coaches can only dream of, and he is worthy of our approbation on this
special occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor Coach Chad Hall on earning his 500th career victory, commend

his outstanding contributions to high school athletics, and extend to him our best wishes for
much continued success as the head coach of the Richland High School Lady Raiders.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared

the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
