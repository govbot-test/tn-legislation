HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1067
By Crawford
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Bristol Rhythm & Roots
Reunion on the occasion of its twenty-fifth
anniversary.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly take great pleasure in recognizing
those cultural institutions that preserve the history, heritage, and artistic traditions of this State
while contributing to its economic vitality; and
WHEREAS, for twenty-five years, Bristol Rhythm & Roots Reunion has brought together
musicians, artists, residents, and visitors from across the nation and around the world to
celebrate Bristol's unique role as the "Birthplace of Country Music"; and
WHEREAS, established in 2001, Bristol Rhythm & Roots Reunion is a community-
driven, grassroots project that honors the deep musical traditions of Appalachia and fosters a
shared appreciation for roots music, cultural heritage, and creative expression; and
WHEREAS, over the years, the festival has grown into one of the region's premier
cultural events, serving as a nationally recognized showcase for Appalachian music and arts
while reinforcing Bristol's identity as a center of musical innovation and authenticity; and
WHEREAS, Bristol Rhythm & Roots Reunion has generated significant economic
benefits for the region by attracting tourism, supporting local businesses, creating jobs, and
contributing to the long-term revitalization of downtown Bristol and surrounding communities;
and
WHEREAS, the ongoing success of Bristol Rhythm & Roots Reunion reflects the
strength of collaboration between Bristol, Tennessee, and Bristol, Virginia, demonstrating how
shared cultural assets can unite communities, strengthen regional partnerships, and advance
common economic and civic goals; and

014052
- 1 -
WHEREAS, since 2012, Bristol Rhythm & Roots Reunion has been organized by the
Birthplace of Country Music nonprofit organization, which also manages the Smithsonian
Institution-affiliated Birthplace of Country Music Museum and the Radio Bristol radio station and
invests in education, youth programming, and community engagement to ensure that the
region's musical traditions are preserved and passed on to future generations while
encouraging new voices and creative growth; and
WHEREAS, the festival's continued success is attributable to the hard work and
dedication of numerous organizers, staff members, volunteers, artists, sponsors, and
community partners, as well as the visionary leadership of Executive Director Leah Ross; and
WHEREAS, the longevity of Bristol Rhythm & Roots Reunion reflects its sustained
excellence, adaptability, and strong community support and underscores its importance as a
cultural institution and a model for arts-driven regional development; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Bristol Rhythm & Roots Reunion on the occasion
of its twenty-fifth anniversary, expressing our sincere appreciation for its role in preserving
Appalachian musical heritage and strengthening regional identity, pride, and collaboration.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
