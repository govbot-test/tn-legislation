HOUSE RESOLUTION 247
By Keisling
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Miley Vanhook, a 2026
Honor Graduate at Oneida High School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Miley Vanhook is one such exceptional student, who was recently named
an Honor Graduate of the 2026 graduating class at Oneida High School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Ms. Vanhook has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in her remarkable high school career, Miley Vanhook has earned an
exemplary grade point average while participating in numerous extracurricular activities; and
WHEREAS, her display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well for her
future success; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Vanhook's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for
her family, her school, and her community; and
WHEREAS, Miley Vanhook's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of our respect,
admiration, and commendation; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we commend
and honor Miley Vanhook on being named a 2026 Oneida High School Honor Graduate,
