HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1231
By Stevens
A RESOLUTION to honor the Eagleville High School Eagles girls'
volleyball team on winning the 2025 TSSAA
Class A state championship.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should salute those successful athletic teams that,
through their magnificent exploits, bring honor to Tennessee and serve as exceptional
ambassadors for this State; and
WHEREAS, proving to be the fiercest of competitors and, as such, deserving of the
highest regard, the Eagleville High School Eagles girls' volleyball team captured the 2025
TSSAA Class A state championship; and
WHEREAS, arriving at the state tournament in great form, the Eagles won three
consecutive games, including a 3-1 victory over Gordonsville in the final match, to take home
the state championship trophy with an incredible 43-5 overall record; and
WHEREAS, the Eagles were fortunate to enjoy the expertise and leadership of Coach
Deanna Freise, and her assistants, Kara Leathers and Gretchen Thompson; and
WHEREAS, the following young women are proud to fly like an Eagle: Zylinn Anderson,
Brylee Bain, Kaylen Baker, Alexandria Bolden, Harper Bugg, Autumn Burns, Kinni Rose
Campbell, Preslee Campbell, Ansley Cannon, Liza Freise, RoseMary Johns, Presley Knowles,
Campbell Leathers, Ella Lee, Alissa Morgan, Rosadelia Perez, Sophie Smith, Lily Smith, and
Aubry Tibbs; and
WHEREAS, the members of the Eagleville Eagles girls' volleyball team epitomize all that
is good in today's student athletes, as they have achieved a premier level of success in
competition while exhibiting the highest degree of character in all aspects of life; and
WHEREAS, their perseverance, determination, work ethic, and talent not only made
possible a championship season but also surely bode well for each player's future success; and
HJR1231
016465
- 1 -
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to acknowledge and applaud the Eagleville High
School Eagles girls' volleyball team, not only for winning the state championship but also for the
players' superior sportsmanship and the joy they have brought to their fellow students and fans;
now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate the Eagleville High School Eagles girls'
volleyball team upon winning the 2025 TSSAA Class A state championship, salute these
exceptional young women for their many accomplishments, both on and off the court, and
extend to them our best wishes for much continued success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
