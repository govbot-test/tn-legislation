Section 1:
By Martin G
SENATE BILL 1742
By Gardenhire
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 55,
Chapter 4, relative to hybrid electric vehicles.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 55-4-116(e), is amended by deleting
subdivision (2) and substituting instead the following:
(2) "Hybrid electric vehicle":
(A) Means a vehicle propelled by a combination of an electric motor and
an internal combustion engine or other power source and components thereof;
and
(B) Does not include a low speed vehicle or a medium speed vehicle;
and

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
