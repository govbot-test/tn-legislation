SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 625
By Johnson
A RESOLUTION to confirm the appointment of Susan Marsh
Mayfield to the advisory board for the University
of Tennessee Southern.
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-501, established advisory boards
for the University of Tennessee, Knoxville; University of Tennessee at Martin; University of
Tennessee at Chattanooga; University of Tennessee Health Science Center; and University of
Tennessee Southern; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-501, provides that the governor
shall appoint five members to each advisory board; and
WHEREAS, Governor Bill Lee has appointed Susan Marsh Mayfield to serve as a
member of the advisory board for the University of Tennessee Southern for a term that expires
on June 30, 2030; and
WHEREAS, a career educator, Susan Mayfield has enjoyed a long history in the Giles
County school system, teaching a range of grades from K-8 and serving as a gifted instructor for
many years; she also helps run Mayfield Land & Cattle, which has over 500 acres of farmland
and over 200 head of Angus cattle; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Mayfield attended the University of Tennessee, Knoxville, and holds an
undergraduate degree from Middle Tennessee State University, a master's degree from
Tennessee State University, and certifications from Tennessee State University and Belmont
University; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-501, provides that each member
appointed by the governor to the advisory board for the University of Tennessee Southern shall
be confirmed by the general assembly; now, therefore,
