Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 66-37-102(4), is amended by adding
the following as a new subdivision:
(E) Any provision of a document requiring a transferor or transferee to pay a fee,
charge, assessment, dues, contribution, deposit, or other amount to purchase, issue,
reissue, maintain, or transfer a club membership that is related to the real property or the
community in which the property is sited.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
