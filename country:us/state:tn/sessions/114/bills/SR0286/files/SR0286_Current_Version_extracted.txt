Section 1:
SENATE RESOLUTION 286
By Johnson
A RESOLUTION to confirm the appointment of Christi Branscom
of Knoxville to the Tennessee board of regents.
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-8-101, provides for the creation of
the Tennessee board of regents; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-8-201, provides that the governor
shall appoint ten members to the Tennessee board of regents; and
WHEREAS, Governor Bill Lee has appointed Christi Branscom of Knoxville to the
Tennessee board of regents as the member representing the 2nd Congressional District to a
term that will expire on June 30, 2031; and
WHEREAS, an accomplished individual, Christi Branscom is Principal, General Counsel,
and Managing Broker for Partners Development, having returned to the company after nearly
six years of service as Commissioner of the Department of General Services for the State of
Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Branscom earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Finance from the
University of Tennessee and a Juris Doctorate from the University of Memphis; and
WHEREAS, Christi Branscom is a person of integrity and an active citizen whose
benevolence, acumen, and strong commitment to public service have helped to enhance the
well-being of this State and its citizens; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-8-201, provides that each member
appointed by the governor to the Tennessee board of regents shall be confirmed by the senate;
now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that this body does hereby confirm
SR0286
011594
- 1 -
the appointment of Christi Branscom to the Tennessee board of regents for a term that will
expire on June 30, 2031.
