Senate Health and Welfare 1
Amendment No. 1 to SB1926
Crowe
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 1926* House Bill No. 1991
by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 63-5-108(c), is amended by adding
the following as a new subdivision:
(6) Prior to a dentist seeing a new or existing patient, a dental hygienist working
under direct supervision of the dentist may:
(A) Complete diagnostic radiographs;
(B) Assess and record existing hard and soft tissue data;
(C) Perform prophylaxis on new or existing patients; and
(D) Apply fluoride agents.
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 63-5-108(h)(1), is amended by
deleting the subdivision and substituting:
(1) Notwithstanding a law to the contrary, the practice of dental hygiene also
includes prescriptive authority limited to fluoride agents, topical oral anesthetic agents,
silver diamine fluoride, curodont, sealants, and desiccation therapy that:
(A) Are not controlled substances under state and federal drug laws; and
(B) Do not require a license from the federal drug enforcement agency.
SECTION 3. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 63-5-108(h)(4), is amended by
deleting the subdivision and substituting:
(4) The board shall determine by rule the educational and training requirements
necessary for a dental hygienist to exercise prescriptive authority pursuant to this
section, including a training course for the placement of silver diamine fluoride.
