SENATE RESOLUTION 325
By Walley
A RESOLUTION to honor Delk's Custom Auto Body of
Brownsville.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should recognize those historic Tennessee businesses
that have a long-standing commitment to providing exceptional service to their customers; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable entity is Delk's Custom Auto Body in Brownsville, which
celebrates its forty-eighth anniversary of operation this year, a commendable milestone for this
exemplary business; and
WHEREAS, established in 1978, Delk's Custom Auto Body is owned and operated by
Ivy Delk, and located at 1326 East Main Street in Brownsville; and
WHEREAS, Delk's Custom Auto Body strives every day to provide the best service at
the very best price and is proud to serve the residents of Haywood, Tipton, Fayette, Madison,
Lauderdale, Crockett, and Shelby counties; and
WHEREAS, for nearly five decades, Delk's Custom Auto Body has provided quality
products and superior customer service to the people of West Tennessee, and it is fitting that
the business be recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and congratulate
Delk's Custom Auto Body on forty-eight years of operation, wishing the iconic Tennessee
business much continued success in the future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
