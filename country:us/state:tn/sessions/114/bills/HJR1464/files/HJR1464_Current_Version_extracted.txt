HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1464
By Leatherwood
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Kenneth Walker III on
being named Most Valuable Player of Super
Bowl LX.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly are pleased to recognize an
Arlington High School alumnus who has reached the acme of professional football as a Super
Bowl champion and that game's Most Valuable Player (MVP); and
WHEREAS, Kenneth Walker III had a great 2025 season overall as the starting running
back for Seattle, but he definitely saved the best for last, rushing for 135 yards and making four
receptions for 26 yards to lead the Seahawks to victory in Super Bowl LX and claim MVP
honors in the process; and
WHEREAS, Kenneth Walker III was proud to have his father in attendance for these
milestones; the elder Mr. Walker had never before attended an NFL game but was persuaded to
set aside his aversion to crowds and was delighted to witness in-person his son's MVP
performance and Seattle's Super Bowl victory; and
WHEREAS, nicknamed Thunder Shoes, Kenneth Walker III was truly a workhorse for
Seattle; he played in all 17 games of the 2025 regular season, rushing for 1,027 yards and five
touchdowns and making 36 receptions for 282 yards; and
WHEREAS, after a teammate was injured, Mr. Walker became the primary back for
Seattle's three playoff games; he obviously relished the opportunity, because he cleared the
Seahawks' path to the Super Bowl with three brilliant performances; and
WHEREAS, Kenneth Walker III rushed for 116 yards and three touchdowns in Seattle's
41-6 rout of San Francisco in the divisional round; and
WHEREAS, facing off against the Los Angeles Rams in the NFC Championship Game,
Kenneth Walker III rushed for 62 yards and a touchdown while also adding four receptions for

49 yards, as the Seahawks secured a 31-27 victory and punched their ticket to Super Bowl LX;
and
WHEREAS, the rest is history, as Mr. Walker recorded his best performance of the
season to lead Seattle to the promised land as the Super Bowl's MVP; and
WHEREAS, at the very beginning of his post-game interview, Kenneth Walker III
thanked God for His blessing in guiding his personal journey to the Super Bowl and beyond; and
WHEREAS, a humble man of faith, Mr. Walker is grateful to God for inspiring his football
success and enlightening his everyday life; and
WHEREAS, a dominant football player at Arlington High School, Kenneth Walker III
began his collegiate career at Wake Forest before transferring to Michigan State for the 2021
season; and
WHEREAS, he blossomed into a star player with the Spartans, earning first team All-
American honors and winning the prestigious Doak Walker Award as the nation's top college
running back; and
WHEREAS, Kenneth Walker III has reached the very pinnacle of professional football,
and he is most worthy of our approbation; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Kenneth Walker III on being named Most
Valuable Player of Super Bowl LX as a vital member of the NFL champion Seattle Seahawks
and extend to him our best wishes for much continued success on the gridiron and in life.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
