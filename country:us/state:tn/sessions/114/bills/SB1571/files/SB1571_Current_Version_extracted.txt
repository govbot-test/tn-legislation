SENATE BILL 1571
By Lowe
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 68,
Chapter 30, relative to anatomical gifts.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 68-30-205(a), is amended by adding
the following at the end of the subsection:
This subsection (a) does not apply if the data is collected as part of a clinical trial or
other biomedical research study conducted in accordance with 28 CFR Part 202.
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
