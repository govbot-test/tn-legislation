SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 717
By Yarbro
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate the University School
of Nashville's boys' cross country team, 2025
TSSAA Division II, Class A runners-up.
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that we honor those Tennessee students who, through their
athletic prowess, have achieved the pinnacle of success and serve as exceptional ambassadors
of the Volunteer State; and
WHEREAS, one such group of estimable individuals is the University School of
Nashville's boys' cross country team, which claimed second place laurels at the 2025 TSSAA
Division II, Class A state championship; and
WHEREAS, success in statewide competition is achieved only through hard work,
dedication, and the commitment to working together as a team and excelling with a cooperative
spirit; the Tigers exhibited each of these rare qualities this season; and
WHEREAS, the boys' cross country team benefited from the astute leadership of Head
Coach George Flatau and assistant coaches Robert McKay and Eric Royer; and
WHEREAS, the University School of Nashville Tigers' noteworthy achievement is a
tremendous source of pride for their families, their classmates, and their entire community; and
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to acknowledge and applaud the members of the
boys' cross country team of University School of Nashville for serving as exemplars of the high
quality of the youth of Tennessee; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate the members and
coaches of the University School of Nashville boys' cross country team, runners-up at the 2025

TSSAA Division II, Class A state championship, and extend to them our best wishes for
continued success in all their future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared

the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
