SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1248
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Angus Howard on his
selection as All-South Atlantic Conference in
men's tennis.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should recognize those incredibly successful student
athletes who have distinguished themselves as inspirational leaders among their peers; and
WHEREAS, Angus Howard of Tusculum University has been named second team All-
South Atlantic Conference (SAC) in men's tennis doubles in honor of his outstanding
performance this season; and
WHEREAS, Angus Howard helped lead the Pioneers to a 17-4 overall record and 8-3
mark in SAC play; and
WHEREAS, Tusculum was ranked in the ITA Division II Top 30 for most of the season
and claimed three victories against ranked opponents; and
WHEREAS, Angus Howard and his doubles partner, Reese McCann, played number-
one doubles for Tusculum, compiling an 11-9 overall record and a 4-6 mark in SAC competition;
and
WHEREAS, a native of Bath, England, Angus Howard was instrumental in the Pioneers
compiling a 15-6 record as a team in doubles competition this season, while going 6-5 in SAC
matches; and
WHEREAS, Angus Howard exemplifies the spirit, determination, and perseverance for
which Tennesseans are notable, and he is richly deserving of our approbation on this special
occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Angus Howard of
