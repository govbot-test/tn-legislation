SENATE RESOLUTION 403
By Yager
A RESOLUTION to commend Brant Edward Hall II for exemplary
service as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this legislative body is pleased to recognize certain
persons who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment of the
legislative process; and
WHEREAS, Brant Edward Hall II is one such individual, who has distinguished himself in
his role as a legislative intern for Senator Ken Yager; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Hall's work, his exceptionally personable demeanor, and his
professionalism have resulted in innumerable benefits for the legislators he assisted, the
constituents of Senate District 12, and the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, Brant Hall brought leadership and sixteen years of professional experience
from serving in the Army National Guard to his time with the legislature; and
WHEREAS, a student at Tennessee Technological University, where he is highly
regarded by his peers, Mr. Hall has achieved academic success while pursuing Bachelor of
Science degrees in Political Science and History; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize himself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further his studies, Brant Hall brought his many abilities
and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2026 session of the One Hundred Fourteenth
General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Hall has performed his many duties with extraordinary insight and rare
decorum; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby commend Brant
