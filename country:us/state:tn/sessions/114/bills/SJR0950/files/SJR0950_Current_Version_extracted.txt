SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 950
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to commend Audrey Fairman for exemplary
service as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, the members of this General Assembly are
pleased to recognize certain persons who have committed themselves, through their adroit
service, to the betterment of the legislative process; and
WHEREAS, Audrey Fairman is one such individual, who has distinguished herself in her
role as a legislative intern for Senator Steve Southerland; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Fairman's work has resulted in innumerable benefits for the legislators
she assisted and the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, highly regarded by her peers, Ms. Fairman earned a Bachelor of Arts
degree in Law and Politics from Freed-Hardeman University, where she was on both the Dean's
List and President's List; she is currently earning her Master of Public Administration degree
from Lipscomb University; and
WHEREAS, a resident of Crossville, Audrey Fairman has brought her creativity and
passion for politics and public policy to her time with the legislature once more, having
previously served the Tennessee General Assembly as a legislative intern in 2025, in which role
she conducted bill research, constituent work, and office duties; and
WHEREAS, a leader in her community and her faith, Audrey Fairman participated in an
internship program at Crossville Church of Christ in 2023 and 2024, teaching youth Bible
classes and assisting with several events; and
WHEREAS, additionally, Ms. Fairman participated in the Tennessee Intercollegiate State
Legislature, where she served as a representative and worked on the organization's social
media; and

WHEREAS, eager to familiarize herself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further her studies as a public administration professional,
Audrey Fairman brought her many abilities and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2026
session of the One Hundred Fourteenth General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, throughout the 2026 legislative session, Ms. Fairman has surpassed all
criteria that define a devoted and conscientious legislative intern; and
WHEREAS, Audrey Fairman has performed her many duties with extraordinary insight
and rare decorum; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby commend Audrey Fairman for
outstanding service as a legislative intern for Senator Steve Southerland and extend to her our
best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
