SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1054
By Pody
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend William M. "Bill" Grady
for his service as President of the Mt. Juliet
Breakfast Rotary Club.
WHEREAS, it is most fitting that the members of this General Assembly should
recognize those Tennesseans who have performed with uncommon devotion and enthusiasm
throughout their tenure in public service; and
WHEREAS, having rendered exemplary service as the 2025-2026 President of the Mt.
Juliet Breakfast Rotary Club, William M. "Bill" Grady will soon conclude his successful tenure in
this esteemed leadership role, having demonstrated outstanding leadership and a deep
commitment to the Rotary ideal of "Service Above Self"; and
WHEREAS, the Mt. Juliet Breakfast Rotary Club, chartered in 1990, has faithfully served
the citizens of Mt. Juliet and Wilson County for thirty-five years through community engagement,
charitable initiatives, and educational support, including efforts such as literacy programs
benefiting local students; and
WHEREAS, under Bill Grady's leadership and alongside the members of the Mt. Juliet
Breakfast Rotary Club, the organization continues to strengthen community relationships, foster
civic engagement, and promote service-oriented leadership within Wilson County; and
WHEREAS, Bill Grady's involvement in Rotary reflects his personal commitment to
building goodwill, encouraging fellowship, and advancing meaningful service in his community;
and
WHEREAS, in addition to his Rotary leadership, Bill Grady has demonstrated a
longstanding dedication to public service through his military and legal career, having served
honorably on active duty in the United States Army Judge Advocate General's Corps and
continuing his service in the United States Army Reserve at the rank of Lieutenant Colonel; and

WHEREAS, Bill Grady also served as a federal prosecutor with the United States
Department of Justice, where he led and supported complex investigations involving healthcare
fraud and child exploitation, working alongside federal, state, and local partners; and
WHEREAS, in his professional career as a real estate broker in Middle Tennessee, Bill
Grady has demonstrated excellence and leadership, earning recognition among the top agents
in the State of Tennessee while contributing to professional standards and civic engagement
through REALTOR® association leadership; and
WHEREAS, a graduate of Vanderbilt University and the University of Tennessee College
of Law, Bill Grady exemplifies a life dedicated to leadership, integrity, and service; and
WHEREAS, Bill Grady is also a member of the National Eagle Scout Association,
reflecting a lifelong commitment to the values of character, leadership, and service; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Grady is a devoted husband and father who resides in Wilson County
with his wife and their two children, and he brings the same integrity, care, and commitment to
his family that he demonstrates in his professional and civic leadership; and
WHEREAS, throughout his Rotary presidency and public service, Bill Grady has
exemplified the highest ideals of leadership, civic engagement, and personal integrity, earning
the respect and admiration of his colleagues and community alike; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend William M. "Bill" Grady for
his service as President of the Mt. Juliet Breakfast Rotary Club and extend to him our heartfelt
gratitude and best wishes for continued success in all his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
