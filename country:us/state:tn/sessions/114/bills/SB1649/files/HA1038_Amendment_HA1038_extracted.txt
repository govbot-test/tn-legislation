SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-8-201(f)(3), is amended by
designating the existing language as subdivision (A) and adding the following as a new
subdivision (B):
(B) Notwithstanding subdivision (f)(3)(A), the members of a state university
board appointed by the governor on or after January 1, 2027, must be confirmed by joint
resolution of the senate and house of representatives prior to beginning a term of office.
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting "the effective date of this act" wherever it appears in the
amendatory language of Section 2 and substituting "January 1, 2027".
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting "the effective date of this act" in subdivision (f)(9)(A)(iii) of

Section 3:
Section 3 and substituting "January 1, 2027," and by deleting subdivision (f)(9)(A)(iv) of Section
3 and substituting :
(iv) Beginning on January 1, 2027, the terms of members appointed pursuant to
subdivision (f)(1)(B) begin on February 1 and expire six (6) years thereafter. In order to
align the terms of existing members with members appointed by the governor on or after
January 1, 2027, the terms of members appointed pursuant to subdivision (f)(1)(B) who
are serving a term on the state university board for Tennessee State University on
January 1, 2027, are extended to January 31 immediately following the date on which
