SENATE RESOLUTION 348
By Walley
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Chad Austin of
Adamsville High School on being named District
13 Coach of the Year.
WHEREAS, the members of this legislative body take great pleasure in paying tribute to
those outstanding coaches who, through hard work and commitment, distinguish themselves
among the storied coaches and legendary high school programs of Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, Chad Austin of Adamsville High School was recently named the District 13
Coach of the Year, an honor that truly reflects the commitment to athletic excellence displayed
by Coach Austin and the Adamsville Lady Cardinals; and
WHEREAS, Coach Austin recently led the Lady Cardinals to a 56-33 victory in the
district tournament against Scotts Hill High School, after being tied at 8 each at the end of the
first quarter; and
WHEREAS, Coach Chad Austin epitomizes the spirit and commitment that are
characteristic of a true Tennessean; he has served with great diligence and dedication,
positively influencing the lives of countless young people with his display of character and
integrity; and
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to acknowledge and applaud Chad Austin for his
outstanding leadership as head coach of the Adamsville High School girls' basketball team;
now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and congratulate
Chad Austin of Adamsville High School upon being named the District 13 Coach of the Year
and extend to him our best wishes for much continued success both on and off the court.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
