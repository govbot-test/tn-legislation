HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 927
By Powers
A RESOLUTION to recognize Mark Goins, 2026-2027 President
of the National Association of State Election
Directors.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
specially recognize those estimable public servants who have dedicated themselves to
improving the quality of life for their fellow citizens; and
WHEREAS, Mark Goins is one such admirable individual, who evinces the greatest
integrity and probity in all his chosen endeavors; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Goins was recently sworn in to serve as President of the National
Association of State Election Directors (NASED) for the 2026-2027 term, a testament to his
leadership among his peers and the respect he has earned throughout his career; and
WHEREAS, as NASED President, he will guide the organization's executive board and
membership in advancing secure, accessible, and trustworthy election administration
nationwide; and
WHEREAS, a trusted leader in Tennessee and nationally, Mark Goins has served as
Coordinator of Elections for the State of Tennessee for seventeen years; during this time, his
commitment to making elections both accessible and secure has helped set a national standard;
and
WHEREAS, as Coordinator of Elections for the State of Tennessee, Mr. Goins has
overseen statewide initiatives and helped implement policies such as photo identification
requirements, non-U.S. citizen cross-checks, anti-election hacking statutes, and online voter
registration; and
WHEREAS, under Mr. Goins's leadership, Tennessee has been ranked first in the nation
for election integrity for four consecutive years; and

WHEREAS, prior to his current post, he served for four years on the Tennessee State
Election Commission and was a three-term member of the Tennessee House of
Representatives, where he served on the Elections Subcommittee; and
WHEREAS, nationally, he is a former Chair of the U.S. Election Assistance Commission
Standards Board, and he has long been active within NASED, serving on committees and
participating in working groups; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Goins is a native of LaFollette and a graduate of Campbell County High
School; he holds degrees from Lincoln Memorial University, where he is a member of the
Professional Hall of Fame, and Regent University School of Law and is a licensed attorney; and
WHEREAS, Mark Goins has honored and continued the legacy of his father, Whit Goins,
who served Campbell County for more than fifty years as an elected official; and
WHEREAS, throughout his exemplary tenure as Coordinator of Elections for the State of
Tennessee, Mr. Goins has carried out his responsibilities with diligence and integrity; his
remarkable success as a public servant and civic leader is directly attributable to his reliance on
the time-honored values of hard work and common sense and his uncommon ability to work
well with people from all walks of life; and
WHEREAS, a strong advocate for the citizens of Tennessee, Mark Goins has compiled
a singular record of success during his exemplary public service career, and he should be
specially recognized on this special occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and congratulate Mark Goins as he begins his term
as President of the National Association of State Election Directors, extending our best wishes
for every success.
