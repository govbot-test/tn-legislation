HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1493
By Keisling
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Jacob Adam "Jake"
Pedigo on becoming an Eagle Scout.
WHEREAS, at a time when this nation's youth face difficult challenges, the Boy Scouts
of America is one of our finest assets, providing young people with an educational program that
contributes significantly to their character development, citizenship training, and improved
mental and physical fitness; and
WHEREAS, the highest rank a Scout can attain in the Boy Scouts of America Scouts
BSA program is that of Eagle Scout, and Jacob Adam "Jake" Pedigo, after considerable effort
and diligent preparation, has joined the prestigious Eagle Court of Honor; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Pedigo has demonstrated considerable ability as a leader among his
peers, and his notable record of achievement within his Boy Scout troop includes numerous
merit badges; and
WHEREAS, Jake Pedigo's Eagle project greatly contributed to the betterment of his
community and reflected the true spirit of volunteerism; and
WHEREAS, Jake's many endeavors in community service, responsible citizenship, and
peer leadership are a source of great pride for his parents, Karen and Josh Pedigo; and
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that we should recognize Jake Pedigo as one of the most
outstanding young people in our State; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Jacob Adam "Jake" Pedigo on becoming an
Eagle Scout and extend to him our best wishes for every future success.
HJR1493
018235
- 1 -
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
