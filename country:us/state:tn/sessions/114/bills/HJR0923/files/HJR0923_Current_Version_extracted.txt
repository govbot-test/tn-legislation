HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 923
By Haston
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend the Hardin County High
School Lady Tigers upon winning the TSSAA
Division I girls' bowling state championship
tournament.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that this General Assembly should salute those student athletes
who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as true champions of
whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such exemplary group of lasses is the Hardin County High School Lady
Tigers, whose members have proven themselves to be the fiercest of competitors and truly
worthy of admiration by winning the TSSAA Division I girls' bowling state championship
tournament while posting a perfect 16-0 season record; and
WHEREAS, entering the state tournament without a single defeat and a 13-0 record, the
Lady Tigers easily bested Franklin 23-4 to emerge victorious in their quarterfinal match-up and
then defeated Heritage 19-8 in the semi-final round; and
WHEREAS, in the final match, Hardin County trounced Dobyns Bennett 26-1 to claim
the state title and bring home the gold; and
WHEREAS, the Lady Tigers' superb performance would not have been possible without
the stellar leadership of their coaches and the unfailing support of their families, their fans, and
their community; and
WHEREAS, the members of the Hardin County girls' bowling team epitomize all that is
good in today's student athletes, as they have achieved a premier level of success in
competition while exhibiting the highest degree of character in all aspects of life; and

WHEREAS, the perseverance, determination, work ethic, and talent of this group of
young women not only made possible an outstanding season but also surely bode well for their
future success; and
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly find it appropriate to acknowledge
and applaud the Hardin County Lady Tigers for their championship season on the lanes; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend the members and coaches of the Hardin County
High School Lady Tigers upon winning the TSSAA Division I girls' bowling state championship
tournament and extend to them our best wishes for every continued success in all their future
endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
