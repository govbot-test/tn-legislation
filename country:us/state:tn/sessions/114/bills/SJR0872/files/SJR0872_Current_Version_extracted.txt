SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 872
By Walley
A RESOLUTION to recognize and honor Patricia Jones upon
being named the NFIB's Small Business
Champion Award winner.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should salute those citizens who, through their
extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as community leaders of whom we can all
be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable person is Patricia Jones, who was named the National
Federation of Independent Business's (NFIB) 2025 Small Business Champion Award winner;
and
WHEREAS, the Small Business Champion Award honors NFIB members who go above
and beyond to help the association promote and protect the right of its members to own,
operate, and grow their businesses; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Jones currently serves as the vice president of finance of Jones
Contractors, Inc., a family-owned and -operated company based in Henderson that specializes
in a wide range of construction services in upstream, midstream, downstream, and renewable
energy sectors. She previously owned Jones Lumber Co. and currently serves as a member of
the NFIB Tennessee Leadership Council as well as on NFIB's State Political Action Committee
and Labor Advisory Council; Jones Contractors has been a member of NFIB since 2012; and
WHEREAS, throughout her fruitful career, Patricia Jones has been an outstanding
advocate for small businesses and issues at the State and federal levels; she knows all of her
elected officials, and they know her, and she is outstanding at expressing her concerns and
views articulately, helping her grow her business and support her community; and

WHEREAS, through her work with the NFIB, Ms. Jones ensures her elected officials in
Nashville and Washington, D.C., are aware of how their policy proposals and decisions impact
small businesses in Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, Patricia Jones is heavily involved in her community, including community
and leadership initiatives such as the Chester County Chamber of Commerce, Delta Leadership
Institute Class of 2013, and WestStar Leadership Class of 2017; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Jones epitomizes the spirit and commitment that are characteristic of a
true Tennessean; and
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to acknowledge and applaud Patricia Jones for her
many contributions to the good people of Tennessee, her unwavering commitment to others,
and her astute leadership in Henderson; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and commend Patricia Jones on
being named the National Federation of Independent Business's Small Business Champion
Award winner and extend to her our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
