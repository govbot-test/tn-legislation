SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 957
By Pody
A RESOLUTION to recognize Mt. Olivet Baptist Church on the
occasion of its 225th anniversary.
WHEREAS, the churches of our nation have been an integral part of our history as a
people and have had an unparalleled impact in forming and maintaining a stable and just
society; and
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that we should recognize those venerable religious
institutions and their congregations that, through their unflagging capacity for love, dedication of
spirit, and faith in God, enrich the lives of people in their communities; and
WHEREAS, one such dynamic and historic community of faith is Mt. Olivet Baptist
Church, whose members are celebrating the church's 225th anniversary this year; and
WHEREAS, founded in 1801 in the Mt. Juliet community, the congregation at Mt. Olivet
Baptist Church then began ministering and serving families in Middle Tennessee and
contributing greatly to society in the name of Jesus Christ; and
WHEREAS, in the present day, Mt. Olivet Baptist Church continues to flourish, and the
devout members of the congregation have faithfully striven to carry out God's work in the world;
and
WHEREAS, Mt. Olivet Baptist Church has stood on the rock-solid foundation of Christian
belief, service, and love for 225 years, serving as a bastion of faith and outreach to the people
of its community; and
WHEREAS, thanks to the church's faith, leadership, and strength of its members, Mt.
Olivet Baptist Church has persevered and continues to be a light in the community; it is truly one
of the great religious institutions and catalysts for positive spiritual change in our State and
nation, and the members of this exceptional congregation should be appropriately honored for
