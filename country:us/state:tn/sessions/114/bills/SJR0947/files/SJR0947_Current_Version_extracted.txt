SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 947
By Crowe
A RESOLUTION to honor David Frizzell on receiving NFIB's 2025
Small Business Champion Award.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly take great pleasure in recognizing a
gentleman who has been a dedicated and effective advocate for small business owners
throughout his professional journey as a certified public accountant (CPA); and
WHEREAS, David Frizzell of Johnson City has been honored with the National
Federation of Independent Business's (NFIB's) 2025 Small Business Champion Award, which
honors those professionals who go the extra mile to help the association promote and protect
the right of its 300,000 members to own, operate, and grow their businesses; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Frizzell is an active member of the NFIB Tennessee Leadership
Council, influencing public policymaking at the State and federal levels through his cogent
communication with elected officials; and
WHEREAS, as a member of NFIB's Tax Advisory Council, Mr. Frizzell reviews federal
and State tax proposals for economic impact and is an expert at identifying legislation that could
harm our State's job creators; and
WHEREAS, dedicated to the accounting profession, Mr. Frizzell is a member of the
American Institute of Certified Public Accountants and the Tennessee Society of CPAs
(TSCPA); he is a past president of the TSCPA's Appalachian Chapter and currently serves on
the TSCPA's Federal Tax Committee; and
WHEREAS, a graduate of Georgetown College, Mr. Frizzell worked in Cincinnati, Ohio,
for a time, but he eventually moved back to his hometown of Johnson City, where he worked for
a firm that eventually merged with another firm; and

WHEREAS, sensing that the time was right, David Frizzell founded his own accounting
firm in 2015 and has never since regretted his decision to become an entrepreneur; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Frizzell's professional association with his clientele has been extremely
fruitful for him and his clients alike, and his longstanding commitment to the small business
owners in his community has been crucial to the growth of the local economy; and
WHEREAS, presently rendering public service as a member of the Johnson City
Evening Rotary Club, Mr. Frizzell is a past president and board member of the organization; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Frizzell is an active member of Piney Flats Presbyterian Church, where
he serves as an elder; and
WHEREAS, his many personal and professional accomplishments aside, David Frizzell
is most grateful for the love, companionship, and support he shares with his wife, Margaret Ann;
and
WHEREAS, David Frizzell, CPA, is most worthy of our approbation on this special
occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate David Frizzell on
receiving the National Federation of Independent Business's 2025 Small Business Champion
Award and recognize him as a faithful advocate, protector, and servant of small business, the
economic engine of our nation.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
