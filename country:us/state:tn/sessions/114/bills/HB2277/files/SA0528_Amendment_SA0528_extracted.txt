Section 1:
Senate Government Operations 1
Amendment No. 1 to SB2403
Jackson
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting the first sentence in § 49-6-6018(a) of SECTION 11 and substituting:
There is established the assessment review board of the Tennessee
comprehensive assessment program, referred to in this section as the assessment
review board, to annually review, under the direction of the department of education, the
Tennessee comprehensive assessment program (TCAP) tests administered to students
in the most recent school year.
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting the amendatory language in SECTION 12 and
substituting:
( ) Assessment review board of the Tennessee comprehensive assessment
program, created by § 49-6-6018;
