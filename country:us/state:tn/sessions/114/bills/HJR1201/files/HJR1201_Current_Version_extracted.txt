HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1201
By Reneau
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Daryl Slaughter upon his
receipt of the Investigator of the Year Award
from the Chattanooga Police Department.
WHEREAS, our nation was conceived by individuals who were willing to sacrifice their
personal concerns to ensure the public safety and welfare, and the Volunteer State is especially
proud to be the home of so many valiant individuals who have performed above and beyond the
call of duty in service of public safety; and
WHEREAS, Daryl Slaughter was recently honored with the Chattanooga Police
Department's (CPD's) Investigator of the Year Award in recognition of his tireless work in the
Homicide Unit since 2016; and
WHEREAS, his exceptional investigative skills are evident in his unprecedented ninety-
three percent clearance rate for twenty-eight homicide cases, far exceeding the sixty-one
percent national average; and
WHEREAS, in 2025, his five-year effort secured a conviction in a heinous 2020 double
homicide and led to arrests in two other 2020 cold cases, providing justice and initiating healing
for affected families and the community; and
WHEREAS, beyond his official duties, Investigator Daryl Slaughter volunteers on the
Hostage Negotiation Team, assists the SWAT Team, and serves on various department boards;
and
WHEREAS, Investigator Daryl Slaughter is an exemplary, selfless detective, a strong
leader, and a dependable family man, and he is highly deserving of this honor; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
HJR1201
016032
- 1 -
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Daryl Slaughter upon his receipt of the
Investigator of the Year Award from the Chattanooga Police Department.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
- 2 - 016032
