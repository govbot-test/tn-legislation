Senate Commerce and Labor 1
Amendment No. 1 to SB0231
Bailey
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 231* House Bill No. 296
by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:
SECTION 1. The Tennessee advisory commission on intergovernmental relations
(TACIR) shall conduct a study on the feasibility of implementing and potential effects of enacting
the insurance coverage requirements proposed in Senate Bill 231 of the 114th General
Assembly, as originally filed with the chief clerk of the senate on January 21, 2025. TACIR shall
publish a report of its findings and recommendations and deliver a copy of the report to the chief
clerk of the senate, the chief clerk of the house of representatives, and the legislative librarian
no later than July 31, 2026.
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
