HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1407
By Powell
A RESOLUTION to honor and celebrate the longstanding
friendship and partnership between the State of
Tennessee and the Federal Republic of
Germany.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly find it appropriate to commend
those international relationships that have enriched the economic, cultural, educational, and
civic life of this State; and
WHEREAS, the State of Tennessee and the Federal Republic of Germany enjoy
longstanding and mutually beneficial ties rooted in trade, investment, manufacturing, education,
culture, and citizen diplomacy; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee has become a leading destination for international investment
and job creation and has actively recruited and supported German investment across the State;
and
WHEREAS, German companies represent a substantial source of foreign direct
investment in Tennessee, contributing to advanced manufacturing, automotive production, and
supply chain development, and supporting thousands of jobs for Tennesseans; and
WHEREAS, the presence of Volkswagen in Chattanooga stands as one of the most
visible and impactful examples of the strong economic relationship between Tennessee and
Germany, supporting jobs, wages, and tax revenue in Tennessee communities; and
WHEREAS, the people-to-people relationship between Tennessee and Germany has
also been strengthened through official sister-city and friendship ties, including, among others,
Nashville and Magdeburg, Chattanooga and Hamm, Chattanooga and Wolfsburg, and
Franklin/Williamson County and Bad Soden am Taunus; and

WHEREAS, these relationships have fostered student exchanges, professional
collaboration, cultural programming, municipal cooperation, and enduring friendships between
Tennesseans and the German people; and
WHEREAS, opportunities for collaboration between Tennessee and Germany in
workforce development, apprenticeship programs, and higher education partnerships offer
meaningful potential to strengthen both economies and prepare the next generation of skilled
workers; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee is served by the Consulate General of the Federal Republic of
Germany in Atlanta and by an Honorary Consul of the Federal Republic of Germany in
Nashville, and German-American business ties in this State are further supported through the
German American Chamber of Commerce of the Southern United States; and
WHEREAS, in an increasingly competitive global economy, Tennessee benefits from
strengthening ties with trusted democratic allies that share a commitment to innovation, skilled
workforce development, advanced manufacturing, entrepreneurship, and long-term economic
cooperation; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and celebrate the longstanding friendship and
partnership between the State of Tennessee and the Federal Republic of Germany and
commend the contributions of German businesses, civic institutions, educational partners, and
municipal leaders whose work has strengthened Tennessee's economy and enriched the lives
of Tennesseans.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that we welcome continued cooperation in the areas of
trade, advanced manufacturing, automotive innovation, workforce development, education,
tourism, and cultural exchange, and celebrate continued dialogue and engagement with
