HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1045
By Hale
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Christopher Vance on
his selection as the 2025-2026 Cannon County
Grades 9-12 Teacher of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
specially recognize those outstanding teachers who have devoted their careers to providing the
highest-quality education for Tennessee's students; and
WHEREAS, Christopher Vance is one such estimable educator, who has distinguished
himself as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Vance has been selected as the 2025-2026 Cannon County Grades 9-
12 Teacher of the Year, a most befitting honor that recognizes the positive influence he has on
so many young lives; and
WHEREAS, a teacher for thirty-seven years, Christopher Vance is in his third year of
teaching at Cannon County High School, where he serves as the ninth grade English language
arts teacher; and
WHEREAS, currently, he teaches one honors class and five English 9 classes; as the
sole English teacher for ninth grade, he also serves as the main sponsor for the Class of 2029;
and
WHEREAS, as the class sponsor, Mr. Vance is responsible for collecting money,
ordering t-shirts for the class, and ordering supplies for homecoming; he is also a member of the
prom committee and helps students gather ideas, order materials, and set up on the day of
prom, in addition to attending as a chaperone; and
WHEREAS, prior to working at Cannon County High School, Mr. Vance taught at Whites
Creek High School, Mt. Juliet High School, Beech High School, and DeKalb County High
School; and

WHEREAS, an extremely talented and compassionate educator, Mr. Vance works
assiduously to motivate his students to reach their full potential, both as scholars and as
citizens; and
WHEREAS, Christopher Vance serves as an inspiration to his colleagues and students
alike, and his friendship and counsel are highly valued by those who are fortunate to know him;
and
WHEREAS, Mr. Vance truly epitomizes the dedicated teacher who is wholly committed
to the highest ideals of public service and quality education for all, and he should be specially
recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and congratulate Christopher Vance on being chosen as
the 2025-2026 Cannon County Grades 9-12 Teacher of the Year, commend his exceptional
service to the children of Tennessee, salute his professional expertise and personal excellence,
and wish him all the best in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
