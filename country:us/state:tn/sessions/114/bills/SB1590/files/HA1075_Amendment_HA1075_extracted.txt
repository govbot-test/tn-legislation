AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 1590* House Bill No. 1510
by adding the following section immediately preceding the effective date section and
renumbering the subsequent section accordingly:
SECTION __. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 11-13-111(b), is amended by
adding the following as a new subdivision (3):
(3) Subdivision (b)(1) does not prohibit construction, operation, or expansion of a
landfill that has a valid landfill permit on the effective date of this act, and that is located
within two (2) miles of the center of:
(A) The portion of the Buffalo River that lies within Wayne, Perry,
Humphreys, and Lewis counties;
(B) The segment of Beaverdam Creek beginning with the confluence of
Brush Fork and Piney Fork in Hickman County and ending with its confluence
with the Duck River in Hickman County;
(C) The segment of Lick Creek beginning with the confluence of North
Fork Lick Creek and South Fork Lick Creek in Williamson County and ending
with its confluence with the Duck River in Hickman County;
(D) The segment of the Piney River beginning with the confluence of the
West Piney River and the East Piney River in Dickson County and ending with its
confluence with the Duck River in Hickman County;
(E) The segment of Swan Creek beginning with the confluence of Dry
Branch and Swan Creek in Hickman County and ending with its confluence with
the Duck River in Hickman County; and
