SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 804
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Trey Bailey on being
named to the TSWA Class 2A All-State cross
country team.
WHEREAS, we are pleased to recognize and commend those successful high school
athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring honor to Tennessee and serve as
exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable individual is Trey Bailey of Greeneville High School, an
athlete of extraordinary talent, skill, and dedication, who has proven himself a formidable
competitor; and
WHEREAS, the Tennessee Sports Writers Association (TSWA) recently named Trey
Bailey to the 2025 Class 2A All-State cross country team in recognition of his excellence and his
display of drive and determination; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Bailey epitomizes all that is good in today's student athlete, as he has
achieved a high level of success in sports while evincing great character both on and off the
field; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Bailey won the Class 2A State meet with a time of 15:12:89, and his
notable achievement is a source of great pride for his family, his school, and his community; and
WHEREAS, Trey Bailey is a winner both on and off the course, and his dedication to
excellence is clearly deserving of the respect, admiration, and commendation of this General
Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Trey Bailey of
Greeneville High School on being named to the Tennessee Sports Writers Association's Class

2A All-State cross country team, wishing him much success and happiness in his future
endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared

the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
