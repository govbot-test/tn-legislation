Section 1:
By Reeves
HOUSE BILL 2517
By Todd
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 44,
relative to animal health.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 44-2-101, is amended by adding the
following new subdivisions:
( ) "Monitored disease" means a disease that is endemic in the United States as
listed by the USDA;
( ) "Notifiable disease" means a disease and condition consisting of emergency
incidents, emerging disease incidents, and regulated disease incidents as listed by the
USDA;

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 44-2-102, is amended by deleting
subdivision (4) and substituting:
(4)
(A) Recommend tests or vaccinations of animals within the state or
imported into the state for the purpose of protecting the health of animals; and
(B) Order tests or vaccinations of animals within the state or imported
into the state for the purpose of detecting or treating a notifiable disease or
monitored disease;

Section 4:
SECTION 3. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
