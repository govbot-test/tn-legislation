HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1287
By Bulso
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Gavin Johnson on
being named the 2025 WILLCO Awards Boys'
Track & Field Athlete of the Year.
WHEREAS, we are pleased to recognize and commend those successful high school
athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring honor to Tennessee and serve as
exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable individual is Gavin Johnson of Centennial High School,
an athlete of extraordinary talent, skill, and dedication, who has proven himself to be a
formidable competitor in track & field; and
WHEREAS, in recognition of his success, Mr. Johnson has been named the WILLCO
Awards Boys' Track and Field Athlete of the Year; and
WHEREAS, Gavin Johnson won the 400-meter race at the Class AAA championships;
he is only the second Centennial High runner to win a WILLCO award; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Johnson epitomizes all that is good in today's student athlete, as he has
achieved a high level of success in track & field while evincing great character; and
WHEREAS, Gavin Johnson's notable achievements are a source of great pride for his
family, his community, and his school, and it is appropriate that we recognize this accomplished
athlete; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Gavin Johnson on being named the 2025
WILLCO Awards Boys' Track & Field Athlete of the Year, wishing him much success and
happiness in his future endeavors.
