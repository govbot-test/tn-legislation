SENATE RESOLUTION 258
By Walley
A RESOLUTION to recognize David Baker, 2026 President of the
Tennessee School Boards Association.
WHEREAS, the members of this legislative body take great pleasure in paying tribute to
those exemplary professionals who have ascended to the very pinnacle of their chosen
professions; and
WHEREAS, David Baker was recently named President of the Tennessee School
Boards Association (TSBA) 2026 Board of Directors, a station most befitting of his many years
of dedication and excellence in Tennessee education; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Baker has served in numerous leadership roles within Tennessee,
greatly contributing to strategic initiatives and fostering collaboration among board members;
and
WHEREAS, David Baker's tenure includes serving on Hardin County's School Board as
vice chairman; and
WHEREAS, throughout his career, Mr. Baker has been recognized for his tireless efforts
and his ability to navigate complex issues, earning the widespread respect and admiration of his
colleagues and community alike; and
WHEREAS, David Baker continues to inspire his peers with his unwavering commitment
to education and his community, and he is richly deserving of our approbation on this special
occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we recognize and honor David
Baker, 2026 President of the Tennessee School Boards Association, extending our best wishes
for much continued success in all his future endeavors.
