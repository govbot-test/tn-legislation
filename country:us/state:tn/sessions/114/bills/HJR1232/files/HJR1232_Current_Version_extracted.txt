HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1232
By Stevens
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate the Eagleville High
School softball team upon winning the 2025
TSSAA Division I, Class A State Championship.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should salute those
student athletes who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as true
champions of whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, proving themselves to be the fiercest of competitors and, as such,
deserving of the highest regard, the Eagleville High School softball team captured the 2025
Tennessee Secondary School Athletic Association (TSSAA) Division I, Class A State
Championship; and
WHEREAS, in the championship game, Eagleville defeated Hollow Rock-Bruceton by a
score of 8-1, having flown by the competition to go undefeated during the state tournament with
prior victories over North Greene and Jo Byrns on their march to claim the crown of state
champion; and
WHEREAS, the members of the state champion Eagleville High School softball team are
Brinli M. Bain, Brylee Bain, Remi Cook, Riley Cook, Shyanne Cook, Cate Darnell, Madison
Davenport, Marilyn Fluty, Payton Gardner, Presley Gardner, Cara Grimes, Ashley Herrington,
Presley Holland, Madison Kendrick, Presley Knowles, Addisyn Linton, Addison Martin, Ellie
Nguyen, Ann-Marie Oliver, Lilly Shapcott, Aubry Tibbs, Ashtyn Walker, Rylee M. Warbritton, and
Jaynie Wray; and
WHEREAS, Eagleville's superb performance would not have been possible without the
expert guidance of Head Coach Chad Leeman; assistant coaches Thomas Bain, Chris Ellis,
Kim Warbritton, and Brandon Whitt; and the unfailing support of the players' families, their fans,
and their community; and

WHEREAS, the members of the Eagleville High School softball team epitomize all that is
good in today's student athletes, as they have achieved a premier level of success in
competition while exhibiting the highest degree of character in all aspects of life; and
WHEREAS, the perseverance, determination, work ethic, and talent of these young
women not only made possible a championship season but also surely bode well for their
success in every future endeavor; and
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly find it appropriate to pause in their
deliberations to acknowledge and applaud the members of the Eagleville High School softball
team for their outstanding performance and for serving as examples of the exceptional quality of
the youth of Tennessee; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and congratulate the players, coaches, and fans of the
Eagleville High School softball team on winning the 2025 TSSAA Division I, Class A State
Championship and extend our best wishes for every continued success in all their future
endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
