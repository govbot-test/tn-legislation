SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1169
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Faye Colyer on the
celebration of her 105th birthday.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pay tribute to
those citizens who are celebrating special occasions in their estimable lives; and
WHEREAS, Faye Colyer of Greene County will celebrate her 105th birthday on May 15,
2026, a milestone to be commemorated as yet another precious souvenir of life's rich pageant;
and
WHEREAS, Mrs. Colyer has lived an amazing life and has experienced firsthand the
breadth and span of American history; and
WHEREAS, Faye Colyer is a joy to be around and a blessing to all who have the good
fortune of knowing her; and
WHEREAS, Mrs. Colyer remains a beloved member of her community who is admired
for her faith, integrity, and enthusiasm for life; and
WHEREAS, we wish to grasp this golden opportunity to specially recognize one of
Tennessee's most outstanding citizens on this very special occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Faye Colyer on the
celebration of her 105th birthday and extend to her our best wishes for many happy returns of
the day.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
