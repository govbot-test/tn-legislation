SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1071
By Seal
A RESOLUTION to honor Chris Williams of Clayton Homes's
Rutledge frame shop on many years of service
to homebuyers.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly take great pleasure in paying tribute
to those outstanding Tennesseans who have helped make the American dream more
accessible by building high-quality, yet affordable, housing; and
WHEREAS, March 26, 2026, truly marked the end of an era, as Clayton Homes's
Rutledge facility turned out its last house frame after forty-two years in operation; and
WHEREAS, this fateful day also saw the beginning of a new era, as Clayton Homes
reassigned many of its Rutledge employees to the Clayton Bean Station frame shop as part of
the company's new venture; and
WHEREAS, Chris Williams was a vital employee of the Rutledge frame shop for many
years and always excelled at his duties; and
WHEREAS, the State of Tennessee treasures its hard-working men and women who,
through the fruits of their labor, help meet the basic needs of their fellow citizens, and nothing is
more essential than a home to call your own; and
WHEREAS, Chris Williams is worthy of our approbation on this momentous occasion;
now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor Chris Williams of Clayton Homes's
Rutledge frame shop on his many years of service to homebuyers and extend to him our best
wishes for continued success and happiness in his future endeavors.
SJR1071
017141
- 1 -
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
