HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1243
By Capley
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Emily Golden, 2026 Top
Ten Senior of Hampshire Unit School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Emily Golden is one such exceptional student, who was recently named a
Top Ten Senior of the 2026 graduating class at Hampshire Unit School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Ms. Golden has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in her remarkable high school career, Emily Golden has earned an
exemplary grade point average while participating in numerous extracurricular activities,
including serving as an FFA officer and FCA service representative and being a member of
Senior Beta, HOSA, FBLA, Read-it Plus Club, and Yearbook Club; and
WHEREAS, she has generously volunteered her time to FCA events, the Blueberry
Festival, the Fall Festival, and haunted houses; and
WHEREAS, apart from her superlative scholastic work, she has experience repairing
and operating heavy equipment, including backhoes, truck loaders, rollers, tire loaders, and
bulldozers; and
WHEREAS, Emily Golden's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes
well for her future success at Columbia State Community College, where she will study
accounting; and

WHEREAS, Ms. Golden's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for
her parents, Melody and David Golden, her school, and her community; and
WHEREAS, Emily Golden's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of the members of this General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor Emily Golden on being named a 2026
Hampshire Unit School Top Ten Senior, salute her impressive academic achievement, and
extend to her our best wishes for a happy, successful future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
