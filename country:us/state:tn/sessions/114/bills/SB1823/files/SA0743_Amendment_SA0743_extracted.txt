by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 12-4-703, is amended by deleting the
language "forty-five (45) days" and substituting "thirty (30) days".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 12-4-704, is amended by adding the
following as a new subsection:
(c)
(1) Not later than September 15, 2027, and each September 15
thereafter, each agency that makes payments to a business under a contract
pursuant to § 12-4-703 shall report for the immediately preceding calendar year:
(A) The number of late payments made and that remain
outstanding and the aggregate amount of such payments; and
(B) The amount of interest paid on such late payments and that
remains unpaid on outstanding late payments.
(2) The report submitted pursuant to subdivision (c)(1) must be submitted
to the chair of the state and local government committee of the senate and to the
chair of the committee of the house of representatives with jurisdiction over
public contracts.

Section 4:
SECTION 3. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.
