by deleting Section 4 and substituting:
SECTION 4. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-6-2602(3), is amended by
deleting subdivision (D) and substituting:
(D) Is a member of a household with an annual income for the previous year that
does not exceed four (4) times the federal income eligibility guidelines for free or
reduced-price lunch;
SECTION 5. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-6-3504, is amended by adding
the following as a new subsection:
(f) If the number of scholarship applications received by the department exceeds
the number of scholarships available for the respective school year, then the department
may consider an eligible student's completed scholarship application as that student's
application for an education savings account for the same school year and may award
that student an education savings account in lieu of a scholarship if the student is an
eligible student as defined in § 49-6-2602 and otherwise meets the requirements in part
26 of this chapter for the student to receive an education savings account.
SECTION 6. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-6-2604(a)(2), is amended by
adding the following as a new subdivision:
(C) A process, including, but not limited to, the establishment of additional
application windows, forms, and procedures, for the department to consider the
completed application of an eligible student, as defined in § 49-6-3502, for a scholarship,
as defined in § 49-6-3502, as the student's application for an education savings account
