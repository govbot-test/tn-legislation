Senate Health and Welfare 1
Amendment No. 1 to SB2414
Crowe
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 2414* House Bill No. 2278
by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 68-11-1303, is amended by adding
the following as a new subsection:
(i)
(1) Any certificate of public advantage in effect on June 30, 2028,
automatically expires; provided, that any pricing restrictions in a certificate of
public advantage that are in effect as of the expiration date remain in effect until
the earlier of the fifth anniversary of the expiration date or the date that a new
inpatient acute care hospital opens in the geographic service area covered by the
expired certificate of public advantage.
(2) After June 30, 2028, the pricing restrictions must be supervised by
the attorney general and reporter pursuant to a supervision agreement, and may
only be modified by the mutual written agreement of the entity subject to the
pricing restrictions and the attorney general and reporter. The entity subject to
the pricing restrictions is responsible for the costs of the attorney general and
reporter's supervision of the pricing restrictions.
(3) This section does not limit or impair the authority of the attorney
general and reporter to enforce state or federal antitrust law.
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
