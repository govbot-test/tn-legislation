HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1439
By Camper
A RESOLUTION to honor Representative Harold M. Love, Jr.,
upon his induction into the Board of Preachers at
the 41st Annual Martin Luther King, Jr., College
of Ministers and Laity ceremony at Morehouse
College.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly appropriately
acknowledge and recognize our fellow members who realize special accomplishments in their
professional lives; and
WHEREAS, such an event has occurred in the life of our colleague, Representative
Harold M. Love, Jr., of Nashville, who has been selected for induction into the Board of
Preachers at the 41st Annual Martin Luther King, Jr., College of Ministers and Laity ceremony at
Morehouse College in Atlanta, Georgia, on April 9, 2026; and
WHEREAS, ordained as an elder by the A.M.E. Church in 1999, Representative Harold
Love is the pastor of Lee Chapel AME Church in Nashville, having previously served as pastor
of Hopewell A.M.E. Church in Columbia and as a presiding elder of the South Nashville District
of the A.M.E. Church; and
WHEREAS, a 1990 graduate of Whites Creek High School, Representative Love holds
an undergraduate degree from Tennessee State University, a master's degree from the
Vanderbilt University School of Divinity, and a Doctor of Philosophy degree in Public Policy and
Administration from Tennessee State University; and
WHEREAS, Representative Love renders sterling service as a member of the House
Finance, Ways and Means Committee, the House Insurance Committee, the House TennCare
Subcommittee, and the Tennessee Advisory Commission on Intergovernmental Relations; and

WHEREAS, a member of the House Democratic Caucus, which he currently serves as
Assistant Leader, Representative Love is also the current president of the National Black
Caucus of State Legislators; and
WHEREAS, a Mason and a Shriner, Representative Love gives back to his community
through his service as a member of the board of the 18th Avenue Family Enrichment Center;
and
WHEREAS, it is most appropriate that we recognize our esteemed colleague on this
special occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate our friend Representative Harold M. Love, Jr.,
upon his induction into the Board of Preachers at the 41st Annual Martin Luther King, Jr.,
College of Ministers and Laity ceremony at Morehouse College.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
