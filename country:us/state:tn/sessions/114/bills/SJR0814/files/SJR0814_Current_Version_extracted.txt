SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 814
By Watson
A RESOLUTION to recognize Howard J. Kittell on the occasion of
his retirement.
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that this General Assembly should honor those citizens who
have performed with uncommon devotion and enthusiasm throughout their careers in public
service; and
WHEREAS, Howard J. Kittell is one such individual, who has retired after a distinguished
and purpose-filled career in public service, culminating in sixteen years of exceptional service as
president and chief executive officer of the Andrew Jackson Foundation; and
WHEREAS, during his tenure, Mr. Kittell has received credit for a number of projects that
have ultimately advanced the mission and the reputation of the Andrew Jackson Foundation,
including restoration of The Hermitage Mansion, the log slave cabins, the Cabin-by-the-Spring,
The Hermitage Church, and the Tulip Grove Mansion; and
WHEREAS, additional projects included infrastructure improvements and renovations to
several buildings, such as the Andrew Jackson Center; the creation of the award-winning
"Andrew Jackson: Born for a Storm" exhibit; the publication of an illustrated Andrew Jackson
biography and the Hermitage Guidebook; and the production of the introductory film Jackson,
among other educational materials; and
WHEREAS, no stranger to awards and accolades, Mr. Kittell recently received the 2025
Lewis R. Donelson Award in recognition of his leadership and support of The Hermitage and its
preservation efforts; and
WHEREAS, throughout his estimable career, Howard Kittell has demonstrated the
utmost professionalism, ability, and integrity, winning the unbridled respect and admiration of his
colleagues and community alike; now, therefore,
SJR0814
010005
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we extend to Howard J. Kittell our heartfelt wishes
for a happy and fulfilling retirement, applaud his dedication to the Andrew Jackson Foundation,
and wish him much happiness and every continued success in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
