SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1058
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Marlin Hensley upon
winning first place at the National Sweet
Sorghum Producers and Processors
Association's annual Sweet Sorghum Syrup
Contest.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should recognize
those Tennesseans who, through dedication to their craft and preservation of traditional
practices, bring distinction to this State; and
WHEREAS, Marlin Hensley has distinguished himself through his exceptional skill in the
time-honored art of sorghum syrup production; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Hensley was awarded first place at the National Sweet Sorghum
Producers and Processors Association's fortieth annual conference and national contest held
March 4-6 in Pigeon Forge, earning national recognition for the superior quality of his product;
and
WHEREAS, this achievement is a testament to Marlin Hensley's commitment to
excellence, as well as his deep knowledge of the traditional methods required to produce
sorghum syrup of outstanding flavor, color, and consistency; and
WHEREAS, having learned the craft from his father, Mr. Hensley continues a proud
family tradition, contributing to the preservation of Tennessee's rich agricultural heritage; and
WHEREAS, through his work at Hensley Farm in Ernestville, Marlin Hensley not only
produces award-winning syrup but also helps sustain and pass forward the cultural and
agricultural practices that define many rural communities across our State; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Hensley's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of our respect,
admiration, and commendation; now, therefore,
SJR1058
017007
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Marlin Hensley upon
winning first place at the National Sweet Sorghum Producers and Processors Association's
annual Sweet Sorghum Syrup Contest and extend to him our best wishes for continued
success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
- 2 - 017007
