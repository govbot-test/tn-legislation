SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 942
By Pody
A RESOLUTION to recognize the Sattler Foundation on the
occasion of its tenth anniversary.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should honor and
commend those notable organizations that endeavor to enrich the lives of the young people of
this State; and
WHEREAS, the Sattler Foundation is one such sterling institution that will celebrate its
tenth anniversary this year on March 21, 2026, at the Lebanon Municipal Airport; and
WHEREAS, for the past ten years, the Sattler Foundation has held as its mission to
provide Middle Tennessee students with exposure to aviation, space education, and related
employment opportunities in the aerospace industry; and
WHEREAS, the Sattler Foundation has inspired students through hands-on aerospace
camps and educational experiences, helping young minds explore aviation, space, and STEM,
and encouraging curiosity, confidence, and future career pathways in aerospace; and
WHEREAS, through the Sattler Foundation, students develop critical thinking and
problem-solving skills while concurrently learning about aviation and space, encouraging young
people to become innovative critical thinkers through engineering design, hands-on
experimentation, and solving real-world problems; and
WHEREAS, the Sattler Foundation's tenth anniversary celebration features a special
keynote address by Elizabeth McCormick, former U.S. Army Black Hawk helicopter pilot,
leadership expert, and inspirational speaker, as well as reflections on ten years of student
success and community impact and a look ahead at the future of the organization's aerospace
education programs; and

WHEREAS, for a decade, the Sattler Foundation has continued to enrich and inspire the
intellect of Tennessee's youth, and it is appropriate that we honor this organization on the
celebration of its tenth anniversary; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend the Sattler Foundation on
the occasion of its tenth anniversary, extending our best wishes for many years of continued
success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
