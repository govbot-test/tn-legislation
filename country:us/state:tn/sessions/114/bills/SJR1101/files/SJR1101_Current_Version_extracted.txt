SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1101
By Roberts
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Pastor Robert
Gardner on the celebration of his sixty-second
birthday.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those individuals who are celebrating special occasions in their estimable lives; and
WHEREAS, one such gentleman is Pastor Robert Gardner, who celebrates his sixty-
second birthday on March 28, 2026, a milestone commemorated as yet another precious
souvenir of life's rich pageant; and
WHEREAS, born on March 28, 1964, Pastor Gardner earned a degree in electrical
engineering from Tennessee State University in 1989 before receiving his master's degree in
business administration from Trevecca Nazarene University in 2004; and
WHEREAS, an esteemed church leader, Robert Gardner has served the congregation at
Faith Church International in Springfield as pastor for twenty-two years; he is also the founder of
the Master's Table Soup Kitchen, further demonstrating his commitment to ministry and the well-
being of those in his community; and
WHEREAS, in addition to his pastoral career, Pastor Gardner served as director of the
Springfield Electric Department for thirty years, in which position he earned the respect and
admiration of his peers and community alike for the superior quality of his work; and
WHEREAS, his many professional accomplishments aside, Pastor Robert Gardner has
enjoyed the love and support of his cherished wife, Peggy Swan Gardner, for thirty-six years;
and
WHEREAS, as Pastor Gardner marks his sixty-second birthday, he remains a beloved
member of his community who is admired for his faith, wisdom, and enthusiasm for life; and

WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly wish to grasp this golden
opportunity to specially recognize this outstanding individual on this very special occasion; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Pastor Robert Gardner
on the celebration of his sixty-second birthday and extend to him our best wishes for many
happy returns of the day.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
