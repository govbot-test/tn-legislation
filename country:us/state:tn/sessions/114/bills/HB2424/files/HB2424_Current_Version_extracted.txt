HOUSE BILL 2424
By Howell
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 5;
Title 6; Title 7; Title 9; Title 12 and Title 67, relative
to property tax.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 67-5-1609, is amended by deleting
"shall notify" in the second sentence and substituting instead "shall notify in writing".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
