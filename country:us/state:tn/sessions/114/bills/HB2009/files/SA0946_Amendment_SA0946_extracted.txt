Amendment No. 1 to SB2027
Watson

AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 2027* House Bill No. 2009
by adding the following as a new subsection (b) in SECTION 1 and redesignating existing
subsection (b) as a new subsection (c):
(b) If the department approves the request to conduct a highway traffic noise
study pursuant to subsection (a), then the department shall submit to the requesting
county or municipality an invoice of the actual cost to conduct the study. The study must
not be conducted until the cost is paid to the department by the requesting county or
municipality.
