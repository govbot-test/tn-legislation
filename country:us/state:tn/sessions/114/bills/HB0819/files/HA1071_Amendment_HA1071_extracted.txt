Section 1:
Amendment No. 6 to HB0819
Williams
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting the language "described in this subsection (b) for acute care hospitals licensed on or
after July 1, 2030" and substituting "described in this subsection (b) for acute care hospitals
licensed on or after July 1, 2028".
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting the language "shall complete a report by July 1, 2028, that
includes a compilation of payor mix" and substituting "shall complete a report by January 1,
2029, that includes a compilation of payor mix".
