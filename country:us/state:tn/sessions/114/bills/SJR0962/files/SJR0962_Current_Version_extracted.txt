SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 962
By Pody
A RESOLUTION to recognize and honor Roger Haley for decades
of devoted service to his fellow Tennesseans.
WHEREAS, it is most fitting that the members of this General Assembly should
acknowledge and applaud those community leaders who are wholly devoted to enhancing the
quality of life for their fellow citizens and improving and enriching the place they all call home;
and
WHEREAS, one such noteworthy person is Roger Haley of Murfreesboro, who has
distinguished himself as a citizen of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, an honorable veteran of the Vietnam War, Mr. Haley served as a member of
the United States Army from 1968 to 1970; his military service was the first in a long line of
selfless acts in service to his fellow citizens; and
WHEREAS, from 1970 to 1978, Mr. Haley owned and operated eponymous
enterprises—first Haley Remolding Company (1970-1973), then Haley Construction Company
(1973-1978); in 2010, he launched Haley Consulting, which he continues to operate; and
WHEREAS, his entrepreneurial career also included serving as president and part
owner of Murfreesboro Supply Company (1977-1986) and owning and operating Mr. Tool
Rental Center (1979-1990); and
WHEREAS, Roger Haley became City Manager of Murfreesboro in 1988 and held that
post for over two decades, serving with diligence and dedication as he prioritized the needs and
best interests of his neighbors and the place he calls home; he was also a member of the
International City Managers Association and the Tennessee City Manager Association (TCMA),
earning TCMA City Manager of the Year honors in 2008; and

WHEREAS, dedicated to the political process, he served as County Chairman for Lamar
Alexander's 1978 campaign and 1982 re-election campaign for governor and for Don
Sundquist's 1993 gubernatorial campaign; and
WHEREAS, Roger Haley was appointed by Governor Lamar Alexander to serve as
chairman of the Tennessee Law Enforcement Academy and by President Ronald Reagan to
serve as a delegate to the White House Small Business Council; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Haley's community service extended to his good work as a member of
the Murfreesboro City Council, Murfreesboro Water & Sewer Board, Murfreesboro Planning
Commission, Murfreesboro Electric Board, and Tennessee Municipal Board, where he served
on the Transportation, Legislation, Review, and Safety committees; and
WHEREAS, highly regarded in his community and field and sought out for his wisdom
and guidance, he served as vice chairman of the Rutherford County Industrial Board, Rutherford
County Health & Education Board, and Tennessee Municipal Pool; and
WHEREAS, Roger Haley epitomizes the spirit and commitment that are characteristic of
a true Tennessean; and
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly find it appropriate to acknowledge
and applaud Mr. Haley for his dedication to the State of Tennessee and its citizens; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we commend Roger Haley for his innumerable
contributions to the citizens of this State and extend to him our best wishes for continued
success in all his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
