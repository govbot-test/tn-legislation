SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1108
By Harshbarger
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Rylan Kiser upon being
named the 2026 Boys & Girls Club of Greater
Kingsport Youth of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly pause in their
deliberations to salute those young citizens who, through their remarkable efforts, have
distinguished themselves as emerging community and state leaders of whom we can all be
proud; and
WHEREAS, one such deserving person, Rylan Kiser, was recently named the 2026
Boys & Girls Club of Greater Kingsport Youth of the Year; and
WHEREAS, a student at Dobyns-Bennett High School, Rylan Kiser has been a member
of the Boys & Girls Club of Greater Kingsport for six years; and
WHEREAS, the Boys & Girls Clubs exist to serve youth and to be a safe place of
encouragement, character building, security, and love for children; and
WHEREAS, since 1947, the Youth of the Year has been the Boys & Girls Clubs of
America's premier recognition program, celebrating the extraordinary achievements of Club
members; and
WHEREAS, Youth of the Year participants embody the values of leadership, service,
academic excellence, and healthy lifestyles; and
WHEREAS, Youth of the Year participants such as Rylan Kiser exemplify the critical
impact of Boys & Girls Clubs on the lives of young people; and
WHEREAS, through the Youth of the Year program, Rylan Kiser and other Youth of the
Year participants are able to showcase their talents and achievements, share their hopes and
dreams, and work toward a bright and positive future; and

WHEREAS, Rylan Kiser is a shining example that "great futures start here"–at Boys &
Girls Clubs; and
WHEREAS, the success of this State, the strength of our communities, and the overall
vitality of American society depend, in great measure, upon the dedication of young people such
as Rylan Kiser, who use their considerable talents and resources to serve others and who
should be specially recognized for their public and community service; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Rylan Kiser upon being
named the 2026 Boys & Girls Club of Greater Kingsport Youth of the Year, extending to him our
best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
