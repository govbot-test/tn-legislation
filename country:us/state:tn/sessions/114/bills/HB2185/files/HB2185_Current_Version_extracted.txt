SENATE BILL 2204
By Johnson
HOUSE BILL 2185
By Lamberth
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 2,
relative to voter eligibility.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 2-2-401, is amended by adding the
following as a new subsection (c) and redesignating the existing subsection (c) and any
subsequent subsection accordingly:
(c) The portal may also access data from the systematic alien verification for
entitlements (SAVE) program from the United States department of homeland security,
United States citizenship and immigration services (DHS-USCIS), if DHS-USCIS makes
such data available via a secure web service.
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
