SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 693
By White
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Jimmy Pitts on
receiving the 2025 Pinnacle Award.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should salute those
citizens who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as community
leaders of whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable person is Jimmy Pitts, who was recently presented with
the 2025 Pinnacle Award by Leadership Rutherford; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Pitts has devoted more than three decades to leadership and service
across the wireless, healthcare, and real estate industries; and
WHEREAS, a proud resident of Smyrna since 1999, who happened to relocate from
Smyrna, Georgia, Jimmy Pitts has long been invested in the growth, development, and well-
being of the community he calls home; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Pitts has served the citizens of Smyrna in various capacities, including
as a member of the Smyrna Parks and Recreation Advisory Board and the Smyrna Planning
Commission; he has been a dedicated youth mentor and volunteer coach for Smyrna athletics
and the Boys & Girls Club, where he is a former board chair and continues to serve as a board
member; and
WHEREAS, he also contributes to key civic and nonprofit initiatives, serving as Long
Range Planning Chair for the Senior Activity Center and helping launch the town's first Simply
Smyrna Summer Fest; and
WHEREAS, a licensed minister with more than twenty-eight years of experience, Mr.
Pitts currently serves on the pastoral care team at Citipointe Church in Nashville, where he and
