SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 712
By Johnson
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate the Independence
High School boys' cross country team, 2025
TSSAA Division I, Class AAA state champions.
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that we honor those Tennessee students who, through their
athletic prowess, have achieved the pinnacle of success and serve as exceptional ambassadors
of the Volunteer State; and
WHEREAS, one such group of estimable individuals is the Independence High School
boys' cross country team, which won the 2025 TSSAA Division I, Class AAA state
championship; and
WHEREAS, in addition to winning the first boys' cross country state championship in
school history, the young men from Independence High School also won the 2025 Region 7-
AAA championship; and
WHEREAS, in the state finals, key runner Asher Oates finished as the individual state
runner-up while all seven Independence runners placed in the top thirty-five spots to secure the
team title; and
WHEREAS, success in statewide competition is achieved only through hard work,
dedication, and the commitment to working together as a team and excelling with a cooperative
spirit; the Eagles exhibited each of these rare qualities this season; and
WHEREAS, the boys' cross country team benefited from the astute leadership of Head
Coach Luke Finley; and
WHEREAS, the Independence High School Eagles' noteworthy achievement is a
tremendous source of pride for their families, their classmates, and their entire community; and
SJR0712
013137
- 1 -
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to acknowledge and applaud the members of the
boys' cross country team of Independence High School for serving as exemplars of the high
quality of the youth of Tennessee; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate the members and
coaches of the Independence High School boys' cross country team, winners of the 2025
TSSAA Division I, Class AAA state championship, and extend to them our best wishes for
continued success in all their future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
