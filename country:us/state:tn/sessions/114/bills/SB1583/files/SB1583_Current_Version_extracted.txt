Section 1:
SENATE BILL 1583
By Walley
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 38;
Title 39; Title 40; Title 43; Title 53; Title 67 and
Title 68, relative to controlled substances.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 68-7-109(d), is amended by adding
the following language to the end of the subsection:
Reports submitted on or after January 1, 2027, must include an analysis of the effects of
Exec. Order No. 14370 and any subsequent federal action on increasing medical
marijuana and cannabidiol research.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
