SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 956
By Pody
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate 2025 Ms. Wilson
County Fair, Heather Day Barber.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should specially recognize those community leaders who
strive to improve the quality of life for their fellow citizens while bringing beauty, grace, and
purpose to all their endeavors; and
WHEREAS, one such superlative individual is Heather Day Barber, who was crowned
2025 Ms. Wilson County Fair, a most befitting honor, as it recognizes her dedication to
excellence and her devotion to serving her community; and
WHEREAS, a proud native of Middle Tennessee with more than twenty years of
experience in the beauty industry, Ms. Barber has distinguished herself as a talented hairstylist
and entrepreneur, demonstrating creativity, professionalism, and a strong commitment to
serving others; and
WHEREAS, Heather Barber is first and foremost a devoted wife, mother, and
stepmother, and she takes great pride in the strength and support of her family as she pursues
her many personal and professional endeavors; and
WHEREAS, as the owner of Day Dreaming Designs, Ms. Barber has cultivated a
welcoming environment where artistry, connection, and personal transformation come together
in service to her guests and her community; and
WHEREAS, through her work, Heather Barber is passionate about helping women feel
confident, seen, and empowered through beauty and self-expression; and
WHEREAS, in every role she holds, Ms. Barber leads with both heart and
professionalism, and her dedication to service, her commitment to family, and her desire to uplift
others reflect the values she is proud to represent as Ms. Wilson County Fair 2025; and
SJR0956
015690
- 1 -
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly find it appropriate to pause in their
deliberations to acknowledge and applaud this accomplished woman for her noteworthy
achievement; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Heather Day Barber
upon being crowned 2025 Ms. Wilson County Fair, extending to her our best wishes for every
future success and happiness.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
