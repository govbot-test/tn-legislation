HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 996
By Garrett
A RESOLUTION to honor the memory of Norman Pauley.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly were greatly saddened to learn of
the passing of Norman Pauley; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Pauley was a highly respected member of his community and a
consummate professional who worked assiduously to improve the quality of life for his fellow
citizens in numerous capacities; and
WHEREAS, born in 1935, Norman Pauley was a beloved dining room host at Chick-fil-A
Rivergate, where his warm smile and genuine kindness made every guest feel like a part of his
extended family; and
WHEREAS, through his daily interactions and steadfast commitment to hospitality, Mr.
Pauley created an atmosphere of love and care that will long be remembered by the patrons
and staff whose lives he touched; and
WHEREAS, Norman Pauley leaves behind an indelible legacy of integrity and probity in
public life, compassion and loyalty in private life, and diligence and dedication in all his chosen
endeavors; and
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should remember the bountiful life of this exceptional
gentleman; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor the memory of Norman Pauley, reflecting fondly upon his
impeccable character and his stalwart commitment to living the examined life with courage and
conviction.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that we express our sympathy and offer our condolences
to the family of Mr. Pauley.
