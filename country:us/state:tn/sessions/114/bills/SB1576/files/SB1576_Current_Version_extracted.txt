HOUSE BILL 2015
By Cochran
SENATE BILL 1576
By Lowe
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 49,
relative to education.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-1-208, is amended by deleting the
second sentence and substituting instead the following:
The department shall make the form publicly available on its website.
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.
