HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1322
By Reneau
A RESOLUTION to honor the Walden's Ridge Civic Center on the
occasion of the property being placed in the
National Register of Historic Places.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that this General Assembly salute those historic edifices that
capture a portion of Tennessee's history and stand as a testament to the ingenuity and culture
of bygone eras; and
WHEREAS, one such noteworthy property is the Walden's Ridge Civic Center, which,
upon the nomination of the Tennessee Historical Commission, has been placed in the National
Register of Historic Places by the National Park Service of the United States Department of the
Interior; and
WHEREAS, located at 2501 Fairmount Pike in Walden, Walden's Ridge Civic Center
was constructed in 1921; previously known as the Fairmount Auditorium, the edifice served as
the auditorium for Fairmount Grammar School and as an important meeting place for the
surrounding community; and
WHEREAS, after Fairmount Grammar School closed down, the Walden's Ridge Civic
League (WRCL) assumed stewardship of the building in 1946, and the property became the
headquarters for the WRCL, an important organization that orchestrated locally significant
community improvement projects, such as the installation of water lines and road
improvements; and
WHEREAS, important community enrichment activities, recreational events,
entertainment, and important social clubs, such as the Boy Scouts and the Women's
Demonstration Group, were also hosted in the auditorium; and
WHEREAS, WRCL continues to maintain the building and operate it for the community's
benefit; and

WHEREAS, this General Assembly finds it appropriate to honor this historic landmark on
this momentous occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor the Walden's Ridge Civic Center on the occasion of the property
being placed in the National Register of Historic Places.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
