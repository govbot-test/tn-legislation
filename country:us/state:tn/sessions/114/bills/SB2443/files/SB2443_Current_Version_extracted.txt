Section 1:
By Lankford
SENATE BILL 2443
By Powers
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Section
55-8-198, relative to unmanned traffic enforcement
cameras.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 55-8-198(l)(1)(A), is amended by
adding the language "when a warning flasher or flashers are in operation or during times when
children are present if the school zone does not have a warning flasher or flashers" immediately
following the language "Within the designated distance of a marked school zone".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.

HOUSE BILL 2474
By Lankford
SENATE BILL 2443
By Powers
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Section
55-8-198, relative to unmanned traffic enforcement
cameras.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 55-8-198(l)(1)(A), is amended by
adding the language "when a warning flasher or flashers are in operation or during times when
children are present if the school zone does not have a warning flasher or flashers" immediately
following the language "Within the designated distance of a marked school zone".
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.
