Behn
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 468* House Bill No. 571
by deleting §§ 68-107-101 and 68-107-102 in the amendatory language of SECTION 1 and
substituting:
68-107-101. Short title.
This act is known and may be cited as the "Gender Diversity, Safety, and Dignity
Act."
68-107-102. Purpose.
The purpose of this chapter is:
(1) To affirm the rights, safety, and dignity of all people, including
transgender, nonbinary, intersex, and gender nonconforming individuals in this
state; and
(2) To ensure equitable access to safe facilities, housing, and programs,
and to prevent discrimination based on sex, gender identity, or gender
expression.
