HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1420
By Sherrell
A RESOLUTION to congratulate Annabella Culwell on being
crowned the 2025 Warren County Fairest of the
Fair.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should specially
recognize those emerging community leaders who strive to improve the quality of life for their
fellow citizens while bringing beauty, grace, and purpose to all their endeavors; and
WHEREAS, one such superlative individual is Annabella Culwell, who was crowned the
2025 Warren County Fairest of the Fair; and
WHEREAS, as Fairest of the Fair, Miss Culwell chose CASA (Court Appointed Special
Advocate) as her platform; an organization that stands as a voice for the abused, neglected,
and at-risk youth of Warren County, CASA protects those who cannot protect themselves; and
WHEREAS, guided by the belief that service is faith in action, she launched "Where the
Crown Meets the Heart," a project in partnership with CASA that gives children year-round
access to a dedicated supply building as they enter the program; additionally, she is collecting
books for children in foster care, creating a meaningful bridge between children and their
biological parents during visits; and
WHEREAS, Miss Culwell, along with members of the organization and volunteers,
raised more than $7,500 in just three months and donated essential items to children in need,
including clothes, blankets, crafting supplies, toys, food, and personal hygiene products; and
WHEREAS, she also assembled care kits complete with backpacks, suitcases, and 100
blankets, each bearing the Bible verse Psalm 139:14: "For I am fearfully and wonderfully made";
and

WHEREAS, through her fundraising efforts, Annabella Culwell brought a truly
memorable Christmas to twenty-seven children in her community, a moment that has since
become one of the most meaningful chapters of her journey; and
WHEREAS, Miss Culwell's work with CASA has opened doors to deeper community
engagement, allowing her to advocate and raise awareness through local radio interviews,
social media outreach, and participation in several community events; and
WHEREAS, a true leader in her community, Annabella Culwell is a recipient of the 2026
Tennessee State Fair Humanitarian Award and has spoken at the annual Safety Summit in local
schools; through her relationship with Warren County CASA leadership, she was given the
opportunity to attend court proceedings in support of children in foster care; and
WHEREAS, Miss Culwell's Fairest of the Fair pageant victory is a special source of pride
for her family, her friends, and the good people of Tennessee, and it is fitting that we specially
recognize this remarkable young woman and her many accomplishments; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby congratulate Annabella Culwell on being crowned the 2025
Warren County Fairest of the Fair and extend our best wishes for her every future success and
happiness.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
