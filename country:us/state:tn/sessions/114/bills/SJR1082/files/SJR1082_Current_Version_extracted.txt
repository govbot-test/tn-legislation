SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1082
By Harshbarger
A RESOLUTION to commend Bridget Harper-Diaz for exemplary
service as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this General Assembly is pleased to recognize
certain persons who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment
of the legislative process; and
WHEREAS, Bridget Harper-Diaz is one such individual, who has distinguished herself in
her role as a legislative intern for Senator Bobby Harshbarger; Ms. Harper-Diaz's work has
resulted in innumerable benefits for the legislators she assisted and the General Assembly as a
whole; and
WHEREAS, a student at Austin Peay State University, where she is highly regarded by
her peers, Bridget Harper-Diaz has achieved academic success while majoring in political
science and minoring in international studies; and
WHEREAS, during her collegiate career, Ms. Harper-Diaz has exhibited her superlative
leadership qualities by serving as a member and ambassador of the Honors Program at Austin
Peay State University; and
WHEREAS, Bridget Harper-Diaz has studied abroad in Seoul, South Korea, and
interned with United Planet, where she served as a translator and received the United Planet
Global Service Award; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize herself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further her studies, Ms. Harper-Diaz brought her many
abilities and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2026 session of the One Hundred
Fourteenth General Assembly; and

WHEREAS, throughout the 2026 legislative session, Bridget Harper-Diaz has surpassed
all criteria that define a devoted and conscientious legislative intern; and

BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared

without House or Senate designation.
