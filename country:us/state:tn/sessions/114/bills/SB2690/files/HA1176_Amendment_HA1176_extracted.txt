Amendment No. 15 to HB2631

by adding the following as a new, appropriately designated section immediately preceding the
antepenultimate section and redesignating the subsequent sections accordingly:

Section 2:
SECTION __.
Item 1. The level set for the Revenue Fluctuation Reserve on June 30, 2027, as
described in Section 47, Item 2, along with any corresponding appropriation, is reduced
by the nonrecurring sum of $10,000,000.
Item 2. In addition to any other funds appropriated by the provisions of this act,
there is appropriated the sum of $10,000,000 (nonrecurring) to the Department of
Economic and Community Development for rural development fund grants.
