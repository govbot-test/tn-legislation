Section 2:
SECTION __.
Item 1. The appropriation in Section 1, Title III-9, Item 2.1, line item f, to the
Department of Education for the Charter School Facilities Fund, as identified on Page B-
91 of the 2026-2027 Budget Document, is reduced by the nonrecurring sum of
$40,000,000.
Item 2. In addition to any other funds appropriated by the provisions of this act,
there is appropriated the sum of $40,000,000 (nonrecurring) to the Department of
Human Services for the Summer Nutrition Program.
