by adding the following as a new, appropriately designated section immediately preceding the

Section 2:
SECTION __.
Item 1. The appropriation in Section 1, Title III-9, Item 1.5, to the Department of
Education for Education Freedom Scholarship Program, as identified on Page B-90 of
the 2026-2027 Budget Document, is reduced by the recurring amount of $37,650,000.
Item 2. In addition to any other funds appropriated by the provisions of this act,
there is appropriated the sum of $37,650,000 (recurring) to the Department of Education
for the sole purpose of providing teacher raises in the amount of $5,000 per teacher and
providing funds to LEAs to reduce class sizes for grades K-3 to a maximum size of
fifteen (15) students.
