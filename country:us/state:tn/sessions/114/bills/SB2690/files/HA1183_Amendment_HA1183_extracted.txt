Amendment No. 22 to HB2631
Clemmons
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 2690* House Bill No. 2631
by adding the following as a new, appropriately designated section immediately preceding the
antepenultimate section and redesignating the subsequent sections accordingly:
SECTION __.
Item 1. The appropriation in Section 1, Title III-9, Item 1.5, to the Department of
Education for Education Freedom Scholarship Program, as identified on Page B-90 of
the 2026-2027 Budget Document, is reduced by the recurring amount of $112,950,000.
Item 2. In addition to any other funds appropriated by the provisions of this act,
there is appropriated the sum of $84,000,000 (recurring) to the Department of Human
Services for the Summer Nutrition Program.
Item 3. In addition to any other funds appropriated by the provisions of this act,
there is appropriated the sum of $20,000,000 (recurring) to the Department of Education
for the LEA School Facilities / Construction Fund.
Item 4. In addition to any other funds appropriated by the provisions of this act,
there is appropriated the sum $8,950,000 (recurring) to the Department of Safety for the
state highway fund earmarked for public transit.
