HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1224
By Keisling
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Johnathan Elliott on
being named a 2026 Tennessee Arts Academy
Teacher Hero.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should specially recognize those outstanding teachers
who have devoted their careers to providing the highest-quality education for Tennessee's
students; and
WHEREAS, Johnathan Elliott is one such estimable educator, who has distinguished
himself as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Elliott was recently named a 2026 Tennessee Arts Academy Teacher
Hero, an honor most befitting his unwavering dedication to his students; and
WHEREAS, the Tennessee Arts Academy is the nation's premier K-12 professional
development program for arts education and holds its annual TAA Summer Institute on the
campus of Middle Tennessee State University, providing cutting-edge training for more than 325
K-12 arts educators; and
WHEREAS, the Tennessee Arts Academy Teacher Hero award is a peer-nominated
honor presented to a teacher who exemplifies the highest standard of excellence in teaching,
one who puts their talent and creativity on the line each day and goes above and beyond to help
students succeed in the classroom, the arts, and in life; and
WHEREAS, a fine arts teacher at Oneida Middle/High School, Johnathan Elliott is an
inspiration to his colleagues and students alike, and his friendship and counsel are highly valued
by those who are fortunate to know him; and
WHEREAS, his creativity and commitment to his students have made a meaningful
impact on not only Oneida Middle/High School but the entire community; and
HJR1224
016119
- 1 -
WHEREAS, Mr. Elliott truly epitomizes the dedicated arts teacher who is wholly
committed to the highest ideals of public service and quality education for all, and he should be
specially recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Johnathan Elliott on being chosen as a 2026
Tennessee Arts Academy Teacher Hero, commend his exceptional service to the children of
Tennessee, salute his professional expertise and personal excellence, and wish him all the best
in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
