SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1194
By Walley
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Grace Jacobs, 2026
Valedictorian of Rossville Christian Academy.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Grace Jacobs is one such exceptional student, who was recently named
Valedictorian of the 2026 graduating class at Rossville Christian Academy; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Ms. Jacobs has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in her remarkable high school career, Grace Jacobs has earned an
exemplary grade point average while participating in numerous extracurricular activities; and
WHEREAS, her display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well for her
future success; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Jacobs's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for
her family, her school, and her community; and
WHEREAS, Grace Jacobs's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of our respect,
admiration, and commendation; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor the 2026 Rossville
Christian Academy Valedictorian, Grace Jacobs, on her impressive academic achievement and
extend to her our best wishes for a happy, successful future.
SJR1194
018321
- 1 -
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
