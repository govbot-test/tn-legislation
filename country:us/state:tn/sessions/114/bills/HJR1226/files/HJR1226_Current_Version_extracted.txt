HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1226
By Keisling
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Coach Jimmy May upon
his retirement as head coach of the Oneida High
School football program.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly take great pleasure in recognizing
those high school coaches and educators whose devotion and leadership have positively
impacted the lives of countless student athletes during their careers; and
WHEREAS, one such distinguished educator is Coach Jimmy May, who has announced
his retirement as head coach of the Oneida High School football program; and
WHEREAS, Jimmy May devoted thirty-five years of exceptional service to his alma
mater as a chemistry teacher, athletic director, principal, and coach, earning the respect and
admiration of his colleagues and students alike; and
WHEREAS, prior to becoming head coach, Coach May served as an assistant on the
staff of his father, Jim May, helping lead Oneida to a State championship in 1992; and
WHEREAS, the third-winningest football coach in Oneida High School's history, Coach
May began his remarkable tenure as head coach in 1999 and has since compiled an
outstanding 73-43 record; and
WHEREAS, Jimmy May stepped down from his coaching role in 2004 to serve as
principal of Oneida; he returned to coaching once more in 2009 as offensive coordinator, a role
he relished until becoming head coach in 2021; and
WHEREAS, his professional accomplishments aside, Jimmy May is most grateful for the
love and support of his wife, Pam; he is the proud father of his children, Amanda and Brittney,
and grandfather of Aurora; and

WHEREAS, his leadership as a coach and as an educator has been a source of pride
for the Oneida community, and his dedication to his students, school, and community has left an
indelible mark on the Home of the Indians; and
WHEREAS, throughout his distinguished career, Coach Jimmy May has excelled as
both a coach and as a mentor, and he is worthy of our approbation on this special occasion;
now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Coach Jimmy May upon his retirement as head
coach of the Oneida High School football program and extend to him our best wishes for much
continued success and happiness in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
