HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 785
By Capley
A RESOLUTION to honor the memory of Bert Spearman of
Lawrenceburg.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly were greatly saddened to learn of
the passing of Bert Spearman; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Spearman was an exemplary public servant and consummate
professional who worked assiduously to improve the quality of life for his fellow citizens in
numerous capacities; and
WHEREAS, Bert Spearman was born on October 22, 1942, in Anderson, South
Carolina, to his parents, Harlbert and Kathleen Louise Powell Spearman; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Spearman bravely served his country in the United States Army with the
101st Airborne Division and, upon completing his term of service, moved to Lawrenceburg,
where he established deep roots and became a greatly respected member of his community;
and
WHEREAS, as the owner and operator of Spearman Realty and Appraisal, Mr.
Spearman was known for his integrity, expertise, and commitment to providing the best service
to his clients; and
WHEREAS, in addition to owning his real estate company, Bert Spearman also owned
the Lawrence County Monument Company for a time; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Spearman served his community in many capacities, serving as
Lawrence County Commissioner, president of the Rotary Club, a member of the Lawrence
County Senior Citizens Club, and a member of the Railroad Board; and
WHEREAS, Bert Spearman was a devoted member of Calvary Hill Church of God,
which he served faithfully for many years; and

WHEREAS, additionally, Mr. Spearman was a member of The Gideons International and
a 32nd degree Mason and Shriner; and
WHEREAS, Bert Spearman was also deeply dedicated to his family, and he always
endeavored to remain true to family values of the highest order; he found peace in creating
countless memories with his loved ones, who found his enthusiasm to be infectious; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Spearman was a devoted husband to his beloved wife of sixty-three
years, Donna Kaye Dorsey Spearman, and a loving father to his son, Andy Spearman, and his
daughter, Debbie (Barry) Scott; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Spearman's legacy continues through his grandchildren, Drew Scott,
Annalee (Kendriyx) Scott, Paitton Scott, Wesley (Kaleigh) Spearman, and Jenny (Joshua)
Kennedy; and his great-grandchildren, Avery Kennedy, Konner Spearman, Kollin Spearman,
Kalvin Spearman, and Kasey Spearman; and
WHEREAS, Bert Spearman leaves behind an indelible legacy of integrity and probity in
public life, compassion and loyalty in private life, and diligence and dedication in all his chosen
endeavors; and
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should pay tribute to the bountiful life of this exceptional
public servant and human being; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor the memory of Bert Spearman, reflecting fondly upon his
impeccable character and his stalwart commitment to living the examined life with courage and
conviction.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that we extend our sympathy and condolences to the
family and friends of Mr. Spearman.
