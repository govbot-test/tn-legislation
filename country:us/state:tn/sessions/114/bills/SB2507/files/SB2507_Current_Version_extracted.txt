HOUSE BILL 2292
By Hardaway
SENATE BILL 2507
By Oliver
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 13;
Title 62, Chapter 6 and Title 66, relative to
contractors.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 62-6-139, is amended by designating
subsection (a) as subdivision (a)(1) and adding the following new subdivision (a)(2):
(2) Upon posting a disciplinary action under subdivision (a)(1), the board shall
notify the department of commerce and insurance of the disciplinary action as soon as
practicable.
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
