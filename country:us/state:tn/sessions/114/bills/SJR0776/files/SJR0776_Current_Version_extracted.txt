SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 776
By Seal
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Nolan Parton of
Sevier County High School on being named the
Region 1-5A Co-Offensive Lineman of the Year.
WHEREAS, we are pleased to specially honor and commend those successful high
school athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring honor to the State of Tennessee
and serve as exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable athlete is Nolan Parton of Sevier County High School, a
football player of extraordinary talent, skill, and athleticism, who has been named the Region 1-
5A Co-Offensive Lineman of the Year; and
WHEREAS, a powerhouse on the football field, Mr. Parton had forty-nine pancake
blocks and two sacks for the Smoky Bears in addition to scoring a touchdown against Cocke
County this past season; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Parton epitomizes all that is good in today's student athlete, as he has
achieved a high level of success in sports while evincing great character both on and off the
field; and
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that we recognize and commend this elite athlete and
outstanding young gentleman; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Nolan Parton of Sevier
County High School on being named the Region 1-5A Co-Offensive Lineman of the Year and
extend to him our best wishes for much continued success in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
