Senate State and Local Government 1
Amendment No. 1 to SB2200
Briggs
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 2200* House Bill No. 2182
by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 67-6-103(a)(3)(G), is amended by
designating the existing language as subdivision (a)(3)(G)(i) and adding the following as a new
subdivision (a)(3)(G)(ii):
(ii) Notwithstanding subdivision (a)(3)(G)(i) or any other law, the department of
revenue and the department of finance and administration shall not use the revised and
certified population estimates for the allocation and distribution of moneys to counties
and municipalities until July 1, 2031.
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
