SENATE BILL 2425
By Crowe
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 71,
Chapter 5, relative to TennCare.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 71-5-106, is amended by adding the
following as a new subsection:
(s) When determining the eligibility of an individual for residential facility benefits,
any scoring or evaluation that takes into consideration the individual's physical and
mental well-being must evaluate the individual's physical condition and mental condition
separately. As used in this subsection (s), "residential facility" includes, but is not limited
to, a nursing home, skilled nursing facility, recuperation center, rehabilitation facility,
home for the aged, and adult care home.
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
