SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1162
By Lowe
A RESOLUTION to commend Josh Rogers for his honorable and
astute service to his fellow citizens as a member
of the Bradley County Commission.
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that the members of this General Assembly should honor
those citizens who have performed with uncommon devotion and enthusiasm throughout their
years of public service; and
WHEREAS, Josh Rogers is one such estimable person, who evinces the greatest
integrity and probity in all his chosen endeavors; and
WHEREAS, having served with distinction since September 1, 2022, as a member of the
Bradley County Commission representing District 7, Josh Rogers is taking his much-deserved
retirement from that esteemed body; and
WHEREAS, during his tenure, Mr. Rogers has rendered valuable service to the citizens
of his district, working to represent their interests and contribute to the effective governance of
Bradley County; and
WHEREAS, known for his approachable manner and cooperative spirit, Josh Rogers
has fostered positive working relationships with his colleagues and constituents alike; and
WHEREAS, he has further demonstrated a spirit of kindness and community
engagement, often educating young children, albeit his own, on the importance of friendliness
within their neighborhood; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Rogers is known for his general delightful demeanor and
cooperativeness, character traits that have served him well in his public service; and
WHEREAS, Josh Rogers exemplifies the spirit and allegiance to family and community
that are characteristic of a true Tennessean; and

WHEREAS, Mr. Rogers is wholly committed to the noble precepts of public service that
have earned Tennessee recognition as the Volunteer State, and he should be specially
recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we commend Josh Rogers for his honorable and
astute service to his fellow citizens as a member of the Bradley County Commission and extend
to him our best wishes for every continued success in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
