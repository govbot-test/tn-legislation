Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 57-7-108(a)(2)(B), is amended by
deleting the subdivision and substituting:
(B) There is imposed a tax upon the sale of HDCPs at wholesale in liquid form in
the amount of four dollars and forty cents ($4.40) per gallon of liquid HDCP, or at a
proportional rate if the liquid HDCP is sold or distributed in a container in which the
volume is not measured by gallonage; provided, that if the product is inhalable in
cartridge form there is imposed an additional tax of ten percent (10%) on the product's
wholesale cost.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.
