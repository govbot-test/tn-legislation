Section 1:
By Reeves
HOUSE BILL 2400
By Sparks
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 13;
Title 16; Title 33; Title 39 and Title 40, relative to
the Equal Access to Public Property Act of 2012.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 39-14-414(e), is amended by
deleting "ninety (90)" wherever it appears and substituting "one hundred twenty (120)".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
