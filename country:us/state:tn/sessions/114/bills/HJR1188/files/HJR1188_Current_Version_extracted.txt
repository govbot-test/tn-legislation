HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1188
By Reneau
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend the United States
Attorney's Office for the Eastern District of
Tennessee upon its receipt of the Federal
Partner of the Year Award.
WHEREAS, our nation was conceived by individuals who were willing to sacrifice their
personal concerns to ensure the public safety and welfare, and the Volunteer State is especially
proud to be the home of so many valiant individuals who have performed above and beyond the
call of duty in service of public safety; and
WHEREAS, such individuals comprise the staff of the United States Attorney's Office of
the Eastern District of Tennessee, who were recently honored with the Chattanooga Police
Department (CPD) Federal Partner of the Year Award, in recognition of their exceptional and
unwavering commitment to justice, public safety, and collaboration within our community; and
WHEREAS, the prosecutors and staff of the United States Attorney's Office of the
Eastern District of Tennessee have been true partners to the Chattanooga Police Department,
consistently working side by side with the CPD's officers and investigators, ensuring precision
and integrity in every matter and offering clear guidance on complex cases; and
WHEREAS, committed to excellence, the staff of the U.S. Attorney's Office stand out
due to their professionalism, accessibility, and shared dedication to the mission of the CPD. By
fostering open communication and mutual respect, they embody a unified approach to
upholding the highest stands of the justice system and protecting the Chattanooga community;
and
WHEREAS, the support provided by the United States Attorney's Office for the Eastern
District of Tennessee has made a lasting impact on the Chattanooga Police Department; now,
therefore,

BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend the United States Attorney's Office for the Eastern
District of Tennessee upon its receipt of the Federal Partner of the Year Award from the
Chattanooga Police Department.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
