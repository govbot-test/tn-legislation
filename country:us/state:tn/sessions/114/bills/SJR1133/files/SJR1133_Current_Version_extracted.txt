SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1133
By Massey
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend the Webb School of
Knoxville Lady Spartans girls' basketball team
upon winning the 2026 TSSAA, Division II-AA
state championship.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that this General Assembly should salute those student athletes
who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as true champions of
whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such exemplary group of lasses is the Webb School of Knoxville Lady
Spartans girls' basketball team, whose members have proven themselves to be the fiercest of
competitors and truly worthy of admiration by winning the 2026 TSSAA Division II-AA state
championship; and
WHEREAS, in the thrilling championship game, the Lady Spartans overcame a 12-point
second-half deficit to defeat Lipscomb Academy by a score of 48-47 to bring home the gold ball
trophy and claim their second consecutive Division II-AA state title and seventh in program
history; and
WHEREAS, the Lady Spartans relied heavily upon the talent, competitive spirit, and
leadership of the tournament's Most Valuable Player, Meeyah Green, who made a steal and
score to give Webb the lead with just 6.1 seconds remaining in the game; and
WHEREAS, the Lady Spartans' superb performance would not have been possible
without the stellar leadership of Head Coach Greg Hernandez and the unfailing support of their
families, their fans, and their community; and
WHEREAS, the members of the Webb School of Knoxville girls' basketball team
epitomize all that is good in today's student athletes, as they have achieved a premier level of
success in competition while exhibiting the highest degree of character in all aspects of life; and

WHEREAS, the perseverance, determination, work ethic, and talent of this group of
young ladies not only made possible an outstanding season but also surely bode well for their
future success; and
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly find it appropriate to acknowledge
and applaud the Webb School of Knoxville Lady Spartans for their championship season on the
hardwood; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend the members and coaches
of the Webb School of Knoxville Lady Spartans girls' basketball team upon winning the 2026
TSSAA Division II-AA state championship and extend to them our best wishes for every
continued success in all their future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
