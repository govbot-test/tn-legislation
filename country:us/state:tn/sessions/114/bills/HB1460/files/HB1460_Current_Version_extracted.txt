Section 1:
HOUSE BILL 1460
By Atchley
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 54,
relative to signage on Interstate 40 near Exit 407.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Notwithstanding a law to the contrary, the Department of Transportation
shall replace or modify the sign located near mile marker 405.4 on Interstate 40 eastbound to
replace references to a stadium formerly located off Exit 407 with the language "The Island in
Pigeon Forge".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
