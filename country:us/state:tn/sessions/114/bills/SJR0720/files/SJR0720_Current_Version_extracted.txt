SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 720
By Yarbro
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend the Lipscomb Academy
Lady Mustangs upon winning the TSSAA
Division II girls' bowling state championship.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that this General Assembly should salute those student athletes
who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as true champions of
whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such exemplary group of lasses is the Lipscomb Academy Lady
Mustangs, whose members have proven themselves to be the fiercest of competitors and truly
worthy of admiration by winning the TSSAA Division II girls' bowling state championship while
posting an outstanding 13-2 season record; and
WHEREAS, entering the state tournament with a 10-2 record, the Lady Mustangs easily
bested Friendship 21-6 to emerge victorious in their quarterfinal match-up and then destroyed
Boyd Buchanan 24-3 in the semi-final round; and
WHEREAS, in the final match, Lipscomb trounced GPS 21-6 to claim the state title and
bring home the gold; and
WHEREAS, the Lady Mustangs' superb performance would not have been possible
without the stellar leadership of their coaches and the unfailing support of their families, their
fans, and their community; and
WHEREAS, the members of the Lipscomb Academy girls' bowling team epitomize all
that is good in today's student athletes, as they have achieved a premier level of success in
competition while exhibiting the highest degree of character in all aspects of life; and

013029

WHEREAS, the perseverance, determination, work ethic, and talent of this group of
young women not only made possible an outstanding season but also surely bode well for their
future success; and
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly find it appropriate to acknowledge
and applaud the Lipscomb Academy Lady Mustangs for their championship season on the
lanes; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend the members and coaches
of the Lipscomb Academy Lady Mustangs upon winning the TSSAA Division II girls' bowling
state championship and extend to them our best wishes for every continued success in all their
future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
