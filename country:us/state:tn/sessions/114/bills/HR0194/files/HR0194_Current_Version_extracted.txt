HOUSE RESOLUTION 194
By Clemmons
A RESOLUTION to commend Shamariah Houston for exemplary
service as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this body is pleased to recognize certain persons
who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment of the legislative
process; and
WHEREAS, Shamariah Houston is one such individual, who has distinguished herself in
her role as a legislative intern for Representative John Ray Clemmons; Ms. Houston's work has
resulted in innumerable benefits for the legislators she assisted and the General Assembly as a
whole; and
WHEREAS, a student at the University of Tennessee at Martin, where she is highly
regarded by her peers, Ms. Houston has achieved academic success while pursuing a graduate
degree in criminal justice; after graduation, she plans to pursue a career in advocacy or with the
Tennessee Bureau of Investigation; and
WHEREAS, originally from Brownsville, Shamariah Houston attended the University of
Tennessee at Martin for her undergraduate education, where she studied criminal justice with a
minor in sociology and was an active member of the Criminal Justice Club; and
WHEREAS, in her free time, Ms. Houston enjoys spending time with friends and family,
and she is deeply devoted to her faith; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize herself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further her studies, Shamariah Houston brought her many
abilities and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2026 session of the One Hundred
Fourteenth General Assembly; and

WHEREAS, throughout the 2026 legislative session, Ms. Houston has been a
tremendous help to Representative Clemmons and the House Democratic Caucus's entire staff;
she arrives every day with a great attitude and desire to help and learn; Ms. Houston has
surpassed all criteria that define a devoted and conscientious legislative intern, including helping
with newsletters and legislation, attending session, assisting with press conferences, and
addressing constituents' concerns; and
WHEREAS, she has proven herself an invaluable member of Representative
Clemmons's efforts to ably represent the residents of House District 55; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Houston has performed her many duties with extraordinary insight and
rare decorum; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we commend
Shamariah Houston for outstanding service as a legislative intern for Representative John Ray
Clemmons and extend to her our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
