HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1143
By Raper
A RESOLUTION to commemorate National Donate Life Month.
WHEREAS, more than 100,000 Americans and 3,000 Tennesseans are currently on the
national transplant waiting list; and
WHEREAS, another person is added to the waiting list every eight minutes, and, on
average, thirteen people die each day while waiting for a lifesaving organ transplant; and
WHEREAS, the most effective way to address this health crisis is to encourage citizens
to first learn more about living donation at DonateLife.net and then register their decision to be
an organ, eye, and tissue donor at DonateLifeTN.org or at their local driver services center; and
WHEREAS, by becoming an organ, eye, and tissue donor, one individual can save and
heal the lives of more than seventy-five people; and
WHEREAS, during National Donate Life Month, we pause to honor the compassion and
generosity of registered donors, donor families, and living donors and recognize the
commitment of medical professionals, researchers, national partners, and all those who work
tirelessly to save and heal lives through donation and transplantation; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor those individuals who have recognized the lifesaving power of
donating their organs, eyes, and tissue and the transplant recipients who have the honor of
carrying on their donor's legacy, as we commemorate National Donate Life Month during April
2026.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that we extend our gratitude to the many Tennesseans
who have registered their decision to be an organ, eye, and tissue donor.
