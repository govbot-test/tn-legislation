SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 989
By Akbari
A RESOLUTION to commend Donyell Hess for exemplary service
as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this General Assembly is pleased to recognize
certain persons who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment
of the legislative process; and
WHEREAS, Donyell Hess is one such individual who has distinguished herself in her
role as a legislative intern for Senator Raumesh Akbari; Ms. Hess's work has resulted in
innumerable benefits for the legislators she assisted and the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, a student at the University of Memphis, where she is highly regarded by her
peers, Donyell Hess has achieved academic success while pursuing a degree in social work
with a minor in sociology; and
WHEREAS, during her collegiate career, Ms. Hess has assisted numerous county
officials with their campaigns and currently works with the Shelby County Election Commission,
where she assists citizens with voting registration; and
WHEREAS, Donyell Hess has further served her community through volunteering with
multiple organizations, including Hospitality Hub, the Botanical Gardens, and Room in the Inn;
and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize herself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further her studies, Ms. Hess brought her many abilities
and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2026 session of the One Hundred Fourteenth
General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, throughout the 2026 legislative session, Donyell Hess has surpassed all
criteria that define a devoted and conscientious intern; and

WHEREAS, Ms. Hess has performed her many duties with extraordinary insight and rare
