HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1498
By Carringer
A RESOLUTION to congratulate Sloan Darby on being crowned
the 2026 Miss Greenfield Princess.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
specially recognize those emerging community leaders who strive to improve the quality of life
for their fellow citizens while bringing beauty, grace, and purpose to all their endeavors; and
WHEREAS, one such superlative individual is Sloan Darby, who was recently crowned
the 2026 Miss Greenfield Princess, a most befitting honor that recognizes her confidence,
elegance, and charm; and
WHEREAS, from a young age, Miss Darby has embodied grace, kindness, and a love
for her community, and her warm spirit and bright smile have captured the hearts of those who
have been blessed to know her; and
WHEREAS, Sloan Darby is a shining example of what it means to lead with heart and
dedication, and her display of ambition surely bodes well for her future success; and
WHEREAS, her noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for her
parents, Representative Tandy Darby and Kasi Darby, and her entire community; and
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we specially recognize this remarkable young woman and
her accomplishments; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we congratulate Sloan Darby on being crowned the 2026 Miss Greenfield
Princess and extend our best wishes for her every future success and happiness.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
