HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1577
By Sparks
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize David Johnson, 2026
Valedictorian of Smyrna High School.
WHEREAS, it is only fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
pay tribute to those students in our State who, through their own natural abilities and
unwavering pursuit of academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom;
and
WHEREAS, David Johnson is one such individual, who has been named Valedictorian
of the 2026 graduating class at Smyrna High School; and
WHEREAS, through dedication to his studies, careful recollection of vast amounts of
data, composure through examinations, and a steadfast commitment of time and talent, Mr.
Johnson has achieved a remarkable academic feat and joined the ranks of the brightest and
most notable students in the State of Tennessee and the nation; and
WHEREAS, in his educational career, Mr. Johnson has maintained an exemplary grade
point average while participating in a great number of extracurricular clubs and activities,
including the Youth in Government Club, Model United Nations, Service Dawgs, Interact Club,
Key Club, Creative Writing Club, First Priority organization, National Beta Club, Book Club,
Distributive Education Club of America (DECA), and Mu Alpha Theta; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Johnson has earned several prestigious awards and honors; he won the
Washington Youth Tour Essay Contest; received Certificates of Academic Excellence in
Advanced Placement Biology, Advanced Placement Precalculus, and Dual Enrollment United
States History; earned the designation of Questbridge College Prep Scholar; and was
recognized for his achievements by Tennessee American Legion Boys State and the
Tennessee Governor's School for the Arts; and

WHEREAS, his intellectual aptitude and ambition most certainly bode well for his future
success; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Johnson's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for
his parents, Kevin and Asenath Johnson; his sister, Rachel; his school; and his community; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Johnson's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of this General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend David Johnson of Smyrna High School on being
named the 2026 Valedictorian, salute his impressive academic achievement, and extend to him
our best wishes for a happy, successful future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
