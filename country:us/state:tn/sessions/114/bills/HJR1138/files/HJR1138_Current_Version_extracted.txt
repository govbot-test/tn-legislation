HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1138
By Parkinson
A RESOLUTION to commend Yoddyashley Nicole Cortes
Figueroa for exemplary service as a legislative
intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this General Assembly is pleased to recognize
certain persons who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment
of the legislative process; and
WHEREAS, Yoddyashley Nicole Cortes Figueroa is one such individual, who has
distinguished herself in her role as a legislative intern for Representative Antonio Parkinson; Ms.
Figueroa's work has resulted in innumerable benefits for the legislators she assisted and the
General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, Ashley Figueroa was born on August 29, 2002, in Mayagüez, Puerto Rico,
and attended Shelbyville Central High School; and
WHEREAS, a student at Belmont University, where she is highly regarded by her peers,
Ms. Figueroa has achieved academic success studying political science with a minor in German
while participating in numerous extracurricular activities; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Figueroa has aspirations to intern for the Tennessee Bureau of
Investigation, and after graduation, she plans to move to Spain to gain Spanish citizenship; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize herself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further her studies, Ashley Figueroa brought her many
abilities and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2026 session of the One Hundred
Fourteenth General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, throughout the 2026 legislative session, Ms. Figueroa has surpassed all
criteria that define a devoted and conscientious legislative intern; and
