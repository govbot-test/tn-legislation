SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 847
By Lowe
A RESOLUTION to celebrate Outdoors Day on the Hill and to
honor the Tennessee Outdoor Partnership for its
contributions to outdoor recreation,
conservation, and economic development
across Tennessee.
WHEREAS, the State of Tennessee is blessed with an abundance of natural resources,
scenic landscapes, waterways, forests, parks, and wildlife that contribute significantly to the
health, heritage, and prosperity of its citizens; and
WHEREAS, outdoor recreation and conservation efforts strengthen community well-
being, enhance quality of life, support workforce recruitment and retention, and generate
substantial economic impact throughout the Volunteer State; and
WHEREAS, the Tennessee Outdoor Partnership (TOP) is a public-private collaboration
uniting State agencies, nonprofit organizations, and private partners to advance outdoor
recreation, conservation, and economic development across Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, TOP's mission is to connect investments in outdoor assets to community
health, workforce retention, and overall quality of life, recognizing that Tennessee's natural
resources are vital to both economic vitality and citizen well-being; and
WHEREAS, the Tennessee Outdoor Partnership brings together a distinguished
coalition of partners, including the Tennessee Department of Environment and Conservation,
the Tennessee Department of Agriculture, the Tennessee Department of Tourist Development,
the Tennessee Department of Economic and Community Development, the Tennessee
Department of Health, the Tennessee Department of Transportation, the Tennessee Wildlife
Resources Agency, the University of Tennessee Institute of Agriculture, the Tennessee Wildlife
Federation, The Nature Conservancy, and the Tennessee Valley Authority; and
SJR0847
013899
- 1 -
WHEREAS, this broad collaboration exemplifies Tennessee's commitment to
responsible stewardship of natural resources while fostering innovation, tourism, economic
opportunity, and public health; and
WHEREAS, Outdoors Day on the Hill, hosted by the Tennessee Outdoor Partnership,
will be held on Tuesday, March 24th, from 8:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m. CDT, providing an opportunity
for policymakers, partners, and citizens to celebrate and highlight the importance of outdoor
recreation and conservation initiatives across the State; and
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of the General Assembly recognize the
significance of this event and commend the Tennessee Outdoor Partnership and its partners for
their leadership and dedication to preserving and enhancing Tennessee's outdoor heritage;
now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby join in the celebration of March 24, 2026,
as Outdoors Day on the Hill in Tennessee and commend the Tennessee Outdoor Partnership
and its public and private partners for their continued efforts to advance outdoor recreation,
conservation, economic development, and community well-being across the Volunteer State.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
