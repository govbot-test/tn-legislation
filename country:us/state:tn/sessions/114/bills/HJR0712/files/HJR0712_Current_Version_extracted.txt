HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 712
By Powers
A RESOLUTION to congratulate Adam Bullock upon being
selected for inclusion in The National Trial
Lawyers: Top 10 Probate and Estate Trial
Lawyers Association.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should honor those citizens who have performed with
uncommon devotion and enthusiasm throughout their careers; and
WHEREAS, Adam Bullock of the Bullock Law Firm, PLLC, in La Follette is one such
estimable individual, who has distinguished himself as a professional of the greatest dedication,
ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Bullock was recently selected for inclusion into the Top 10 Probate and
Estate Trial Lawyers Association, an association of The National Trial Lawyers; this honor is
given to only a select group of lawyers, based on their superior skills and qualifications in the
field; and
WHEREAS, The National Trial Lawyers: Top 10 Probate and Estate Trial Lawyers
Association is a professional honorary organization composed of the top ten probate and estate
trial lawyers from each state who serve individuals and families who need attorneys to represent
them in the American legal system; and
WHEREAS, membership in this exclusive organization is by invitation only and is limited
to the top ten attorneys in each state or region; the selection process for this elite honor is
based on a multi-phase process that includes peer nominations combined with third-party
research; and
WHEREAS, The National Trial Lawyers: Top 10 Probate and Estate Trial Lawyers
Association provides accreditation to these distinguished attorneys and aims to provide
HJR0712
009694
- 1 -
essential legal news, information, and continuing education to probate and estate attorneys
across the United States; and
WHEREAS, as The National Trial Lawyers: Top 10 Probate and Estate Trial Lawyers
Association is an essential source of networking and information for probate and estate
attorneys throughout the nation, the result of the selection process is a credible and
comprehensive list of the most outstanding probate and estate attorneys chosen to represent
their state or region; and
WHEREAS, Adam Bullock's selection for inclusion in the association is a testament to
his superior qualifications, leadership skills, and performance in his area of legal practice; and
WHEREAS, throughout his estimable career, Mr. Bullock has demonstrated the utmost
professionalism, ability, and integrity, winning the respect and admiration of his colleagues and
the citizens he has so ably served; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we commend and congratulate Adam Bullock upon being selected for
inclusion in The National Trial Lawyers: Top 10 Probate and Estate Trial Lawyers Association,
wishing him every continued success in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
