HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1245
By Capley
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Taylor Rae Troutman,
2026 Salutatorian of Hampshire Unit School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Taylor Rae Troutman is one such exceptional student, who was recently
named Salutatorian of the 2026 graduating class at Hampshire Unit School, which school she
has attended for all thirteen years of her academic career; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Ms. Troutman has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in her remarkable high school career, Taylor Troutman has earned an
exemplary grade point average while participating in numerous extracurricular activities,
including cheer, FFA, Future Business Leaders of America (FBLA), Fellowship of Christian
Athletes (FCA), Beta Club, Read-It Plus club, and yearbook; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Troutman is a devoted leader in many capacities, having served as an
officer for FFA, vice president and secretary of FBLA, and the vice president of FCA; and
WHEREAS, Taylor Troutman's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes
well for her future success at Tennessee Technological University, where she plans to study
mechanical engineering; and

WHEREAS, dedicated to serving her community, Ms. Troutman has volunteered with
Feed NC and Meals on Wheels and also worked in the T-shirt lab at Hampshire Unit School;
and
WHEREAS, Taylor Troutman is the daughter of Christie Troutman and the late Bradley
Troutman; her noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for her family, her
school, and her community; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Troutman's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of the members of this General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor Taylor Rae Troutman on being named the
2026 Hampshire Unit School Salutatorian, salute her impressive academic achievement, and
extend to her our best wishes for a happy, successful future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
