SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 621
By Crowe
A RESOLUTION to recognize Robert Allan Clark on the occasion
of his retirement from the Tennessee Division of
Veterans Services.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly take great pleasure in honoring
those public servants who, throughout their distinguished careers, have served with honor and
an unswerving commitment to duty; and
WHEREAS, one such individual is Robert Allan Clark of Mount Carmel, who recently
took his much-deserved retirement as a Veterans Service Officer with the Tennessee Division of
Veterans Services after a distinguished ten-year tenure; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Clark proudly served his country in the Army National Guard and was
called into active duty during Desert Shield and again during Desert Storm; for his military
service, he was awarded the South West Asia Service Medal with Bronze Service Star, four
Army Reserve Achievement Medals, two National Defense Service Medals, the Meritorious
Service Medal, the Army Service Ribbon, the Army Reserve Component Overseas Training
Ribbon, and the Armed Forces Reserve Medal; and
WHEREAS, following his service with the U.S. Army National Guard, he was hired as an
administrative assistant for the Tennessee Division of Veterans Services, where he has served
since 2015; after two years as an administrative assistant, he was promoted to the post of
Veterans Service Officer, serving in that capacity until his retirement in November 2025; and
WHEREAS, during his decade of service, Mr. Clark helped more than 5,000 veterans
receive the assistance and medical care they deserved, including veterans who are now serving
as elected representatives in the United States Congress and the Tennessee Legislature; and
WHEREAS, in 2019, Robert Allan Clark was awarded the Tennessee Division of
Veterans Services Advocate of the Year award, the division's highest honor; and

WHEREAS, Mr. Clark enjoys the love and support of his wife, Vickie Clark, with whom
he shares three children, Jarred, Kaitlyn, and Emily; and
WHEREAS, throughout his distinguished career, Mr. Clark has served his fellow
veterans with honor and distinction, and it is wholly fitting that we recognize him on the occasion
of his retirement; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we recognize Robert Allan Clark on the occasion of
his retirement from the Tennessee Division of Veterans Services, extending our gratitude for his
many years of selfless service and our best wishes for his every future happiness and success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
