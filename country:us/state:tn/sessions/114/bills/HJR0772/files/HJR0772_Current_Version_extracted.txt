HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 772
By Farmer
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Dr. Mitchell Whaley on
his selection as the 2025-2026 Sevier County
School System Principal of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
specially recognize those outstanding professionals who have devoted their careers to providing
the highest quality education for Tennessee's students; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Mitchell Whaley is one such estimable administrator, who has
distinguished himself as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Whaley has been selected as the 2025-2026 Sevier County School
System Principal of the Year, a most befitting honor that recognizes the positive influence he
has on so many young lives; and
WHEREAS, a dedicated leader in the Sevier County School System for more than two
decades, Dr. Whaley served as an English teacher and vice principal at Pigeon Forge High
School for thirteen years before serving as the principal of Pigeon Forge Junior High School for
four years; he began his current role as the principal of Sevier County High School in the
summer of 2022; and
WHEREAS, a graduate of Sevier County High School, Dr. Whaley has a deep
appreciation for the school, its families, and the traditions that make it a special place for
learning and growth; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Mitchell Whaley is passionate about creating meaningful learning
experiences that inspire curiosity, confidence, and success in all students; he believes in the
power of collaboration and works closely with teachers, parents, and community members to
ensure that every child has the resources and support needed to succeed; and

WHEREAS, under Dr. Whaley's leadership, Sevier County High School has continued to
thrive, emphasizing academic excellence, student well-being, and a strong sense of community;
and
WHEREAS, Dr. Whaley is an extremely talented and compassionate administrator who
works assiduously to motivate his students, teachers, and staff to reach their full potential as
scholars, professionals, and citizens; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Whaley serves as an inspiration to the faculty and staff of Sevier County
High School, and his friendship and counsel are highly valued by those who are fortunate to
know him; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Whaley truly epitomizes the dedicated principal who is wholly committed
to the highest ideals of public service and quality education for all, and he should be specially
recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and congratulate Dr. Mitchell Whaley on being chosen as
the 2025-2026 Sevier County School System Principal of the Year, commend his exceptional
service to the children of Tennessee, salute his professional expertise and personal excellence,
and wish him all the best in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
