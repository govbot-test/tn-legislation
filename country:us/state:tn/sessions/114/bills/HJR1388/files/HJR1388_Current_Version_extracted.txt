HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1388
By Vital
A RESOLUTION to honor the forty-seventh annual Chattanooga
Area Leadership Prayer Breakfast.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
honor and congratulate those community events that are sources of great civic pride; and
WHEREAS, on May 19, 2026, the Chattanooga Area Leadership Prayer Breakfast will
host its forty-seventh annual event at the Chattanooga Convention Center; and
WHEREAS, established in 1978, the Chattanooga Area Leadership Prayer Breakfast
gathers 1,800-2,200 attendees to pray for the community and hear inspiring stories of leaders
whose lives embody the values rooted in the purpose of the Prayer Breakfast: to encourage
morality and ethics in our personal and professional lives; and
WHEREAS, nearly fifty years ago, John Stophel, John Wright, John Steffner, and Ted
DeMoss met with Mayor Pat Rose to share a collective vision for an event that promotes
morality and ethics among leaders in business, government, and other professions; originally
known as the Mayor's Prayer Breakfast, the event became the Chattanooga Area Leadership
Prayer Breakfast in the early 1990s; and
WHEREAS, since its inception, the Chattanooga Area Leadership Prayer Breakfast has
grown from several hundred attendees in 1978 to an attendance of approximately 2,000 in
2025, and it is the largest community-wide seated event in the Chattanooga area; and
WHEREAS, this year's Prayer Breakfast will feature keynote speaker Barry "Butch"
Wilmore, retired NASA astronaut and United States Navy captain; and
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly recognize the
Chattanooga Area Leadership Prayer Breakfast and congratulate the event's organizers and
speakers on this special occasion; now, therefore,

BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
