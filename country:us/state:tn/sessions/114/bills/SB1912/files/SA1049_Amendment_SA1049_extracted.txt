Amendment No. 2 to SB1912
Watson

AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 1912* House Bill No. 1886
by deleting "and" at the end of subdivision (a)(1)(D) in SECTION 2.
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting the period at the end of subdivision (a)(1)(E)(v) in
SECTION 2 and substituting instead the language ";".
AND FURTHER AMEND by inserting the following as new subdivisions immediately after
subdivision (E) in subdivision (a)(1) in SECTION 2:
(F) Rules designed to prohibit inappropriate use by employees of the LEA or
public charter school of the internet access provided by the LEA or public charter school;
and
(G) Appropriate measures to be taken against employees who violate the policy.
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting "Limitations on accessing websites" in subdivision
(a)(1)(E) in SECTION 2 and substituting instead "Limitations on students and employees
accessing websites".
