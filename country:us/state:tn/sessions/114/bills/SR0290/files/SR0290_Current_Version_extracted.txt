SENATE RESOLUTION 290
By Oliver
A RESOLUTION to honor and commemorate the opening of
Forward Creative Commons at Bordeaux.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should recognize those
occasions that enrich the civic and cultural life of our communities and bring Tennesseans
together in purpose, creativity, and service; and
WHEREAS, the opening of Forward Creative Commons at Bordeaux marks such an
occasion, representing a visionary new space for the people of North Nashville—a place where
learning, art, connection, and wellness come together under one roof; and
WHEREAS, this transformative initiative is the result of a meaningful partnership
between FiftyForward Bordeaux and Creative Girls Rock, two respected nonprofit organizations
whose missions reflect deep commitments to empowerment and lifelong growth; and
WHEREAS, FiftyForward has long served as a trusted resource and advocate for adults
aged fifty and older in Middle Tennessee, enhancing lives through programs that promote
independence, wellness, community connection, and purposeful aging; and
WHEREAS, Creative Girls Rock's mission is to educate and empower young girls and
women to utilize their creative talents to enhance their lives and the world around them,
fostering opportunities for creative expression, confidence, resilience, and personal growth; and
WHEREAS, Forward Creative Commons at Bordeaux embodies the values of both
organizations, offering an intergenerational and inclusive environment where older adults, youth,
artists, families, and community leaders may gather, create, and grow together; and
WHEREAS, this milestone would not have been possible without the vision and
leadership of Sallie Hussey, Chief Executive Officer of FiftyForward, and Charmin Bates,

Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Creative Girls Rock, whose dedication to service and
equity continues to inspire lasting impact across Nashville and beyond; and
WHEREAS, Forward Creative Commons stands not only as a physical space, but as a
symbol of what is possible through collaboration, creativity, and shared investment in the future
of our neighborhoods; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and commemorate
the opening of Forward Creative Commons at Bordeaux, celebrate the powerful partnership
between FiftyForward and Creative Girls Rock, and commend Sallie Hussey and Charmin Bates
for their extraordinary leadership and service to the people of Tennessee.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
