HOUSE BILL 1478
By Hale
SENATE BILL 1594
By Rose
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Section
71-5-142, relative to the comptroller of the
treasury.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 71-5-142(a), is amended by deleting
"either" and inserting ", the comptroller of the treasury," immediately after "department of
commerce and insurance".
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
