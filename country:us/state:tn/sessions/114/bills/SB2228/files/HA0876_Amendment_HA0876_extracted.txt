by deleting Section 5 and substituting instead the following:
SECTION 5. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-11-805, is amended by deleting
"division of vocational education" in subsection (a) and substituting "division of college, career,
and military readiness" and by deleting "division of vocational technical education" in subsection
(b) and substituting "division of college, career, and military readiness".
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting Section 11 and substituting instead the following:
SECTION 11. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-6-3004(i), is amended by
deleting "An LEA" wherever it appears in the subsection and substituting "An LEA or public
charter school", by deleting "an LEA" wherever it appears in the subsection and substituting "an
LEA or public charter school", and by deleting "the LEA" wherever it appears in the subsection
and substituting "the LEA or public charter school".
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting "state college or university maintaining a training school"
wherever it appears in the amendatory language of Section 16 and substituting "state college or
university maintaining a training school pursuant to § 49-8-105".
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting "state colleges or universities maintaining a training
school, private colleges or universities maintaining a training school" in the amendatory
language of Section 20 and substituting "state colleges or universities maintaining a training
school pursuant to § 49-8-105, private colleges or universities maintaining a training school
pursuant to § 49-2-105".
