Section 1:
SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 647
By Johnson
A RESOLUTION to confirm the appointment of Dr. Randall Davis
to the advisory board for the University of
Tennessee Health Science Center.
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-501, established an advisory
board for the University of Tennessee Health Science Center; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-501, provides that the governor
shall appoint five (5) members to such advisory board; and
WHEREAS, Governor Bill Lee has appointed Dr. Randall Davis to the advisory board for
the University of Tennessee Health Science Center for a term that shall expire on June 30,
2029; and
WHEREAS, a Tennessee native, Randall Davis, M.D., joined Memphis Radiological P.C.
in 1999; he received his undergraduate degree in biology and chemistry from the University of
Memphis, earned his medical degree at the University of Tennessee, and completed his
residency in Diagnostic Radiology at Methodist University Hospital of Memphis and a fellowship
in Nuclear Radiology at the University of Arkansas for Medical Sciences; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Davis is board-certified by the American Board of Radiology and holds
memberships in the American College of Radiology, the Tennessee Radiological Society, the
Radiological Society of North America, and the American Society of Nuclear Medicine; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-501, provides that each member
appointed by the governor to the advisory board for the University of Tennessee Health Science
Center shall be confirmed by the general assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby confirm the appointment of Dr. Randall
