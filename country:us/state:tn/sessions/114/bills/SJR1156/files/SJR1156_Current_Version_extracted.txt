SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1156
By Lowe
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend the members of the
Bradley Central High School Bears girls'
basketball team upon its 2025-2026 season.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that this General Assembly should salute those student athletes
who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as true champions of
whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such exemplary group of young women is the Bradley Central High
School girls' basketball team, a legendary program in the annals of Tennessee high school
basketball, whose members have proven themselves to be the fiercest of competitors and truly
worthy of admiration by winning the district, regional, and sectional championships; and
WHEREAS, in the 2025-2026 season, the Bradley Central Bears compiled a record of
32-1, exhibiting a dominating offense and tenacious defense to make their fourth consecutive
trip to the TSSAA Division I, Class 4A state tournament at the Murphy Center in Murfreesboro;
and
WHEREAS, the members of the 2025-2026 Bradley Central Bears girls' basketball team
are Kimora Fields, Libby Stewart, Kenley Johnson, Kiley Johnson, Hallie Rymer, Paisley Payne,
Ayzlyn Stroud, Taryn Felder, Shyla Beard, Sanaa Long, Addie Green, Jada Francis, Makayla
Broome, Serena Baldwin, Zoey Dunsford, and Maggie Hendrix; and
WHEREAS, a true display of grit, effort, and sisterhood, the Bears' superb performance
would not have been possible without the stellar leadership of Head Coach Jason Reuter and
assistants, Adam Belford, Keith Freeman, and Tia Stovall, and the unfailing support of the
players' families, their fans, and their community; and
SJR1156
018024
- 1 -
WHEREAS, the members of the Bradley Central girls' basketball team epitomize all that
is good in today's student athletes, as they have achieved a premier level of success in
competition while exhibiting the highest degree of character in all aspects of life; and
WHEREAS, the perseverance, determination, work ethic, and talent of this group of
young women not only made possible an outstanding season but also surely bode well for their
future success; and
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly find it appropriate to acknowledge
and applaud the Bradley Central Bears for their outstanding season on the hardwood; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend the members and coaches
of the Bradley Central High School Bears girls' basketball team upon their outstanding 2025-
2026 season and extend to them our best wishes for every continued success in all their future
endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
