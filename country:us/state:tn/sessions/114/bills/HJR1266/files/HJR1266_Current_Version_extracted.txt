HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1266
By Bulso
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Evangeline Monge
upon being named the 2025 WILLCO Awards
Girls' Wrestling Athlete of the Year.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly are pleased to recognize and
commend those successful high school athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring
honor to Tennessee and serve as exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable individual is Evangeline Monge of Centennial High
School, an athlete of extraordinary talent, skill, and dedication, who has proven herself a
formidable competitor in wrestling; and
WHEREAS, in recognition of her success in athletic competition, Evey Monge was
named the Girls' Wrestling Athlete of the Year at the 2025 WILLCO Awards celebration; and
WHEREAS, Evey Monge epitomizes all that is good in today's student athlete, as she
has achieved a high level of success in sports while evincing great character; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Monge's notable achievements are a source of great pride for her
family, her community, and her school, and it is appropriate that the members of this General
Assembly recognize this accomplished athlete; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Evangeline Monge upon being named the 2025
WILLCO Awards Girls' Wrestling Athlete of the Year, wishing her much success and happiness
in her future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
