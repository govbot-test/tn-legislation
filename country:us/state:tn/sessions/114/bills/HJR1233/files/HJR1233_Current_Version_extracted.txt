HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1233
By Stevens
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate the Eagleville High
School Eagles baseball team upon winning its
fourth consecutive TSSAA Class A state
championship.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should salute those
student athletes who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as true
champions of whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, again proving to be the fiercest of competitors and, as such, deserving of
the highest regard, the Eagleville High School Eagles baseball team captured the 2025 TSSAA
Class A state championship; and
WHEREAS, the Eagles had won state titles in 2022, 2023, and 2024 to complete the
three-peat; the 2025 championship marks the quadruple, or four consecutive titles, a previously
unimaginable feat; and
WHEREAS, arriving at the state tournament with an excellent record, the Eagles won
four consecutive games, including a 6-5 victory over Coalfield in the final tilt, to take home the
state championship trophy; and
WHEREAS, the Eagles were fortunate to enjoy the expertise and leadership of Coach
Brandon Bassham and his assistant coaches, Tanner Mabry and Todd Williamson; and
WHEREAS, the following young men are proud to fly like an Eagle: Koltt Bassham,
Carter Blair, Maddox Bouldin, Alex Bryant, Krymson Burks, Brady Burns, Aiden Davis, Sam
Dodd, Cooper Duke, Jr Duke, Brycen Edging, Joseph Fox, Kelten Freeze, Brody Gleason,
Ryder Henderson, Brayden Hillard, Karsyn Knowles, Lawson Latta, Wyatt McDougal, Tucker
McLeary, Blain Poynor, Brody Range, Spencer Robbins, Stephen Robbins, Drake
HJR1233

Rzemieniewski, Adrian Sevieri, Micah Sevieri, Marshall Spann, Mason Spears, Jacob Sweeney,
Carter Warbritton, and Elijah Watkins; and
WHEREAS, the members of the Eagleville High School baseball team epitomize all that
is good in today's student athletes, as they have achieved a premier level of success in
competition while exhibiting the highest degree of character in all aspects of life; and
WHEREAS, the perseverance, determination, work ethic, and talent of these young men
not only made possible a fourth consecutive championship season but also surely bode well for
their success in every future endeavor; and
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly find it appropriate to acknowledge
and applaud the members of the Eagleville High School baseball team for their outstanding
performance and for serving as examples of the exceptional quality of the youth of Tennessee;
now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate the Eagleville High School Eagles baseball
team upon winning its fourth consecutive TSSAA Class A state championship in 2025 and
extend to each of these gifted athletes our best wishes for every continued success in all future
endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
