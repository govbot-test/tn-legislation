Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 40-25-130, is amended by
redesignating the existing language as subsection (a) and adding the following new subsection
(b):
(b) A defendant who has paid costs for which the state or county are liable under
subsection (a) may file a petition with the court in which the costs where charged to
recover the amount paid.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.
