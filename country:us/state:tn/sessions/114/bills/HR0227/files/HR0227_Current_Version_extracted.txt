HOUSE RESOLUTION 227
By Keisling
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Ella Eads, 2026
Salutatorian of Clay County High School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should pause to pay tribute to those students who,
through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to academic excellence, have
distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Ella Eads is one such exceptional student, who was recently named
Salutatorian of the 2026 graduating class at Clay County High School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Ms. Eads has achieved outstanding academic success
and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country; and
WHEREAS, in her remarkable high school career, Ella Eads has earned an exemplary
grade point average while participating in a number of extracurricular activities; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Eads's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well for
her future success; and
WHEREAS, her noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for her family,
her school, and her community; and
WHEREAS, Ella Eads's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of the members of this legislative body; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby
honor and recognize the 2026 Clay County High School Salutatorian, Ella Eads, on her
impressive academic achievement and extend to her our best wishes for a happy, successful
future.
