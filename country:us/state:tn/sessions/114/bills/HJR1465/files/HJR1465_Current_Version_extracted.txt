HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1465
By Reeves
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Leah Calcutt on her
perfect ACT score.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pay tribute to
those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to academic
excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom and beyond; and
WHEREAS, Leah Calcutt, a student at Fairview High School, is one such exceptional
student, who received a perfect composite score of 36 on the ACT exam in 2025; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Ms. Calcutt has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, her laudable dedication and drive are similarly exhibited in her
extracurricular activities, and all her endeavors speak of a passion for learning and self-
betterment; and
WHEREAS, Leah Calcutt's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well
for her future success; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Calcutt's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for
her family, her school, and her community; and
WHEREAS, Leah Calcutt's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of our respect,
admiration, and commendation; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we commend and honor Leah Calcutt of Fairview High School on her

perfect ACT score, salute her impressive academic achievement, and extend to her our best
wishes for a happy, successful future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared

the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
