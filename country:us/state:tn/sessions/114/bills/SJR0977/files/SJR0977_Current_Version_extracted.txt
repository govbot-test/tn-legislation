SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 977
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Da'Mori Lytch on
being named the TCCAA Player of the Year in
men's basketball.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should recognize those incredibly successful student
athletes who have distinguished themselves as inspirational leaders among their peers; and
WHEREAS, Da'Mori Lytch of Walters State Community College was recently named the
Tennessee Community College Athletic Association Player of the Year in men's basketball
based upon his outstanding performance on the hardwood this season; and
WHEREAS, Da'Mori Lytch was the very epitome of consistency for the Senators during
the 2025-2026 season, shooting 69.8% from the field and averaging 13 points and 6.6 rebounds
per game; and
WHEREAS, Da'Mori Lytch scored double-digits in 19 games this season; in his best
game of the year, he scored 24 points and pulled eight rebounds versus Roane State; and
WHEREAS, Da'Mori Lytch exemplifies the spirit, determination, and perseverance for
which Tennesseans are notable, and he is richly deserving of our approbation on this special
occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Da'Mori Lytch of Walters
State Community College upon being named the Tennessee Community College Athletic
Association Player of the Year in men's basketball and extend to him our best wishes for every
continued success on the court and in life.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
