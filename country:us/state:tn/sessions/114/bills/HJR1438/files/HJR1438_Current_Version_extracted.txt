HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1438
By Rudd
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend the members of the 196th
Field Artillery Brigade Embedded Training Team
(ETT 4.0) for their distinguished service in
Afghanistan from July 2005 through July 2006.
WHEREAS, the members of the 196th Field Artillery Brigade Embedded Training Team
(ETT 4.0), composed of more than one hundred courageous and dedicated soldiers, answered
the call to serve their country with honor, integrity, and unwavering commitment; and
WHEREAS, from July 2005 through July 2006, these soldiers deployed to Afghanistan in
support of Operation Enduring Freedom during a pivotal time in that nation's history; and
WHEREAS, serving under the leadership of Colonel Joe C. Geren, the soldiers of ETT
4.0 were embedded alongside units of the Afghan National Army, providing critical training,
mentorship, and leadership development to help establish a capable, professional, and self-
sustaining security force; and
WHEREAS, their mission demanded not only tactical and technical excellence but also
extraordinary adaptability, cultural awareness, and perseverance while operating in austere and
hazardous conditions; and
WHEREAS, during their deployment, members of ETT 4.0 were present for and
supported the first free and fair democratic elections in Afghanistan's history, contributing to the
advancement of liberty, representative government, and the rule of law; and
WHEREAS, the team further contributed to provincial reconstruction efforts, helping to
oversee and support initiatives that strengthened infrastructure, enhanced stability, and
improved the quality of life for the Afghan people; and

WHEREAS, the families, spouses, and children of these soldiers bore the burden of
separation and sacrifice at home, providing steadfast support that made the success of this
mission possible; and
WHEREAS, the service and sacrifice of the members of ETT 4.0 reflect great credit
upon themselves, the 196th Field Artillery Brigade, the United States Army, and the State of
Tennessee; the twentieth anniversary reunion of the unit will be held in Murfreesboro, on the
evening of April 18, 2026; and
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly recognize and pay
tribute to these soldiers and their families for their valor, dedication, and lasting contributions to
global security and freedom; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and commend the members of the 196th Field Artillery
Brigade Embedded Training Team (ETT 4.0) for their distinguished service in Afghanistan from
July 2005 through July 2006 and extend to them and their families our profound gratitude for
their courage, sacrifice, and commitment to the cause of freedom.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
