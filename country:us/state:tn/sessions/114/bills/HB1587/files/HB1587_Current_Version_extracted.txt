HOUSE BILL 1587
By Lafferty
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 4,
Chapter 29 and Title 4, Chapter 3, Part 7, relative
to the local government planning advisory
committee.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 4-29-247(a), is amended by deleting
subdivision (35).

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 4-29-248(a), is amended by inserting
the following as a new subdivision:
( ) Local government planning advisory committee, created by § 4-3-727;

Section 4:
SECTION 3. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
