SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 738
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Luke Stuart of
Gatlinburg-Pittman High School on being named
the Region 1-3A Co-Lineman of the Year.
WHEREAS, we are pleased to specially honor and commend those successful high
school athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring honor to the State of Tennessee
and serve as exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable athlete is Luke Stuart of Gatlinburg-Pittman High
School, a football player of extraordinary talent, skill, and athleticism, who has been named the
Region 1-3A Co-Lineman of the Year; and
WHEREAS, a powerhouse on the football gridiron, Luke Stuart record fifty-five tackles
and three sacks for the Highlanders this past season; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Stuart epitomizes all that is good in today's student athlete, as he has
achieved a high level of success in sports while evincing great character both on and off the
field; and
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that we recognize and commend this elite athlete and
outstanding young gentleman; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Luke Stuart of
Gatlinburg-Pittman High School on being named the Region 1-3A Co-Lineman of the Year and
extend to him our best wishes for much continued success in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
