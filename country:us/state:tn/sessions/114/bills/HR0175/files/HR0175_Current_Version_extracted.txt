HOUSE RESOLUTION 175
By Reeves
A RESOLUTION to recognize and honor Westwood Elementary
School on the occasion of its twenty-fifth
anniversary.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should recognize those exemplary educational institutions
that have made significant contributions, through an abiding commitment to excellence, to their
respective communities and to this State; and
WHEREAS, one such noteworthy institution is Westwood Elementary School, which is
celebrating its twenty-fifth anniversary on April 2, 2026; and
WHEREAS, Westwood Elementary School has enjoyed the teaching services of
educators of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity and a staff committed to the highest
ideals of public service and quality education for all; and
WHEREAS, the dedication to academic excellence demonstrated by all the fine
individuals affiliated with Westwood Elementary School is clearly deserving of our respect,
admiration, and commendation on this most auspicious occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor
Westwood Elementary School on the occasion of its twenty-fifth anniversary, commend its
administration, faculty, staff, and students, both past and present, for their dedication to the
highest tenets of education, and extend to them our best wishes for much continued success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
