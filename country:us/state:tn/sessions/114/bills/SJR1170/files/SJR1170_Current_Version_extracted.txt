SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1170
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Bailey Cheek on her
selection as The Greeneville Sun's Teacher of
the Month.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
specially recognize those outstanding teachers who have devoted their careers to providing the
highest-quality education for Tennessee's students; and
WHEREAS, Bailey Cheek is one such estimable educator, who has distinguished herself
as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Cheek was selected as The Greeneville Sun's Teacher of the Month for
March 2026, a most befitting honor that recognizes the positive influence she has on so many
young lives; and
WHEREAS, a teacher at Hal Henard Elementary School, Bailey Cheek is an extremely
talented and compassionate educator who works assiduously to motivate her students to reach
their full potential, both as scholars and as citizens; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Cheek serves as an inspiration to her colleagues and students alike,
and her friendship and counsel are highly valued by those who are fortunate to know her; and
WHEREAS, Bailey Cheek truly epitomizes the dedicated teacher who is wholly
committed to the highest ideals of public service and quality education for all, and she should be
specially recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and congratulate Bailey Cheek on
being chosen as The Greeneville Sun's Teacher of the Month, commend her exceptional
