HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 965
By Helton-Haynes
A RESOLUTION to recognize East Ridge City Manager J. Scott
Miller on the occasion of his retirement.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
specially recognize those citizens who have performed with uncommon devotion and
enthusiasm throughout their years of public service; and
WHEREAS, J. Scott Miller is one such estimable individual who, having rendered years
of sterling service to the City of East Ridge, will take his much-deserved retirement from his role
as city manager this year; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Miller was appointed city manager in 2022, marking his second tenure
in that esteemed position; his initial term as East Ridge City Manager spanned from 2016 to
2018; and
WHEREAS, J. Scott Miller's impact on the City of East Ridge is immeasurable; from
2022 to 2026, East Ridge has secured more than $13 million in grant funding, which continues
to support public safety equipment, roadway improvements, community center upgrades, park
redevelopment, youth programming, and infrastructure investments; and
WHEREAS, during Mr. Miller's distinguished tenure as city manager, East Ridge has
made significant investments in infrastructure and transportation improvements, including road
maintenance, stormwater systems, and landmark and directional signage, among additional
projects that have enhanced the community's identity; and
WHEREAS, public safety investments at the direction of Mr. Miller have included the
substantial modernization and improvement of public safety equipment and department staffing.
Developments were made to the fire department and its equipment; the sanitation department;

WHEREAS, the City of East Ridge experienced substantial economic development and

WHEREAS, J. Scott Miller exemplifies the spirit and allegiance to family and community

WHEREAS, throughout his illustrious tenure, Mr. Miller has carried out his
responsibilities with diligence and integrity, and his remarkable success as a public servant and
civic leader has set the mold for all who will follow; and
WHEREAS, a strong advocate for the citizens of East Ridge, J. Scott Miller has
compiled a singular record of success during his exemplary public service career, and he should
be specially recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend J. Scott Miller for his honorable and astute service
to the people of Tennessee as city manager of East Ridge and extend to him our best wishes
for a happy and fulfilling retirement.
