HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1204
By Reneau
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Seven Mingo upon his
receipt of the Rookie Officer of the Year Award
from the Chattanooga Police Department.
WHEREAS, our nation was conceived by individuals who were willing to sacrifice their
personal concerns to ensure the public safety and welfare, and the Volunteer State is especially
proud to be the home of so many valiant individuals who have performed above and beyond the
call of duty in service of public safety; and
WHEREAS, Seven Mingo was recently honored with the Chattanooga Police
Department's (CPD's) Rookie Officer of the Year Award in recognition of his exceptional
productivity, dedication, initiative, and professionalism; and
WHEREAS, during the past year, Officer Mingo has shown remarkable commitment to
citizen safety, proactively arresting violent offenders, individuals exploiting others, and
unauthorized drivers; and
WHEREAS, his actions resulted in 259 arrests, accounting for 3.74 percent of CPD's
total and ten times the department-wide average; and
WHEREAS, Officer Mingo's arrest activity has not compromised his district
responsibilities, as he has filed 739 incident and miscellaneous reports, double the number of
his peers; and
WHEREAS, Officer Seven Mingo has made a lasting impact on the Chattanooga Police
Department through his infectious positive attitude, and he is highly deserving of this honor;
now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
HJR1204
016029
- 1 -
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Seven Mingo upon his receipt of the Rookie
Officer of the Year Award from the Chattanooga Police Department.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
- 2 - 016029
