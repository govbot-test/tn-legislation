by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 40-25-129(a)(3), is amended by
adding the following as a new subdivision:
(C) A county incurring expenses pursuant to a criminal prosecution under this
subdivision (a)(3) shall provide an invoice of the expenses imposed on the county to the
department of correction. Within sixty (60) days of receiving an invoice pursuant to this
subdivision (a)(3)(C), the department shall pay the amount of the invoiced expenses
permitted by law to the county.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
