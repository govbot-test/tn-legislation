HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1353
By Crawford
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Margaret Tranbarger
Cooper on the celebration of her 100th birthday.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should recognize
those citizens who are celebrating milestones in their venerable lives; and
WHEREAS, Margaret Tranbarger Cooper celebrates her 100th birthday on April 27,
2026, an occasion commemorated with great joy in remembering the past, giving thanks for the
present, and eagerly anticipating the future; and
WHEREAS, the ninth of ten children, Margaret Cooper was born and raised in Church
Hill; and
WHEREAS, in 1947, Margaret Cooper was joined in holy matrimony with Walter Claude
"W.C." Cooper, and the loving couple enjoyed thirty-five years of blissful happiness together;
and
WHEREAS, she became a valuable resident of Church Hill while working at Tennessee
Eastman; she raised two sons, Walter Claude Cooper, Jr., and James Alan Cooper, while
managing a laundromat in Church Hill for ten years; and
WHEREAS, after her husband's passing, Margaret Cooper fulfilled the remaining term
for his position of Hawkins County Commissioner in 1985-1986; she also served on the Election
Commission of the State of Tennessee and on the Board of the Laurel Run Park in Church Hill
in memory of W.C. Cooper; and
WHEREAS, throughout her long and productive life, she has been active with her local
church, loving the Lord and endeavoring to live an exemplary Christian life; and
WHEREAS, she has supported her friends and community by visiting and providing
meals when in need; and

WHEREAS, she has been blessed with six grandchildren and four great-grandchildren,
who know her as "Coopie"; and
WHEREAS, Margaret Cooper attributes her longevity and incredibly sharp mind to her
love and faith in God and her commitment to eating home-cooked meals every day, which she
offers to everyone who visits her; and
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to pause in our deliberations to specially recognize
one of Tennessee's finest citizens; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Margaret Tranbarger Cooper on the celebration
of her 100th birthday and extend to her our best wishes for many happy returns of the day.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
