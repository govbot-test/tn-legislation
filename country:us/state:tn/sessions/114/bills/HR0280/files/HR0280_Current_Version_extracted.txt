HOUSE RESOLUTION 280
By Chism
A RESOLUTION to honor Davina Jones upon her recognition at
the Influential 20 Black Tie Awards Gala.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should specially recognize those estimable individuals
who have dedicated their time and energies toward enhancing the quality of life in their
communities; and
WHEREAS, one such individual is Davina Jones, who is being honored this year at the
Influential 20 Black Tie Awards Gala in Memphis; and
WHEREAS, the Influential 20 Black Tie Awards Gala seeks to identify, honor, and
celebrate a cross section of some of the most accomplished and influential business leaders,
trailblazers, and hidden gems from a wide range of industries; and
WHEREAS, these individuals are committed to business growth, professional
excellence, leadership, and community service and have risen up through the ranks of their
respective companies or industries; and
WHEREAS, throughout her estimable career, Davina Jones has been committed to
excellence and community service, and she should be recognized on this special occasion;
now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we
congratulate Davina Jones upon her recognition at the Influential 20 Black Tie Awards Gala,
applaud her outstanding community leadership, salute her professional expertise and personal
excellence, and extend to her our best wishes for continued success in her future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
