Section 1:
By Jones R
SENATE BILL 2434
By Crowe
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 39
and Title 49, relative to unmanned aircraft over
schools.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 39-13-903(a)(6), is amended by
deleting "subdivision (a)(6)" in subdivisions (a)(6)(B) and (a)(6)(C) and substituting "subdivision
(a)(7)".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 39-13-903(a), is amended by adding
the following as a new subdivision (5) and renumbering the subsequent subdivisions:
(5) Knowingly uses an unmanned aircraft over the grounds of a school;

Section 4:
SECTION 3. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 39-13-903, is amended by deleting
subsection (b) and substituting:
(b)
(1) An offense under subdivisions (a)(1)-(6) is a Class C misdemeanor.
(2) An offense under subdivision (a)(7) is a Class E felony.

Section 5:
SECTION 4. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.
