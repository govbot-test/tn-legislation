HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1127
By Capley
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Warren Camfield, 2026
Top Ten Senior of Wayne County High School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Warren Camfield is one such exceptional student, who was recently named
a Top Ten Senior of the 2026 graduating class at Wayne County High School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Mr. Camfield has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in his remarkable high school career, Warren Camfield has earned an
exemplary 99.2 grade point average while participating in numerous extracurricular activities,
including Math Club, Beta Club, Interact Club, Future Business Leaders of America, and
Fellowship of Christian Athletes; and
WHEREAS, he is also a member of the basketball, baseball, and golf teams; he serves
as a Wildcat Ambassador for Wayne County High School and has accumulated over 200 hours
of community service; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Camfield's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well
for his future success; and
WHEREAS, Warren Camfield's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride
for his parents, Michael and Amanda Camfield, his school, and his community; and

WHEREAS, Mr. Camfield's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of the members of this General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor Warren Camfield on being named a 2026
Wayne County High School Top Ten Senior, salute his impressive academic achievement, and
extend to him our best wishes for a happy, successful future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
