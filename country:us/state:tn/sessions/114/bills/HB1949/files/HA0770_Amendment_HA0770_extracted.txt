Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 33-11-105, is amended by adding
the following as new subsections (b) and (c) and redesignating the existing subsection (b) as
subsection (d):
(b) Except as provided in subsection (c), the council may approve an advance
payment of disbursed funds awarded as a grant contract to a nonprofit organization for
statewide, regional, or local opioid abatement and remediation purposes. For purposes
of this section only, an "advance payment" means a one-time request by a nonprofit
organization made within the first three (3) months following the date of execution of a
grant contract for an amount not to exceed four (4) months of the projected employee
salaries, benefits, and taxes of the nonprofit organization.
(c)
(1) An advance payment must not be disbursed or otherwise provided
directly or indirectly to an organization that has not been awarded the grant
contract by the council.
(2) In order to be eligible to receive an advance payment, the nonprofit
organizations shall provide the council with written documentation demonstrating
that the nonprofit organization is recognized by the internal revenue service as a
tax-exempt nonprofit entity under § 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code (26
U.S.C. § 501(c)(3)) and has an annual operating budget of no more than five
hundred thousand dollars ($500,000).

(3) The nonprofit organization requesting an advance payment shall
submit written documentation as requested by the council demonstrating the
