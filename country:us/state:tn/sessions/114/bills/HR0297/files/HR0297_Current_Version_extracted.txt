HOUSE RESOLUTION 297
By Chism
A RESOLUTION to commend Representative G.A. Hardaway for
his honorable and distinguished service to the
people of Tennessee as a member of the House
of Representatives.
WHEREAS, it is most appropriate that the members of this legislative body should honor
those fellow legislators who have performed their duties with the utmost integrity and whose
efforts during their time in office have been dedicated to providing thoughtful and exemplary
service to their districts and to this great State; and
WHEREAS, Representative G.A. Hardaway of Memphis is one such distinguished public
servant, who has served both his constituency and this legislative body with honor and ability;
and
WHEREAS, during his tenure on Capitol Hill, Representative Hardaway has been a
highly influential figure in the political, social, and civic life of Shelby County, and he has served
his constituents in numerous capacities, always working diligently at his elected duties and
expending the necessary effort to understand the opposing sides of complex issues; and
WHEREAS, G.A. Hardaway has distinguished himself as a public-spirited citizen of the
highest order and as an exceptional asset to the good people of the 93rd House District, whom
he has represented as a member of the 105th, 106th, 107th, 108th, 109th, 110th, 111th, 112th, 113th,
and 114th General Assemblies; and
WHEREAS, throughout his service, Representative G.A. Hardaway has been prominent
as a dedicated and well-informed legislator who always votes his conscience; and
WHEREAS, an active and dynamic participant in the legislative process during his time
in the General Assembly, Representative Hardaway has rendered sterling leadership as a

and Utilities Subcommittee; and
WHEREAS, beyond his legislative duties, Representative Hardaway has remained
deeply engaged in civic and charitable life, serving as a member of the Board of Directors of the
Africa in April Festival and Black Family Channel; Al-Kadosh Shrine Temple; Black Business
Association; CBU Oliver Thornton Scholarship Fund; and Herndon #10 Masonic Lodge; and
WHEREAS, his community leadership has also included service as founder/visionary,

member of the NAACP, Rosa Parks Academy, Southern Christian Leadership Conference;
political director of Rainbow PUSH Coalition; and charter member of the Juvenile Court
Advisory Council; and
WHEREAS, Representative Hardaway has been recognized with numerous honors and

College High School Appreciation Award, and the Rozelle-Annesdale Association Outstanding

WHEREAS, born in Meridian, Mississippi, G.A. Hardaway graduated from DePaul
University with a Bachelor of Science degree in Finance; and
WHEREAS, throughout his outstanding career as a public servant, Representative G.A.
Hardaway has worked tirelessly on behalf of his constituents, bringing to his office an honorable
bearing and the traditional values of hard work and common sense; and
WHEREAS, Representative Hardaway is wholly committed to the noble precepts of
public service that have earned Tennessee recognition as the Volunteer State, and he should
be specially recognized for his exemplary tenure in the General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby
