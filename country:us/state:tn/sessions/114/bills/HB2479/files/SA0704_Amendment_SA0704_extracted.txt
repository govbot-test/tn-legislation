Senate Energy, Ag., and Nat. Resources 1
Amendment No. 1 to SB2486
Reeves
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 2486* House Bill No. 2479
by deleting § 43-41-105 in the amendatory language of Section 1 and substituting:
43-41-105.
(a) Except as provided in this section, the one-hundred-dollar fee collected
pursuant to § 43-41-104(c) must be used only for the administration of the registration
process.
(b) By each June 30, beginning in 2027, the department of agriculture shall
review its actual and expected fee revenue and expenditures for the registration process
for the current fiscal year and the next two (2) fiscal years in order to determine surplus
revenue.
(c) Each July 1, beginning in 2027, ninety percent (90%) of the surplus revenue
identified in subsection (b) must be transferred or allocated to the comptroller of the
treasury to be used solely for the property tax relief program for disabled veterans and
their spouses established under § 67-5-704.
