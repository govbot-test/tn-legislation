Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 67, Chapter 6, Part 3, is amended by
adding the following as a new section:
(a) As used in this section:
(1) "Qualified building materials" means tangible personal property
purchased during the qualified capital investment period, that becomes part of
the real property comprising a facility described in subsection (b); and
(2) "Qualified capital investment period" means a period beginning July
1, 2026, and ending December 31, 2032.
(b) Subject to approval by the commissioner pursuant to subsection (c), qualified
building materials are exempt from the sales and use tax imposed by this chapter if the
qualified building materials are used in the construction, expansion, or renovation of one
(1) or more qualified facilities located within the boundaries of an industrial development
board established pursuant to title 7, chapter 53, part 1, if:
(1) The industrial development board's jurisdiction is limited to a center
city area and areas adjacent to the center city area; and
(2) The taxpayer or the taxpayer's lessee makes a capital investment of
at least sixty million dollars ($60,000,000) in the construction, expansion, or
renovation of the facility and related facilities at the same location during the
qualified capital investment period.
(c)
