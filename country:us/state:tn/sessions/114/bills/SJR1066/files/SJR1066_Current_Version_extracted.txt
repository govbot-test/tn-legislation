SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1066
By Walley
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Freed-Hardeman
University Head Coaches Abby Stutts and Drew
Stutts.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pay tribute to
the excellent coaches who have led their teams to unprecedented success in athletic
competition; and
WHEREAS, Head Coaches Abby Stutts and Drew Stutts, siblings hailing from Florence,
Alabama, have led the Freed-Hardeman University (FHU) Lions and Lady Lions to Mid-South
Conference (MSC) Tournament titles and into the NAIA National Tournaments, delivering
dominant seasons and cultivating a culture of discipline, preparation, belief, and overall
success; and
WHEREAS, the Freed-Hardeman Lions finished conference play undefeated for the first
time in school history with a 12-0 record, completed the regular season 27-1, and secured their
fourth consecutive MSC Tournament title with a 72-64 win over Bethel; the Lady Lions finished
the season 27-3 and defeated the Bethel Wildcats 64-56 to win the MSC Tournament; and
WHEREAS, the Lions completed a twenty-six-game winning streak, and the Lady Lions
a five-game winning streak, for a combined thirty-one games; and
WHEREAS, together, the Stutts siblings have compiled a 223-61 record throughout their
basketball careers, reflecting their lifelong love for the game, which was instilled in them in a
home where discipline, faith, and sports were foundational; and
WHEREAS, Drew Stutts, who is currently in his seventh year as head coach, began his
coaching career at Mars Hill Bible School and later coached at Chester County High School
before officially stepping into an assistant role at FHU; and

WHEREAS, Abby Stutts joined the FHU staff as an assistant in 2020 and has served as
head coach of the Lady Lions for the past two years; and
WHEREAS, Abby Stutts and Drew Stutts have distinguished themselves as great
basketball coaches, tacticians, and motivators of young men and women, and they should be
appropriately recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Freed-Hardeman
University Head Coaches Abby Stutts and Drew Stutts, salute their outstanding careers on the
basketball court, and extend to them our best wishes for much continued success on the field of
play and in life.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
