HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 883
By Warner
A RESOLUTION to recognize Marshall County Mayor Mike Keny
on the occasion of his retirement.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should specially
recognize those citizens who have performed with uncommon devotion and enthusiasm
throughout their years of public service; and
WHEREAS, one such outstanding individual is Mayor Mike Keny, a lifelong resident of
Marshall County, who is taking his well-earned retirement after a distinguished career in county
government; and
WHEREAS, Mayor Keny began his service as Marshall County mayor in 2018, and
throughout his tenure, he has served on various committees and boards both locally and
regionally; and
WHEREAS, his integrity, transparency, and financially prudent approach to government
have earned him the respect and admiration of citizens and colleagues alike, and his dedication
has not gone unnoticed; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Keny graduated from Marshall County High School in 1976 and
attended Columbia State Community College before graduating in 1978; he then attended the
University of Tennessee Memphis Pharmacy School, graduating in 1981. During high school
and college breaks, he wrote articles for The Lewisburg Tribune; and
WHEREAS, before becoming mayor, Mr. Keny served as a member on the Marshall
County School Board for twelve years; he also was a partner and owner of H & S Pharmacy for
more than thirty years; and
WHEREAS, from 2000-2017, Mike Keny worked as a sports announcer for WJJM,
starting with high school basketball; he launched the show Talking Sports in 2002; and

WHEREAS, a civic-minded individual, Mayor Keny is a longtime member of the Rotary
Club and was the emcee for Miss Marshall County for several years; and
WHEREAS, throughout his illustrious tenure, Mike Keny has carried out his
responsibilities as mayor of Marshall County with diligence and integrity; his remarkable
success as a public servant and civic leader is directly attributable to his reliance on the time-
honored values of hard work and common sense and his uncommon ability to work well with
people from all walks of life; and
WHEREAS, a strong advocate for the citizens of Marshall County, Mayor Keny has
compiled a singular record of success during his exemplary public service career, and he should
be specially recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we commend Mike Keny for his honorable and astute service to the good
people of Tennessee as mayor of Marshall County, salute his unswerving commitment to living
the examined life with courage and conviction, and extend our best wishes for a happy and
fulfilling retirement.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
