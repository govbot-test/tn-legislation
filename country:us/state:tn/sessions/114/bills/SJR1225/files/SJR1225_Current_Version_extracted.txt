SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1225
By Roberts
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Sarai Gonzalez, 2026
Valedictorian of Dayspring Academy.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Sarai Gonzalez is one such exceptional student, who was recently named
Valedictorian of the 2026 graduating class at Dayspring Academy; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Ms. Gonzalez has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in her remarkable high school career, Sarai Gonzalez has earned an
exemplary grade point average while participating in a number of extracurricular activities; and
WHEREAS, her display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well for her
future success; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Gonzalez's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for
her family, her school, and her community; and
WHEREAS, Sarai Gonzalez's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the
respect, admiration, and commendation of the members of this General Assembly; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor the 2026 Dayspring
