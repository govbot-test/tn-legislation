SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 594
By Seal
A RESOLUTION to recognize Jabo's All-Wood Products, LLC, on
the occasion of its fortieth anniversary.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
recognize those Tennessee businesses that have a long-standing commitment to providing
exceptional service to their customers; and
WHEREAS, one such notable establishment is Jabo's All-Wood Products, LLC, which
celebrates its fortieth anniversary this year, a commendable milestone for this family-owned
business; and
WHEREAS, located at 851 Lakeshore Drive in Bean Station, Jabo's All-Wood Products
is known throughout the region for its commitment to quality materials, traditional woodworking,
and exceptional customer service; and
WHEREAS, under the leadership of owners Estes and Terry Johnson, Jabo's All-Wood
Products has earned a reputation for durable handcrafted products, specializing in all-wood
home furnishings and cabinetry, including bookcases, China hutches, firearm cabinets, and
kitchen and bath cabinets; and
WHEREAS, for four decades, Jabo's All-Wood Products has remained an important part
of the Grainger County community and the surrounding region, serving as a trusted partner for
contractors and homeowners alike, and it is most fitting that we honor this exemplary
Tennessee business on this milestone occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we congratulate Jabo's All-Wood Products, LLC, on
