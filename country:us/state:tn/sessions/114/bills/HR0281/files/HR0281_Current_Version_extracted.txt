HOUSE RESOLUTION 281
By Chism
A RESOLUTION to honor Devereau J. Williams upon his
recognition at the Influential 20 Black Tie Awards
Gala.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should specially recognize those estimable individuals
who have dedicated their time and energies toward enhancing the quality of life in their
communities; and
WHEREAS, one such individual is Devereau J. Williams, who is being honored this year
at the Influential 20 Black Tie Awards Gala in Memphis; and
WHEREAS, the Influential 20 Black Tie Awards Gala seeks to identify, honor, and
celebrate a cross section of some of the most accomplished and influential business leaders,
trailblazers, and hidden gems from a wide range of industries; and
WHEREAS, these individuals are committed to business growth, professional
excellence, leadership, and community service and have risen up through the ranks of their
respective companies or industries; and
WHEREAS, throughout his estimable career, Devereau J. Williams has been committed
to excellence and community service, and he should be recognized on this special occasion;
now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we
congratulate Devereau J. Williams upon his recognition at the Influential 20 Black Tie Awards
Gala, applaud his outstanding community leadership, salute his professional expertise and
personal excellence, and extend to him our best wishes for continued success in his future
endeavors.
