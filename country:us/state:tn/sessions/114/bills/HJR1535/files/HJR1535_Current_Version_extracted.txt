HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1535
By Sparks
A RESOLUTION to commend Sidney Ware, Brandon Randolph,
and Fatima Grandos Macareno, along with the
Mu Zeta Chapter of Omega Psi Phi Fraternity,
Inc., and No Labels at Middle Tennessee State
University, for their outstanding leadership in
promoting bipartisan outreach, civil debate, and
constructive civic engagement.
WHEREAS, Sidney Ware, Brandon Randolph, and Fatima Grandos Macareno serve as
dedicated student leaders at Middle Tennessee State University (MTSU) and have worked
tirelessly to foster dialogue across political lines in an era often characterized by division and
partisanship; and
WHEREAS, through the Mu Zeta Chapter of Omega Psi Phi Fraternity, Inc., members
have embodied the fraternity's cardinal principles of Manhood, Scholarship, Perseverance, and
Uplift by organizing and participating in initiatives that encourage respectful engagement
between students of differing political perspectives; and
WHEREAS, through the work of No Labels at MTSU, members have advanced the
mission of the national No Labels movement by creating nonpartisan spaces on campus for
open discussion, voter registration drives, candidate forums, and community events that bring
together Democrats, Republicans, Independents, and unaffiliated students; and
WHEREAS, these individuals' efforts have included hosting bipartisan panels, facilitating
civil-debate workshops, and building coalitions that emphasize shared values and common-
sense solutions rather than partisan conflict, thereby strengthening democratic participation and
civic literacy among MTSU students and the broader Middle Tennessee community; and
WHEREAS, such work is especially commendable at a public university in the Volunteer
State, where cultivating informed, respectful discourse among future leaders is vital to the health
of our republic and the continued strength of Tennessee's civic institutions; now, therefore,
