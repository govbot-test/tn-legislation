HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1251
By Doggett
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend the Richland High
School Cheerleading Team.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly are pleased to specially honor and
commend those successful high school athletic teams that, through their magnificent exploits,
bring honor to Tennessee and serve as exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, the Richland High School Cheerleading Team is one such group of young
athletes, who are dedicated to teamwork and have enjoyed an outstanding record of success
during this past year; and
WHEREAS, no strangers to awards and accolades, the Cheerleading Team from
Richland High School won the Space Center and Smoky Mountain Regional traditional
cheerleading championships, took ninth place as a gameday finalist at the 2026 National High
School cheerleading championship, and finished runner-up at the 2025 Super Varsity
Nontumbling Gameday State competition; and
WHEREAS, the Richland High School Cheerleading Team brought the energy and
school spirit to games in support of the football team, which reached the quarterfinals in the
playoffs; the boys' basketball team, which reached the Substate round of the postseason; and
the girls' basketball team, which won the TSSAA Division I, Class 1A State Championship; and
WHEREAS, the members of this outstanding cheerleading team are Abbi Watts, Adalee
Nelson, Addie Kirkpatrick, Aniston Gordon, Aubreeah Helton, Aurelia Risner, Brayden Norris,
Breanna Birdsong, Cameron Jackson, Caroline Jackson, Caydence Dunavant, Claire Taylor,
Courtney Curran, Emma Gibson, Gabby Braden, Grayson Williams, Holley Wise, Jack Curry,
Jaycee Lane, Kailynne Hawkins, Kaylee Hall, Kylie Jo Kennedy, Krysten Hood, Langston Norris,
Lauren Cunningham, Leah Costello, Peyton McCrary, Ryah Greene, and Tenley Rose; and

WHEREAS, the Richland High School cheerleaders are fortunate to have the invaluable,
devoted guidance of coaches Beth Hall and Holly Gibson; and
WHEREAS, displaying great sportsmanship and outstanding character throughout the
season, the members of the Richland High School Cheerleading Team have conducted
themselves in a manner appropriate to the integrity of the State of Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, the determination, talent, and work ethic of this group of young athletes not
only made possible a stellar triumph but also surely bode well for their future success; and
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to acknowledge and applaud the Richland High
School Cheerleading Team for its outstanding performance; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we commend and honor the members and coaches of the Richland High
School Cheerleading Team for their commitment to excellence, congratulate them on their many
achievements this past season, and extend to them our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
