SENATE BILL 1612
By Bailey
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 4 and
Title 8, relative to state employees.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 8-50-1301(b), is amended by
deleting the language "state-owned office buildings" and substituting "state-owned or leased
office buildings".

SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.

HOUSE BILL 2268
By Hale
SENATE BILL 1612
By Bailey
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 4 and
Title 8, relative to state employees.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 8-50-1301(b), is amended by
deleting the language "state-owned office buildings" and substituting "state-owned or leased
office buildings".
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
