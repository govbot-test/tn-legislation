HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 787
By Capley
A RESOLUTION to honor the memory of William Allen Dorning of
Lawrence County.
WHEREAS, we were greatly saddened to learn of the passing of William Allen Dorning
of Lawrence County; and
WHEREAS, the retired sheriff of Lawrence County, William Dorning was an exemplary
citizen and consummate professional who worked assiduously to improve the quality of life for
his fellow citizens in numerous capacities; and
WHEREAS, a native of Harvest, Alabama, William Allen Dorning was the son of Robert
Irving and Clarice Steelman Dorning; and
WHEREAS, a proud military veteran, he rendered sterling service with the United States
Air Force and was an esteemed member of American Legion Post 146; and
WHEREAS, a career law enforcement officer and member of the Tennessee Sheriff's
Association, Chief William Dorning served with the Tennessee Highway Patrol and as a Saint
Joseph Police Officer before winning election in 1994 to the post of Sheriff of Lawrence County,
where he would continue to serve until 2006; and
WHEREAS, a man of deep and abiding faith, he was a member of New Prospect Baptist
Church, which he served in numerous capacities; and
WHEREAS, Chief Dorning was deeply devoted to his family, and he always endeavored
to remain true to family values of the highest order; and
WHEREAS, a beloved family patriarch, he was the proud father of Cindy Dorning,
Christie Dorning (Donnie) White, and John (Guinn) Dorning; doting grandfather of Kelli (Chris)
Gartner, Will (Jennifer) Hutton, Ben (Kelly) Hutton, Lauren White (Brian) Arrington, Luke (Julia)
White, Catie Dorning (Brady) Crews, Carli Dorning (Jake) Clark, and Sophie Dorning; and loving
