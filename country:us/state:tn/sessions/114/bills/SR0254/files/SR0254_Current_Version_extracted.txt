SENATE RESOLUTION 254
By Walley
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Amy Wooley, on her
selection as the 2025-2026 West Grand Division
Principal of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should specially
recognize those outstanding citizens who have devoted their careers to providing the highest
quality education for Tennessee's students; and
WHEREAS, Amy Wooley is one such estimable administrator, who has distinguished
herself as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, the principal of West Chester Elementary School in Chester County, Ms.
Wooley has been selected as the 2025-2026 West Grand Division Principal of the Year, an
honor most befitting her outstanding tenure as a principal, as it recognizes the positive influence
she has had on so many young lives; and
WHEREAS, with more than thirty years of experience in education, Amy Wooley is an
extremely talented and compassionate administrator who works assiduously to motivate her
students, teachers, and staff to reach their full potential as scholars, professionals, and citizens;
and
WHEREAS, she serves as an inspiration to the faculty and staff at West Chester
Elementary School, and her friendship and counsel are highly valued by those who are
fortunate to know her; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Wooley truly epitomizes the ideal of the dedicated principal who is
wholly committed to the highest precepts of public service and quality education for all, and she
should be specially recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby honor and
congratulate Amy Wooley of West Chester Elementary School on being chosen as the 2025-
