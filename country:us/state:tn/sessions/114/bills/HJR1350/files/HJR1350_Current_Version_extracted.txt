HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1350
By Travis
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Cade Reel, 2026
Drive, Chip, and Putt National Finalist.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly are pleased to recognize and
commend those successful young athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring honor
to Tennessee and serve as exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable individual is Cade Reel of Dayton, an athlete of
extraordinary talent, skill, and dedication, who has proven himself a formidable competitor on
the golf course; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Reel, aged eleven, finished fifth in the Boys 10-11 division at the 2026
Drive, Chip, and Putt National Finals at Augusta National Golf Club on Sunday, April 5, 2026, a
feat that marks what is certain to be a long and successful golfing career for this talented young
man; and
WHEREAS, Cade Reel was one of three junior golfers representing Tennessee and one
of only eighty junior golfers in the nation competing in the finals; and
WHEREAS, his top-five finish comes after he advanced as far as the regional qualifier in
2023; through relentless determination, he continued to practice and refine his skills to
ultimately earn his place as a finalist after winning in his age group at the Pinehurst Regional
Qualifier in 2025; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Reel began playing golf only three years ago, in 2023, after viewing
videos online that piqued his interest; at the age of nine, he hit a hole-in-one at the Dayton Golf
& Country Club, of which he is a member, and since then, he has played in the Junior Tour
Winter Nationals; and

WHEREAS, Mr. Reel has achieved a high level of success in sports while evincing great
character both on and off the golf course; he has earned a reputation for his resolve and
dedication to practicing and continual improvement; and
WHEREAS, Cade Reel's notable achievement is a source of great pride for his family
and his community; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Reel's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of this General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Cade Reel of Dayton on his top-five finish at
the 2026 Drive, Chip, and Putt National Finals, wishing him much success and happiness in his
future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
