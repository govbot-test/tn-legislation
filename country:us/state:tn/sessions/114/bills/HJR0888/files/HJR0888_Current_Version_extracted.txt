HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 888
By Lamberth
A RESOLUTION to confirm the appointment of Garnett Decosimo
to the advisory board of the University of
Tennessee at Chattanooga.
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-501, established advisory boards
for the University of Tennessee, Knoxville; University of Tennessee at Martin; University of
Tennessee at Chattanooga; and University of Tennessee Health Science Center; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-501, provides that the governor
shall appoint five members to each advisory board; and
WHEREAS, Governor Bill Lee has appointed Garnett Decosimo to serve as a member
of the advisory board for the University of Tennessee at Chattanooga for a term that expires on
June 30, 2029; and
WHEREAS, Garnett Decosimo began his professional career as a certified public
accountant with KPMG in Atlanta, Georgia, before embarking on a career in public service that
saw him serve on the policy staffs of former U.S. Senators Bob Corker of Tennessee and Mike
Enzi of Wyoming; and
WHEREAS, in Tennessee, he has served as a senior policy analyst to Governor Bill Lee
and as the Director of Federal Relations in the Office of the Governor, where he was
responsible for developing and executing the State's federal policy agenda; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-501, provides that each member
appointed by the governor to the advisory board for the University of Tennessee at Chattanooga
shall be confirmed by the general assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that this body does hereby confirm the appointment of Garnett Decosimo to the
