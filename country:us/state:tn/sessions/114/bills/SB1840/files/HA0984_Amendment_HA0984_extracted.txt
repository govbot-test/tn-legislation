AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 1840* House Bill No. 2432
by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 40-2-101, is amended by adding the
following as a new subsection:
(t) Notwithstanding subsection (b), prosecution for vehicular homicide, as
prohibited by § 39-13-213, that is committed on or after July 1, 2026, shall commence
within ten (10) years from the date the offense occurs.
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.
