HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1068
By Camper
A RESOLUTION to honor the memory of Colby Jacob Palmer.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly were greatly saddened to learn of
the untimely passing of Colby Jacob Palmer; and
WHEREAS, Colby Palmer was a kind and respectful gentleman who worked assiduously
to improve the quality of life for his family, his friends, and his community in numerous
capacities; and
WHEREAS, born on July 7, 1998, in Easton, Maryland, Mr. Palmer was the beloved son
of Georgie Palmer and Sharon Palmer; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Palmer was a resident of Denton, Maryland; he attended school in
Talbot County and graduated from Easton High School in 2017; and
WHEREAS, a talented athlete throughout his years in school, Colby Palmer played
lacrosse and football, but he especially loved and excelled at wrestling, receiving numerous
medals and competing in state wrestling competitions; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Palmer was a music enthusiast and loved to dance, and he was proud
to work for his father's lawn service business; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Palmer was also wholly devoted to his family and always endeavored to
remain true to family values of the highest order; he was adored by many and maintained
special relationships with several of his friends and family members, especially his grandfather
and his brothers; and
WHEREAS, in addition to his parents, he will be sadly missed but fondly remembered by
his brothers, Rashaad Harris and Isaiah Palmer; his grandmother, Dorothy Camper; his uncles,
Bruce Camper, Sr., James (Tracy) Palmer, Tony Palmer, Robert (Dorothy) Palmer, Leonard
(Doris) Palmer, James (Barbara) Camper, and Lawrence Brummell; his aunts, Kathy Thomas,

WHEREAS, Mr. Palmer leaves behind an indelible legacy of integrity and probity in

WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
remember the bountiful life of this exceptional human being; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor the memory of Colby Jacob Palmer, reflecting fondly upon his
impeccable character and his stalwart commitment to living the examined life with courage and
conviction.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that we express our sympathy and offer our condolences
to the family and friends of Mr. Palmer.
