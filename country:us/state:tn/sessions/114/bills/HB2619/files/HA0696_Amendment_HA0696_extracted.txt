Section 1:
House Insurance 1
Amendment No. 1 to HB2619
Kumar
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting § 56-7-3909 in Section 1 and substituting:
56-7-3909.
(a) A health insurance entity that owns, operates, or otherwise controls,
including through investment interests, the whole or part of a healthcare provider in this
state shall disclose such ownership, operation, or control to a covered individual under a
health benefit plan offered by the health insurance entity before the covered individual
receives covered services from such healthcare provider or when the covered individual
is referred to such healthcare provider.
(b) A healthcare provider that owns, operates, or otherwise controls, including
through investment interests, the whole or part of the health insurance entity that
provides a covered individual's health benefit plan shall disclose such ownership,
operation, or control to the covered individual before the covered individual receives
covered services from such healthcare provider.
(c) A person that directly or indirectly owns, operates, or otherwise controls,
including through investment interests, the whole or any part of both a healthcare
provider in this state and a health insurance entity shall disclose such ownership,
operation, or control to:
(1) The commission;
(2) A policyholder of the health insurance entity; and
(3) The department of commerce and insurance.
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting Sections 6, 7, and 8 and substituting:

Section 2:
SECTION 6. All rules and regulations issued or promulgated by the department of
commerce and insurance that relate to the functions, duties, and responsibilities of the
Tennessee commission of insurance review are transferred to the commission, to be
administered and enforced by the commission on the effective date of this act.

Section 3:
SECTION 7. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it, and
applies to conduct occurring on or after that date.
