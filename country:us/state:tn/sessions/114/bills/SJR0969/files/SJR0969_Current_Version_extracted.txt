SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 969
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Jerry Nichols on being
named TCCAA Coach of the Year for men's
basketball.
WHEREAS, Jerry Nichols of Walters State has been named Tennessee Community
College Athletic Association (TCCAA) Coach of the Year in men's basketball for the second time
in three years, a most impressive feat; and
WHEREAS, Coach Nichols has expertly led the Senators to an excellent 27-1 overall
record, a perfect 15-0 record at home, and a No. 13 national ranking during the 2025-2026
season; and
WHEREAS, under Coach Nichols's leadership, the Senators claimed the TCCAA regular
season title with a 19-1 record and then proceeded to win the TCCAA Region VII Appalachian
District Tournament, thus earning a coveted berth in the 2026 NJCAA Division I Men's
Basketball Championship; and
WHEREAS, Jerry Nichols has distinguished himself as a great basketball coach,
tactician, and motivator of young men, and he should be appropriately recognized on this
special occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Jerry Nichols on being
named 2025-2026 TCCAA Coach of the Year for men's basketball and extend to him our best
wishes for much continued success on the court and in life.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
