HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1038
By Jones J
A RESOLUTION to commemorate the tenth anniversary of Gift
Horse and honor its owner, Jessica Maloan
Vastagh.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly take great pleasure in recognizing
those local business owners who contribute significantly to both the economic and artistic
aspects of their community, remaining true to their values while supporting local creators; and
WHEREAS, Jessica Maloan Vastagh is truly one such local business owner whose Gift
Horse shop in East Nashville has been going strong since August 2016; and
WHEREAS, a native of West Tennessee, Jessica Maloan Vastagh moved to Nashville
for college, studying graphic design while working at a beloved local bookstore; and
WHEREAS, she subsequently became interested in printmaking and sold her work at
craft fairs; and
WHEREAS, perceiving a void in the Nashville community for affordable places to show
and sell artwork, Jessica Maloan Vastagh helped found Porter Flea, a small outdoor craft
market; and
WHEREAS, she then decided to focus her efforts on building a permanent brick-and-
mortar store to support Nashville's many talented artists and designers through the sale of
affordable art and thoughtful gifts; and
WHEREAS, to that end, Jessica Maloan Vastagh leased space in the Shoppes on
Fatherland in August 2016 and opened Gift Horse, a shop that specializes in stationery, paper
goods, and locally made gifts; and
WHEREAS, featuring one of the largest selections of greeting cards and art prints in
Nashville, Gift Horse buys its products directly from independent artists and illustrators from
Nashville and beyond; and

WHEREAS, offering unique and affordable gifts of all kinds, Gift Horse invests in its
neighborhood by supporting local talent; and
WHEREAS, Gift Horse and Jessica Maloan Vastagh are deserving of our approbation
on this special occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we commemorate the tenth anniversary of Gift Horse and honor its owner,
Jessica Maloan Vastagh, for her ongoing support of the Nashville arts community as a local
business owner.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
