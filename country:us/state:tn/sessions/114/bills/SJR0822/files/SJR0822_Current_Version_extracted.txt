SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 822
By Stevens
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate the Westview High
School girls' soccer team on winning the 2025
TSSAA Division I-A state championship.
WHEREAS, we are pleased to specially honor and commend those successful young
athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring honor to Tennessee and serve as
exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, the Westview High School girls' soccer team of Martin achieved an
extraordinary level of success during the 2025 season, winning the TSSAA Division I-A state
championship after finishing as runner-up in 2024; and
WHEREAS, Westview High School first won the District 12 and Region 6 championships
in pursuit of their ultimate goal; and
WHEREAS, making their third consecutive appearance in the state tournament (2023-
2025), the Chargers blasted Smith County 9-0 and routed University School of Jackson 7-0 to
advance to the championship game; and
WHEREAS, in the title tilt, Westview defeated Merrol Hyde 2-0 to avenge a loss in the
2024 finals and win the 2025 state championship, completing their season with a 15-3-1 overall
record; and
WHEREAS, the Chargers were indeed a dominant team on their march to the promised
land, allowing zero goals while scoring 18 goals in the state tournament; and
WHEREAS, displaying great sportsmanship and outstanding character, both individually
and collectively, the players and coaches of the Westview High School girls' soccer team
conducted themselves in a manner befitting the State of Tennessee; and
SJR0822
013244
- 1 -
WHEREAS, the determination, commitment, talent, and teamwork required to reach this
level of success made possible a championship season, and the members of the Westview
High School girls' soccer team are some of the finest young people the Volunteer State has to
offer; and
WHEREAS, the members of the Westview High School girls' soccer team displayed
uncommon talent, determination, and perseverance in their quest for athletic excellence and
championship laurels, and it is wholly fitting that they should be commended for attaining their
lofty goals; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate the Westview High
School girls' soccer team on winning the 2025 TSSAA Division I-A state championship and
extend our best wishes for continued success in their future endeavors as athletes, scholars,
and citizens.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
