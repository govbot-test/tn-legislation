SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 979
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Terryauna Griffin on
her selection as TCCAA Player of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should recognize those successful student athletes who
have distinguished themselves as leaders among their peers; and
WHEREAS, Terryauna Griffin of the Walters State Community College Lady Senators
was recently named Tennessee Community College Athletic Association (TCCAA) Player of the
Year in recognition of her outstanding 2025-2026 basketball season; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Griffin was also named TCCAA Offensive Player of the Year and First
Team All-TCCAA after leading the conference in scoring, averaging 21.7 points per game and
totaling 563 points in 26 games; and
WHEREAS, she ranked third nationally in points per game and recorded 54 three-
pointers and 71 steals during the season; and
WHEREAS, on February 18, 2026, Terryauna Griffin broke the Walters State all-time
scoring record and currently has accumulated more than 1,100 career points; and
WHEREAS, she scored in double digits in 24 of 26 games this season, including twelve
games of 20 or more points and eight games of 30 or more points; and
WHEREAS, Terryauna Griffin exemplifies the spirit, determination, and perseverance for
which Tennesseans are notable, and she is richly deserving of our approbation on this special
occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Terryauna Griffin on her
