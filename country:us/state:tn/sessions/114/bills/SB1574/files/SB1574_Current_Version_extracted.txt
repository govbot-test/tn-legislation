Section 1:
SENATE BILL 1574
By Lowe
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 2;
Title 3; Title 4; Title 5; Title 6; Title 7; Title 8 and
Title 12, relative to lobbying.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 3-6-302(a), is amended by deleting
"seven (7) days" wherever it appears in the subsection and substituting instead "seven (7)
business days".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 3-6-302(c), is amended by deleting
"seven (7) days" and substituting instead "seven (7) business days".

Section 4:
SECTION 3. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
