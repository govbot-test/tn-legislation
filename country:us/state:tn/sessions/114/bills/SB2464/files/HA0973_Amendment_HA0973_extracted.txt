SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 8, Chapter 7, Part 3, is amended by
adding the following language as a new section:
(a) Effective July 1, 2026, for the protection of the people of this state and in
furtherance of public safety, there are created one hundred eighteen (118) additional
assistant district attorney general positions, thirty-three (33) additional assistant victim
witness coordinator positions, twenty-three (23) criminal secretary positions, twelve (12)
criminal investigator positions, thirty-two (32) DUI coordinator positions, one (1)
administrative assistant - DUI position, one (1) network engineer position, one (1)
software engineer position, one (1) computer support specialist position, one (1) human
resources specialist position, one (1) fiscal accounting clerk position, ten (10) paralegal
positions, twelve (12) legal intern positions, and two (2) public information officer
positions.
(b)
(1) At least one (1) new assistant district attorney general in each district
must be trained and focus on the prevention and prosecution of violent crime and
those crimes related to and stemming from violent crime.
(2) At least one (1) new assistant district attorney general in each district
must be trained and focus on the prevention and prosecution of human trafficking
offenses and those crimes related to and stemming from human trafficking.
