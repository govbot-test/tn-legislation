SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 616
By Johnson
A RESOLUTION to provide for a recess in the proceedings of the
One Hundred Fourteenth General Assembly
because of forecasted inclement weather.
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that the One Hundred Fourteenth General Assembly
stand in recess at the close of business in both houses on Thursday, January 22, 2026, and:
(1) The Senate, because of forecasted inclement weather, reconvene on
Monday, February 2, 2026, at 5:00 p.m. (CST); and
(2) The House of Representatives reconvene on Wednesday, January 28, 2026,
at 10:00 a.m. (CST); provided, that in the case of inclement weather and upon written
notice of the Speaker of the House of Representatives, the House of Representatives
reconvene on Monday, February 2, 2026, at 5:00 p.m. (CST).
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that pursuant to Article II, Section 16, of the Constitution
of Tennessee, the House of Representatives, by its concurrence in this resolution, consents to
this recess.
