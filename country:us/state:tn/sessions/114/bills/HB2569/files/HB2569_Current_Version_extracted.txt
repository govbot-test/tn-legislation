Section 1:
By Haile
HOUSE BILL 2569
By Kumar
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 68,
relative to immunization.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 68-11-266, is amended by deleting
the section and substituting:
In accordance with the latest recommendations of the advisory committee on
immunization practices of the federal centers for disease control and prevention, unless
contraindicated and subject to the availability of vaccine, a hospital shall:
(1) Offer an inpatient who is fifty (50) years of age or older immunization
against influenza disease each year from October 1 through March 1 prior to
discharging the inpatient; and
(2) Offer an inpatient who is fifty (50) years of age or older immunization
against pneumococcal disease prior to discharging the inpatient.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.
