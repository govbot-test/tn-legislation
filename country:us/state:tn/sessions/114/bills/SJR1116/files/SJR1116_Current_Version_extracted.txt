SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1116
By Johnson
A RESOLUTION to commend and congratulate Todd Moore,
National HealthCare Corporation Administrator
of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is our privilege to recognize those working in the healthcare field whose
knowledge, ingenuity, and dedication contribute greatly to the health and well-being of the
citizens of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such individual is Todd Moore, who was recently named the 2025
National HealthCare Corporation (NHC) Administrator of the Year, a most befitting honor that
recognizes his many contributions to health care; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Moore has served as Administrator at NHC Cool Springs since July
2019; and
WHEREAS, NHC is a senior care company based in Murfreesboro with eighty
healthcare centers that operates more than 10,300 beds nationally and provides assisted living,
home care, hospice, mental health, pharmacy, insurance, and management services; and
WHEREAS, an employee of NHC since 1995, Todd Moore earned a Bachelor of
Science degree from Lipscomb University in 1993 and began his healthcare career as a
dietitian; additionally, he is a graduate of NHC's Administrator in Training Program; and
WHEREAS, under Mr. Moore's leadership, NHC Cool Springs was designated an NHC
5-Star Center for Excellence in 2025 and had a Consumer View score of 96.83 percent, a
partner retention rate of 65.28 percent, a net patient satisfaction score of 82 percent, a weight-
loss rate of 3.40 percent, and a pressure ulcer rate of 0.18 percent; and
WHEREAS, in 2020, NHC Cool Springs received the Bronze National Quality Award
from the American Health Care Association and National Center for Assisted Living; it has also

been a finalist for and won numerous NHC Honor Club and Legends Awards in previous years;
and
WHEREAS, throughout his many successes, Todd Moore has enjoyed the love and
support of his wife, Carrie; they are the proud parents of four children, Henry, Jake, Amelia, and
Ethan; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Moore has proven himself a leader in his profession, and the patients
and partners of NHC Cool Springs have benefited greatly from his guidance and service; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we commend and congratulate Todd Moore,
National HealthCare Corporation Administrator of the Year, extending our best wishes for his
continued success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
