House State & Local Government 1
Amendment No. 1 to HB0308
Crawford
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 337 House Bill No. 308*
by adding the following to the end of § 67-6-715(a) in the amendatory language of Section 1:
The county shall furnish a certified copy of the adopted resolution to the department of
revenue. The tax becomes effective on the first day of the calendar month occurring at
least one hundred eighty (180) days after the department receives the certified copy.
AND FURTHER AMEND by adding the following as a new subsection (c) in Section 1 and
redesignating the existing subsection (c) accordingly:
(c) A resolution levying a tax under this section does not become effective until
approved in an election held pursuant to § 67-6-706.
