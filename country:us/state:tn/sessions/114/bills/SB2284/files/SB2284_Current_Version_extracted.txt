Section 1:
By Crawford
SENATE BILL 2284
By Briggs
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 5;
Title 6; Title 7 and Title 67, relative to tourism.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-69-109(c), is amended by adding
the following to the end of the subsection:
Each authority shall also file the annual report of its business affairs and
transactions with the department of tourist development.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
