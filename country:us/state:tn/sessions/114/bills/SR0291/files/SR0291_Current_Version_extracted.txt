SENATE RESOLUTION 291
By Oliver
A RESOLUTION to honor keynote speaker Maxine Waters and
the Nashville Branch of the NAACP during the
2026 Freedom Fund Banquet.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should salute those venerable citizens and stalwart
organizations who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as true
leaders most worthy of our respect and approbation; and
WHEREAS, one such noteworthy individual is United States Congresswoman Maxine
Waters, who will be the keynote speaker at the 2026 Freedom Fund Banquet hosted by the
Nashville Branch of the NAACP; and
WHEREAS, the Freedom Fund Banquet will commemorate 106 years of service,
advocacy, and progress in the fight for civil rights and equity, bringing together community
leaders, advocates, partners, and supporters for an evening centered on reflection, recognition,
and renewed commitment to justice; and
WHEREAS, proceeds from the Freedom Fund Banquet will directly support the NAACP
Nashville Branch's programs and initiatives focused on advocacy, education, economic
empowerment, and civic engagement throughout Middle Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, as the event's keynote speaker, United States Representative (D-CA)
Maxine Waters will share insight, experience, and perspective shaped by decades of public
service and leadership; and
WHEREAS, Congresswoman Waters is a true inspiration, and her speech will certainly
be a highlight of the 2026 Freedom Fund Banquet; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and commend

Congresswoman Maxine Waters and the Nashville Branch of the NAACP, extending our best
wishes for great success during the 2026 Freedom Fund Banquet.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
