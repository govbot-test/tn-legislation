deleting "made orally, in writing, or otherwise" and substituting "made orally, on a social media

Section 4:
SECTION 3. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 55-10-501, is amended by deleting
subdivision (2) and substituting:
(2) "Participant" means a person who operates a motor vehicle upon the public
highways of this state, or that of any municipality or political subdivision thereof, for the
purpose of drag racing, and also any person who arranges for, organizes, promotes,
supervises, or in any way and manner sets in motion any drag racing, regardless of
whether or not such person may be the operator of, a passenger in, or a spectator of any
motor vehicle participating in drag racing; and

( ) "Drifting" means the act of steering a vehicle in a turn in an attempt to make

uncontrolled skid sideways;

Section 6:
SECTION 5. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 55-10-502(a), is amended by
deleting "shopping center, trailer park" and substituting "shopping center, commercial property,
trailer park".

Section 7:
SECTION 6. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 55-10-502, is amended by deleting
subsection (c) and substituting:
(c) A motor vehicle used to commit the offense of drag racing or to flee after the
commission of the offense of drag racing is:
(1) Subject to being impounded for the purpose of collecting evidence
pertaining to the offense. All towing and impounding fees must be assessed
against the person charged with a violation of subsection (a); and
(2) Upon conviction for the offense, subject to seizure and forfeiture as
provided in title 40, chapter 33, part 1.

Section 8:
SECTION 7. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 55-10-502, is amended by adding
the following as a new subsection:
(d) A law enforcement officer who has arrested a person for the commission of
the offense of drag racing shall not issue a citation to the arrested person in lieu of the
continued custody and the taking of the arrested person before a magistrate.

Section 9:
SECTION 8. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 39-13-103(b), is amended by adding
the following new subdivision (b)(5) and redesignating the current subdivision (b)(5) as
subdivision (b)(6):
(5) Reckless endangerment by drag racing, as defined in § 55-10-501, is a Class
E felony.

Section 10:
SECTION 9. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.
