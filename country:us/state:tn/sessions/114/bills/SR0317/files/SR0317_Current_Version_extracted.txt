SENATE RESOLUTION 317
By Kyle
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Jackson Roberts of
St. Benedict at Auburndale on finishing as a
finalist in the TSSAA Division II boys' individual
bowling state championship.
WHEREAS, we take great pleasure in recognizing those dedicated student athletes who
serve as the heart and soul of their schools and communities while excelling in competition; and
WHEREAS, one such outstanding individual is Jackson Roberts of St. Benedict at
Auburndale, who recently finished as a finalist in the TSSAA Division II boys' individual bowling
state championship tournament; and
WHEREAS, recording 1,370 pins and a 228.3 average in the semi-final round, Mr.
Roberts bested forty-seven bowlers to finish first and advance to the state championship match;
and
WHEREAS, Jackson Roberts epitomizes all that is good in today's student athlete, as he
has achieved a premier level of success in competition while exhibiting the highest degree of
character in all aspects of life; and
WHEREAS, the perseverance, determination, work ethic, and talent of this young man
not only made possible an outstanding season but also surely bode well for his future success;
and
WHEREAS, Mr. Roberts has distinguished himself as a dominant bowler of whom we
can all be proud, and he is most worthy of our approbation at this time; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and congratulate
Jackson Roberts of St. Benedict at Auburndale on finishing as a finalist in the TSSAA Division II
boys' individual bowling state championship and wish him all the best in his future endeavors.
