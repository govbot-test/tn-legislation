HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1318
By Reneau
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Firefighter Etta Schafer
of the Signal Mountain Fire Department for her
heroism.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should honor those
individuals who, during the course of their duties, endeavor to protect the lives of their fellow
citizens, braving great dangers and putting themselves at risk to save others; and
WHEREAS, Firefighter Etta Schafer of the Signal Mountain Fire Department Green Shift
was truly heroic in her response to an emergency situation on October 15, 2025, helping to save
the life of a citizen in peril; and
WHEREAS, on that date, Firefighter Schafer and her fellow crew members responded to
a cardiac arrest at a local residence, where immediate and decisive action was required to
preserve life; and
WHEREAS, through their exceptional training and coordinated efforts, the team provided
critical care to the patient, stabilizing her condition and ensuring she received the urgent
medical attention necessary for survival; and
WHEREAS, as a result of these efforts, the patient was released from the hospital just
days later with a full recovery and no neurological deficits; and
WHEREAS, Firefighter Etta Schafer's quick thinking and compassionate action were
invaluable in helping to bring about a positive outcome in a potentially deadly situation; and
WHEREAS, it is wholly fitting that we recognize Firefighter Etta Schafer for her
exceptional act of heroism; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor Firefighter Etta Schafer of the Signal Mountain Fire Department
