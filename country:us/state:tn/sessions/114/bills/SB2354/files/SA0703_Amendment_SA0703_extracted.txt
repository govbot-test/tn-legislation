Senate Energy, Ag., and Nat. Resources 1
Amendment No. 1 to SB2354
Reeves
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 2354* House Bill No. 2499
by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 70-4-404, is amended by adding the
following as a new subsection:
Notwithstanding subsection (d), a raccoon may be possessed without the
payment of a personal possession permit fee if the raccoon is:
(1) Obtained from a legal source and has been vaccinated;
(2) Non-native and was not captured from the wild; and
(3) A possession permit for the raccoon has been issued by the agency.
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.
