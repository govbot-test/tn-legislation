HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 919
By Lamberth
A RESOLUTION to provide for a recess in the proceedings of the
One Hundred Fourteenth General Assembly.
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that the One Hundred Fourteenth General Assembly stand in recess at the
close of business in both houses on Thursday, February 12, 2026, and the House of
Representatives reconvene on Thursday, February 19, 2026, at 9:00 a.m. (CST) and the
Senate reconvene on Thursday, February 19, 2026, at 8:30 a.m. (CST).
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that pursuant to Article II, Section 16, of the Constitution
of Tennessee, the Senate, by its concurrence in this resolution, consents to this recess.
HJR0919
