SENATE RESOLUTION 274
By Walley
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Fire Chief Kenny Newell
on the occasion of his retirement from the
Adamsville Fire Department.
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that we should honor those citizens who have performed
with uncommon devotion and enthusiasm throughout their years of public service; and
WHEREAS, Fire Chief Kenny Newell is one such estimable individual, who has served
with distinction as the chief of the Adamsville Fire Department and who is taking his much-
deserved retirement this year; and
WHEREAS, during his distinguished tenure, Fire Chief Newell has served the good
people of Adamsville with a great deal of pride, always doing his utmost to bring honor, integrity,
and compassionate service to his office; and
WHEREAS, firefighting has been a way of life for Fire Chief Kenny Newell, and he has
wholly devoted himself to serving his community, earning the admiration and respect of his
colleagues and a grateful public; and
WHEREAS, Fire Chief Newell exemplifies the spirit and allegiance to family and
community that are characteristic of a true Tennessean; and
WHEREAS, he has demonstrated the utmost professionalism, ability, and integrity while
serving and protecting the residents of Adamsville, and it is most fitting that he be honored on
this special occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we recognize and commend
Fire Chief Kenny Newell on his exemplary career with the Adamsville Fire Department,
extending our gratitude for his many years of devoted service and our best wishes for much
continued success and happiness during his retirement.
