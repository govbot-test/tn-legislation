HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 882
By Lamberth
A RESOLUTION to confirm the appointment of Dr. Tyler Forrest to
the heritage conservation trust fund board of
trustees.
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 11-7-104(a), provides for the creation
of the heritage conservation trust fund board of trustees; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 11-7-104(b), provides that the
governor shall appoint eleven members to the board; and
WHEREAS, Governor Bill Lee has appointed Dr. Tyler Forrest as a member of the
heritage conservation trust fund board of trustees representing East Tennessee to a term that
will expire on October 31, 2026; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Tyler Forrest is the President of Tennessee Wesleyan University (TWU)
and a seasoned higher-education executive with experience in land management, conservation,
and public service; and
WHEREAS, his leadership at TWU has been marked by strategic growth and resource
stewardship; and
WHEREAS, Tyler Forrest holds a Ph.D. in Learning and Leadership, a Master of
Business Administration degree, and a Bachelor of Science degree in Finance from the
University of Tennessee at Chattanooga, where he also served as Vice Chancellor for Finance
and Administration; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Forrest has hands-on experience with land preservation and
redevelopment, having led the transformation of TWU's Kilbride Nature Sanctuary in Rhea
County into a grassland sanctuary, spearheaded the redevelopment of the former Ingleside
School property in Athens into a student and community recreation area, and managed a 120-

WHEREAS, Dr. Forrest's commitment to conservation is complemented by his service

WHEREAS, Dr. Tyler Forrest is a person of integrity and an active citizen whose
benevolence, acumen, and strong commitment to public service have helped to enhance the
well-being of this State and its citizens; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 11-7-104(b)(2), provides that each
member appointed by the governor to the heritage conservation trust fund board of trustees
shall be confirmed by joint resolution of the general assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that this body confirms the appointment of Dr. Tyler Forrest to the heritage
conservation trust fund board of trustees for a term that will expire on October 31, 2026.
