SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 627
By Johnson
A RESOLUTION to confirm the appointment of Dr. Charles Doug
Haney to the advisory board for the University of
Tennessee Southern.
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-501, established advisory boards
for the University of Tennessee, Knoxville; University of Tennessee at Martin; University of
Tennessee at Chattanooga; University of Tennessee Health Science Center; and University of
Tennessee Southern; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-501, provides that the governor
shall appoint five members to each advisory board; and
WHEREAS, Governor Bill Lee has appointed Dr. Charles Doug Haney to serve as a
member of the advisory board for the University of Tennessee Southern for a term that expires
on June 30, 2029; and
WHEREAS, the owner of Physicians and Surgeons, Inc., Dr. Doug Haney is affiliated
with Southern Tennessee Regional Health System, Pulaski; he serves on the boards of First
Pulaski National Corporation, the First National Bank of Pulaski, and the University of
Tennessee Foundation; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Haney attended Martin Methodist College before graduating from the
University of Tennessee, Knoxville, with a bachelor's degree; he then attended the University of
Tennessee's Health Science Center College of Medicine, graduating with a Doctor of Medicine
in 1979, specializing in family medicine; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-501, provides that each member
appointed by the governor to the advisory board for the University of Tennessee Southern shall
be confirmed by the general assembly; now, therefore,
SJR0627
011625
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby confirm the appointment of Dr. Charles
Doug Haney to the advisory board for the University of Tennessee Southern for a term that
expires on June 30, 2029.
