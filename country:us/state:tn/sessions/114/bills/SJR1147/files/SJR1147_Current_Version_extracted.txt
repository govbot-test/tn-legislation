SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1147
By Pody
A RESOLUTION to honor MJ Lucas, the Best DJ in Small Market
Radio.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly take great pleasure in honoring a
radio professional who is celebrating forty years in the industry; and
WHEREAS, MJ Lucas has represented the heart of local broadcasting throughout her
tenure at WANT FM/WCOR AM radio in Wilson County; and
WHEREAS, for the past three decades, MJ Lucas has been the warm and familiar
morning voice of WANT/WCOR, where she earned renown for her cheerful greeting, "Get out of
bed, sleepy head"; and
WHEREAS, she has forged the station's identity as its music and public service director
while creating community favorites such as the Birthday Club and Adopt-A-Pet; and
WHEREAS, during her time off the air, MJ Lucas has distinguished herself as a stalwart
public servant through her support of local organizations, her volunteer service as an emcee at
charity events, and her advocacy for survivors of child abuse; and
WHEREAS, a native of Florida, MJ Lucas began her broadcasting career at WMOP in
Ocala before moving to Middle Tennessee, where she become a beloved local radio
personality; and
WHEREAS, MJ Lucas's professional career is marked by community service and a deep
connection to her community, and she is richly deserving of our approbation; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend MJ Lucas as the Best DJ in

Small Market Radio, recognize her forty-year career as an authentic and engaging local radio
personality, and extend to her our best wishes for much continued success and happiness.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
