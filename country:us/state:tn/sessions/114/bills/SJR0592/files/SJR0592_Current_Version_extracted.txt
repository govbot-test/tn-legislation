SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 592
By Harshbarger
A RESOLUTION to designate March 12, 2026, as "Be the One to
End Suicide Day" in Tennessee.
WHEREAS, on June 1, 2023, the American Legion began an initiative called Be The
One with the mission to end veteran suicide; and
WHEREAS, the American Legion Department of Tennessee's Be The One Commission
to End Veterans Suicide, with a motto of "Veterans Offering Lifesaving Service," was launched
on August 16, 2025, with one of its missions to end veteran suicide; and
WHEREAS, nationally, approximately twenty-two veterans a day commit suicide; and
WHEREAS, suicide is the second leading cause of death for children and young adults
aged ten to twenty-four, behind unintentional injuries, with an estimated 6,500 individuals in that
demographic taking their own lives every year; and
WHEREAS, the suicide rate in Tennessee is 14.7 percent above the national rate,
according to 2025 data from the Tennessee Department of Health; and
WHEREAS, the American Legion Department of Tennessee is committed to end suicide
in every demographic from the young to the old alike; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby designate March 12, 2026, as "Be The
One to End Suicide Day" in the Volunteer State and urge public and private entities across
Tennessee's ninety-five counties to hold suicide prevention training annually, promote the 988
suicide prevention hotline, and display awareness messaging in appropriate locations.
