HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1065
By Martin G
A RESOLUTION to recognize Notre Dame High School on the
occasion of its 150th anniversary.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
recognize those exemplary educational institutions that have made significant contributions,
through an abiding commitment to excellence, to their respective communities and to this State;
and
WHEREAS, one such noteworthy institution is Notre Dame High School of Chattanooga,
which is celebrating its 150th anniversary of operation this year; and
WHEREAS, Notre Dame High School was founded in January 1876 by the Dominican
Sisters of the St. Cecilia community in Nashville, who opened the parish school in Chattanooga;
since its inception 150 years ago, Notre Dame has continued to develop high school students
into motivated, prepared, and compassionate young adults; and
WHEREAS, a top-rated, coeducational private high school, Notre Dame cultivates each
student's strengths and Christian faith while nurturing self-confidence to ensure each student is
prepared to be their personal best and make a positive difference in the world; and
WHEREAS, under the astute leadership of Head of School Dr. Eric Schexnaildre, Notre
Dame High School has held to its mission and values for 150 years, fully developing each
student in mind, body, and soul; sharing the Catholic faith in an environment that fosters
spiritual growth and academic excellence; and embracing religious, academic, cultural, and
economic diversity; and
WHEREAS, the 150th anniversary of Notre Dame High School offers an ideal opportunity
to honor its distinguished history, its enduring role in the Chattanooga community, and the
