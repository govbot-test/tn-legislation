Section 1:
By Yarbro
HOUSE BILL 2435
By McKenzie
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 10,
Chapter 1, Part 3 and Title 49, Chapter 6, relative
to the Tennessee Electronic Library.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-6-3803, is amended by adding
the following new subsection:
(j) This section does not apply to any materials in the Tennessee Electronic
Library created in § 10-1-301.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
