SECTION 9. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 68-11-204, is amended by adding
the following as a new subsection:
(e)
(1) A person or entity shall not establish or operate a satellite emergency
department unless the satellite emergency department is licensed as an
outpatient department of a hospital licensed under this title.
(2) A person or entity shall not initiate or provide cardiac catheterization
services unless the facility providing such services is licensed as an outpatient
department of a hospital licensed under this title.
(3) Subdivision (e)(2) does not apply to a facility providing cardiac
catheterization services that was in operation as of the effective date of this act.
SECTION 10. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 68-11-204(a)(1), is amended by

"hospitals".
SECTION 13. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 68-11-202(a)(1), is amended by
