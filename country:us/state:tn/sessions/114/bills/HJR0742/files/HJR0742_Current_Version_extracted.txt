HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 742
By Lamberth
A RESOLUTION to call a joint convention of the Senate and
House of Representatives for the purpose of
hearing a State of the State address by
Governor Bill Lee and to authorize transmittal of
the budget document and legislation
implementing the budget for fiscal year 2026-
2027.
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 3-1-108, provides for a joint
convention of the Senate and House of Representatives to be called annually for the purpose of
hearing a State of the State address by the governor; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that the Senate and House of Representatives meet in joint convention on
Monday, February 2, 2026, at 5:45 p.m. (CST) in the chamber of the House of Representatives
for the purpose of hearing the State of the State address by the Honorable Bill Lee, Governor of
Tennessee.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that Governor Lee be invited to address the joint
convention on Monday, February 2, 2026, at 6:00 p.m. (CST) in the chamber of the House of
Representatives.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that pursuant to Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 9-
4-5105, the General Assembly authorizes the transmittal of the state budget document or
documents for fiscal year 2026-2027 prior to the close of business in both houses on Monday,
February 2, 2026.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that pursuant to Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 9-
4-5105, the General Assembly authorizes the transmittal and filing of the legislation necessary
to give legal sanction to the budget for fiscal year 2026-2027 as follows:
