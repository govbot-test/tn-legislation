HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 788
By Martin G
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Gary Jared on the
occasion of his retirement.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
honor those individuals who have answered a divine call to the ministry and have served with
dedication, vigor, and enthusiasm to perform God's work for their congregations and
communities; and
WHEREAS, one such individual is Gary Jared, senior pastor of Stuart Heights Baptist
Church, who took his much-deserved retirement on December 31, 2025, following thirty-five
years of devout service in that role; and
WHEREAS, Gary Jared has been an influential figure in religious ministry and always
followed the direction of the good Lord; he delivered his final sermon as senior pastor of Stuart
Heights Baptist Church on Sunday, December 28, 2025; and
WHEREAS, under his astute leadership as pastor, Stuart Heights Baptist Church grew
from one location and approximately two dozen members to three locations and 1,000 members
and hosted the Easter at Coolidge Program for fifteen years; and
WHEREAS, during his retirement, Pastor Jared plans to remain an active member of
Stuart Heights Baptist Church and will serve as pastor emeritus; and
WHEREAS, he earned a bachelor's degree, a seminary degree, and an honorary
doctorate degree from Temple University; and
WHEREAS, his professional accomplishments aside, Pastor Jared is a family-oriented
and selfless gentleman, who is most grateful for the love and support of his beloved wife,
Nancy, their four daughters, and several cherished grandchildren; and

WHEREAS, Gary Jared has continued to demonstrate excellence in his calling as a man
of God; the members of his congregation have been proud to call him their pastor, leader, and
friend; and
WHEREAS, a true shepherd of the flock, Gary Jared has an abiding love for the ministry;
his many contributions to his church, his community, and this State are deeply appreciated, and
it is wholly fitting that he be recognized on this special occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Gary Jared on the occasion of his retirement,
thank him for his meritorious service as senior pastor of Stuart Heights Baptist Church, and
extend to him our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
