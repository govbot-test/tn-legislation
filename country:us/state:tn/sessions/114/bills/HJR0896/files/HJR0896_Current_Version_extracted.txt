HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 896
By Lamberth
A RESOLUTION to confirm the appointment of Alec Richardson to
the advisory board for the University of
Tennessee at Martin.
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-501, established an advisory
board for the University of Tennessee at Martin; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-501, provides that the governor
shall appoint five (5) members to such advisory board; and
WHEREAS, Governor Bill Lee has appointed Alec Richardson to the advisory board for
the University of Tennessee at Martin for a term that will expire on June 30, 2029; and
WHEREAS, Alec Richardson, a native of Linden, holds a bachelor's degree in education
from the University of Tennessee at Martin; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Richardson currently serves as state director for U.S. Senator Bill
Hagerty, having previously served for seven years in Governor Lee's administration, most
recently as a senior advisor member of the Governor's Cabinet; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-501, provides that each member
appointed by the governor to the advisory board for the University of Tennessee at Martin shall
be confirmed by the general assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby confirm the appointment of Alec Richardson to the advisory
board for the University of Tennessee at Martin for a term that expires on June 30, 2029.
