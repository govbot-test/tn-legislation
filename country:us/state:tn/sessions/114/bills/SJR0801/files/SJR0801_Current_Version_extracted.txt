SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 801
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Taren Claridy of
Greeneville High School on being named to the
TSWA Class 4A All-State football team.
WHEREAS, we are pleased to recognize and commend those successful high school
athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring honor to Tennessee and serve as
exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable individual is Taren Claridy of Greeneville High School,
an athlete of extraordinary talent, skill, and dedication, who has proven himself a formidable
competitor on the football field; and
WHEREAS, the Tennessee Sports Writers Association (TSWA) recently named Taren
Claridy to the 2025 Class 4A All-State football team as a defensive back in recognition of his
excellence on the gridiron for Greeneville High School; and
WHEREAS, an important contributor on both sides of the football, Taren Claridy was
also honored this season as Region 1-4A Athlete of the Year; and
WHEREAS, Taren Claridy epitomizes all that is good in today's student athlete, as he
has achieved a high level of success in sports while evincing great character both on and off the
field; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Claridy's notable achievement is a source of great pride for his family,
his community, and his school, and it is appropriate that we recognize this accomplished
athlete; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Taren Claridy of
SJR0801

Greeneville High School on being named to the Tennessee Sports Writers Association's Class
4A All-State football team, wishing him much success and happiness in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
