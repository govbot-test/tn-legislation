HOUSE RESOLUTION 159
By Bulso
A RESOLUTION approving the amendments and revisions to the
Tennessee Rules of Criminal Procedure,
promulgated by the Supreme Court of
Tennessee pursuant to the provisions of
Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 16, Chapter 3,
Part 4.
WHEREAS, on January 14, 2026, the Supreme Court of Tennessee, acting pursuant to
Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 16, Chapter 3, Part 4, filed an order promulgating certain
amendments and revisions to the Tennessee Rules of Criminal Procedure, previously
promulgated and adopted by the Court; and
WHEREAS, on January 21, 2026, a certified copy of such order, together with a certified
copy of the proposed amendments and revisions to the Tennessee Rules of Criminal
Procedure, was reported and filed with the General Assembly by the Chief Justice for approval
by a resolution of both houses; and
WHEREAS, such amendments and revisions to the Tennessee Rules of Criminal
Procedure have been duly considered by the House of Representatives of the One Hundred
Fourteenth General Assembly of the State of Tennessee; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that the
amendments and revisions to the Tennessee Rules of Criminal Procedure, as promulgated and
adopted by the Supreme Court in its order dated January 14, 2026, a copy of which was filed
with the Clerks of the House of Representatives and Senate on January 21, 2026, as aforesaid,
are hereby ratified and approved.
