HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1012
By Boyd
A RESOLUTION to honor the memory of Jayden Bailey of Wilson
County.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly were greatly saddened to learn of
the untimely passing of Jayden Bailey of Wilson County; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Bailey was a true gentleman, a courageous hero, and a beautiful soul
who was a blessing to all who were fortunate to know him; and
WHEREAS, a junior at Lebanon High School, Jayden Bailey was an inspiring basketball
player for the Blue Devils, "with an infectious smile, a huge personality, and manners that would
make his mom proud"; and
WHEREAS, diagnosed with cancer during middle school, Jayden chose to live life to the
fullest and continued to play the sport he loved until the very end; he was truly beloved by his
friends and family, who remember him as a bright light whose positivity was contagious; and
WHEREAS, Jayden Bailey lived with courage, compassion, and enthusiasm, and his
kind spirit will continue to serve as an inspiration for others; and
WHEREAS, it is most fitting that we honor the life of this esteemed gentleman at this
time; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor the memory of Jayden Bailey of Wilson County, expressing our
deepest sympathy and offering our condolences to his family.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
