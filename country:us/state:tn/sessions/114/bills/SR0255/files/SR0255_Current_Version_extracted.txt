SENATE RESOLUTION 255
By Walley
A RESOLUTION to honor Caleb Murphy of Hardin County High
School on his participation in the West
Tennessee All-Star Football Game.
WHEREAS, the State of Tennessee has long treasured and immortalized the dynamic
exploits of its high school gridiron greats; and
WHEREAS, one such outstanding football player is Caleb Murphy of Hardin County High
School, who recently participated in the 2025 Sports Plus West Tennessee All-Star Football
Game held at Crockett County High School in Alamo on December 12, 2025; and
WHEREAS, displaying his exceptional athletic skills, Mr. Murphy started at left tackle for
the North team and played the entire game; and
WHEREAS, high school football is an essential part of Tennessee's heritage and
lifestyle, and this young man, who exemplifies the ideal of the devoted student athlete, is most
worthy of our approbation; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor Caleb Murphy of
Hardin County High School upon participating in the 2025 Sports Plus West Tennessee All-Star
Football Game, applaud his athletic prowess, and extend to him our best wishes for every
success in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
