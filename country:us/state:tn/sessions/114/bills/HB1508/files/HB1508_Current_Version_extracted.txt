Section 1:
By Hensley
HOUSE BILL 1508
By Warner
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 38
and Title 39, relative to enhanced handgun carry
permits.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 39-17-1351, is amended by deleting
subdivision (x)(5)(B) and substituting:
(B)
(i) Served for at least ten (10) years prior to retirement and was POST-
certified, or had equivalent training, on the date the officer retired; or
(ii) Served less than ten (10) years prior to retirement, was POST-
certified, or had equivalent training, on the date the officer retired, and retired
from service due to an injury received in the line of duty as a law enforcement
officer;

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
