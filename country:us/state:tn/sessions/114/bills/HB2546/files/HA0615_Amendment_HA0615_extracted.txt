Amendment No. 1 to HB2546
Crawford

AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 1886* House Bill No. 2546
by deleting SECTION 1 and substituting:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 4-3-5404(d), is amended by deleting
the subsection and substituting:
(d) The commissioner shall call the first meeting of the board after April 4, 2019.
At the first meeting, and at the first meeting held every two (2) years thereafter, the
board shall elect from its members:
(1) A chair, vice chair, and any other officers deemed necessary; and
(2)
(A) An executive committee composed of six (6) members,
including the:
(i) Commissioner;
(ii) Chair of the board;
(iii) Vice chair of the board; and
(iv) One (1) member representing each grand division of
this state.
(B) The commissioner shall serve as chair of the executive
committee.
