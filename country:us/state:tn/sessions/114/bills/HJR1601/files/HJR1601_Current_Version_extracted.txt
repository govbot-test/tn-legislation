HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1601
By Capley
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Tinslee Brewer, 2026
Salutatorian at Collinwood High School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Tinslee Brewer is one such exceptional student, who was recently named
Salutatorian of the 2026 graduating class at Collinwood High School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Ms. Brewer has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in her remarkable high school career, Tinslee Brewer has earned an
exemplary 98.444 grade point average while participating in numerous extracurricular activities;
and
WHEREAS, her display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well for her
future success pursuing a Bachelor of Science degree in Education to become a middle school
science teacher; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Brewer's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for
her parents, Shannon Brewer and Erin Risner, her school, and her community; and
WHEREAS, Tinslee Brewer's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of our
respect, admiration, and commendation; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE

CONCURRING, that we commend and honor the 2026 Collinwood High School Salutatorian,
Tinslee Brewer, on her impressive academic achievement and extend to her our best wishes for
