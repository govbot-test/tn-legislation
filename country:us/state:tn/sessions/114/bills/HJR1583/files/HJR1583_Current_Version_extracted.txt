HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1583
By Capley
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Carter Dunn, 2026 Top
Ten Senior of Mt. Pleasant High School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Carter Dunn is one such exceptional student, who was recently named a
Top Ten Senior of the 2026 graduating class at Mt. Pleasant High School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Mr. Dunn has achieved outstanding academic success
and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country; and
WHEREAS, in his remarkable high school career, Carter Dunn has earned an exemplary
grade point average while participating in numerous extracurricular activities; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Dunn's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well for
his future success; and
WHEREAS, his noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for his family,
his school, and his community; and
WHEREAS, Carter Dunn's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of the members of this General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor Carter Dunn on being named a 2026 Mt.
Pleasant High School Top Ten Senior, salute his impressive academic achievement, and
extend to him our best wishes for a happy, successful future.
HJR1583
018050
- 1 -
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
