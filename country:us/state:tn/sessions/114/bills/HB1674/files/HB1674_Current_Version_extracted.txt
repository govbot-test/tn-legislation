Section 1:
By Harshbarger
HOUSE BILL 1674
By Stinnett
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Section
67-1-202(a)(1), relative to duties of the division of
property assessments.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 67-1-202, is amended by deleting
subdivision (a)(1) and substituting instead:
(1) Supervise and direct all reappraisals and revaluation programs;

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
