HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 876
By Vital
A RESOLUTION to honor the memory of Sherry Lee Howard
Hoppe.
WHEREAS, we were greatly saddened to learn of the passing of Sherry Lee Howard
Hoppe; and
WHEREAS, Mrs. Hoppe was an exemplary public servant and consummate professional
who worked assiduously to improve the quality of life for her fellow citizens in numerous
capacities; and
WHEREAS, Sherry Hoppe was born on July 23, 1947; she attended the University of
Tennessee, Knoxville, and the University of Tennessee at Chattanooga before serving as a
teacher and counselor at Chattanooga Valley High School; and
WHEREAS, she then moved into higher education administration, serving in various
administrative positions at Chattanooga State Community College, as interim president of
Nashville State Community College, as president of Roane State Community College, and as
president of Austin Peay State University; and
WHEREAS, in retirement, Mrs. Hoppe used her life experiences to author several books,
including one on the life and historic trial of her husband, two on grief, and one on addiction, as
well as five devotional books; and
WHEREAS, a woman of deep and abiding faith, her mission was to help those who read
her books focus on God's presence–His everlasting love, His amazing creation, and His help in
times of trouble or sorrow; and
WHEREAS, she will be sadly mourned but fondly remembered by her son, Kevin Hoppe;
grandson, Ryan Hoppe; sisters, Sylvia Yates and Flavia Fleming; three nieces and one nephew;
and two grand-nephews and two grand-nieces; and

WHEREAS, she was preceded in death by her beloved husband, Robert "Bobby"
Hoppe, and her loving parents, Kermit and Helen Howard; and
WHEREAS, Sherry Lee Hoppe leaves behind an indelible legacy of integrity and probity
in public life, compassion and loyalty in private life, and diligence and dedication in all her
chosen endeavors; and
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should remember the bountiful life of this exceptional
public servant and human being; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor the memory of Sherry Lee Howard Hoppe, reflecting fondly upon
her impeccable character and her stalwart commitment to living the examined life with courage
and conviction.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that we extend our sympathy and offer our condolences
to the family of Mrs. Hoppe.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
