Section 1:
By Akbari
HOUSE BILL 2297
By Hardaway
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 49,
relative to K-12 education.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-6-304(c), is amended by deleting
the language "one (1) high-traffic, highly and clearly visible public area that is" and substituting
"two (2) high-traffic, highly and clearly visible public areas that are".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
