Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 65-13-103(14), is amended by
deleting subdivisions (C) and (D) and substituting:
(C) A public utility, as defined in § 65-4-101, that provides utility service;
(D) A cooperative, as defined in § 65-25-102; or
(E) A rail carrier, as defined in 49 U.S.C. § 10102, engaged in the transportation
of passengers or property for hire and operating within this state; and

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 65, Chapter 13, Part 7, is amended by
adding the following as a new section:
65-13-710. Authority's purview regarding utilities that are rail carriers.
(a) In exercising its authority under this chapter, the authority:
(1) Shall not:
(A) Issue an order that regulates rail transportation or
unreasonably interferes with the rail operations of a rail carrier; or
(B) Compel a rail carrier to permit the construction, operation, or
maintenance of a transit project within the right-of-way of a rail carrier if
doing so would be preempted by federal law; and
(2) May:
(A) Determine whether the construction, operation, or
maintenance of a transit project would:

(i) Unreasonably interfere with or interrupt the rail
operations of, or rail transportation by, a rail carrier; or
(ii) Create a railroad safety or security concern with the rail
operations of, or rail transportation by, a rail carrier; and
(B) Authorize the subsurface construction, operation, and
maintenance of a transit project that does not result in a condition
specified in subdivision (a)(2)(A)(i) or (ii).
(b) If any provision of this section or its application to any person or
circumstance is held invalid, then the invalidity does not affect other provisions or

application, and to that end, the provisions of this section are severable.

initially obtained through competitive procurement under title 12, chapter 3.
