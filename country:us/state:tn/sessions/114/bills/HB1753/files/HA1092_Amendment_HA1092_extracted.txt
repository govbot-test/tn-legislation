House State & Local Government 1
Amendment No. 1 to HB1753
Crawford
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 2004 House Bill No. 1753*
by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 58, Chapter 2, Part 1, is amended by
adding the following as a new section:
(a) Each local emergency management agency shall, on an annual basis:
(1) Require all employees to receive training provided by TEMA on the
Mutual Aid and Emergency and Disaster Assistance Agreement Act of 2004,
compiled in title 58, chapter 8, and the lnterlocal Cooperation Act, compiled in
title 12, chapter 9; and
(2) Document the completion of the training required by subdivision (a)(1)
in the employee's personnel or training file.
(b) The chief executive of each police department, sheriff's department, fire
department, public works department, highway department, municipal utility, or county or
municipal finance office that is a participating governmental entity, as defined by § 58-8-
102, is encouraged to designate employees to receive the training provided by TEMA in
subdivision (a)(1).
(c) TEMA shall document the completion of training required by subdivision
(a)(1).
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.
