SENATE RESOLUTION 398
By Jackson
A RESOLUTION to commend Jasmine Heard for exemplary
service as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this legislative body is pleased to recognize certain
persons who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment of the
legislative process; and
WHEREAS, Jasmine Heard is one such individual, who has distinguished herself in her
role as a legislative intern for Senator Ed Jackson; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Heard is originally from Cincinnati, Ohio, but has lived in Nashville since
2021; and
WHEREAS, Jasmine Heard's work, her exceptional organizational and communication
skills, and her professionalism have resulted in innumerable benefits for the legislators she
assisted, the constituents of Senate District 25, and the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, a student at Tennessee State University, where she is highly regarded by
her peers, Ms. Heard has achieved academic success while pursuing a degree in political
science; she will graduate in May 2026 as Summa Cum Laude; and
WHEREAS, Jasmine Heard aspires to attend law school and become a sports and
entertainment attorney or sports agent, focusing on contract law; and
WHEREAS, in her free time, Ms. Heard enjoys learning new things, reading, and
meeting new people; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize herself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further her studies, Jasmine Heard brought her many
abilities and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2026 session of the One Hundred
Fourteenth General Assembly; and
SR0398

WHEREAS, Ms. Heard has performed her many duties with extraordinary insight and
rare decorum; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby commend Jasmine
Heard for outstanding service as a legislative intern for Senator Ed Jackson and extend to her
our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
