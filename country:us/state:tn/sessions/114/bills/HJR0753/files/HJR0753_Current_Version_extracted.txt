HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 753
By Reedy
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Chemours New
Johnsonville on attaining Silver Level status in
the Tennessee Veteran Ready Business
Recognition Program.
WHEREAS, the contributions and sacrifices made by the men and women who serve in
the U.S. Armed Forces are vital in maintaining our freedoms and our way of life; and
WHEREAS, because our servicemembers and veterans have given so much, it is only
fitting that we should offer them the opportunity to contribute to their communities as valuable
employees and breadwinners for their family; and
WHEREAS, Chemours New Johnsonville has attained Silver Level status in the
Tennessee Veteran Ready Business Recognition Program; and
WHEREAS, awarded by the Tennessee Department of Veterans Services, this
designation honors Chemours New Johnsonville for actively recruiting, hiring, and retaining
servicemembers, veterans, and their families; and
WHEREAS, Chemours New Johnsonville employs nearly 500 full-time workers and
approximately 350 contractors; this manufacturing site is the second largest employer in
Humphreys County and one of the largest industrial employers in the Tennessee Valley; and
WHEREAS, the Chemours New Johnsonville manufacturing site is now recognized as a
leader in advancing Tennessee's transition from "Veteran Friendly" to "Veteran Ready"; and
WHEREAS, under the leadership of plant manager Amanda Smith, Chemours New
Johnsonville has made an ongoing commitment to maintaining a work environment in which
servicemembers and veterans feel valued, supported, and empowered to utilize their skills and
experience in productive employment; and
HJR0753
011170
- 1 -
WHEREAS, Chemours New Johnsonville is proud to partner with the Tennessee
Department of Veterans Services and utilize the agency's resources and opportunities for
collaboration to ultimately strengthen the site's veteran-focused initiatives; and
WHEREAS, the owners and management of Chemours New Johnsonville are worthy of
our approbation for prioritizing the needs of our servicemembers and veterans; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and recognize Chemours New Johnsonville on attaining Silver
Level status in the Tennessee Veteran Ready Business Recognition Program and thank the
company for its steadfast commitment to employing servicemembers and veterans in
Humphreys County and throughout the Tennessee Valley.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
