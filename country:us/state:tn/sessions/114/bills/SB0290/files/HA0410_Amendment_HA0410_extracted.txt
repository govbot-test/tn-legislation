Amendment No. 2 to HB0764
Pearson
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 290* House Bill No. 764
by deleting subdivision (a)(3) in Section 3 and substituting:
(3) If a student is under eighteen (18) years of age, then the principal must notify
the student's parent or legal guardian of the need to conduct a physical search of the
student and receive the consent of the student's parent or legal guardian to conduct the
search prior to conducting the search.
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting "as a new subsection" in the directory language of Section
2 and substituting "as new subsections" and adding the following as a new subsection in
Section 2:
(f) If a student is under eighteen (18) years of age, then the principal must notify
the student's parent or legal guardian of the need to conduct a search of the student's
property pursuant to this section and receive the consent of the student's parent or legal
guardian to conduct the search prior to conducting the search.
