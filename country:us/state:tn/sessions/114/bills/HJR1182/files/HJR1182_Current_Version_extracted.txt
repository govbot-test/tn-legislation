HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1182
By Reneau
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Investigator Joshua
Branam upon his receipt of the Victim Support–
Investigations Award.
WHEREAS, our nation was conceived by individuals who were willing to sacrifice their
personal concerns to ensure the public safety and welfare, and the Volunteer State is especially
proud to be the home of so many valiant individuals who have performed above and beyond the
call of duty in service of public safety; and
WHEREAS, one such individual is Investigator Joshua Branam, who was recently
honored with the Chattanooga Police Department Victim Support–Investigations Award, in
recognition of his exceptional and extended dedication to victim support; and
WHEREAS, through his work with the investigations bureau, Investigator Branam has
exhibited the utmost support to victims of crime, taking time to understand situations while also
empathizing and learning how to best prioritize services and resources for victims and their
families; and
WHEREAS, in 2025, Joshua Branam truly excelled, extending support to a young
bicycle accident victim and exceeding the initial requirements of his investigation; Investigator
Branam maintained personal contact with the victim and his family throughout his recovery,
visiting him in the hospital and at home; and
WHEREAS, Investigator Branam's support included personally presenting the child with
a specially chosen helmet and ensuring a victim support specialist connected the family with
necessary services; and
WHEREAS, Investigator Branam further provides steadfast support in court settings; in a
pedestrian-struck case, he was observed attending several court dates with the victim, offering
