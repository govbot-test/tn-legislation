HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 745
By Jones J
A RESOLUTION to honor the memory of Renee Nicole Good.
WHEREAS, it was with great sadness and a profound sense of loss the members of this
General Assembly learned of the tragic death of Renee Nicole Good, a thirty-seven-year-old
United States citizen who was shot and killed by an ICE agent on January 7, 2025, just a few
blocks from her home in Minneapolis, Minnesota; and
WHEREAS, a stay-at-home mother of three, Ms. Good had dropped off her six-year-old
son at his school and was driving home when she was fatally shot; and
WHEREAS, a new resident of Minneapolis, Ms. Good was a loving mother, a poet, and
a partner who was treasured by her family and respected by her neighbors; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Good was warmly described by her family members as "loving,
forgiving, and affectionate," "an amazing human being," and "one of the kindest people I've ever
known"; and
WHEREAS, the circumstances surrounding her tragic death have shocked and
saddened her family, her neighbors across Minneapolis, and strangers around the world; and
WHEREAS, a native of Colorado, Ms. Good loved to sing and participated in her high
school's chorus before studying vocal performance in college; and
WHEREAS, an excellent student at Old Dominion University, she took advanced
courses on creative writing and the craft of fiction and graduated with a bachelor's degree in
English; and
WHEREAS, in 2020, Ms. Good was awarded Old Dominion's American Academy of
Poets Prize; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Good was a devoted Christian who took part in youth mission trips to
Northern Ireland when she was younger; and

WHEREAS, as we reflect upon her untimely passing, we should focus on her bountiful
life as "a reminder of what unites us: freedom, love, and peace" while committing ourselves to
"compassion, healing, and reflection" in these troubling times; and
WHEREAS, Renee Nicole Good has left behind an inspiring legacy as a mother,
daughter, partner, friend, neighbor, artist, and citizen; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor the memory and celebrate the life of Renee Nicole Good and
extend our sympathy and condolences to her family and friends.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
