HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1197
By Reneau
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Tactical Medicals Justin
Krantz and Jason Kile upon their receipt of the
Citizen Service Medal from the Chattanooga
Police Department.
WHEREAS, our nation was conceived by individuals who were willing to sacrifice their
personal concerns to ensure the public safety and welfare, and the Volunteer State is especially
proud to be the home of so many valiant individuals who have performed above and beyond the
call of duty in service of public safety; and
WHEREAS, Tactical Medicals Justin Krantz and Jason Kile were recently honored with
the Chattanooga Police Department's (CPD's) Citizen Service Medal in recognition of their
volunteer service with the Department's SWAT Team; and
WHEREAS, on September 15, 2025, the CPD's SWAT Team responded to an
aggravated domestic assault involving an armed suspect barricaded inside a residence; after
hours of unsuccessful contact attempts, K9 Diesel was deployed to clear a room that could not
be accessed safely by officers; and
WHEREAS, during the search, K9 Diesel sustained severe lacerations to both hind legs
from broken glass; under cover of the Bearcat, Tactical Medics Justin Krantz and Jason Kile
immediately began lifesaving care, identifying significant hemorrhaging, applying direct
pressure, packing wounds with hemostatic gauze, and securing pressure bandages to control
the bleeding; and
WHEREAS, Medic Krantz accompanied K9 Diesel and his handler to the emergency
veterinary hospital, continuing reassessment and treatment en route; veterinary staff later
advised that without the medics' rapid intervention, Diesel likely would not have survived
transport. However, despite exhaustive medical consultation, the severity of his injuries

ultimately led to the humane euthanasia of K9 Diesel on September 17, 2025, surrounded by

FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE

upon their receipt of the Citizen Service Medal from the Chattanooga Police Department.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
