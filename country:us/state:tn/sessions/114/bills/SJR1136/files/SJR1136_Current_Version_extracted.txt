SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1136
By Briggs
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize James Le Nguyen, 2026
Salutatorian of Farragut High School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, James Le Nguyen is one such exceptional student, who was recently
named Salutatorian of the 2026 graduating class at Farragut High School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Mr. Nguyen has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in his remarkable high school career, James Le Nguyen has earned an
exemplary grade point average while participating in numerous extracurricular activities,
including serving as vice president of the Chess Club, captain of the chess team, and a member
of the Science Club and playing on the varsity tennis team; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Nguyen also has years of intern and research experience; he interned
at Oak Ridge National Laboratory, where he co-authored a published research paper about
energy efficiency; he has been involved with molecular biology research at the University of
Tennessee and works as a patient care assistant at Fort Loudon Medical Center; and
WHEREAS, for the past two years, James Le Nguyen has served on the Knox County
Health Department's Youth Health Board; and
WHEREAS, in addition to his extracurricular activities, Mr. Nguyen has received many
honors and accolades throughout his time at Farragut High School; he has received the Strata-

WHEREAS, James Le Nguyen is a nationally and internationally recognized chess
player and has competed in thirty-two different states as well as throughout Europe; a five-time
Tennessee chess champion, he received the National Master title from the United States Chess
Federation in 2022 and the International Candidate Master title from the International Chess
Federation in 2025; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Nguyen's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well
for his future success at Duke University, where he plans to study neuroscience; and
WHEREAS, James Le Nguyen's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of
pride for his parents, Dr. David Tho Nguyen and Taylor Thuong Nguyen, his school, and his
community; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Nguyen's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of the members of this General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor James Le Nguyen
on being named the 2026 Salutatorian of Farragut High School, salute his impressive academic
achievement, and extend to him our best wishes for a happy, successful future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
