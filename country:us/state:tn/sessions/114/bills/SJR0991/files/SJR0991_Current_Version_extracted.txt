SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 991
By White
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Master Jericho Noah
Gabriel Shearon of Murfreesboro on his
wrestling championship.
WHEREAS, it is fitting and appropriate that we congratulate, support, and encourage
young people to learn who they are, discover their talents, find their purpose in life, put their gifts
to good use, and live for God; and
WHEREAS, Master Jericho Noah Gabriel Shearon was born to Mr. Nathaneal and Mrs.
Jennifer Shearon of Murfreesboro on July 7, 2014; and
WHEREAS, Master Shearon is a fifth-grade student at Blackman Elementary School
and a member of the 5th Grade Blackman Wrestling Club in Murfreesboro; and
WHEREAS, on Saturday, March 1, 2026, Master Shearon won the Tennessee State
Amateur Athletic Union (AAU) 12-and-under 130-pound division state championship in Franklin;
and
WHEREAS, Master Shearon was named the Most Outstanding Wrestler of the
Tennessee AAU state tournament on March 1, 2026; and
WHEREAS, Master Shearon also won the 2026 Tennessee AAU Grand Champion
Award, winning Top 100 honors in Gatlinburg, Memphis, Chattanooga, Region 3, and at the
state tournament; and
WHEREAS, Master Shearon is undefeated, 34-0, in Tennessee AAU competition,
allowing zero points and winning every match by a fall or technical fall; and
WHEREAS, Master Shearon has wrestled in seven states and, over the past twelve
months, has compiled a record of 81-15 against top national competition, including 31 eighth-
grade matches, while he was just a fourth-grade or fifth-grade student; and
SJR0991
016061
- 1 -
WHEREAS, Master Jericho Shearon has, through his success on the wrestling mat,
brought honor to the State of Tennessee, and it is wholly fitting that he be formally recognized
for his tremendous accomplishments; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we commend and congratulate Master Jericho Noah
Gabriel Shearon on his championship victory at the Tennessee State Amateur Athletic Union
wrestling tournament, extending our best wishes for his every future success, both on and off
the wrestling mat.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the Tennessee Senate has a Wrestling Caucus,
chaired by the Honorable Senator Bo Watson, and it is highly appropriate that young Master
Jericho Shearon be inducted as an honorary member.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
