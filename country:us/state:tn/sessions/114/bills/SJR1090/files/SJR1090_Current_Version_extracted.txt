SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1090
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Kelly Bales on her
receipt of the Governor's Volunteer Stars Award.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
specially recognize those estimable citizens who have dedicated their time and energies to
helping others; and
WHEREAS, each year, 1.9 million Tennessee volunteers give over 134 million hours of
community service, contributing $4 billion to Tennessee's economy; and
WHEREAS, these volunteers are the backbone of our communities, and by donating
their time and talents to fulfill needs that would otherwise go unmet, they embody the spirit of
giving; and
WHEREAS, Kelly Bales is one such exemplary person, who is a recipient of the
Governor's Volunteer Stars Award in recognition of her laudable commitment to her community;
and
WHEREAS, Ms. Bales is a dedicated co-leader of the Gatlinburg Girl Scout Troop, in
which troop her daughter, Lyra, is a Girl Scout Senior; and
WHEREAS, Kelly Bales is a model citizen; her commitment to helping others through
volunteerism epitomizes the spirit of the Volunteer State, and she should be specially
recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor Kelly Bales for her outstanding community
leadership, congratulate her as an esteemed recipient of the Governor's Volunteer Stars Award,
and extend to her our best wishes for every future success.
