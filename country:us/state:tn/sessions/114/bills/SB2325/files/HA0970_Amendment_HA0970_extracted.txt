AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 2325* House Bill No. 2563
by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 8, Chapter 14, Part 1, is amended by
adding the following new section:
Effective July 1, 2026, there are created twenty (20) additional assistant district
public defender positions, twenty (20) additional legal secretary positions, twelve (12)
additional investigator positions, and seven (7) additional social worker positions.
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.
