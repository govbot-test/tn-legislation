HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1082
By Lamberth
A RESOLUTION to commend Representative Jeremy Faison for
his honorable and distinguished service to the
people of Tennessee as a member of the House
of Representatives.
WHEREAS, it is most appropriate that the members of this General Assembly should
honor those fellow legislators who have performed their duties with the utmost integrity and
whose efforts during their time in office have been dedicated to providing thoughtful and
exemplary service to their districts and to this great State; and
WHEREAS, Representative Jeremy Faison is one such distinguished public servant,
who has served both his constituency and this legislative body with honor and ability; and
WHEREAS, during his tenure on Capitol Hill, Representative Faison has faithfully served
the citizens of the 11th House District, currently encompassing Cocke and portions of Hamblen
and Jefferson counties, and previously part of Greene and Jefferson counties, since his election
in 2010, representing the district from the 107th through the 114th General Assemblies; and
WHEREAS, Representative Jeremy Faison is a devoted Christian who is married to
Miriam Faison and is the proud father of five children, Rebekah, Reagan, Gage, Asher, and
Tucker, and the proud grandfather of one grandchild, Zion; and
WHEREAS, throughout his years of public service, Representative Faison has remained
deeply committed to his family, ensuring that the demands of public life never overshadow the
importance of his faith and loved ones; those who have worked in and around the State Capitol
have often been reminded of that devotion by the presence of his children, who could frequently
be seen about the Capitol grounds, bringing warmth and joy to the building; and
WHEREAS, as the owner of Rocky Top Pest Control, Representative Faison has
brought real-world experience and a strong work ethic to his legislative duties; he previously

WHEREAS, an active and dynamic participant in the legislative process during his

Operations Education, Health and General Welfare Subcommittee, the Joint Government

114th General Assembly; the House Commerce Committee, the House Health Committee, the
House Finance, Ways and Means Subcommittee, and the House Banking and Consumer
Affairs Subcommittee of the First Extraordinary Session of the 113th General Assembly; the
House Calendar and Rules Committee, the House Finance, Ways and Means Committee, and
the House COVID-19 Committee of the Third Extraordinary Session of the 112th General
Assembly; the House Calendar and Rules Committee and the House Finance, Ways and
Means Committee of the Second Extraordinary Session of the 112th General Assembly; and the
House Calendar and Rules Committee and the House Electronic Delivery of Healthcare
Committee of the Second Extraordinary Session of the 111th General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, throughout his service in the General Assembly, Representative Faison has
been a champion for small businesses and rural Tennessee interests, a fierce defender of the
Second Amendment, and a steadfast advocate for adoption and for unborn children; and
WHEREAS, an avid outdoorsman, Representative Jeremy Faison is a member of the
Tennessee Sportsmen's Caucus and was an inaugural member and performer in the annual
Capitol Jam, a live music event held at the Tennessee Performing Arts Center that showcases
the talents of legislative members, staff, and others; and
WHEREAS, through his sponsorship and support of enacted legislation, including
measures to protect vulnerable populations, such as enhancements for child exposure to
fentanyl, as well as legislation concerning prohibitions, veteran support, public health and
safety, education remediation, and making adoption more accessible, Representative Jeremy
Faison has consistently advocated for limited government, individual liberties, family values, and
the betterment of Tennessee communities; and
WHEREAS, after more than fifteen years of steadfast legislative service, Representative
Faison has formed many lifelong friendships throughout his district that he will cherish for the
rest of his life; throughout his tenure, he has exemplified integrity, leadership, and dedication in
advancing the interests of his constituents and the entire State of Tennessee; and

WHEREAS, Representative Jeremy Faison is wholly committed to the noble precepts of
public service that have earned Tennessee recognition as the Volunteer State, and he should
be specially recognized for his exemplary tenure in the General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby honor Representative Jeremy Faison for his meritorious service
to Tennessee as a member of the House of Representatives, commend him for his countless
contributions and many good works in service of the citizens of the 11th House District, and
extend to him our best wishes for much continued success and happiness.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
