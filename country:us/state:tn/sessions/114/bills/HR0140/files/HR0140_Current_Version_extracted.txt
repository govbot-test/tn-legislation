HOUSE RESOLUTION 140
By Warner
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Benny Jett upon his
receipt of the National Society Daughters of the
American Revolution Mary Desha Medal for
Youth.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should salute those
Tennesseans who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as young
leaders of whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such exemplary person is Benny Jett, recipient of the National Society
Daughters of the American Revolution (NSDAR) Mary Desha Medal for Youth, a most befitting
honor that recognizes his outstanding service and leadership to his community, state, and
nation; and
WHEREAS, Benny Jett's selection was made by the Brigadier General Richard Winn
Chapter of the NSDAR, and he will receive the national award on February 24, 2026, at Spring
Hill City Hall; and
WHEREAS, Benny Jett's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for
his family, his school, and his community; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Jett has distinguished himself as a young Tennessean of whom we can
all be proud; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and
commend Benny Jett upon his receipt of the Brigadier General Richard Winn Chapter of the
National Society Daughters of the American Revolution Mary Desha Medal for Youth and
extend to him our best wishes for a happy and successful future.
