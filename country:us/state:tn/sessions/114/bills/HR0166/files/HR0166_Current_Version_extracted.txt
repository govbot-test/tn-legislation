HOUSE RESOLUTION 166
By Powers
A RESOLUTION to recognize Kailei Beeler upon her appointment
to the United States Naval Academy.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should honor those Tennesseans whose proven
dedication to excellence is a great point of pride for this State; and
WHEREAS, one such individual is Kailei Beeler, who recently received an appointment
to the United States Naval Academy, a true honor and an auspicious beginning to her career of
service; and
WHEREAS, established in 1845 in Annapolis, Maryland, the Naval Academy is the
second oldest of the five U.S. service academies and educates midshipmen for service in the
officer corps of the United States Navy and United States Marine Corps; and
WHEREAS, the mission of the Naval Academy is to develop midshipmen morally,
mentally, and physically and to imbue them with the highest ideals of duty, honor, and loyalty in
order to graduate leaders who are dedicated to a career of naval service and have potential for
future development in mind and character to assume the highest responsibilities of command,
citizenship, and government; and
WHEREAS, a senior at Union County High School, where she is a four-year member
and Commanding Officer of the Navy JROTC program, Kailei Beeler holds a 4.0 grade point
average and will graduate third in her class; and
WHEREAS, a faithful member of Alder Springs Church, Ms. Beeler has participated in a
number of extracurricular activities during her high school career, including Beta Club,
Fellowship of Christian Athletes, basketball, softball, golf, cross country, and track; and
WHEREAS, Kailei Beeler is an exemplary young lady whose work ethic, integrity, and
ability will serve her well as she embarks upon her next chapter; and
HR0166

WHEREAS, Ms. Beeler is one of Tennessee's most noteworthy young people, and it is
most fitting that she be recognized on this milestone occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we recognize
Kailei Beeler upon her appointment to the United States Naval Academy, extending our best
wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
