by adding the following as a new, appropriately designated section immediately preceding the

Section 2:
SECTION __.
Item 1. The appropriation in Section 61, Item 48, to the Tennessee Historical
Commission for the Andrew Jackson Foundation, is reduced by the nonrecurring sum of
$3,200,000.
Item 2. In addition to any other funds appropriated by the provisions of this act,
there is appropriated the sum of $3,200,000 (nonrecurring) to the Department of
Environment and Conservation for the sole purpose of engineering and constructing a
parking lot for the Harris Ridge Trail at Radnor Lake State Park.
