AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 2690* House Bill No. 2631
by adding the following as a new, appropriately designated section immediately preceding the

SECTION __.
Item 1. Ten percent (10%) of the Tennessee Valley Authority payments retained
in a fund by the state under Tennessee Code Annotated § 67-9-101(a)(1) are transferred
to the Wildlife Resources Fund, created in Tennessee Code Annotated § 70-1-401, to be
used for boating regulation and wildlife resources.
