Amendment No. 6 to HB2631
Hemmer
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 2690* House Bill No. 2631
by adding the following as a new, appropriately designated section immediately preceding the
antepenultimate section and redesignating the subsequent sections accordingly:
SECTION __.
Item 1. The appropriation in Section 59, Item 1, line item 1, to the Department of
Environment and Conservation for the Bicentennial Capitol Mall State Park History Wall,
is reduced by the nonrecurring sum of $12,500,000.
Item 2. In addition to any other funds appropriated by the provisions of this act,
there is appropriated the sum of $3,200,000 (nonrecurring) to the Department of
Environment and Conservation for the sole purpose of engineering and constructing a
parking lot for the Harris Ridge Trail at Radnor Lake State Park.
Item 3. In addition to any other funds appropriated by the provisions of this act,
there is appropriated the sum of $8,850,000 (nonrecurring) to the University of
Tennessee Health Science Center for the sole purpose of funding a pilot program to
provide live, online childbirth education classes to residents of Tennessee through a
partnership with a digital platform provider.
Item 4. In addition to any other funds appropriated by the provisions of this act,
there is appropriated the sum of $300,000 (nonrecurring) to the Department of Finance
and Administration for the sole purpose of making a grant in such amount to Persist
Nashville, Inc., to be used for providing college enrollment and persistence coaching to
1,200 Davidson County graduates.
