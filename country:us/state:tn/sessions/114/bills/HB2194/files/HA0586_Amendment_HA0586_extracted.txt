AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 2259* House Bill No. 2194
by deleting subsection (d) in SECTION 3 and substituting:
(d) The chief executive officer or chief academic officer of an institution governed
by a state university board or board of regents retains the authority to impose
disciplinary actions, including suspension or termination for cause, on faculty members
who violate institutional policies or professional standards, regardless of whether the
faculty member is tenured or non-tenured, that are consistent with the policies adopted
by the governing board pursuant to subsection (b).
