HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1340
By Hurt
A RESOLUTION to honor the memory of Angela Carol Allmand of
Ripley.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly were greatly saddened to learn of
the passing of Angela Carol Allmand of Ripley; and
WHEREAS, Angela Allmand was an exemplary public servant and consummate
professional who worked assiduously to improve the quality of life for her fellow citizens in
numerous capacities; and
WHEREAS, Mrs. Allmand was a faithful servant of her community, giving not just her
time, but her heart, her sense of purpose, and a constant belief in others; and
WHEREAS, Angela Allmand's unwavering dedication, passion, and leadership guided
her resolve in every endeavor, as she firmly stood for what was right; and
WHEREAS, her steadfast values and her work, including her astute leadership in the
Republican Party, have left a lasting mark of conviction and strength; and
WHEREAS, Angela Allmand was also deeply devoted to her family, and she always
endeavored to remain true to family values of the highest order; and
WHEREAS, Mrs. Allmand will be sadly missed but fondly remembered by her husband,
Thomas M. Allmand; her mother, Margaret E. Shoemake; her children, Misty Garrett, Caleb
(Katie) Phillips, Colby (Melodie) Phillips, and Jack Allmand; her brother, Chris Shoemake; and
seven grandchildren; and
WHEREAS, with the humility and courage of a true leader, Angela Allmand led with
conviction, loved her community deeply, and always gave her all, never hesitating or holding
back; her memory will live on through the countless lives she touched and the community she
so ably served; and

WHEREAS, Mrs. Allmand leaves behind an indelible legacy of integrity and probity in
public life, compassion and loyalty in private life, and diligence and dedication in all her chosen
endeavors; and
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should pause to remember the bountiful life of this
exceptional public servant and human being; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor the memory of Angela Carol Allmand, reflecting fondly upon her
stalwart commitment to living the examined life with courage and conviction.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that we express our sympathy and offer our condolences
to the family and friends of Mrs. Allmand.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
