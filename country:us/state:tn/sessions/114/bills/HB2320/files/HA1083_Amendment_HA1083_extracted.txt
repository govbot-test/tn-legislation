(1) The name of the authority, which must be the convention center authority [or
agricultural exposition authority (as applicable)] of the city, county, or metropolitan
government of _______, where the blank is to be filled in with the name of the
municipality, if the name is available for use by the authority, and if not available, then
the chief executive officer of the municipality shall designate some other similar name
that is available;

Section 9:
SECTION 7. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-89-105(a)(7), is amended by
deleting the language "convention center".

Section 10:
SECTION 8. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-89-108, is amended by adding the
following as a new subsection:
- 2 - 013309
(d) If an agricultural exposition facility owned by an authority is leased or used by
a nonprofit organization to operate an agricultural fair at least once a year, the
organization that operates such agricultural fair may appoint an additional director to the
board of directors of the authority, with the term of such director being established by the
appointing organization, for such period as the organization has a lease or other
agreement to use the facility. If a director is appointed pursuant to this subsection (d),
such director is a voting member of the board of directors of the authority and has all
rights and obligations as the directors appointed by the municipality.

Section 11:
SECTION 9. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-89-109, is amended by deleting the
language "convention center".

Section 12:
SECTION 10. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-89-112(k), is amended by
deleting the language "a convention center authority" and substituting instead "an authority";
deleting the language "convention center facilities" and substituting instead "convention center
or agricultural exposition facilities"; deleting the language "the convention center authority"
wherever it appears and substituting instead "the authority"; and deleting "convention center
facility" wherever it appears and substituting instead "convention center or agricultural
exposition facility".

Section 13:
SECTION 11. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-89-115(c), is amended by
deleting the language "convention center facilities" and substituting instead "convention center
or agricultural exposition facilities".

Section 14:
SECTION 12. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-89-120, is amended by deleting
the language "a convention center authority" and substituting instead "an authority".

Section 15:
SECTION 13. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
