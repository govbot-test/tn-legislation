SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 769
By Seal
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Brooks Hamilton on
being named to the TSWA Class A All-State
soccer team.
WHEREAS, we are pleased to recognize and commend those successful high school
athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring honor to Tennessee and serve as
exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable individual is sophomore Brooks Hamilton of Seymour
High School, an athlete of extraordinary talent, skill, and dedication, who has proven herself a
formidable competitor on the field; and
WHEREAS, the Tennessee Sports Writers Association (TSWA) recently named Brooks
Hamilton to the 2025 Class A All-State soccer team in recognition of her excellence as a
midfielder; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Hamilton epitomizes all that is good in today's student athlete, as she
has achieved a high level of success in sports while evincing great character both on and off the
field; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Hamilton's notable achievement is a source of great pride for her family,
her school, and her community; and
WHEREAS, Brooks Hamilton is a winner both on and off the field of play, and her
dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect, admiration, and commendation of
this General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Brooks Hamilton of
