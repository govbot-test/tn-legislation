HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1144
By Butler
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Scotty Phillips of
Anderson County.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should salute those citizens who, through their
extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as community leaders of whom we can all
be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such noteworthy person is Scotty Phillips of Anderson County, a highly
respected member of his community who has dedicated himself to improving the quality of life
for his fellow citizens in numerous capacities; and
WHEREAS, a lifelong resident of Anderson County, Mr. Phillips works as a bus driver
and is the esteemed longtime owner of the iconic New River General Store; and
WHEREAS, a man of integrity and dedication, Scotty Phillips epitomizes the
commitment to community that is characteristic of a true Tennessean; and
WHEREAS, throughout his lifetime, Mr. Phillips has demonstrated the utmost
professionalism, ability, and probity, winning the respect and admiration of his friends,
neighbors, and customers in Anderson County, and he is most worthy of our approbation; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Scotty Phillips for his many contributions to
Anderson County and the State of Tennessee, salute his professional expertise and personal
excellence, and wish him all the best in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
