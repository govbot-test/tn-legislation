SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1206
By Lowe
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Dina Swafford upon her
retirement as Bradley County Register of Deeds.
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that the members of this General Assembly should honor
those citizens who have performed with uncommon devotion and enthusiasm throughout their
careers in public service; and
WHEREAS, Dina Swafford, Register of Deeds for Bradley County, is one such
individual, who has distinguished herself as a professional of the greatest dedication and
integrity; and
WHEREAS, after an impressive forty-year career and four terms as the Bradley County
Register of Deeds, Mrs. Swafford is retiring from her position this year; and
WHEREAS, Dina Swafford began her estimable career with the Bradley County Register
of Deeds office in 1986, hired under the direction of Odell Swafford, the Register of Deeds at the
time; and
WHEREAS, early in her tenure, Mrs. Swafford meticulously recorded documents by
hand and organized them in large recording books; she was a driving force behind the office's
transition to digital recordkeeping in 1998, dedicating countless nights and long hours to ensure
every document was scanned and digitally accessible; and
WHEREAS, she was later appointed deputy clerk, a role in which her unwavering work
ethic and commitment became indispensable to the office and the community alike; in 2010, she
was elected Register of Deeds, in which position her relentless dedication and exceptional
leadership set a benchmark of excellence; and

WHEREAS, known for her compassion, Mrs. Swafford consistently goes above and
beyond, wholeheartedly serving each individual and family who walks through the office doors
with dignity and care; and
WHEREAS, Mrs. Swafford shared thirty-eight years of marriage with her husband, David
Swafford, until his passing in 2024; he played an important role at the Register's Office and was
frequently seen supporting his wife and her team in various capacities; and
WHEREAS, throughout her distinguished career, Dina Swafford has demonstrated the
utmost professionalism, ability, and integrity, winning the unbridled respect and admiration of
the citizens of Cleveland and Bradley County; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Dina Swafford upon her
retirement as Bradley County Register of Deeds, thank her for her many years of dedicated
service, and extend to her our best wishes for a happy and fulfilling retirement and every
continued success in her future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
