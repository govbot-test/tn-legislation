SENATE RESOLUTION 376
By Rose
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Belen Castro-Hair, 2026
Valedictorian of Tipton-Rosemark Academy.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Belen Castro-Hair is one such exceptional student, who was recently
named Valedictorian of the 2026 graduating class at Tipton-Rosemark Academy; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Ms. Castro-Hair has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in her remarkable high school career, Belen Castro-Hair has earned an
exemplary grade point average while participating in numerous extracurricular activities; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Castro-Hair's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes
well for her future success; and
WHEREAS, Belen Castro-Hair's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of
pride for her family, her school, and her community; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Castro-Hair's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the
respect, admiration, and commendation of the members of this legislative body; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby commend and honor
the 2026 Tipton-Rosemark Academy Valedictorian, Belen Castro-Hair, on her impressive
academic achievement and extend to her our best wishes for a happy, successful future.
