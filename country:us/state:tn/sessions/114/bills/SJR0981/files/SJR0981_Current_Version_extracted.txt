SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 981
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Maliyah Glasper of
Gatlinburg-Pittman High School upon being
named TSSAA Class 2A Miss Basketball.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should recognize
those incredibly successful student athletes who have distinguished themselves as inspirational
leaders among their peers; and
WHEREAS, a statewide committee of sportswriters recently selected Maliyah Glasper of
Gatlinburg-Pittman High School as Tennessee Secondary School Athletic Association (TSSAA)
Class 2A Miss Basketball based upon her outstanding performance on the hardwood during the
2025-2026 season; and
WHEREAS, her superior academic record and upstanding character were also taken
into consideration when the committee selected Ms. Glasper to receive this prestigious award,
which honors the best Class 2A female basketball player in the entire State; and
WHEREAS, a force for the Gatlinburg-Pittman Lady Highlanders, Ms. Glasper has
committed to continuing her basketball career as a student at Tennessee Technological
University; and
WHEREAS, Maliyah Glasper exemplifies the spirit, determination, and perseverance for
which Tennesseans are notable, and she is richly deserving of our approbation on this special
occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Maliyah Glasper of
Gatlinburg-Pittman High School upon being named TSSAA Class 2A Miss Basketball and wish
her much continued success both on and off the court.
