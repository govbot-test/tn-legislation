SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 553
By Roberts
A RESOLUTION to commend Representative Mary Littleton for
her honorable and astute service to the people
of Tennessee as a member of the House of
Representatives.
WHEREAS, it is most appropriate that the members of this General Assembly should
honor those fellow legislators who have performed their duties with the utmost integrity and
whose efforts during their time in office have been dedicated to providing thoughtful and
exemplary service to their districts and to this great State; and
WHEREAS, Representative Mary Littleton of Dickson is one such distinguished public
servant, who has served both her constituency and the General Assembly with honor and
ability; and
WHEREAS, during her tenure on Capitol Hill, Representative Littleton has been a highly
influential figure in the political, social, and civic life of Cheatham and Dickson counties, and she
has served her constituents in numerous capacities, always working diligently at her elected
duties and expending the necessary effort to understand the opposing sides of complex issues;
and
WHEREAS, Mary Littleton has distinguished herself as a public-spirited citizen of the
highest order and as an exceptional asset to the good people of the 78th House District, whom
she has served as a member of the 108th, 109th, 110th, 111th, 112th, 113th, and 114th General
Assemblies; and
WHEREAS, throughout her service, Representative Littleton has been prominent as a
dedicated and well-informed legislator who always votes her conscience; and
WHEREAS, an active and dynamic participant in the legislative process during her time
in the General Assembly, Representative Mary Littleton has rendered sterling service as chair of

WHEREAS, an active part of her community, she is a member of the Cheatham County
Chamber of Commerce, the Dickson County Chamber of Commerce, Dickson County Relay for
Life, the Dickson High Noon Rotary, the South Cheatham Civitan, the North Cheatham Civitan,
Cheatham County Kiwanis, Cheatham County Community Foundation, and the NRA; and
WHEREAS, she rendered sterling service as vice chair of the Tennessee Republican
Party and Tennessee Republican Party State Executive Committee member for 25th District;
and
WHEREAS, no stranger to awards and accolades, Representative Littleton has been
recognized as a Child Advocacy Center Champion for Children and was named the 2001
Statesman of the Year; and
WHEREAS, throughout her outstanding career as a public servant, Mary Littleton has
worked tirelessly on behalf of her constituents, bringing to her office an honorable bearing and
the traditional values of hard work and common sense; and
WHEREAS, Representative Mary Littleton is wholly committed to the noble precepts of
public service that have earned Tennessee recognition as the Volunteer State, and she should
be specially recognized for her exemplary tenure in the General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby honor Representative Mary Littleton for
her meritorious service to Tennessee as a member of the House of Representatives, commend
her for her countless contributions and many good works in service to the citizens of the 78th
House District, and extend to her our best wishes for much continued success and happiness.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
