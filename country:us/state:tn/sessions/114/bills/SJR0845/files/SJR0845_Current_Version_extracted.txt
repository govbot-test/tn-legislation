SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 845
By Bailey
A RESOLUTION to recognize the Putnam County Chamber of
Commerce on the occasion of its eightieth
anniversary.
WHEREAS, we take great pride in recognizing those community-based organizations
that are celebrating momentous occasions in their illustrious histories; and
WHEREAS, the Putnam County Chamber of Commerce is one such organization, which
will mark the eightieth anniversary of its founding this year; and
WHEREAS, for eight decades, the Putnam County Chamber of Commerce has
remained committed to serving the needs of its community, abiding by its mission to encourage
the growth of existing industries and businesses while giving assistance to any new industries or
individuals seeking to locate in Putnam County; and
WHEREAS, the Putnam County Chamber of Commerce currently boasts a membership
comprised of industries, businesses, individuals, and organizations that are incredibly active in
effecting positive and lasting change within the community; and
WHEREAS, for eighty years, the Putnam County Chamber of Commerce has rendered
valuable service to the good people of Putnam County while cultivating positive growth in
education, business, and industry, and it is wholly fitting that we recognize the Chamber on this
auspicious occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we recognize the Putnam County Chamber of
Commerce on the occasion of its eightieth anniversary, applauding its service within Putnam
County these past eight decades and extending our best wishes for its continued success in the
future.

- 1 -
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
