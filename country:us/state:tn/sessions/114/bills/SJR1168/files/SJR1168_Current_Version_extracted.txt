SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1168
By Bailey
A RESOLUTION to honor Mary Denise McNeal on her
distinguished service to the State of Tennessee
on the occasion of her retirement from the
District Public Defender's Office for the 13th
Judicial District.
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that the members of this General Assembly should honor
those citizens who have performed with uncommon devotion and enthusiasm through their
careers in public service; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable person is Mary Denise McNeal, a longtime resident of
Putnam County, who has faithfully served the citizens of the State of Tennessee since 2001 as
an employee of the District Public Defender's Office for the 13th Judicial District; and
WHEREAS, Denise McNeal began her career with the District Public Defender's Office
for the 13th Judicial District as a legal assistant and, through her diligence, skill, and leadership,
rose to serve as office manager, a role in which she became essential to the effective operation
of the office; and
WHEREAS, throughout her tenure, Ms. McNeal has been a constant and trusted
presence, providing invaluable support to attorneys, staff, and clients, and ensuring that the
daily work of the office is carried out with efficiency and care; and
WHEREAS, Ms. McNeal's institutional knowledge, strong work ethic, and attention to
detail have made her an indispensable member of the office, and her willingness to assist
others has left a lasting mark on colleagues both past and present; and
WHEREAS, she has generously shared her experience with new employees, offering
guidance, patience, and practical wisdom, thereby strengthening the office as a whole; and
WHEREAS, beyond her professional accomplishments, Denise McNeal is known for her
compassion and dedication outside the workplace, particularly her deep commitment to animal

welfare, as an active supporter of rescue and foster organizations, including Safe Haven Puppy
Sanctuary, A.A.R.F. (All About Rescue and Fixin' Inc.), and others, reflecting a spirit of care and
service that extends well beyond her official duties; and
WHEREAS, Denise McNeal's humor, steady presence, and remarkable efficiency will be
deeply missed by her colleagues, by the Conference Office with whom she worked closely over
the years, and perhaps most notably by Bella, the office dog, who found both comfort and
companionship napping in her office; and
WHEREAS, Denise McNeal's retirement, effective July 31, 2026, marks the conclusion
of a distinguished career in public service and the beginning of a well-earned new chapter; and
WHEREAS, throughout her estimable career, Mary Denise McNeal has demonstrated
the utmost professionalism, ability, and integrity, winning the unbridled respect and admiration
of the members of the bar and bench and her community alike; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Mary Denise McNeal for
her years of dedicated and astute service with the District Public Defender's Office for the 13th
Judicial District and extend to her our best wishes for a retirement filled with happiness, good
health, and continued purpose.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
