Amendment No. 3 to HB1784
Behn
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 1940 House Bill No. 1784*
by deleting "; now therefore," at the end of the final clause of the preamble and substituting ";
and".
AND FURTHER AMEND by inserting the following immediately after the final clause of the
preamble:
WHEREAS, since Tennessee's near-total abortion ban took effect, women in this State
have been denied medically necessary health care, including treatment for miscarriages,
ectopic pregnancies, and life-threatening conditions; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee physicians have reported leaving this State or ceasing to
practice obstetrics due to fear of criminal prosecution, worsening the State's existing maternal
healthcare crisis; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee has one of the highest maternal mortality rates in the nation,
and restrictions on reproductive health care have been shown to increase maternal death; and
WHEREAS, the unalienable rights recognized in our nation's founding document include
the right of living women to life, liberty, and the pursuit of happiness; and
WHEREAS, a day of observance is unable to substitute for the health care, support, and
dignity that Tennessee women and families deserve; now, therefore,
