Section 1:
Amendment No. 4 to HB1784
Behn
Signature of Sponsor
by designating the existing amendatory language in SECTION 1 as subsection (a) and adding
the following as a new subsection (b):
(b) January 22 of each year is also observed as "Tennessee Women's
Healthcare Day" so that Tennessee may renew its commitment each year to the health,
safety, and dignity of women in this state and honor citizens of this state who have
suffered harm or death as a result of being denied reproductive medical care.
