HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 993
By Hemmer
A RESOLUTION to recognize and honor Cinco de Mayo
restaurant in Nashville.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should commend
those exemplary businesses that, through their unwavering commitment to excellence, are a
credit to this State; and
WHEREAS, one such notable establishment is Cinco de Mayo, which has been serving
fresh, authentic Mexican food in Middle Tennessee since 2000; and
WHEREAS, much to the delight of its patrons, the Cinco de Mayo location at Old Hickory
Boulevard and Franklin Pike takes great pride in using fresh ingredients, serving authentic
dishes, and providing a wonderful atmosphere; and
WHEREAS, under the esteemed leadership of manager Roberto Carrero, Cinco de
Mayo provides exceptional service and delicious food that stand as a timeless testament to
quality, pleasing and delighting the taste buds of local residents and out-of-towners alike; and
WHEREAS, it is most fitting that we pause in our deliberations to specially recognize this
exemplary restaurant; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and recognize the owners and employees of Cinco de Mayo in
Nashville for their tireless dedication to quality food and extend to them our best wishes for
every continued success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
