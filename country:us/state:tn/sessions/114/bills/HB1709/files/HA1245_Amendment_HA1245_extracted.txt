Amendment No. 3 to HB1709
Cochran
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 1901 House Bill No. 1709*
by deleting SECTION 57 and substituting the following:
SECTION 57.
(a) Section 63-1-181(c) in SECTION 42 of this act is subject to House Bill 1711 /
Senate Bill 2108 of the 114th general assembly becoming a law, the public welfare
requiring it, and, upon such occurrence, takes effect July 1, 2026, at 12:01 a.m., the
public welfare requiring it.
(b) Sections 45 and 46 of this act take effect July 1, 2026, at 12:01 a.m., the
public welfare requiring it; provided, that if House Bill 2539 / Senate Bill 2227 of the
114th general assembly becomes a law, then Sections 45 and 46 of this act do not take
effect, the public welfare requiring it.
(c) For all other purposes, this act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public
welfare requiring it, and applies to licenses, permits, certificates, authorizations, or
registrations applied for or renewed on or after that date.
