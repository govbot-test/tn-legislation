HOUSE RESOLUTION 143
By Johnson
A RESOLUTION to honor the memory of Dr. Ashleigh Elain
McKinzie.
WHEREAS, the members of this legislative body along with the members of the Middle
Tennessee State University community were greatly saddened to learn of the tragic passing of
Dr. Ashleigh Elain McKinzie; and
WHEREAS, a tenured professor of Sociology and Women's and Gender Studies at
Middle Tennessee State University, Dr. McKinzie was fatally shot on December 4, 2025; and
WHEREAS, born on April 27, 1984, in Arkansas, Dr. McKinzie graduated from the
University of Georgia, earning her Doctor of Philosophy degree in Sociology before becoming a
professor at Middle Tennessee State University in 2017; and
WHEREAS, a professor for nine years before her untimely death, Dr. Ashleigh McKinzie
had an immeasurable passion for education, teaching topics such as sociological theory, social
inequality, global feminism, and racial and gender inequalities; and
WHEREAS, Dr. McKinzie's passion for education extended far beyond the classroom,
having served as a member of several graduate students' thesis committees; she collaborated
with and empowered students to complete their master's degree theses and conduct their own
research, with many of her students inspired to publish their research and continue their
education through doctoral programs; and
WHEREAS, Dr. McKinzie was devoted to discovering and discussing inequalities
throughout society; her research explored inequalities exacerbated by natural disasters, and
she applied her knowledge and experience of social inequalities by teaching her students and
advocating for the dismantling of power hierarchies and inequalities; and

WHEREAS, Dr. Ashleigh McKinzie will be remembered for her vivacious spirit and her
ability to light up a room with her vibrant presence; and
WHEREAS, Dr. McKinzie is deeply loved and appreciated by her students and
colleagues alike, and she leaves behind an inspiring legacy of education, mentorship, and
advocacy and spreading love, joy, and positivity; and
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should pay tribute to her bountiful life of purpose and
service; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and
celebrate the life of Dr. Ashleigh Elain McKinzie, extending our sympathy and offering our
condolences to her family, friends, colleagues, students, and the Middle Tennessee State
University community.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
