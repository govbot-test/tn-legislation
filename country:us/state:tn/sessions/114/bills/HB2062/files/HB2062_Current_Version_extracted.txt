Section 1:
By Akbari
HOUSE BILL 2062
By Towns
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 39,
Chapter 17, relative to marijuana.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 39-17-418(b), is amended by
deleting "one-half (1/2) ounce (14.175 grams)" and substituting "five (5) ounces (141.748
grams)".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 39-17-418(c)(1), is amended by
deleting the subdivision and substituting the following:
(1) Except as provided in subsections (d) and (e), a violation of this section is a
Class A misdemeanor. Possession of less than five (5) ounces (141.748 grams) of
marijuana is punishable by a five-hundred-dollar fine for a first offense and a one-
thousand-dollar fine for a second or subsequent offense.

Section 4:
SECTION 3. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 39-17-417(g)(1), is amended by
deleting "one-half (1/2) ounce (14.175 grams)" and substituting "five (5) ounces (141.748
grams)".

Section 5:
SECTION 4. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.
