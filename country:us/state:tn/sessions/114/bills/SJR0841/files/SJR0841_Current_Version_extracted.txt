SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 841
By Roberts
A RESOLUTION to honor Irene Ward upon her exemplary service
to the Senate on the occasion of her retirement.
WHEREAS, the members of the General Assembly wish to recognize a highly skilled
and talented professional who, throughout her long and distinguished tenure with the legislature,
evidenced the dedication and excellence of a true public servant; and
WHEREAS, Irene Ward retired from the General Assembly on August 15, 2025, after
thirty-eight years of meritorious service to the Senate; and
WHEREAS, Irene Ward is a native Nashvillian whose family has longstanding ties to the
city and its business, civic, and social communities; and
WHEREAS, a graduate of Hollins College, Irene Ward worked in several jobs before
finding her true métier with Senator Douglas Henry in 1972; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Ward served as Senator Henry's personal administrative assistant for
fifteen years before joining the legislative staff in July 1987; and
WHEREAS, until Senator Henry's retirement in 2014, Irene Ward was his right-hand, Girl
Friday, advisor, confidante, and friend, serving as a vital component of his legislative office; and
WHEREAS, she then worked for the Senate Clerk's office for the remainder of her
career, working out of the office of Senator Kerry Roberts until the General Assembly moved to
the Cordell Hull Building in 2017; and
WHEREAS, Irene Ward manned the first floor help desk at the Cordell Hull Building,
assisting lobbyists, staff members, constituents, and other visitors find their way and providing
essential information to the public; and
WHEREAS, Irene Ward brought immense positivity to her final legislative assignment
through her warm and friendly demeanor; and

WHEREAS, Ms. Ward consistently upheld the highest standards of integrity while
working for the Senate, demonstrating a strong commitment to ethical principles, both in her
work and her personal conduct; and
WHEREAS, beyond her professional excellence, Irene Ward is a genuine and intentional
friend; she brought smiles to people throughout the legislative complex and built meaningful,
lasting friendships with her colleagues; and
WHEREAS, Irene Ward exemplifies the spirit and allegiance to community that are
characteristic of a true Tennessean; and
WHEREAS, throughout her estimable career, Irene Ward demonstrated the utmost
professionalism, ability, and integrity, winning the unbridled respect and admiration of her
colleagues and legislators alike; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Irene Ward upon her
exemplary service to the Senate, thank her for making the General Assembly a more joyful
workplace, and extend to her our heartfelt wishes for a happy and fulfilling retirement and every
continued success in her future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
