SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 701
By Bailey
A RESOLUTION to congratulate the White County Fair and its
board, staff, and volunteers upon being named
the 2026 Champion of Champions Fair by the
Tennessee Association of Fairs.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pay tribute to
those events that promote local business, entertain and educate our citizens, and celebrate the
history and culture of the great State of Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, the White County Fair is one such estimable event, which recently received
the most prestigious honor of being named the 2026 Champion of Champions Fair by the
Tennessee Association of Fairs; and
WHEREAS, among the many county, regional, and state awards that the Tennessee
Association of Fairs annually presents, the Champion of Champions is the organization's
highest accolade; and
WHEREAS, the Champion of Champions title celebrates fairs that demonstrate
exceptional leadership, innovation, community involvement, and overall excellence; earning this
honor places the White County Fair as the best in the State and highlights the tireless efforts of
its board, volunteers, partners, and supporters; and
WHEREAS, the success of the White County Fair and its well-earned recognition bring
great pride to the people of White County, who every year unite with tireless dedication to
celebrate and promote local business, agriculture, and history with this time-honored event;
now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we congratulate the White County Fair and its
