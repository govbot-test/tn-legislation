SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1143
By Reeves
A RESOLUTION to honor Rosalie "Rose" Clement upon the
occasion of her graduation from the fifth grade.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should pay tribute to those students who, through their
natural talents and commitment to academic achievement and community involvement, have
distinguished themselves in the classroom and beyond; and
WHEREAS, Rosalie "Rose" Clement is one such exemplary young Tennessean, who
will graduate from the fifth grade at McFadden School of Excellence in Murfreesboro on May 27,
2026; and
WHEREAS, in her elementary school career, Rose Clement has demonstrated
commendable academic achievement, maintaining a 4.0 grade point average while actively
participating in a number of extracurricular and service-oriented activities; and
WHEREAS, she is a member of the National Beta Club and Rutherford County 4-H,
where she has earned recognition in both speech and demonstration competitions, including top
placements at the county level; and
WHEREAS, she has further distinguished herself through participation in academic and
creative activities at her school, including service on the student broadcast team; and
WHEREAS, Rose Clement has shown dedication and discipline through her
advancement in martial arts, achieving Go Kyu rank certification through the U.S. Eastern
Wado-Ryu Karate-Do Federation; and
WHEREAS, she has also demonstrated a commitment to community involvement
through volunteer service and participation in youth programs that encourage leadership,
creativity, and service to others; and

WHEREAS, Rose Clement's enthusiasm for learning, her willingness to serve others,
and her positive spirit are a source of pride to her family, her school, and her community; and
WHEREAS, her dedication to excellence, even at a young age, is clearly deserving of
our respect, admiration, and commendation; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Rosalie "Rose" Clement
upon the occasion of her graduation from the fifth grade and extend to her our best wishes for
continued success and happiness in all her future pursuits.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
