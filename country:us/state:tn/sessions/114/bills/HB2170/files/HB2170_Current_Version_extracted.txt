HOUSE BILL 2170
By Travis
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 56,
Chapter 7, relative to health care.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 56-7-2003(7), is amended by
deleting "files an annual report" and substituting "files an annual report, which may be filed
electronically,".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
