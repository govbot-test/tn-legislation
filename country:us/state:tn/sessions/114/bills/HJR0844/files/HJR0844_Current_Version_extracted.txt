HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 844
By Stinnett
A RESOLUTION to commend and congratulate Horace Stephens
upon his receipt of the Legacy of Service Award
from the Alcoa City Schools Foundation.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should pause to specially recognize those outstanding
teachers who have devoted their careers to providing the highest-quality education for
Tennessee's students; and
WHEREAS, Horace Stephens was recently recognized by the Alcoa City Schools
Foundation with the Legacy of Service Award, a most befitting honor that acknowledges the
positive influence he has had on so many young lives; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Stephens began his legendary tenure with Alcoa City Schools in 1970
and served for more than forty-four years; a beloved math teacher, he taught physics, physical
science, pre-algebra, and Algebra I, impacting generations of Alcoa students with his high
expectations, steady encouragement, and genuine care; and
WHEREAS, his kindness, dedication, and love for his students left a lasting impression
that continues to be felt throughout the school community; and
WHEREAS, Horace Stephens has long been an inspiration to his colleagues and
students alike, and his friendship and counsel are highly valued by those who are fortunate to
know him; and
WHEREAS, he truly epitomizes the dedicated teacher who is wholly committed to the
highest ideals of public service and quality education for all; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Stephens represents the heart of Alcoa City Schools, and his lifelong
commitment to serving students makes him truly deserving of this special recognition; now,
therefore,
HJR0844
011847
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby congratulate Horace Stephens upon his receipt of the Legacy of
Service Award from the Alcoa City Schools Foundation, commend his exceptional service to the
children of Tennessee, salute his professional expertise and personal excellence, and wish him
all the best in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
- 2 - 011847
