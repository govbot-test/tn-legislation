SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1059
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to commend and congratulate FFA national
competitor Emma Hummer.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly take great pleasure in recognizing
those outstanding young scholars who have distinguished themselves as emerging leaders in
Tennessee's agriculture industry; and
WHEREAS, one such individual is Emma Hummer, who recently represented
Tennessee at the National FFA Nursery-Landscape Career Development Event, placing eighth
in the nation and earning a Gold Emblem alongside her fellow teammates from the Erwin FFA
chapter; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Hummer and her team worked together during a competition that
required them to complete a series of tasks related to the nursery and landscaping industry,
including plant identification, plant propagation techniques, surveying, and landscape design
calculations; and
WHEREAS, Future Farmers of America, now known as FFA, was organized in 1928 to
promote leadership, personal growth, and career success among high school agriculture
students; and
WHEREAS, for many years, FFA has played an important role in ensuring the growth
and prosperity of agriculture in Tennessee and the nation by expertly molding our future
leaders; and
WHEREAS, the success of this State, the strength of our communities, and the overall
vitality of our society depend, in great measure, on the dedication of our students and our
agricultural innovators, who will ensure Tennessee continues to lead the nation in agricultural
production for generations to come; now, therefore,
