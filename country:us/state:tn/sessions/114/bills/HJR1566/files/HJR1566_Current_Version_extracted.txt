HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1566
By Reneau
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Walden's Ridge
Emergency Service on the celebration of its
fiftieth anniversary.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly take great pleasure in recognizing
those honorable institutions that have operated throughout their existence for the betterment of
this State and the protection of its citizenry; and
WHEREAS, one such venerable institution is Walden's Ridge Emergency Service, which
is celebrating its fiftieth anniversary this year; and
WHEREAS, Walden's Ridge Emergency Service was established in 1976 and has
grown from humble beginnings into a highly respected volunteer organization dedicated to the
protection of life and property; and
WHEREAS, composed of more than fifty nonpaid men and women, the members of
Walden's Ridge Emergency Service are devoted volunteers who stand ready at all hours to
respond to emergencies, driven by a steadfast commitment to serve their neighbors, friends,
and families in times of need; and
WHEREAS, providing essential services to the Town of Walden and unincorporated
areas of Hamilton County on Signal Mountain, the organization also responds to calls in the
Town of Signal Mountain, the Lone Oak community, the City of Red Bank, and other
surrounding areas as requested; and
WHEREAS, these dedicated volunteers respond to more than three hundred emergency
calls each year, including structure fires, vehicle accidents, medical emergencies, rescue
operations in mountainous terrain, and hazardous material incidents, demonstrating courage,
skill, and composure under pressure; and

WHEREAS, operating from two locations on Taft Highway and Sawyer Pike, Walden's
Ridge Emergency Service has established itself as an indispensable resource for the
community it serves; and
WHEREAS, through its participation in the Tri-State Mutual Aid Association, Walden's
Ridge Emergency Service is linked with numerous departments across Southeast Tennessee,
Northwest Georgia, and Northeast Alabama, ensuring coordinated and effective responses to
large-scale emergencies; and
WHEREAS, the members of Walden's Ridge Emergency Service epitomize the ideal of
dedicated public servants and reliable neighbors, and because of their remarkable efforts,
countless lives have been protected and many crises averted; and
WHEREAS, since the establishment of Walden's Ridge Emergency Service, its
members have upheld the finest tenets and traditions of emergency medical service, and it is
wholly fitting that we recognize them for their many years of service to their community and this
State; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate the members of Walden's Ridge Emergency
Service upon the celebration of the organization's fiftieth anniversary, extending to them our
best wishes for every future success and thanking them for their tireless and unwavering service
to this State.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
