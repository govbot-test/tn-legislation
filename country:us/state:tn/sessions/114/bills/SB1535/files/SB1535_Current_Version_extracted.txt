HOUSE BILL 1622
By Lafferty
SENATE BILL 1535
By Jackson
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 4,
Chapter 29 and Title 40, Chapter 30, Part 2,
relative to the post-conviction defender oversight
commission.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 4-29-247(a), is amended by deleting
subdivision (41).
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 4-29-253(a), is amended by inserting
the following as a new subdivision:
( ) Post-conviction defender oversight commission, created by § 40-30-203;
SECTION 3. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
