SENATE RESOLUTION 264
By Harshbarger
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Appalachian Miles for
Smiles.
WHEREAS, Appalachian Miles for Smiles is a faith-based ministry and nonprofit
organization dedicated to delivering compassionate healthcare services to underserved and
medically vulnerable populations throughout Northeast Tennessee and Southwest Virginia; and
WHEREAS, since its founding in 2016, Appalachian Miles for Smiles has provided free,
life-changing healthcare services to approximately 21,000 individuals across more than fifteen
counties in Tennessee and eight counties in Southwest Virginia, delivering an estimated ten
million dollars in services back to local communities; and
WHEREAS, Appalachian Miles for Smiles operates five mobile medical units that
function as traveling clinics, enabling the organization to reach rural and underserved areas
where access to basic health care, particularly dental and vision services, is often limited; and
WHEREAS, the organization travels nine months out of the year and conducts twenty-
four or more clinics annually, with each clinic operating as a two-day event to ensure meaningful
access to care for those in need; and
WHEREAS, services provided through Appalachian Miles for Smiles clinics include
dental and vision care, audiology and pulmonary services, mammograms, and women's health
services, all offered at no cost to patients; and
WHEREAS, rooted in a mission of service and faith, Appalachian Miles for Smiles is
committed to treating every individual with dignity, compassion, and respect, seeking not only to
address physical healthcare needs but also to restore hope, promote wellness, and
demonstrate genuine care for neighbors through acts of service and kindness; and

WHEREAS, through its continued dedication to improving lives and expanding access to
essential health care, Appalachian Miles for Smiles exemplifies the power of community-driven
service and compassionate outreach; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and commend
Appalachian Miles for Smiles for its outstanding service to underserved communities, extending
our deepest appreciation for its commitment to compassionate health care and community well-
being and our best wishes for every continued success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
