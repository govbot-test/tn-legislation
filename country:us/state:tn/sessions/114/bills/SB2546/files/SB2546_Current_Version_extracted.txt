Section 1:
By Todd
SENATE BILL 2546
By Reeves
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 4;
Title 5; Title 6; Title 7; Title 11; Title 64; Title 67
and Title 68, relative to solid waste management.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 68-211-107(c), is amended by
adding the following language immediately after the first sentence in the subsection:
The department may authorize facilities to submit the reports required by this subsection
(c) in electronic format.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.
