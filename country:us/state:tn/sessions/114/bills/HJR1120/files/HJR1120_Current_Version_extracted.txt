HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1120
By Capley
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Olivia Townsend, 2026
Valedictorian of Spring Hill High School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Olivia Townsend is one such exceptional student, who was recently named
Valedictorian of the 2026 graduating class at Spring Hill High School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Ms. Townsend has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in her remarkable high school career, Olivia Townsend has earned an
exemplary grade point average while participating in numerous extracurricular activities,
including marching band, National Honor Society, jazz band, All Maury County Honor Band, and
concert band, as well as serving as a member of the Spring Hill Scholar Academy, Overture
Music Academy, and the Catholic Church of the Nativity Fidelis Chapter; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Townsend has additionally accumulated many hours of community
service as a volunteer with organizations and programs such as Vacation Bible School, the
Farm Bureau University of Tennessee Extension of Agriculture, and the Catholic Church of the
Nativity; and
WHEREAS, her display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well for her
future success as a student at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville, where she will study
wildlife biology; and

WHEREAS, in addition to her impressive academic portfolio, Ms. Townsend was a
National History Day Group Exhibit Board State Finalist and finished in third place; she also
enjoys crocheting, painting, hiking, and reading; and
WHEREAS, Olivia Townsend's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride
for her parents, David and Jennifer Townsend, her school, and her community; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Townsend's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of this General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor the 2026 Spring Hill High School
Valedictorian, Olivia Townsend, on her impressive academic achievement and extend to her our
best wishes for a happy, successful future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
