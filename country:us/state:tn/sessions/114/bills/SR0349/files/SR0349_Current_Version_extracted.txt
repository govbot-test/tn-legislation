SENATE RESOLUTION 349
By Hensley
A RESOLUTION to commend Dylan Binns for exemplary service
as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this legislative body is pleased to recognize certain
persons who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment of the
legislative process; and
WHEREAS, Dylan Binns is one such individual, who has distinguished himself in his role
as a legislative intern for Senator Joey Hensley; Mr. Binns's work has resulted in innumerable
benefits for the legislators he assisted and the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, a Peruvian-American born and raised in Nashville, Dylan Binns is a senior
at Belmont University, where he is highly regarded by his peers; he has achieved academic
success majoring in political science with a minor in economics, while participating in numerous
extracurricular activities; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Binns is passionate about politics and the workings of government and
aspires to pursue a career in either state or federal government; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize himself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further his studies, Dylan Binns brought his many abilities
and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2026 session of the One Hundred Fourteenth
General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, throughout the 2026 legislative session, Mr. Binns has surpassed all criteria
that define a devoted and conscientious legislative intern; and
WHEREAS, Dylan Binns has performed his many duties with extraordinary insight and
rare decorum; now, therefore,
SR0349
016067
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby commend Dylan
Binns for outstanding service as a legislative intern for Senator Joey Hensley and extend to him
our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
- 2 - 016067
