Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 5-5-102(c)(1), is amended by
deleting the subdivision and substituting instead:
(1) A county employee is disqualified from serving as a member of the county
legislative body. This subdivision (c)(1) does not apply to a county employee serving on
a county commission on the effective date of this act.
