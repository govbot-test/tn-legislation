HOUSE RESOLUTION 199
By Lamberth
A RESOLUTION to honor Terry Vantrease for his exemplary
service as a Sergeant at Arms for the House of
Representatives.
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that the members of this legislative body should honor
those employees who have performed with uncommon devotion and enthusiasm throughout
their tenure with the Tennessee General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, Terry Vantrease is one such estimable person, who has served with
distinction as a Sergeant at Arms for the House of Representatives since 2016; and
WHEREAS, in his capacity as a Sergeant at Arms, Mr. Vantrease has rendered sterling
service to countless House members and staff; he will be sorely missed by the legislators and
staff he so ably assisted; and
WHEREAS, born in Royal Oak, Michigan, Mr. Vantrease moved to Tennessee at the
age of seven; during his years of industry, he has served tenures as the manager of John
Hatcher's Men's Clothing, assistant business manager of Cumberland Mental Health Services in
Lebanon, and in office administration for the Nashville Convention Center; and
WHEREAS, prior to his service with the General Assembly, he worked for the Nashville
International Airport for sixteen years as an accounts payable clerk, in which position he was
responsible for all expenses for the airport, including all projects; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Vantrease is a gallant military veteran, having served with distinction for
six years in the Army National Guard in Tullahoma; and
WHEREAS, a gentleman of deep and abiding faith, he is an ordained Baptist minister
and has pastored two churches for a total of nine years; and
WHEREAS, a devoted husband and father, Terry Vantrease will celebrate his fiftieth
wedding anniversary with his beloved wife, Kathy Vantrease, in July; they are the proud parents
of two sons and the loving grandparents of three grandchildren and one great-grandchild; and

WHEREAS, Terry Vantrease exemplifies the spirit and allegiance to family and
community that are characteristic of a true Tennessean; and
WHEREAS, throughout his estimable tenure as a House Sergeant at Arms, Terry
Vantrease has demonstrated the utmost professionalism, ability, and integrity, winning the
unbridled respect and admiration of his colleagues and the members of the General Assembly;
now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and
commend Terry Vantrease upon his exemplary service to the House of Representatives as a
Sergeant at Arms, express our gratitude to him for his many contributions to good government,
and congratulate him on a job well done.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
