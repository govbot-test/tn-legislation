SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 917
By Bailey
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Taylor Key on her
selection as the Putnam County Schools High
School Teacher of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
specially recognize those outstanding teachers who have devoted their careers to providing the
highest-quality education for Tennessee's students; and
WHEREAS, Taylor Key is one such estimable educator, who has distinguished herself
as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Key has been selected as the Putnam County Schools High School
Teacher of the Year, a most befitting honor that recognizes the positive influence she has on so
many young lives; and
WHEREAS, a teacher at Upperman High School, Taylor Key is an extremely talented
and compassionate educator who works assiduously to motivate her students to reach their full
potential, both as scholars and as citizens; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Key serves as an inspiration to her colleagues and students alike, and
her friendship and counsel are highly valued by those who are fortunate to know her; and
WHEREAS, Taylor Key truly epitomizes the dedicated teacher who is wholly committed
to the highest ideals of public service and quality education for all, and she should be specially
recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and congratulate Taylor Key on
being chosen as the Putnam County Schools High School Teacher of the Year, commend her
