Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 22-1-103, is amended by adding the
following as a new subsection:
( ) A mother who is breastfeeding a child is excused from jury service upon
providing the court with a supporting medical statement from a licensed physician or a
certified professional midwife. The jury coordinator of the county shall excuse a mother
who is breastfeeding a child from jury service upon receiving a written request to be
excused and a supporting medical statement from a licensed physician or a certified
professional midwife.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
