HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1421
By Keisling
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Izabella Gansore upon
being named the 2026 Boys & Girls Club of The
Cumberland Plateau Youth of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly pause in their
deliberations to salute those young citizens who, through their remarkable efforts, have
distinguished themselves as emerging community and state leaders of whom we can all be
proud; and
WHEREAS, one such deserving person, Izabella Gansore, was recently named the
2026 Boys & Girls Club of The Cumberland Plateau Youth of the Year; and
WHEREAS, Izabella Gansore, a student at Scott High School, has been a member of
the Boys & Girls Club of The Cumberland Plateau for seven years; and
WHEREAS, the Boys and Girls Clubs exist to serve youth and to be a safe place of
encouragement, character building, security, and love for children; and
WHEREAS, since 1947, the Youth of the Year has been the Boys & Girls Clubs of
America's premier recognition program, celebrating the extraordinary achievements of Club
members; and
WHEREAS, Youth of the Year participants embody the values of leadership, service,
academic excellence, and healthy lifestyles; and
WHEREAS, Youth of the Year participants like Izabella Gansore exemplify the critical
impact of Boys & Girls Clubs on the lives of young people; and
WHEREAS, through the Youth of the Year program, Izabella Gansore and other Youth
of the Year participants are able to showcase their talents and achievements, share their hopes
and dreams, and work toward a bright and positive future; and
HJR1421
017376
- 1 -
WHEREAS, Izabella Gansore is a shining example that "great futures start here"–at
Boys & Girls Club; and
WHEREAS, the success of this State, the strength of our communities, and the overall
vitality of American society depend, in great measure, upon the dedication of young people such
as Izabella Gansore, who use their considerable talents and resources to serve others and who
should be specially recognized for their public and community service; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Izabella Gansore upon being named the 2026
Boys & Girls Club of The Cumberland Plateau Youth of the Year, extending to her our best
wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
