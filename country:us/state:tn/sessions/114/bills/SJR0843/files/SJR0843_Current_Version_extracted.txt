SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 843
By Powers
A RESOLUTION to congratulate Cathy Kolb on her appointment
to the State Board of Education.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should salute those
citizens who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as community
leaders of whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such noteworthy individual is Cathy Kolb, who was recently appointed
for a second term to the State Board of Education by Speaker Randy McNally as a member
representing the Seventh Congressional District; and
WHEREAS, a highly respected member of her community, Cathy Kolb is an educator
with more than thirty years of experience and currently serves as a special education teacher at
Moore Magnet STEM Elementary School for the Clarksville-Montgomery County School
System; and
WHEREAS, Mrs. Kolb holds a Bachelor of Science degree from Western Kentucky
University and Master of Arts and Education Specialist degrees from Austin Peay State
University; and
WHEREAS, Cathy Kolb epitomizes the spirit and commitment that are characteristic of a
true Tennessean, and it is appropriate that we acknowledge and applaud her for her dedication
to serving the people of the State of Tennessee; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we congratulate Cathy Kolb on her appointment to
the State Board of Education and extend to her our best wishes for much continued success.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
