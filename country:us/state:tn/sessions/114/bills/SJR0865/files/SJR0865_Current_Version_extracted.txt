SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 865
By Seal
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Rusty Provins, 2012-
2014 TSSAA Class A-AA Boys' Track and Field
Discus and Shot Put State Champion.
WHEREAS, we are pleased to specially honor and commend those successful athletes
who, through their magnificent exploits, have brought honor to Tennessee and serve as
exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable athlete is Rusty Provins, who, as a student at
Cumberland Gap High School, won the 2012-2014 TSSAA Class A-AA Boys' Track and Field
Discus and Shot Put State Championships; and
WHEREAS, born on October 5, 1995, Mr. Provins was a five-time State Champion in the
discus and shot put events from 2012 to 2014; in addition to his high school athletic career, he
was valedictorian of his graduating class and signed with Wake Forest Track and Field; and
WHEREAS, Rusty Provins has proudly served his nation as a captain in the United
States Army, Army Ranger, in the 82nd Airborne Division at Fort Liberty, North Carolina; and
WHEREAS, his many accomplishments aside, Mr. Provins has been most grateful for
the love and support of his wife, Hannah Woods Provins; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Provins's noteworthy achievement has been a tremendous source of
pride for his family, his alma mater, and his community; and
WHEREAS, Rusty Provins's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of the members of this General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Rusty Provins on
