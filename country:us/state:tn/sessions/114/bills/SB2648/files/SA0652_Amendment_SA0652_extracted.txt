Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 55-9-302(a), is amended by deleting
the section and substituting instead:
The driver of a motorcycle, motorized bicycle, or motorscooter, and a passenger
on any of these vehicles, is required to wear a crash helmet meeting federal standards
contained in 49 CFR 571.218 if the driver or passenger is younger than twenty-one (21)
years of age, unless the driver or passenger is riding:
(1) Within an enclosed cab;
(2) An autocycle that is fully enclosed, which means it has side panels, a
roll bar or roof, and a windshield;
(3) A golf cart; or
(4) In a parade, at a speed not to exceed thirty (30) miles per hour, and
the driver or passenger is between eighteen (18) and twenty-one (21) years of
age.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.
