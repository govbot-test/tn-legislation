Amendment No. 7 to HB0793
Pearson
Signature of Sponsor
by adding the following as a new subsection in the amendatory language of Section 1:
(d) Notwithstanding subsection (a), an LEA or public charter school shall not
require the documentation described in subsection (a) to be produced for a student if the
student's parent or legal guardian opts their student out of providing the documentation.
