Section 1:
Amendment No. 6 to HB0793
Pearson
Signature of Sponsor
by adding the following as a new subsection in the amendatory language of Section 1:
( ) Notwithstanding this section to the contrary, an LEA or public charter school
may opt out of requiring all school-age students to produce the documentation described
in subsection (a) and may opt out of providing the information described in subsection
(b) to the department of education if the LEA or public charter school notifies the
department in writing that the LEA or public charter school is opting out of the
requirements of subsection (a) or subsection (b), or both. An LEA or public charter
school shall not be penalized or subject to any adverse action for the LEA's public
charter school's decision to opt out of one (1) or more of the requirements of this section.
