Section 1:
Amendment No. 4 to HB0793
Jones J
Signature of Sponsor
by adding the following as a new subsection in the amendatory language of Section 1:
(d) Notwithstanding subsections (a) and (b), an LEA or public charter school
may elect not to collect the student data described in subsection (a) and not to report
any such information maintained by the LEA or public charter school for one (1) or more
students to the department of education by notifying the department of education in
writing of its decision to opt out of the requirements in subsections (a) and (b). An LEA
or public charter school that opts out of the requirements in subsections (a) and (b) shall
not be penalized or subject to any adverse action by any agency of this state for electing
not to participate in the data collection and reporting requirements of this section.
