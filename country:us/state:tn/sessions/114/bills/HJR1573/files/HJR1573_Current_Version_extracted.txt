HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1573
By Sparks
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Isaac Mozingo, 2026
Valedictorian of Lancaster Christian Academy.
WHEREAS, it is only fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
pay tribute to those students in our State who, through their own natural abilities and
unwavering pursuit of academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom;
and
WHEREAS, Isaac Mozingo is one such individual, who has been named Valedictorian of
the 2026 graduating class at Lancaster Christian Academy; and
WHEREAS, through dedication to his studies, careful recollection of vast amounts of
data, composure through his examinations, and his steadfast commitment of time and talent,
Mr. Mozingo has achieved a remarkable academic feat and joined the ranks of the brightest and
most notable students in the State of Tennessee and the nation; and
WHEREAS, in his educational career, Mr. Mozingo has maintained an exemplary grade
point average while participating in a number of extracurricular clubs and activities, including his
participation in the Beta Club, the SERVE class at Lancaster Christian Academy, and as part of
the LCA Bible Class's community service project; and
WHEREAS, his intellectual aptitude and ambition most certainly bode well for his future
endeavors; and
WHEREAS, Issac Mozingo's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride
for his parents, Mark and Kimberly Mozingo; his siblings, Kenna and Alexandra; his school; and
his community; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Mozingo's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of this General Assembly; now, therefore,
HJR1573
018516
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor Isaac Mozingo of Lancaster Christian
Academy on being named the 2026 Valedictorian, salute his impressive academic achievement,
and extend to him our best wishes for a happy, successful future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
