HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 988
By Lynn
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Andrew Jason Kumm
Sonnichsen on earning the distinguished rank of
Eagle Scout.
WHEREAS, at a time when this nation's youth face difficult challenges, the Boy Scouts
of America is one of our finest assets, providing young people with an educational program that
contributes significantly to their character development, citizenship training, and improved
mental and physical fitness; and
WHEREAS, the highest rank a Scout can attain in the Boy Scouts of America Scouts
BSA program is that of Eagle Scout, and Andrew Sonnichsen, after considerable effort and
diligent preparation, will join the prestigious Eagle Court of Honor on April 11, 2026; and
WHEREAS, as a member of Troop 246 of Middle Tennessee, Mr. Sonnichsen has
earned a notable record of achievement that includes numerous merit badges, distinguished
service as a leader among his peers, and dedication to his community; and
WHEREAS, Andrew Sonnichsen's Eagle project greatly contributed to the betterment of
his community and reflected the true spirit of volunteerism; for his project, he led the removal
and complete rebuilding of a stairway providing access to a dog walking area at True Rescue
Animal Shelter in Mt. Juliet, organizing volunteers to remove the old stairway and design the
new stairway and complete its construction; this new, secure stairway ensures safe access for
shelter volunteers and the animals in their care now and for many years to come; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Sonnichsen's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride
for his family, his school, his community, and this State; and
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that we should pause to recognize Andrew Sonnichsen as
one of the most hardworking and outstanding young people in our State; now, therefore,

BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and commend Andrew Jason Kumm Sonnichsen on
earning the distinguished rank of Eagle Scout, extending to him our best wishes for much
continued success in the years to come.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
