House State & Local Government 2
Amendment No. 2 to HB1711
Crawford
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 2108 House Bill No. 1711*
by deleting § 4-1-605(a) in SECTION 1 and substituting:
(a)
(1) Each LEA and public charter school shall submit the report required pursuant
to title 49, chapter 6, part 30 that identifies the number of students enrolled in the LEA or
public charter school who produced or failed to produce documentation to establish the
student's lawful presence in the United States to the department of education on or
before January 1, 2027, with such data the LEA or public charter school has collected for
the 2026-2027 school year to date, and by each January 1 thereafter for the immediately
preceding school year.
(2) The department of education shall determine the cost for each student who
produced or failed to produce documentation to establish the student's lawful presence
in the United States in each LEA and public charter school.
(3) On or before January 31, 2027, and by each January 31 thereafter, the
department of education shall submit an aggregated report consisting of the data
collected in subdivision (a)(1) and the cost per student determined pursuant to
subdivision (a)(2) to the collecting entities.
