SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 679
By Bailey
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend the members and
coaches of the Upperman Lady Bees basketball
team upon winning the Andrew Johnson Bank
Ladies Classic basketball tournament.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that this General Assembly should salute those student athletes
who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as true champions of
whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such exemplary group of young women is the Upperman High School
Lady Bees basketball team, whose members have proven themselves to be the fiercest of
competitors and truly worthy of admiration by winning the Andrew Johnson Bank Ladies Classic
basketball tournament; and
WHEREAS, the Bees have been buzzing all season, but winning the tournament
championship for the first time in six attempts by defeating the Bearden Lady Bulldogs 59-50 at
Hal Henard Gym in Greeneville will definitely be a cherished accomplishment as they look back
on this season; and
WHEREAS, Upperman's run to the title included a 61-34 win over Gaffney, South
Carolina, a 54-51 win over Farmington, Arkansas, and a 63-55 win over Daniel Boone High
School in the semifinals; and
WHEREAS, trailing at halftime in the championship final, the young women from
Upperman rallied to claim the victory over Bearden, thanks to standout performances on the
court by Offensive MVP Taylor Dolente, all-tournament selection Sadie South, and teammates
Cassie Crouch, Gracie Butler, and Maggie Butler; and

WHEREAS, the Lady Bees' superb performance would not have been possible without
the stellar leadership of Head Coach Dana McWilliams and the unfailing support of their
families, their fans, and their community; and
WHEREAS, the members of the Upperman Lady Bees basketball team epitomize all that
is good in today's student athletes, as they have achieved a premier level of success in
competition while exhibiting the highest degree of character in all aspects of life; and
WHEREAS, the perseverance, determination, work ethic, and talent of this group of
young women not only made possible a tournament championship but also surely bode well for
their future success; and
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly find it appropriate to acknowledge
and applaud the Upperman Lady Bees for their success on the hardwood; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend the members and coaches
of the Upperman High School Lady Bees basketball team upon winning the Andrew Johnson
Bank Ladies Classic basketball tournament and extend to them our best wishes for every
continued success in all their future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
