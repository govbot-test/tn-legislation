HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1286
By Bulso
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Stephen Smith on
being named the 2025 WILLCO Awards Boys'
Tennis Player of the Year.
WHEREAS, we are pleased to recognize and commend those successful high school
athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring honor to Tennessee and serve as
exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable individual is Stephen Smith of Brentwood High School,
an athlete of extraordinary talent, skill, and dedication, who has proven himself to be a
formidable competitor in tennis; and
WHEREAS, in recognition of his success on the court, Stephen Smith has been named
the WILLCO Awards Boys' Tennis Player of the Year for the second consecutive year; and
WHEREAS, Stephen Smith and his doubles partner, Drew Miller, teamed up to repeat
as Class AA State champions; and
WHEREAS, Stephen Smith epitomizes all that is good in today's student athlete, as he
has achieved a high level of success in tennis while evincing great character; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Smith's notable achievements are a source of great pride for his family,
his community, and his school, and it is appropriate that we recognize this accomplished
athlete; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Stephen Smith on being named the 2025
WILLCO Awards Boys' Tennis Player of the Year, wishing him much success and happiness in
his future endeavors.
