House Judiciary 1
Amendment No. 1 to HB1903
Farmer
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 1935* House Bill No. 1903
by adding the following sentence at the end of the amendatory language in SECTION 4:
Subject to § 529A of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 (26 U.S.C. § 529A), as
amended and all rules, regulations, notices, and interpretations released by the United
States department of treasury, including the internal revenue service, and applicable
laws and rules of this state relative to the achieving a better life experience act, some or
all of the thirty-five thousand dollar ($35,000) cash payment up to the achieving a better
life experience program annual contribution limit, made to a minor child or a child
eighteen (18) years of age or older meeting the qualifications to participate in the
achieving a better life experience program, may be deposited into the child's achieving a
better life experience program account by the child or someone on behalf of the child,
including, but not limited to, a court order requiring the cash deposit into the child's
achieving a better life experience program account.
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting SECTION 6 and substituting:
SECTION 6. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
