Amendment No. 2 to SB2634
Roberts
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 2, Chapter 1, is amended by adding the
following as a new section:
(a) This section is known and may be cited as "Colby's Law."
(b) Each public institution of higher education shall, on the date of a regular
November election, implement reasonable measures to promote civic engagement and
facilitate student participation in the election process, such as providing information
about voting locations, encouraging community volunteerism and service for civic
engagement, and removing unnecessary barriers for student participation in the electoral
process.
(c) An institution may observe an academic holiday on the date of a regular
November election. An institution that elects to observe an academic holiday on the
date of a regular November election is not required to close the institution or to suspend
its operations, including, but not limited to, clinical programs, research activities,
internships, practicums, professional programs, online education, student services,
health services, public safety operations, or any other essential institutional function.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
