SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 970
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Brian Carter on being
named to the All-TCCAA Second Team.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly are pleased to recognize and
commend those successful student athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring honor
to Tennessee and serve as exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable individual is Brian Carter of Walters State Community
College, an athlete of extraordinary talent, skill, and dedication, who has proven himself a
formidable competitor on the basketball court; and
WHEREAS, the Tennessee Community College Athletic Association (TCCAA) recently
named Mr. Carter to the All-TCCAA Second Team in recognition of his excellence on the court
for Walters State Community College; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Carter leads the country in field goal percentage at 78.8 percent,
averaging 12.3 points and 5.1 rebounds per game; he shot 75 percent from the free-throw line
and scored double-digit points in eight of the last ten games of the Senators' regular season;
and
WHEREAS, Brian Carter epitomizes all that is good in today's student athlete, as he has
achieved a high level of success in sports while evincing great character both on and off the
court; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Carter's notable achievement is a source of great pride for his family,
his community, and Walters State Community College, and it is appropriate that we recognize
this accomplished athlete; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
