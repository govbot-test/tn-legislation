HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 878
By Lamberth
A RESOLUTION to confirm the appointment of Joshua R. Walker
to the Tennessee Claims Commission for the
Eastern Grand Division.
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 9-8-301(a), provides for the creation
of the Tennessee Claims Commission to consist of three members, one from each Grand
Division of the State; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 9-8-302, provides that the governor
shall appoint each commissioner for a term of eight years; and
WHEREAS, Governor Bill Lee has appointed Joshua R. Walker to serve on the
Tennessee Claims Commission as the Eastern Grand Division Commissioner for a term that will
expire on June 30, 2033; and
WHEREAS, Joshua "Josh" Walker of Knoxville graduated from the University of
Tennessee College of Law, summa cum laude, Order of the Coif, and ranked second in his
2003 class; and
WHEREAS, after practicing law for eight years, Mr. Walker moved to the Office of
General Counsel for the University of Tennessee System, where his primary responsibility was
defending the State of Tennessee in claims before the Claims Commission; in this role, he
appeared in more than 160 claims before the commission; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Walker also represented the university and its employees in federal civil
rights actions and Uniform Administrative Procedures Act contested cases and advised various
university committees on workplace issues; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Walker is a person of integrity and an active citizen whose benevolence,
acumen, and strong commitment to public service have helped to enhance the well-being of this
State and its citizens; and

WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 9-8-302(a), provides that each
commissioner appointed to the Tennessee Claims Commission by the governor shall be subject
to confirmation by resolution of both houses of the general assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby confirm the appointment of Joshua R. Walker as the Eastern
Grand Division Commissioner for the Tennessee Claims Commission for an eight-year term that
will expire on June 30, 2033.
