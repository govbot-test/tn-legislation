SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 980
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend the South Greene High
School yearbook staff members on their recent
victory at the Tennessee High School Press
Association awards ceremony.
WHEREAS, this General Assembly is pleased to recognize those outstanding and gifted
students of our State who bring honor to Tennessee and who act as ambassadors for our State
through their success; and
WHEREAS, the members of the South Greene High School yearbook staff are
especially deserving of acclaim, having recently met with great success at the Tennessee High
School Press Association (THSPA) awards ceremony on March 2, 2026, at Lipscomb
University; and
WHEREAS, the South Greene High School's 2025 yearbook, "Ignite," was named an All-
Tennessee Yearbook; this distinguished recognition is the highest publication rating awarded by
the THSPA and recognizes publications that demonstrate excellence in production, writing,
photography, design, and visual storytelling; and
WHEREAS, the yearbook earned third place for Best Yearbook Overall, a category that
evaluates the entire publication for its theme development, writing, photography, design, and
overall coverage of the school year; and
WHEREAS, under the advisement and leadership of Erin Cloar Jones, several South
Greene High School students received individual honors for their exceptional work; and
WHEREAS, Brynna Wilhoit placed first for Best Organization/Clubs Copy for her story
"Bright Ideas, Big Impact" and second for Best Academic Photo for "Lumos!"; Eli Hardin placed
second for Best Academic Photo for "Controlled Fire"; Chloe Saulsbury placed third for Best

WHEREAS, the students of the South Greene High School yearbook program work

School yearbook staff members on their recent victory at the Tennessee High School Press
Association awards ceremony and wish them much continued success in the future.
