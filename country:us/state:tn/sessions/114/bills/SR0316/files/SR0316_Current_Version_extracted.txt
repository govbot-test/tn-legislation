SENATE RESOLUTION 316
By Rose
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend the Associated Builders
and Contractors West Tennessee Chapter on
the occasion of its fiftieth anniversary.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should recognize those
organizations that have made enduring contributions to the economic vitality, workforce
development, and overall well-being of the communities they serve; and
WHEREAS, the Associated Builders and Contractors (ABC) West Tennessee Chapter is
one such estimable organization, having faithfully served the construction industry and the
citizens of West Tennessee since its founding in 1976; and
WHEREAS, established to represent merit shop construction companies committed to
fair and open competition, quality workmanship, and workforce opportunity, ABC West
Tennessee has remained steadfast in its mission for five decades; and
WHEREAS, throughout its distinguished history, ABC West Tennessee has served as a
leading voice for the construction industry by advocating responsible public policy, promoting
jobsite safety, expanding workforce development, and providing education and training for
contractors, craft professionals, and future industry leaders; and
WHEREAS, the organization has played a vital role in the building of infrastructure,
commercial enterprises, healthcare facilities, educational institutions, places of worship, and
community spaces that enhance the quality of life for citizens across the Mid-South; and
WHEREAS, through accredited apprenticeship programs, safety initiatives, leadership
development, and political advocacy efforts, ABC West Tennessee has strengthened the
construction workforce and created meaningful career pathways that support families and local
economies; and

WHEREAS, the chapter has consistently championed a culture of safety, accountability,
and excellence, helping its member companies achieve high standards in productivity,
professionalism, and worker well-being; and
WHEREAS, for fifty years, the Associated Builders and Contractors West Tennessee
Chapter has remained dedicated to building people, strengthening communities, and fostering a
more prosperous future for this State; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and commend the
Associated Builders and Contractors West Tennessee Chapter on the occasion of its fiftieth
anniversary, recognizing its outstanding contributions to workforce development, economic
growth, and community well-being, and extend to its members our best wishes for continued
success in the years to come.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
