Senate Government Operations 1
Amendment No. 1 to SB2562
Jackson
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 2562 House Bill No. 2255*
by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 4-8-301, is amended by adding the
following as a new subsection:
(d) All vacancies must be filled for the balance of an unexpired term.
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 4-31-103(c), is amended by deleting
subdivision (1) and substituting.
(1) Its membership consists of the governor, the secretary of state, the state
treasurer, the comptroller of the treasury, and the commissioner of finance and
administration, and their successors in office from time to time, by virtue of their
incumbency in such offices and without further appointment or qualification, together
with one (1) member who must be appointed by the speaker of the senate and one (1)
member who must be appointed by the speaker of the house of representatives. Each
appointed member serves for a period of two (2) years, subject to reappointment, or until
such member's successor is duly appointed and qualified.
SECTION 3. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 43-13-103(d), is amended by adding
the following to the end of the subsection:
All vacancies must be filled for the balance of an unexpired term.
SECTION 4. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 54-3-113(c), is amended by adding
the following to the end of the subsection:
All vacancies must be filled for the balance of the unexpired term.

SECTION 5. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 63-1-179(b)(4)(B), is amended by
adding the following to the end of the subdivision:
All vacancies must be filled for the balance of the unexpired term.
SECTION 6. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 70-1-201(b)(1), is amended by
deleting "each member" and substituting "each member appointed by the governor".
SECTION 7. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 70-1-201(b)(3), is amended by
deleting "confirmed" and substituting instead "appointed as provided in subsection (a) or
confirmed".
SECTION 8. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 70-1-201(b)(4), is amended by
deleting the subdivision and substituting instead:
If the general assembly is not in session at the time a member is appointed to fill
a vacancy not resulting from the expiration of a term, then the new appointee shall serve
for the term appointed, unless such appointment must be confirmed pursuant to
subdivision (b)(1) and is not confirmed within sixty (60) calendar days after the general
assembly next convenes in regular session following such appointment.
SECTION 9. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
