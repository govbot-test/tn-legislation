Amendment No. 2 to HB1733
Johnson
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 1774 House Bill No. 1733*
by adding the following sections immediately preceding the effective date section and
renumbering the effective date section accordingly:
SECTION __. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 49, Chapter 6, Part 26, is amended by
adding the following as a new section:
Each participating school shall adopt a policy prohibiting harassment,
intimidation, bullying, or cyber-bullying. A participating school's policy must meet the
same requirements of a school district's policy required in § 49-6-4503.
SECTION __. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 49, Chapter 6, Part 35, is amended by
adding the following as a new section:
Each private school that enrolls recipients shall adopt a policy prohibiting
harassment, intimidation, bullying, or cyber-bullying. A private school's policy must meet
the same requirements of a school district's policy required in § 49-6-4503.
SECTION __. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 49, Chapter 10, Part 14, is amended
by adding the following as a new section:
Each participating school shall adopt a policy prohibiting harassment,
intimidation, bullying, or cyber-bullying. A participating school's policy must meet the
same requirements of a school district's policy required in § 49-6-4503.
