HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1280
By Bulso
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Christian Brown on
being named the 2025 WILLCO Awards Boys'
Basketball Athlete of the Year.
WHEREAS, we are pleased to recognize and commend those successful high school
athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring honor to Tennessee and serve as
exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable individual is Christian Brown of Franklin High School,
an athlete of extraordinary talent, skill, and dedication, who has proven himself a formidable
competitor in basketball; and
WHEREAS, in recognition of his success on the hardwood throughout the school year,
Christian Brown was named Boys' Basketball Athlete of the Year at the 2025 WILLCO Awards
celebration; and
WHEREAS, no stranger to awards and accolades for his excellence on the basketball
court, Mr. Brown was also a Mr. Basketball finalist in Class 4A; and
WHEREAS, Christian Brown epitomizes all that is good in today's student athlete, as he
has achieved a high level of success in sports while evincing great character; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Brown's notable achievements are a source of great pride for his family,
his community, and his school, and it is appropriate that we recognize this accomplished
athlete; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Christian Brown on being named the 2025
WILLCO Awards Boys' Basketball Athlete of the Year, wishing him much success and
happiness in his future endeavors.
HJR1280
016963
- 1 -
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
