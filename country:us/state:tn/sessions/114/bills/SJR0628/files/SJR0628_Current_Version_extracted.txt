SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 628
By Johnson
A RESOLUTION to confirm the appointment of Tom Marino to the
Public Charter School Commission.
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-13-105, provides for the creation of
the Public Charter School Commission; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-13-105(b)(1)(A), provides that the
commission be composed of nine members appointed by the governor; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-13-105(c)(3), provides that at least
three members from each grand division of the state must serve on the commission; and
WHEREAS, Governor Bill Lee, acting pursuant to Tennessee Code Annotated, Section
49-13-105, has appointed Tom Marino as a member of the Public Charter School Commission
representing West Tennessee for a term that will expire on June 30, 2027; and
WHEREAS, Tom Marino is the Executive Director of The Poplar Foundation, a private
foundation based in Memphis that works to reduce poverty by expanding access to high-quality
educational opportunities for low-income students; and
WHEREAS, he gives back to his community through his service on the boards of The
Soulsville Charter School, The Collegiate School of Memphis, Crosstown High School, Memphis
Teacher Residency, Tech901, and the Binghampton Development Corporation; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Marino holds degrees from Mississippi State University and Memphis
Theological Seminary; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-13-105(b)(1)(A), provides that
each appointed member shall be confirmed by joint resolution of the general assembly; now,
therefore,
