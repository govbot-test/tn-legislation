HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 921
By McCalmon
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate the Page High School
girls' golf team, 2025 TSSAA Division I, Class
AA state champions.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should salute those
student athletes who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as true
champions of whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such exemplary group of young athletes is the Page High School girls'
golf team, whose members have proven themselves to be the fiercest of competitors and truly
worthy of admiration by winning the 2025 Tennessee Secondary School Athletic Association
(TSSAA) Division I, Class AA girls' state championship; and
WHEREAS, with an outstanding display of putting accuracy and driving distance, the
Page High School golfers bested their competition during the state tournament, which was held
at the Sevierville Golf Club in Sevierville on October 6-7, 2025; and
WHEREAS, the Page High girls' golf team has captured the state title for the second
year in a row, an exceptional achievement reflecting sustained excellence, discipline, and
teamwork; and
WHEREAS, the championship-winning team, comprised of Brooke Bennett, Shashe
Ekker, Stella Edwards, and Elizabeth McCann, was expertly guided by head coach Jamie
Eickhoff; and
WHEREAS, standout individual performances include Brooke Bennett's third-place finish
overall and Shashe Ekker's tie for fifth place, underscoring both the depth and talent of this
remarkable squad; and

WHEREAS, the members of the Page High School girls' golf team epitomize all that is
good in today's student athletes, as they have achieved a premier level of success in
competition while exhibiting the highest degree of character in all aspects of life; and
WHEREAS, we take great pride in the tremendous accomplishments of those student
athletes who serve as champions both on the golf course and in the classroom, and it is
appropriate that the young women of the Page High School girls' golf team be specially
recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate the members and coaches of the Page High
School girls' golf team upon winning the 2025 TSSAA Division I, Class AA girls' state
championship and extend to them our best wishes for every continued success in all their future
endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
