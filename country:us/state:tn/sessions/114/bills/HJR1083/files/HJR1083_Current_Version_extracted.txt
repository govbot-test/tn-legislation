HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1083
By McCalmon
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Jordan Feliz on the
occasion of his twenty years in the music
business.
WHEREAS, music has long been an integral part of Tennessee's rich cultural heritage,
inspiring audiences around the world; and
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should recognize
those gifted musical artists whose dedication to their craft brings distinction to themselves and
to this State; and
WHEREAS, one such exceptional artist is Christian music singer and songwriter Jordan
Feliz, who is celebrating twenty years in the music business, a milestone worthy of special
recognition; and
WHEREAS, over the course of his career, Mr. Feliz has established himself as a
respected and influential voice in contemporary Christian music, inspiring listeners with songs
that reflect his faith and personal convictions; and
WHEREAS, in recognition of his talent and the impact of his work, Jordan Feliz received
the 2016 Dove Award for New Artist of the Year, one of the highest honors in Christian music;
and
WHEREAS, he has achieved numerous chart successes throughout his career,
including multiple number-one hits, most notably the platinum-certified single "The River," from
his album Beloved, which resonated deeply with audiences and helped establish him as a
leading figure in the genre; and
WHEREAS, throughout his professional journey, Mr. Feliz has remained steadfast in his
commitment to his faith in Christ, allowing his beliefs to guide his path and inform the message
conveyed through his music; and

WHEREAS, Jordan Feliz is also deeply devoted to his family, and the love and support
of his wife, Jamie, and their children, Jolie and Judah, continue to inspire him and contribute to
his ongoing success; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Feliz's twenty years in the music business stand as a testament to his
perseverance, talent, and unwavering dedication to his calling, and it is wholly fitting that we
acknowledge this significant milestone in his career; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Jordan Feliz on the occasion of his twenty
years in the music business, commend him for his achievements in the field of Christian music,
and extend to him our best wishes for continued success and happiness in all his future
endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
