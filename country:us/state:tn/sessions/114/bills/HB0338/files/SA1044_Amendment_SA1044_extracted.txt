Briggs

by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 12-2-116, is amended by adding the
following as a new subsection:
(d)
(1) Notwithstanding subsection (a) and this part, the commissioner of
environment and conservation, with the approval of the state building
commission, may enter into the agreements contemplated in this section for the
private development, redevelopment, construction, and operation of facilities on
lands under its jurisdiction, and the department of environment and conservation
may supervise the construction. Such agreements may include provisions for the
sale, lease, or other conveyance of state-owned real property, subject to the
approval of the commissioner of environment and conservation and the state
building commission. The requirements of § 12-2-112 do not apply to such
conveyances.
(2) Subdivision (d)(1) applies only to Cumberland Mountain State Park.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 12-2-116(a)(1), is amended by
deleting the language "Notwithstanding any other provisions of this part" and substituting
"Notwithstanding this part, except as provided in subsection (d)".

Section 4:
SECTION 3. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
