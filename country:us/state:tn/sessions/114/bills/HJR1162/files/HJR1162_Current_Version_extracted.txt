HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1162
By Powers
A RESOLUTION to recognize William "Ed" Bryant of Campbell
County on the occasion of his retirement.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
specially recognize those citizens who have performed with uncommon devotion and
enthusiasm throughout their years of public service; and
WHEREAS, William "Ed" Bryant is one such estimable individual who, having rendered
over thirty years of dedicated service to the people of Jellico, Newcomb, and surrounding
communities within Campbell County, will take his much-deserved retirement this year; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Bryant has faithfully served one of the most economically distressed
regions of East Tennessee through his work with Catholic Charities as the leader of Crazy Quilt,
an Appalachian outreach ministry program; and
WHEREAS, throughout his years of service and countless volunteer efforts, Ed Bryant
has assisted tens of thousands of his neighbors in need through weekly and monthly food box
distribution, organizing a community toy giveaway that benefits an average of 200-300 children
every Christmas season and hosting a Mountain Arts Camp for local children to express their
creativity, provide enrichment, and foster positive community engagement; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Bryant has also served as a trusted friend and advocate to countless
individuals; he always offers a listening and compassionate ear and helps connect those in
need with resources and assistance; and
WHEREAS, Ed Bryant exemplifies the spirit and allegiance to family and community that
are characteristic of a true Tennessean; and

WHEREAS, throughout his illustrious tenure, Mr. Bryant has carried out his
responsibilities with diligence and integrity, and his remarkable success as a public servant has
set the mold for all who will follow; and
WHEREAS, a strong advocate for the citizens of Tennessee, Ed Bryant has compiled a
singular record of success during his exemplary public service career, and he should be
specially recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend William "Ed" Bryant for his honorable and astute
service to the people of Tennessee and extend to him our best wishes for a happy and fulfilling
retirement.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
