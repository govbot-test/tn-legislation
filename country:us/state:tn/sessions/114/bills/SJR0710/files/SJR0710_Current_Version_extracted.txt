SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 710
By Johnson
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate the Brentwood High
School girls' cross country team, 2025 TSSAA
Class AAA state champions.
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that we honor those Tennessee students who, through their
athletic prowess, have achieved the pinnacle of success and serve as exceptional ambassadors
of the Volunteer State; and
WHEREAS, one such group of estimable individuals is the Brentwood High School girls'
cross country team, who won the 2025 TSSAA Class AAA state championship; and
WHEREAS, the Brentwood High School girls' cross country team secured their sixth
consecutive state championship on November 7, 2025, at Shelby Farms Park in Memphis; and
WHEREAS, during the State meet, all five scoring Bruins finished in the top fourteen: Lia
Banovac in tenth, Nigella Smith in eleventh, Natalie Smith in twelfth, and Julia Womack in
fourteenth; and
WHEREAS, senior Lauren Banovac won the individual state title, taking first place with a
time of 17:17.15 and leading the team to a total score of forty-four points; and
WHEREAS, success in statewide competition is achieved only through hard work,
dedication, and the commitment to working together as a team and excelling with a cooperative
spirit; the Bruins exhibited each of these rare qualities this season; and
WHEREAS, the girls' cross country team benefited from the astute leadership of Head
Coach Christopher Demetra; and
WHEREAS, the Brentwood High School Bruins' noteworthy achievement is a
tremendous source of pride for their families, their classmates, and their entire community; and

WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to acknowledge and applaud the members of the girls'
cross country team of Brentwood High School for serving as exemplars of the high quality of the
youth of Tennessee; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate the members and
coaches of the Brentwood High School girls' cross country team, winners of the 2025 TSSAA
Class AAA state championship, and extend to them our best wishes for continued success in all
their future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
