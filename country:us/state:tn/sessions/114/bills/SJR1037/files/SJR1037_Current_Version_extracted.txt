SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1037
By Hensley
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate the 2026 Mule Day
Queen, Ava Grayson Sykes.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should salute those
young citizens who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as
outstanding Tennesseans of whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such noteworthy person is Ava Grayson Sykes, who was recently
selected as the 2026 Mule Day Court Queen; and
WHEREAS, one of the greatest community events in the State of Tennessee, Mule Day
is an annual celebration of all things related to mules and traces its roots back to the 1840s; and
WHEREAS, 2026 Mule Day Queen Ava Sykes is the daughter of Mandy Barry and Josh
Sykes, and she is pursuing a degree in social studies with a minor in behavioral management;
and
WHEREAS, Ms. Sykes is eager to make an enduring impact in the field of education and
is driven by a passion to shape the next generation, leading with her heart, and proving that true
leadership begins with service and honoring those who have preceded her; and
WHEREAS, Ava Sykes serves with acumen and alacrity at E.A. Cox Middle School,
where she supports students and helps create a positive learning environment; she coaches
girls' basketball, teaching her players skills on the court and important life lessons, such as
resilience and teamwork; and
WHEREAS, a young woman of many talents and interests, Ms. Sykes enjoys spending
quality time with family and friends, serving her community, and cheering on the University of
Tennessee football team; and

WHEREAS, Ava Sykes's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for
her family, her community, and the citizens of Maury County; and
WHEREAS, the Mule Day Queen and her court play a prominent role in the promotion of
Mule Day, participating in media interviews and serving as hostesses during the festivities; and
WHEREAS, 2026 Mule Day Queen Ava Sykes epitomizes the spirit of distinction and the
commitment to greatness that are characteristic of a true Tennessean; her dedication to
excellence is clearly deserving of our respect and admiration; and
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to pause in our deliberations to acknowledge and
applaud this young woman for her remarkable achievement; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Ava Grayson Sykes
upon being selected as the 2026 Mule Day Queen, extending to her our best wishes for every
future success and happiness.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
