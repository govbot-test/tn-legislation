Raw Text:
HOUSE BILL 1836
By Hale
SENATE BILL 1805
By Bailey
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 68
and Title 71, relative to TennCare.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 71-5-165(a), is amended by deleting
"sixty-seven and one-half percent (67.5%)" and substituting "one hundred and ten percent
(110%)".
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 71-5-165(b), is amended by deleting
the subsection.
SECTION 3. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 71, Chapter 5, Part 15, is amended by
deleting the part.
SECTION 4. Any funds remaining in the ambulance service assessment revenue fund
created pursuant to Tennessee Code Annotated, § 71-5-1508(a), after June 30, 2026, must
remain in the fund until expended or disbursed in accordance with Tennessee Code Annotated,
§ 71-5-1508(f) and (g), as applicable and as existed on June 30, 2026.
SECTION 5. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.
