SENATE RESOLUTION 257
By Walley
A RESOLUTION to recognize Doris Watkins Henderson on the
occasion of her retirement.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should pause to
specially recognize those estimable public servants who have dedicated themselves to
improving the quality of life for their fellow citizens; and
WHEREAS, Doris Watkins Henderson of Fayette County is one such admirable person,
who evinces the greatest integrity and probity in all her chosen endeavors; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Henderson estimably served as Fayette County Court Clerk for forty-
three years and has taken her well-deserved retirement; and
WHEREAS, throughout her illustrious tenure, Ms. Henderson carried out her
responsibilities with diligence and integrity, and her remarkable success as a public servant and
civic leader cast the mold for all who will follow; and
WHEREAS, Doris Henderson exemplifies the spirit and allegiance to family and
community that are characteristic of a true Tennessean; and
WHEREAS, a strong advocate for the citizens of Fayette County, Ms. Henderson
compiled a singular record of success during her exemplary public service career, and she
should be specially recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby commend Doris
Watkins Henderson for her honorable and astute service to the people of Tennessee as Fayette
County Court Clerk, salute her unswerving commitment to living the examined life with courage
and conviction, and extend to her our heartfelt wishes for a happy and fulfilling retirement and
every continued success in her future endeavors.
