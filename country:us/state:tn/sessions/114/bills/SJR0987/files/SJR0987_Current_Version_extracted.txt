SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 987
By Stevens
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Robert Taswell "Tas"
Gardner, president of the Tennessee District
Public Defenders Conference.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should salute those
Tennesseans who, through their extraordinary efforts and diligent service to their fellow citizens,
have distinguished themselves as community leaders of whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such noteworthy individual is Robert Taswell "Tas" Gardner of Paris,
2025-2026 president of the Tennessee District Public Defenders Conference (TDPDC), who has
rendered sterling service during his presidential term; and
WHEREAS, as TDPDC president, Mr. Gardner has been the principal executive officer
for supervising and controlling the business and affairs of the Conference while also assisting
the Conference in advising the General Assembly regarding legislation to improve the criminal
justice system; and
WHEREAS, Tas Gardner has served with the 24th Judicial District Public Defender's
Office, encompassing Benton, Carroll, Decatur, Hardin, and Henry counties, since 1992, first as
an investigator, then as assistant public defender, and finally in his current role of public
defender, which he assumed in 2017 after being appointed by Governor Bill Haslam; and
WHEREAS, a member of the Henry County Bar Association and the Tennessee
Association of Criminal Defense Lawyers, Mr. Gardner holds degrees from Columbia State
Community College and the University of Tennessee at Martin, and he earned his Doctorate of
Jurisprudence in 2008 from Nashville School of Law; and
WHEREAS, a respected leader in his field, Mr. Gardner served as a member of the
Board of Judicial Conduct from 2012 to 2015, helping review complaints filed against judges;

WHEREAS, Tas Gardner is also devoted to serving his community as a member of the
Tennessee River Longbeards Committee and the Paris Henry County Ducks Unlimited
Committee, chairman of the Paris Henry County Ducks Unlimited Sponsor Program, and
elected board member of the Springville Waterfowl Association; and
WHEREAS, he is a former board member of the Tennessee National Wild Turkey
Federation and previously served the Springville Waterfowl Association as vice president and
president; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Gardner epitomizes the spirit and commitment that are characteristic of
a true Tennessean; and
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to acknowledge and applaud Mr. Gardner for his
dedication to serving the people of this State; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Robert Taswell "Tas"
Gardner, 2025-2026 president of the Tennessee District Public Defenders Conference, as his
exemplary term comes to a close, extending our best wishes for his continued success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
