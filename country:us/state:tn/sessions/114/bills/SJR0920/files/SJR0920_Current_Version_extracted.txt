SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 920
By Pody
A RESOLUTION to congratulate Donna Highers upon her
induction into the Tennessee Aviation Hall of
Fame.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should salute those citizens who, through their
remarkable efforts, have distinguished themselves as community leaders of whom we can all be
proud; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable individual is Donna Highers of Lebanon, who has been
inducted into the Tennessee Aviation Hall of Fame, a testament to the important role she has
played in the history and advancement of aviation in Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Highers has served with acumen and alacrity as the lead instructor of
A&P/IA courses at Baker School of Aeronautics since 1988, in which role she has trained,
taught, and guided thousands of students to FAA licensure; and
WHEREAS, a leader in her profession, Donna Highers has devoted forty years of
service to teaching within the aeronautics industry, a feat that has resulted in her national
recognition as an expert in the field; and
WHEREAS, the success of this State, the strength of our communities, and the overall
vitality of American society depend, in great measure, upon the dedication of people such as
Donna Highers, who use their considerable talents and resources to serve others and who
should be specially recognized for their public and community service; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we recognize and congratulate Donna Highers upon
her induction into the Tennessee Aviation Hall of Fame, extending our best wishes for much
continued success.
SJR0920
014924
- 1 -
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
