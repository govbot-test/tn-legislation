HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1223
By Keisling
A RESOLUTION to commend the Scott County Fair on the
occasion of its 100th anniversary.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly take pleasure in honoring those
community traditions across Tennessee that bring residents together and celebrate local
heritage; and
WHEREAS, the Scott County Fair has served as a longstanding community gathering in
Scott County, offering an opportunity for residents and families to come together each year; and
WHEREAS, the fair has provided a place for local youth and residents to participate in
exhibits, competitions, and agricultural displays that highlight the talents and traditions of the
community; and
WHEREAS, the continued operation of the Scott County Fair is made possible through
the efforts of volunteers, organizers, and community supporters who contribute their time and
energy each year; and
WHEREAS, this year, the Scott County Fair will celebrate its 100th anniversary, an
important milestone for this local tradition that reflects the community spirit of Scott County;
now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that, on the occasion of its 100th anniversary, we honor and commend the Scott
County Fair, extending our best wishes for a successful event.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
