HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1517
By Littleton
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Mary Clement for her
distinguished career and service to the State of
Tennessee.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly take great pleasure in recognizing
those outstanding citizens whose leadership, vision, and service have made a lasting impact on
their communities and this State; and
WHEREAS, Mary Clement has dedicated her life to public service, education, and
community development, earning the respect and admiration of all who have had the privilege to
know and work alongside her; and
WHEREAS, a woman of exceptional ability and commitment, Mary Clement has played
a vital role in advancing civic engagement and public service initiatives across Tennessee,
contributing her talents to both government and nonprofit endeavors; and
WHEREAS, she served with distinction in the administration of Governor Phil Bredesen
as Director of Consumer Affairs, working diligently to protect and inform Tennessee consumers
while demonstrating a steadfast commitment to fairness, transparency, and accountability in
State government; and
WHEREAS, earlier in her career, she contributed her talents to the campaign of
Governor Winfield Dunn, helping to advance a vision of leadership and progress that would
benefit the citizens of this State; and
WHEREAS, Mary Clement has also made significant contributions to higher education,
most notably through her association with Cumberland University in Lebanon, where she
founded the Phoenix Ball, a signature event that has supported the university's mission and
strengthened its financial standing and connection to the broader community; and

WHEREAS, her dedication to preserving Tennessee's rich history is exemplified through
her service on the Advisory Committee of the Clement Railroad Hotel Museum at the Hotel
Halbrook State Historic Site in Dickson, where her guidance and leadership have helped
advance the Museum's mission of education, preservation, and public engagement; and
WHEREAS, through her many endeavors, Mary Clement has demonstrated a lifelong
commitment to service, leadership, and the betterment of her community, embodying the very
best of Tennessee's civic spirit; and
WHEREAS, alongside her husband, former Congressman Robert Nelson Clement, she
has contributed to a legacy of public service that has enriched this State and inspired countless
others to follow in their footsteps; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and commend Mary Clement for her outstanding
contributions to public service, education, and historic preservation and extend to her our best
wishes for continued success and happiness in all her future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
