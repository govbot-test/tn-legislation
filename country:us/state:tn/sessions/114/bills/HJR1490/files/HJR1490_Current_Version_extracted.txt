HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1490
By Keisling
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend the Byrdstown Water
Department, TAUD Region 4 Best Tasting Water
Contest winner.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should recognize
those essential utilities that have a long-standing commitment to providing exceptional service
and products to their customers; and
WHEREAS, one such exemplary entity is the Byrdstown Water Department, which was
recently named the winner of the Tennessee Association of Utility Districts (TAUD) Region 4
Best Tasting Water Contest; and
WHEREAS, TAUD's Region 4 includes Macon, Clay, Trousdale, Jackson, Smith,
Putnam, DeKalb, White, Warren, Pickett, Fentress, Overton, and Cumberland counties; and
WHEREAS, TAUD's Best Tasting Water Contest is a key annual event in the region,
offering utility districts an opportunity to showcase water quality and connect with peers in their
community, sharing best practices and achievements; and
WHEREAS, this honor highlights Byrdstown Water Department's commitment to
providing clean, fresh, and high-quality potable water for citizens throughout the community; and
WHEREAS, winning TAUD's Region 4 contest also allows the Byrdstown Water
Department to advance to statewide TAUD competition, offering further exposure and a chance
to compete for Tennessee's best-tasting water award; and
WHEREAS, the employees of Byrdstown Water Department show up every day focused
on their customers' needs and the health and welfare of the community; this recent honor
affirms that the work they do represents the best in Tennessee and reflects their commitment to
continuous improvement and responsible service; and
HJR1490
018070
- 1 -
WHEREAS, Byrdstown Water Department has worked tirelessly to improve the lives of
citizens in the community, and it is most fitting that this exceptional utility be honored on this
special occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Byrdstown Water Department upon being named
the winner of the Tennessee Association of Utility Districts (TAUD) Region 4 Best Tasting Water
Contest, extending our best wishes for continued success in all further endeavors to devotedly
serve others.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
