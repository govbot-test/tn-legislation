SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 609
By Massey
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Deputy Governor Butch
Eley on his service to the State of Tennessee.
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that we should honor those citizens who have performed
with uncommon devotion and enthusiasm throughout their careers in public service; and
WHEREAS, one such individual is Butch Eley, who has served the State of Tennessee
with distinction for nearly seven years of leadership in Governor Bill Lee's cabinet and most
recently served as Deputy Governor and Commissioner of the Tennessee Department of
Transportation; and
WHEREAS, Deputy Governor Butch Eley has been a key figure in advancing statewide
infrastructure, financial stewardship, and government modernization; he played a leading role in
the Hurricane Helene recovery effort and the passage of the Transportation Modernization Act;
and
WHEREAS, Deputy Governor Eley began his service in the Lee administration as Chief
Operating Officer, overseeing twenty-three departments and 35,000 employees, during which
tenure he led the implementation of Tennessee's first four-year strategic planning process; and
WHEREAS, next appointed to the position of Commissioner of the Tennessee
Department of Finance and Administration, he served as the State's Chief Financial Officer and
oversaw the establishment of the largest general fund in State history and guided Tennessee
through an unprecedented economic crisis during the COVID-19 pandemic; and
WHEREAS, during his second term, Governor Bill Lee appointed Butch Eley
Commissioner of the Tennessee Department of Transportation in order to prepare the Volunteer
State's infrastructure for its extraordinary economic growth; and

WHEREAS, Deputy Governor Eley exemplifies the spirit and allegiance to family and
community that are characteristic of a true Tennessean; and
WHEREAS, throughout his estimable career with the State of Tennessee, Butch Eley
has demonstrated the utmost professionalism, ability, and integrity, winning the unbridled
respect and admiration of his colleagues and a grateful public; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we extend to Deputy Governor Butch Eley our
heartfelt wishes for a happy and fulfilling retirement from public service, offering our deepest
gratitude for his dedicated service to the State of Tennessee and wishing him every continued
success in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
