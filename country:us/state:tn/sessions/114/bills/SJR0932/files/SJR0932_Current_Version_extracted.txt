SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 932
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to recognize and commend Chloee Stokely upon
her runner-up finish in the Creed contest at the
East Tennessee regional FFA competition.
WHEREAS, it is most fitting that we specially recognize those young people who
participate in organizations whose mission is to promote agricultural education and development
in this State; and
WHEREAS, one such individual is Chloee Stokely, who recently finished as runner-up in
the Creed contest at the East Tennessee FFA regional competition held at the University of
Tennessee, Knoxville on January 29, 2026; and
WHEREAS, Future Farmers of America, now known as FFA, was organized in 1928 to
promote leadership, personal growth, and career success among high school agriculture
students; and
WHEREAS, the Creed contest is only open to freshmen FFA members and requires
contestants to recite the FFA Creed from memory and answer questions from a panel
afterwards; and
WHEREAS, a student at Cocke County High School, Ms. Stokely now advances to the
state finals, to be held at the Tennessee State FFA Convention in March 2026; and
WHEREAS, for many years, FFA has played an important role in ensuring the growth
and prosperity of agriculture in Tennessee and the nation by expertly molding our future
leaders; and
WHEREAS, the success of this State, the strength of our communities, and the overall
vitality of our society depend, in great measure, on the dedication of our students and our
agricultural innovators, who will ensure Tennessee continues to lead the nation in agricultural
production for generations to come; now, therefore,

BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF

in the Creed contest at the East Tennessee FFA regional completion, and wish her much
success as she competes at the Tennessee State FFA Convention.
