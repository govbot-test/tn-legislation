SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 899
By Campbell
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Aryanna Smith upon
being selected the 2026 Boys & Girls Clubs of
Tennessee State Youth of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly pause in their
deliberations to salute those young citizens who, through their remarkable efforts, have
distinguished themselves as emerging community and state leaders of whom we can all be
proud; and
WHEREAS, one such deserving person is Aryanna Smith, who was recently named the
2026 Youth of the Year for the Boys & Girls Club of Tennessee, a most befitting honor that
recognizes her leadership, community service, and perseverance; and
WHEREAS, a member of the Boys & Girls Club's North Nashville Teen Center, Aryanna
is a student at Nashville Big Picture High School; and
WHEREAS, she has been a member of the Boys & Girls Club for twelve years, during
which time she took part in the Summer for America's Youth, flying to Washington, D.C., and
spoke with the Tennessee Congressional members about food insecurity issues; and
WHEREAS, in addition to participating in the Boys & Girls Club, Aryanna Smith is a
member of Fellowship of Christian Athletes and serves as creative director and event planner at
her high school in addition to maintaining a job at a local restaurant; and
WHEREAS, upon graduation, she plans to attend Belmont University to pursue a degree
in nursing, with the ultimate goal of serving as a pediatric nurse; and
WHEREAS, the Boys & Girls Clubs exist to serve youth and provide a safe place of
encouragement, character building, security, and love for children; and

WHEREAS, since 1947, the Youth of the Year honor has been the Boys & Girls Clubs of
America's premier recognition program, celebrating the extraordinary achievements of Club
members; and
WHEREAS, Youth of the Year participants such as Aryanna exemplify the critical impact
of Boys & Girls Clubs on the lives of young people, embodying the values of leadership, service,
academic excellence, and healthy lifestyles; and
WHEREAS, through the Youth of the Year program, Aryanna and other Youth of the
Year participants are able to showcase their talents and achievements, share their hopes and
dreams, and work toward a bright and positive future; and
WHEREAS, Aryanna is a shining example that GREAT FUTURES START HERE—at
Boys & Girls Clubs; and
WHEREAS, the success of this State, the strength of our communities, and the overall
vitality of American society depend, in great measure, upon the dedication of young people such
as Aryanna Smith, who use their considerable talents and resources to serve others and who
should be specially recognized for their public and community service; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Aryanna Smith upon being
named the 2026 Tennessee State Boys & Girls Clubs Youth of the Year. We extend to her our
best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
