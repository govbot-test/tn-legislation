Section 1:
Senate Finance, Ways, and Means 1
Amendment No. 1 to SB2191
Watson
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting subsection (b) from § 7-51-2802 in SECTION 1 and substituting:
(b) A political subdivision has sixty (60) days from the date on which the approval is
requested of the political subdivision by which to make a determination.
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting subsection (c) from § 7-51-2802 in SECTION 1 and
redesignating the subsequent subsection accordingly.
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting the header "Definitions." for § 7-51-2801 in SECTION 1
and substituting the header "Part definitions.".
