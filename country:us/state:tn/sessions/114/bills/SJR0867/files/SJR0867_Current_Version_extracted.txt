SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 867
By Seal
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Lindsey Snodgrass,
2011 TSSAA Division I, Class A-AA Girls' Track
and Field 300-Meter Hurdles State Champion.
WHEREAS, we are pleased to specially honor and commend those successful athletes
who, through their magnificent exploits, have brought honor to Tennessee and serve as
exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable athlete is Lindsey Snodgrass, who, as a student at
Claiborne High School, won the 2011 TSSAA Division I, Class A-AA Girls' Track and Field 300-
Meter Hurdles State Championship held at Middle Tennessee State University on May 23-27,
2011; and
WHEREAS, in the finals section of the track and field championships, Ms. Snodgrass
took fourth place in the 100-meter hurdles and triple jump events; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Snodgrass's noteworthy achievements have been a tremendous source
of pride for her family, her alma mater, and her community; and
WHEREAS, Lindsey Snodgrass's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the
respect, admiration, and commendation of the members of this General Assembly; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Lindsey Snodgrass on
winning the 2011 TSSAA Division I, Class A-AA Girls' Track and Field 300-Meter Hurdles State
Championship and extend to her our best wishes for much continued success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
