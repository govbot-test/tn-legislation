SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 854
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Luke Stuart of
Gatlinburg-Pittman High School upon being
named All-County in football.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly are pleased to recognize and
commend those successful high school athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring
honor to Tennessee and serve as exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable individual is Luke Stuart of Gatlinburg-Pittman High
School in Sevier County, an athlete of extraordinary talent, skill, and dedication, who has proven
himself a formidable competitor on the football field; and
WHEREAS, Luke Stuart was recently named to the All-County team in recognition of his
excellence on the gridiron for Gatlinburg-Pittman High School; and
WHEREAS, a senior defensive end, Mr. Stuart was also named to the All-Region 1-3A
first team and was honored as Region 1-3A Lineman of the Year; and
WHEREAS, Luke Stuart recorded 72 tackles, 16 tackles for loss, two forced fumbles,
and three sacks during the 2025 season; and
WHEREAS, Luke Stuart epitomizes all that is good in today's student athlete, as he has
achieved a high level of success in sports while evincing great character both on and off the
field; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Stuart's notable achievement is a source of great pride for his family, his
community, and his school, and it is appropriate that we recognize this accomplished athlete;
now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Luke Stuart of

much success and happiness in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
