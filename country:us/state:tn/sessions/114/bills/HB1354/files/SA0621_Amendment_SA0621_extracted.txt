Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 39-11-611(c), is amended by
designating the existing language as subdivision (1) and adding the following as a new
subdivision (2):
(2)
(A) A victim of a human trafficking offense using force against a person
who is committing or attempting to commit a human trafficking offense is
presumed to have held a reasonable belief of imminent death or serious bodily
injury to self, family, a member of the household, or a person visiting as an
invited guest when that force is used.
(B) A person must prove the person's status as a victim of human
trafficking by clear and convincing evidence. A person may provide clear and
convincing evidence of the person's status as a victim of human trafficking
through testimony.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 39-11-611(d), is amended by
deleting "subsection (c)" and substituting "subdivision (c)(1)".

Section 4:
SECTION 3. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 39-11-611(a), is amended by adding
the following as a new, appropriately designated subdivision:
( ) "Human trafficking offense" means the commission of an act that would
constitute the criminal offense of:
(A) Trafficking a person for a commercial sex act under § 39-13-309;
