HOUSE RESOLUTION 267
By Keisling
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate David Jeffers upon his
receipt of the Award of Excellence from the East
Tennessee Historical Society.
WHEREAS, the members of this legislative body take great pleasure in recognizing
those distinguished citizens who have made meaningful contributions to the preservation and
promotion of Tennessee's rich history; and
WHEREAS, David Jeffers is one such individual, who has been honored with the Award
of Excellence by the East Tennessee Historical Society in recognition of his efforts to preserve
the history of East Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, the Awards of Excellence program annually recognizes individuals and
organizations for significant contributions to the preservation, promotion, and interpretation of
the region's history through such endeavors as exhibits, lectures, publications, and other
educational initiatives; and
WHEREAS, through his dedication and commitment to historical preservation, Mr.
Jeffers has helped ensure that the stories, heritage, and cultural legacy of East Tennessee are
maintained for the benefit and education of present and future generations; and
WHEREAS, it is wholly fitting that we recognize David Jeffers for his outstanding
contributions to preserving the rich history of this State and extend to him our best wishes for
every continued success; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and
commend David Jeffers upon his receipt of the Award of Excellence from the East Tennessee
Historical Society, applaud his commitment to preserving the history of East Tennessee, and
extend to him our best wishes for every continued success in his future endeavors.
