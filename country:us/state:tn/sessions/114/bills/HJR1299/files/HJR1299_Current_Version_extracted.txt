HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1299
By Warner
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Katie Mason of Page
High School on being named a 2025-2026 John
Maher Builders Scholar-Athlete.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pay tribute to
those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to excellence,
have distinguished themselves in the classroom and beyond; and
WHEREAS, Katie Mason is one such exceptional student, who was recently named a
John Maher Builders Scholar-Athlete for the 2025-2026 school year in recognition of her
excellence in the classroom, in athletic competition, and in the community; and
WHEREAS, for the past twenty years, John Maher Builders has partnered with the
Williamson Herald to highlight local high school scholar-athletes who are then eligible for
scholarships that will be presented to two female and two male scholar-athletes at the John
Maher Builders Scholar-Athlete banquet in May; and
WHEREAS, a senior at Page High School, Katie Mason is a two-time state wrestling
qualifier and region finalist, having reached the 138-pound Region 6 finals after a thrilling 10-7
overtime win in the semifinals; and
WHEREAS, as captain of the wrestling team, she played an instrumental role in helping
to strengthen Page High School's wrestling program, lending her support to her male
counterparts as they claimed district and regional runner-up laurels and finished fourth at the
Class AA Dual State Wrestling Championships; and
WHEREAS, Katie Mason is also a champion in the classroom with a 4.54 grade point
average and a standout among her peers as a JROTC leader; and

WHEREAS, she has rendered sterling service as a volunteer at numerous community
events, including the Cooper Trooper Foundation Pumpkin Patch, Honor the Fallen 5K,
Creekside Elementary Otterfest, and the Battle of Franklin luminaries; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Mason's display of intellectual aptitude and athletic excellence surely
bodes well for her future success; and
WHEREAS, her noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for her
parents, John and Jennifer Mason of Franklin, her school, and her community; and
WHEREAS, Katie Mason's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of our respect,
admiration, and commendation; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Katie Mason on being named a 2025-2026
John Maher Builders Scholar-Athlete and extend to her our best wishes for a happy, successful
future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
