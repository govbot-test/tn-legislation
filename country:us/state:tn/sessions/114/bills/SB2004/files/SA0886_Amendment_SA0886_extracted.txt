Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 58, Chapter 2, Part 1, is amended by
adding the following as a new section:
Each local emergency management agency shall, on an annual basis:
(1) Provide training to all employees on the Mutual Aid and Emergency
and Disaster Assistance Agreement Act of 2004, compiled in title 58, chapter 8,
and the lnterlocal Cooperation Act, compiled in title 12, chapter 9; and
(2) Document the completion of the training required by subdivision (1) in
the employee's personnel or training file.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.
