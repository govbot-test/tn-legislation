HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 735
By Vital
A RESOLUTION to recognize and commend Chief Duane Pitts of
the Tri-Community Volunteer Fire Department.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should honor those
citizens who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as public
leaders of whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, Duane Pitts is one such estimable individual, who has served with
distinction for fifty years as Fire Chief of the Tri-Community Volunteer Fire Department; and
WHEREAS, Chief Duane Pitts officially began his service as Fire Chief in 1966, and his
wealth of experience and extensive training has made him an unparalleled asset to the
department's team; and
WHEREAS, during his distinguished tenure, Chief Pitts has been responsible for the
management, planning, and coordination of both firefighting and emergency response efforts of
the Tri-Community Volunteer Fire Department, always doing his utmost to bring honor, integrity,
and compassionate service to his office and his community; and
WHEREAS, firefighting has been a way of life for Chief Pitts; he has devoted himself to
serving his community for five decades, earning the admiration and respect of his colleagues
and a grateful public and embodying the spirit of volunteerism, mentorship, and resilience; and
WHEREAS, Chief Duane Pitts is wholly committed to the noble precepts of public
service that have earned Tennessee recognition as the Volunteer State, and he should be
specially recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Duane Pitts for fifty years of service as Fire Chief
