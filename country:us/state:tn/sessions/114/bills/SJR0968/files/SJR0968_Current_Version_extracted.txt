SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 968
By Massey
A RESOLUTION to honor Jeremy Mitchell upon being inducted as
the 2026 president of the Tennessee Road
Builders Association.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should salute those
citizens who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as business
leaders of whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such noteworthy person is Jeremy Mitchell, the 2026 president of the
Tennessee Road Builders Association; and
WHEREAS, on February 21, 2026, Mr. Mitchell was installed as the eightieth president
of the Tennessee Road Builders Association (TRBA), an honor most befitting his commitment to
and leadership in his profession; and
WHEREAS, a native of Southeast Missouri, Jeremy Mitchell now resides in Chapel Hill
with his wife, Leah Mitchell, and their daughters, Claira and Nora; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Mitchell graduated from Murray State University with a Bachelor of
Science degree in Civil Engineering Technology and began his career at Bell Construction in
2000; and
WHEREAS, he has excelled in numerous roles with Bell, including Project Manager and
Cost Estimator, Senior Project Manager, Project Executive, and currently, Executive Vice
President of the Heavy Highway/Civil Division; he is renowned for delivering very complex and
high-profile transportation projects across Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, a noted and trusted leader in his profession, Mr. Mitchell served on the
TRBA Board for two years as chairman of the Bridge and Concrete Division before being
elected TRBA Second Vice President in 2024 and First Vice President and Treasurer in 2025;

WHEREAS, he also serves on the TRBA Investment Committee, the Alternate Delivery
Committee, and the Tennessee Highway Contractors Political Action Committee Board; and
WHEREAS, Jeremy Mitchell is the second person from Bell Construction to serve as
TRBA president, following Keith Pyle in 2010; and
WHEREAS, Jeremy Mitchell epitomizes the spirit and commitment that are characteristic
of a true Tennessean; and
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly find it appropriate to acknowledge
and applaud Mr. Mitchell for his dedication to the highway construction industry; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor Jeremy Mitchell upon being inducted as
the 2026 president of the Tennessee Road Builders Association, commend him for his
meritorious service to the State of Tennessee, and extend to him our best wishes for every
future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
