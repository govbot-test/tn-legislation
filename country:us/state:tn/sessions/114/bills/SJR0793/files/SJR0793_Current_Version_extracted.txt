SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 793
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Eli Archer of
Morristown-Hamblen West High School on being
named to the Tennessee Music Education
Association All-State choir.
WHEREAS, we are pleased to recognize and commend those successful high school
artists who, through their natural talents and extraordinary efforts, bring the joys of creative
expression and culture to the citizens of this State while promoting the importance of the
performing arts; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable individual is Eli Archer of Morristown-Hamblen West
High School, a performer of extraordinary talent, skill, and dedication, who has proven himself
an exceptional vocalist; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Archer was recently named to the Tennessee Music Education
Association All-State choir in recognition of his excellence as a vocalist at Morristown-Hamblen
West High School; and
WHEREAS, he will be participating in All-State events in Nashville at the Gaylord
Opryland Resort in April 2026; and
WHEREAS, Eli Archer epitomizes all that is good in today's student artist, as he has
achieved a high level of success in music while evincing great character both on and off the
stage; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Archer's notable achievement is a source of great pride for his family,
his community, and his school, and it is appropriate that we recognize this accomplished artist;
now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
SJR0793
011484
- 1 -
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Eli Archer of Morristown-
Hamblen West High School on being named to the Tennessee Music Education Association All-
State choir, wishing him much success and happiness in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
- 2 - 011484
