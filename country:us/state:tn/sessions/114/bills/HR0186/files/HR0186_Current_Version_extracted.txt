HOUSE RESOLUTION 186
By Vaughan
A RESOLUTION to honor Reuben Henry Sanders for his
exemplary service as a Sergeant at Arms for the
House of Representatives.
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that the members of this legislative body should honor
those employees who have performed with uncommon devotion and enthusiasm throughout
their tenure with the General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, Reuben Henry Sanders of Goodlettsville is one such estimable person, who
has served with distinction as a Sergeant at Arms for the House of Representatives since 2017;
and
WHEREAS, in his capacity as a Sergeant at Arms, Mr. Sanders has rendered sterling
service to the House Commerce Committee and Chairman Kevin Vaughan; he will be sorely
missed by the legislators and staff he so ably assisted; and
WHEREAS, born on March 24, 1949, Reuben Sanders is a graduate of Fisk University,
where he was a member of the basketball team, and enjoyed an exemplary career with the
Nashville Fire Department from 1978 to 2011; and
WHEREAS, Reuben Sanders exemplifies the spirit and allegiance to family and
community that are characteristic of a true Tennessean; and
WHEREAS, throughout his estimable tenure as a House Sergeant at Arms, Reuben
Sanders has demonstrated the utmost professionalism, ability, and integrity, winning the
unbridled respect and admiration of his colleagues and the members of the General Assembly;
now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and
commend Reuben Sanders upon his exemplary service to the House of Representatives and
