SENATE RESOLUTION 273
By Walley
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Josh Moore on his
selection as McNairy County Health Department
director.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should pause to give
special recognition to those individuals who have achieved significant milestones in their
careers; and
WHEREAS, Josh Moore is one such estimable citizen, who has distinguished himself as
a medical professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Moore was recently selected by the West Tennessee Regional Health
Office to serve as director of the McNairy County Health Department, a most befitting
appointment, as it recognizes his commitment to the health and prosperity of the citizens he so
ably serves; and
WHEREAS, highly regarded among his peers and his community, Mr. Moore has served
the West Tennessee Regional Health Office for a number of years, and his extensive
background and years of experience will surely bode well for his success as director; and
WHEREAS, in his role as director, Mr. Moore will oversee numerous health programs
within the McNairy County Health Department, including primary care; immunizations; GIFTS,
Tennessee's pregnancy smoking cessation program; TNSTRONG, a youth-led movement
advocating for tobacco-free lifestyles; Women, Infants, and Children, or WIC; and vital records,
among several additional services; and
WHEREAS, throughout his exemplary tenure as a public health professional, Mr. Moore
has carried out his responsibilities with diligence, integrity, and a steadfast commitment to
excellence; his remarkable success as a public servant and leader is directly attributable to his

reliance on the time-honored values of hard work and common sense and his uncommon ability
to work well with people from all walks of life; and
WHEREAS, Josh Moore exemplifies the spirit and allegiance to family and community
that are characteristic of a true Tennessean; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Moore is a public-spirited citizen of the highest order and most worthy of
our approbation; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby honor and
congratulate Josh Moore on his selection as McNairy County Health Department director and
extend to him our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
