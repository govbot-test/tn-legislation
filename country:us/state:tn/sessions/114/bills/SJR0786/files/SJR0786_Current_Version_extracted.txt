SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 786
By Seal
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate John Mason of Sevier
County High School on receiving an All-Region
1-5A honorable mention.
WHEREAS, we are pleased to recognize and commend those successful high school
athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring honor to Tennessee and serve as
exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable individual is John Mason of Sevier County High School,
an athlete of extraordinary talent, skill, and dedication, who has proven himself a formidable
competitor on the football field; and
WHEREAS, John Mason recently received an All-Region 1-5A honorable mention in
recognition of his excellence on the gridiron for Sevier County High School; and
WHEREAS, John Mason epitomizes all that is good in today's student athlete, as he has
achieved a high level of success in sports while evincing great character both on and off the
field; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Mason's notable achievement is a source of great pride for his family,
his community, and his school, and it is appropriate that we recognize this accomplished
athlete; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate John Mason of Sevier
County High School on receiving an All-Region 1-5A honorable mention, wishing him much
success and happiness in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
