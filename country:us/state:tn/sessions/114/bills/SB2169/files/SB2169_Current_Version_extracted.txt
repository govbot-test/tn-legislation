SENATE BILL 2169
By Yager
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 8,
Chapter 14 and Title 9, Chapter 4, relative to the
district public defender system.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 8, Chapter 14, Part 1, is amended by
adding the following as a new section:
8-14-115.
Effective July 1, 2026, there are created five (5) additional social worker positions
in the public defender system.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.
