SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 972
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Taquez Butler on
being named to the All-TCCAA First Team.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly are pleased to recognize and
commend those successful student athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring honor
to Tennessee and serve as exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable individual is Taquez Butler of Walters State Community
College, an athlete of extraordinary talent, skill, and dedication, who has proven himself a
formidable competitor on the basketball court; and
WHEREAS, the Tennessee Community College Athletic Association (TCCAA) recently
named Mr. Butler to the All-TCCAA First Team in recognition of his excellence on the court for
Walters State Community College; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Butler averaged 11.7 points and dished out five assists per game,
shooting forty-eight percent from the field and leading the conference with 140 assists; his
assist-to-turnover ratio was 4.0, and he made fifty-eight 3-pointers; and
WHEREAS, Taquez Butler epitomizes all that is good in today's student athlete, as he
has achieved a high level of success in sports while evincing great character both on and off the
court; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Butler's notable achievement is a source of great pride for his family, his
community, and Walters State Community College, and it is appropriate that we recognize this
accomplished athlete; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Taquez Butler of Walters
