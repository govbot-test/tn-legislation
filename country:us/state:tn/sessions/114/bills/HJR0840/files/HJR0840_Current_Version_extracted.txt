HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 840
By Jones J
A RESOLUTION to honor the memory of Alex Jeffrey Pretti.
WHEREAS, it was with great sadness and a profound sense of loss that the members of
this General Assembly learned of the tragic death of Alex Jeffrey Pretti, a thirty-seven-year-old
United States citizen who was shot and killed by ICE agents on January 24, 2026; and
WHEREAS, on January 24, 2026, Mr. Pretti was legally recording the actions of ICE and
federal agents occupying his city before he was killed; video footage shows Mr. Pretti stepping
between a woman and an agent who is pepper spraying her; other agents then pepper spray
Mr. Pretti, who is holding a phone in one hand and nothing in the other hand, and pull him to the
ground; and
WHEREAS, video evidence shows that federal agents appear to have fired at least ten
shots within five seconds at Mr. Pretti as he was lying on the sidewalk; and
WHEREAS, the circumstances surrounding Alex Pretti's tragic death have shocked and
saddened his family, his neighbors across Minneapolis, and strangers around the world; and
WHEREAS, an intensive care unit nurse with the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs,
Alex Pretti grew up in Green Bay, Wisconsin, where he played football and baseball and ran
track for Preble High School; he was a Boy Scout and sang in the Green Bay Boy Choir; and
WHEREAS, after his high school graduation, Mr. Pretti attended the University of
Minnesota and graduated in 2011 with a bachelor's degree in biology, society, and the
environment; he worked as a research assistant before returning to school to become a
registered nurse; and

WHEREAS, Mr. Pretti was warmly described by his colleagues as "a really great friend
[whose] default look on his face was a smile" and as a person who "wanted to be helpful, to help
humanity, and have a career that was a force of good in the world"; and
WHEREAS, Alex Jeffrey Pretti has left behind an inspiring legacy as a son, brother,
nurse, friend, neighbor, and citizen; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor the memory and celebrate the life of Alex Jeffrey Pretti and
extend our sympathy and condolences to his family and friends.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
