SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 930
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to recognize and commend Silas Poland upon
winning the East Tennessee FFA Star
Greenhand award.
WHEREAS, it is most fitting that we specially recognize those young people who
participate in organizations whose mission is to promote agricultural education and development
in this State; and
WHEREAS, one such individual is Silas Poland, who recently won the East Tennessee
FFA Star Greenhand award at the FFA regional competition held at the University of
Tennessee, Knoxville on January 29, 2026; and
WHEREAS, Future Farmers of America, now known as FFA, was organized in 1928 to
promote leadership, personal growth, and career success among high school agriculture
students; and
WHEREAS, the Star Greenhand award is open to ninth-grade FFA members and is
based on their agriculture experience outside the classroom; Mr. Poland works for his family's
Goodwater Winery; and
WHEREAS, he also completed an extensive application, interviewed with multiple
judges, and passed an FFA knowledge exam to receive this prestigious award; and
WHEREAS, a student at Cocke County High School, Mr. Poland now advances to the
state finals, to be held at the Tennessee State FFA Convention in March 2026; and
WHEREAS, for many years, FFA has played an important role in ensuring the growth
and prosperity of agriculture in Tennessee and the nation by expertly molding our future
leaders; and
WHEREAS, the success of this State, the strength of our communities, and the overall
vitality of our society depend, in great measure, on the dedication of our students and our
