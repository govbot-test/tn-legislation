by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 11-13-104, is amended by adding
the following language under the designation "Class II Pastoral River Areas":
Big South Fork of the Cumberland River – The entire segment of the river in
Scott County.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 11-13-111(b), is amended by adding
the following as a new subdivision (2) and redesignating the existing subdivision (2) as
subdivision (3):
(2)
(A) Except as provided in subdivision (b)(2)(B), a landfill for the disposal
of solid or hazardous wastes is not permitted within the entirety of the territory of
a county in which two (2) rivers join to form a Class II river that flows through a
national river and recreation area and, upon crossing the border of that county,
enters the territory of a neighboring state.
(B) Subdivision (b)(2)(A) does not prohibit a permit renewal for, or
expansion of, a landfill that is operating under a valid permit on the date that this
act becomes a law.

Section 4:
SECTION 3. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 68-211-118, is amended by adding a
new subsection (c):
(c) Section 11-13-111 and subsection (a) do not apply to the operation,
modification, or expansion of any landfill that is:
