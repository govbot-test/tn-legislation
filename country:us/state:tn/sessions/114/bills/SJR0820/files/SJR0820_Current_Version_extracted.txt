SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 820
By Stevens
A RESOLUTION to congratulate Abigail Wallace of McKenzie
High School, 2025 TSSAA Class A Girls' Cross
Country Individual Runner-Up.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly are pleased to specially honor and
commend those successful high school athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring
honor to Tennessee and serve as exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable athlete is Abigail Wallace of McKenzie High School,
who claimed runner-up laurels at the 2025 TSSAA Class A Girls' Individual State Cross Country
Championship; and
WHEREAS, a member of the Class of 2028, Ms. Wallace exhibited athleticism and
ambition that surely bode well for her future success; and
WHEREAS, Abigail Wallace's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride
for her family, her school, and her community; and
WHEREAS, her dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of our respect, admiration,
and commendation; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Abigail Wallace of
McKenzie High School, runner-up in the 2025 TSSAA Class A Girls' Cross Country Individual
Championship, and extend to her our best wishes for much continued success in her future
endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
