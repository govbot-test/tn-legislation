Section 1:
Amendment No. 3 to HB1271
Behn
Signature of Sponsor
by adding the following as a new subdivision (a)(7) in SECTION 1:
(7) The general assembly finds that local governments are closest to the people
they serve and are best positioned to adopt policies that reflect the needs and values of
their communities. This act overrides that local authority and substitutes the state's
judgment for that of elected local officials, in direct contradiction to the principle of local
control this body has historically championed.
