Section 1:
HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 880
By Lamberth
A RESOLUTION to confirm the appointment of Pete Claussen to
the heritage conservation trust fund board of
trustees.
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 11-7-104(a), provides for the creation
of the heritage conservation trust fund board of trustees; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 11-7-104(b), provides that the
governor shall appoint eleven members to the board; and
WHEREAS, Governor Bill Lee has appointed Pete Claussen as a member of the
heritage conservation trust fund board of trustees representing East Tennessee to a term that
will expire on October 31, 2029; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Claussen is Chairman of Gulf & Ohio Railways, Inc.; he previously held
leadership positions with the Tennessee Valley Authority and the 1982 World's Fair; and
WHEREAS, he has also served as chairman of the board of the National Museum of
American History, a member of the Smithsonian National Board of Directors, and co-chairman
of the Smithsonian development committee; and
WHEREAS, he gives back to his community through his service as chair of the Knoxville
Zoo board and as a board member of the Legacy Parks Foundation, the Museum of Appalachia,
and the Alliance for Better Non-Profits; and
WHEREAS, Pete Claussen is a person of integrity and an active citizen whose
benevolence, acumen, and strong commitment to public service have helped to enhance the
well-being of this State and its citizens; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 11-7-104(b)(2), provides that each
member appointed by the governor to the heritage conservation trust fund board of trustees
shall be confirmed by joint resolution of the general assembly; now, therefore,
HJR0880
011601
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that this body confirms the appointment of Pete Claussen to the heritage
conservation trust fund board of trustees for a term that will expire on October 31, 2029.
- 2 - 011601
