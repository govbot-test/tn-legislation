Section 1:
By Hatcher
HOUSE BILL 2200
By Keisling
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 8,
Chapter 36, relative to hazardous duty benefits.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 8-36-212(a)(5), is amended by
adding the following language immediately after the language "and not the state":
. A governing body of a political subdivision passing a resolution pursuant to this
subdivision (a)(5) shall forward a copy of the resolution to the board of trustees.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
