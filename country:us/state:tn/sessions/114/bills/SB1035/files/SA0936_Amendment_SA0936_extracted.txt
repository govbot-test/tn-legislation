Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 39-11-621, is amended by deleting
the language "or threaten to use" and by adding the following to the end of the section:
A threat to use deadly force, in itself, is not considered use of deadly force for purposes
of this section. A private citizen shall not threaten to use deadly force unless the arrest
is for:
(1) A crime committed or threatened against the person making the
arrest; or
(2) A violent crime committed or threatened in the presence of the person
making the arrest.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 39-11-602, is amended by deleting
"or intended use," in subdivision (2) and by adding the following new subdivision:
( ) "Violent crime" means:
(A) A criminal offense the elements of which include intentionally causing
bodily injury to another; or
(B) Any degree of arson, burglary, carjacking, homicide, kidnapping,
robbery, sexual battery, rape, and human trafficking.

Section 4:
SECTION 3. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.
