by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-6-2101(a), is amended by
designating the subsection as subdivision (1) and adding the following as a new subdivision (2):
(2) If a board of education provides school transportation facilities for children
pursuant to subdivision (a)(1), then the board shall provide school transportation facilities
for all children who live more than one and one-half (1 1/2) miles by the nearest
accessible route from the school to which they are assigned by the board and in which
they are enrolled, unless transportation is prevented due to road conditions or the
remoteness of the child's residence.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-6-2101(b), is amended by
designating the subsection as subdivision (1) and adding the following as a new subdivision (2):
(2) If a board of education provides school transportation facilities for children
pursuant to subdivision (b)(1), then the board shall provide school transportation facilities
for all children who live less than one and one-half (1 1/2) miles by the nearest
accessible route from the school in which they are enrolled, unless transportation is
prevented due to road conditions or the remoteness of the child's residence.

Section 4:
SECTION 3. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.
