HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1300
By Warner
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Brodie Melzoni of
Nolensville High School on being named a 2025-
2026 John Maher Builders Scholar-Athlete.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pay tribute to
those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to excellence,
have distinguished themselves in the classroom and beyond; and
WHEREAS, Brodie Melzoni is one such exceptional student, who was recently named a
John Maher Builders Scholar-Athlete for the 2025-2026 school year in recognition of his
excellence in the classroom, in athletic competition, and in the community; and
WHEREAS, for the past twenty years, John Maher Builders has partnered with the
Williamson Herald to highlight local high school scholar-athletes who are then eligible for
scholarships that will be presented to two female and two male scholar-athletes at the John
Maher Builders Scholar-Athlete banquet in May; and
WHEREAS, a student at Nolensville High School, Brodie Melzoni is a two-time Class AA
state wrestling champion, having won the 175-pound title as a senior and the 165-pound
championship as a junior last year; he led the Knights to a third-place finish overall this past
season; and
WHEREAS, a highly decorated wrestler, Mr. Melzoni is a two-time state champion,
three-time state medalist, Wrestler of the Year award winner at the regional and sectional levels,
and a two-time member of the Middle Tennessee Dream Team; his final victory in the state
championship match broke the school record for most wins in a career with 173; and
WHEREAS, Brodie Melzoni is also a champion in the classroom with a 4.69 grade point
average and a 35 ACT score; he is a member of the National Honor Society and Spanish
Honors; and

WHEREAS, Mr. Melzoni's display of intellectual aptitude and athletic excellence surely
bodes well for his future success as a student at Davidson, where he will continue his education
and wrestling career; and
WHEREAS, his noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for his parents,
Kim and Johnnie Melzoni, his school, and his community; and
WHEREAS, Brodie Melzoni's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of our
respect, admiration, and commendation; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Brodie Melzoni on being named a 2025-2026
John Maher Builders Scholar-Athlete and extend to him our best wishes for a happy, successful
future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
