SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 975
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to congratulate the Walters State Community
College women's basketball team on winning the
TCCAA/Region 7 Championship.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that this General Assembly should pay tribute to those exemplary
student athletes who have attained the pinnacle of competition, thus bringing honor and acclaim
to the State of Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, the Walters State Community College women's basketball team epitomizes
excellence both on and off the basketball court; and
WHEREAS, the Walters State Lady Senators recently enjoyed remarkable success with
an 85-42 victory over Columbia State to win the Tennessee Community College Athletic
Association (TCCAA)/Region 7 Championship; and
WHEREAS, this victory added luster to Walters State's perfect 20-0 regular-season
record and advanced the Lady Senators to the NJCAA Division I Basketball Tournament for the
second consecutive season and the twenty-first time in the program's history; and
WHEREAS, in the championship game, the Lady Senators were led by tournament MVP
Nyjah Gibbs, with sixteen points and nine rebounds; Terryauna Griffin, with twenty-five points,
four steals, and four blocks; and Malon Smith, with thirteen points, seven assists, and six
rebounds; and
WHEREAS, Walters State also received standout performances from Raegan Starks,
with nine points and nine rebounds; Tierra Coleman, with eight points and nine rebounds;
Anaya Mulholland-Natt, with five points and four steals; Paradise Fisher, with four points;
Tahara Diallo, with three points and four rebounds; and Mariana Calero Zuniga, with two points;
and

WHEREAS, through hard work and dedication, the members of the Walters State Lady
Senators prepared themselves for all the challenges of this season, growing and improving both
as individual players and as a team; and
WHEREAS, the members of the Walters State Community College women's basketball
team have proven themselves to be deserving victors, and it is fitting that the team be honored;
now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate the Walters State
Community College women's basketball team on winning the TCCAA/Region 7 Championship
and extend to them our best wishes for every continued success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
