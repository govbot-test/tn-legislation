HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1071
By Keisling
A RESOLUTION to honor Brittany Cooper upon being named
Coach of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those excellent coaches who have led their players to success in athletic competition
and in life; and
WHEREAS, Coach Brittany Cooper has established herself as the head girls' basketball
coach of Pickett County High School and guided the Lady Bobcats to unprecedented heights of
achievement; and
WHEREAS, Brittany Cooper was named Coach of the Year by her peers in recognition
of her devoted leadership and success as the head girls' basketball coach at Pickett County
High School; and
WHEREAS, in her very first year of coaching the Lady Bobcats, Coach Brittany Cooper
led her team to the 7-A District Championship over Jackson County; additionally, the team was
ranked No. 6 in the Tennessee Sports Writers Association's Top 10; and
WHEREAS, Brittany Cooper has distinguished herself as a great coach, tactician, and
motivator of young athletes, and she should be appropriately recognized on this special
occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Brittany Cooper upon being named Coach of
the Year and extend to her our best wishes for much continued success both on and off the
court.
