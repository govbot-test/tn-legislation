SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1041
By Bailey
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend the White County High
School Lady Warriors basketball team.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that this General Assembly should salute those student athletes
who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as true champions of
whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such exemplary group of young athletes is the White County High
School Lady Warriors basketball team, whose members have all proven themselves to be the
fiercest of competitors and truly worthy of admiration by finishing runner-up in the Tennessee
Secondary School Athletic Association (TSSAA) Division I, Class 3A state tournament
championship; and
WHEREAS, last season, the White County Lady Warriors made their first appearance in
the state tournament since 1997; this year, they took the next step, reaching the state
championship game for the first time in thirty-four years to reclaim their mantle as a high school
basketball powerhouse in the State of Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, the Lady Warriors enjoyed the stellar leadership and extensive basketball
knowledge of Head Coach Michael Dodgen and the tremendous support of their families, their
fans, and their community; and
WHEREAS, the members of the White County High School Lady Warriors basketball
team epitomize all that is good in today's student athletes, as they have achieved a premier
level of success in competition while exhibiting the highest degree of character in all aspects of
life; and

WHEREAS, the perseverance, determination, work ethic, and talent of this group of
young women not only made possible a special season but also surely bode well for their future
success; and
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly find it appropriate to pause in their
deliberations to acknowledge and applaud the members of the White County High School Lady
Warriors basketball team for their outstanding performance this season; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and commend the members and
coaches of the White County High School Lady Warriors basketball team upon their runner-up
finish in the TSSAA Division I, Class 3A state tournament championship and extend to them our
best wishes for every continued success in all their future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
