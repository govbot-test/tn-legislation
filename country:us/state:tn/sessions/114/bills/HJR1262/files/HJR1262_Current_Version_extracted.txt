HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1262
By Sparks
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Jacob Pagel for
exemplary service as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this legislative body is pleased to recognize certain
persons who have committed themselves, through their exceptional service, to the betterment of
the legislative process; and
WHEREAS, Jacob Pagel is one such individual, who has distinguished himself in his
role as a legislative intern for Representative Mike Sparks; his work has resulted in innumerable
benefits for the legislators he assisted and the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, a student at Middle Tennessee State University (MTSU), where he is highly
regarded by his peers, Mr. Pagel is an active participant in campus leadership with the Student
Government Association, and he was competitively selected to participate in the prestigious
2026 Tennessee Legislative Internship Program; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize himself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further his studies, Jacob Pagel brought his many abilities
and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2026 session of the One Hundred Fourteenth
General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, throughout the 2026 legislative session, Mr. Pagel has demonstrated a
strong work ethic, attention to detail, and a commitment to public service, gaining valuable
insight into the workings of State government while representing MTSU and the broader student
community with distinction; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Pagel's dedication reflects positively on the next generation of
Tennessee leaders and underscores the importance of experiential learning opportunities in
fostering informed and engaged citizens; and

WHEREAS, Mr. Pagel has shown an ability to learn and adapt to any situation,
challenge, or roadblock throughout his time as a legislative intern; he has never let his disability
stop him from getting a job done or prevent him for achieving his goals; and
WHEREAS, Jacob Pagel has performed his many duties with extraordinary insight and
rare decorum; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby commend Jacob Pagel for outstanding service as a legislative
intern for Representative Mike Sparks and extend to him our best wishes for every future
success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
