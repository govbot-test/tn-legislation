HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1061
By Freeman
A RESOLUTION to commend April Christian James for exemplary
service as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this General Assembly is pleased to recognize
certain persons who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment
of the legislative process; and
WHEREAS, April Christian James is one such individual, who has distinguished herself
in her role as a legislative intern for Representative Bob Freeman; Ms. James's work has
resulted in innumerable benefits for the legislators she assisted and the General Assembly as a
whole; and
WHEREAS, a student at Freed-Hardeman University, where she is highly regarded by
her peers, April James has achieved remarkable academic success while participating in
numerous extracurricular activities; in May 2026, she will receive a Bachelor of Arts degree in
Law and Politics with a minor in Business Administration; and
WHEREAS, Ms. James is the president of the Freed-Hardeman University Law Society,
the secretary of the Student Government Association, a member of Phi Kappa Alpha, and a
team member of Campus Delegate, positions that further exemplify her exceptional leadership
qualities and character; and
WHEREAS, she also participated in the Tennessee Intercollegiate Legislature's
Appellate Moot Court Collegiate Challenge for two years; and
WHEREAS, in her free time, April James loves to travel, read, watch movies, and play
board games; and
WHEREAS, Ms. James is the daughter of Mike James and the late Shannon James and
the stepdaughter of Ann Morrison James; and

WHEREAS, eager to familiarize herself with the myriad aspects and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further her studies, Ms. James brought her many abilities
and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2026 session of the One Hundred Fourteenth
General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, throughout the 2026 legislative session, April James has surpassed all
criteria that define a devoted and conscientious legislative intern; and
WHEREAS, Ms. James has performed her many duties with extraordinary insight and
rare decorum, and her efforts are surely a source of pride for her family; her university; and her
community; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby commend April Christian James for outstanding service as a
legislative intern for Representative Bob Freeman and extend to her our best wishes for every
future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
