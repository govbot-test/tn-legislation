HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 985
By Faison
A RESOLUTION to commend Representative Debra Moody for
her honorable and astute service to the people
of Tennessee as a member of the House of
Representatives.
WHEREAS, it is most appropriate that the members of this General Assembly should
honor those fellow legislators who have performed their duties with the utmost integrity and
whose efforts during their time in office have been dedicated to providing thoughtful and
exemplary service to their districts and to this great State; and
WHEREAS, Representative Debra Moody of Covington is one such distinguished public
servant, who has served both her constituency and this legislative body with honor and ability;
and
WHEREAS, during her tenure on Capitol Hill, Representative Moody has been a highly
influential figure in the political, social, and civic life of Tipton and Haywood counties, and she
has served her constituents in numerous capacities, always working diligently at her elected
duties and expending the necessary effort to understand the opposing sides of complex issues;
and
WHEREAS, Debra Moody has distinguished herself as a public-spirited citizen of the
highest order and as an exceptional asset to the good people of the 81st House District, whom
she has served as a member of the 108th, 109th, 110th, 111th, 112th, 113th,and 114th General
Assemblies; and
WHEREAS, throughout her service, Representative Moody has been prominent as a
dedicated and well-informed legislator who always votes her conscience; and
WHEREAS, an active and dynamic participant in the legislative process during her time
in the General Assembly, Representative Debra Moody has rendered sterling service as chair of

Community Bible Study, Tipton County Republican Women, the Tipton County Republican

South Military Museum; she attended Arkansas State University and the University of Memphis;
and
WHEREAS, she has also served as a member of the Individualized Education Account
Advisory Council and the Human Trafficking Task Force; and
WHEREAS, no stranger to awards and accolades, she was named Tennessee's Most
Conservative Legislator by Club for Growth and an NCSL Early Learning Fellow in 2019 and a
recipient of the Loyalty Day Award from the Veterans of Foreign Wars and the AWAKE
Advocacy Award; and
WHEREAS, her many professional accomplishments aside, Representative Moody is
most thankful for the love and support of her husband, Terry, and their four children; and
WHEREAS, throughout her outstanding career as a public servant, Debra Moody has
worked tirelessly on behalf of her constituents, bringing to her office an honorable bearing and
the traditional values of hard work and common sense; and
WHEREAS, Representative Debra Moody is wholly committed to the noble precepts of
public service that have earned Tennessee recognition as the Volunteer State, and she should
be specially recognized for her exemplary tenure in the General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby honor Representative Debra Moody for her meritorious service
to Tennessee as a member of the House of Representatives, commend her for her countless
contributions and many good works in service of the citizens of the 81st House District, and
extend to her our best wishes for much continued success and happiness.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to

without House or Senate designation.
