HOUSE BILL 1606

AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 4,
Chapter 29 and Title 68, Chapter 142, Part 1,
relative to the Tennessee child fatality prevention
team.

Section 2:

subdivision (55).

Section 3:

the following as a new subdivision:
( ) Tennessee child fatality prevention team, created by § 68-142-102;
