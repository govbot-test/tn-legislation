deleting the period at the end of the subdivision and substituting:
, or who, using clinical judgment, determines whether a treatment or procedure is
clinically appropriate for the symptoms and diagnosis of such condition. This subdivision
(a)(1) does not create a private right of action. This subdivision (a)(1) does not apply to

during a contested case procedure conducted pursuant to title 4, chapter 5, part 3.

accordance with the laws of this state.

SECTION 4. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 63-9-106(a), is amended by adding

The practice of osteopathic medicine also includes determining, using clinical judgment,
