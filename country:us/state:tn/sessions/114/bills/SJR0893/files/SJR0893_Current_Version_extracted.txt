SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 893
By Seal
A RESOLUTION to recognize and commend the members of the
Claiborne High School Band for their selection to
represent the State of Tennessee at the Salute
to Independence Parade.
WHEREAS, it is the privilege of the members of this General Assembly to recognize
those exemplary Tennessee public high school music programs that embody exceptional talent,
dedication, and service, proudly representing our State on the local, national, and international
stages; and
WHEREAS, the Claiborne High School Band has been selected for the extraordinary
honor of representing the State of Tennessee at the Salute to Independence Parade in
Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, celebrating the 250th anniversary of the founding of the United
States of America; and
WHEREAS, this national event will place Claiborne High band students on a world stage
as they perform near Independence Hall, where the Declaration of Independence was signed;
and
WHEREAS, since first taking the field in 1967, the Blue Battalion has built a legacy of
excellence in musicianship, leadership, and discipline; the band consistently earns superior
ratings at competitions and has appeared for four consecutive years at the Tennessee State
Division II Championships; and
WHEREAS, the success of this program can be measured beyond trophies, as it boasts
a 100-percent graduation rate among participating students and has counted among its ranks
multiple Valedictorians and Salutatorians over the years; band members average ACT scores of
23 (five points above the school average) and regularly earn college scholarships to universities
that include East Tennessee State University, Tennessee Technological University, Carson-

WHEREAS, high-quality music and arts education programs significantly contribute to

WHEREAS, it is most fitting that we recognize the members of the Claiborne High

BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we recognize and commend the members of the
Claiborne High School Band for their selection to represent the State of Tennessee at the
Salute to Independence Parade in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, celebrating the 250th anniversary
of the founding of the United States of America.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
