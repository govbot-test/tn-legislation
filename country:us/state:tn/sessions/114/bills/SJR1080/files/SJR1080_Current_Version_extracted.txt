SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1080
By Oliver
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize PENCIL.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly take great pleasure in paying tribute
to Public Education Needs Community Involvement and Leadership (PENCIL), an organization
committed to helping young people achieve academic success and prepare for life; and
WHEREAS, PENCIL is a nonprofit organization in Nashville that links Metro Nashville
Public Schools (MNPS) with community resources to better serve the needs of both students
and educators; and
WHEREAS, for forty-four years, PENCIL has worked with private industry, city officials,
and other nonprofits to achieve strategic goals for MNPS students through community
partnerships; and
WHEREAS, PENCIL and its network of partners are committed to meeting the growing
needs of MNPS, serving more than 79,000 students across 159 schools, and ensuring equal
access to education for all students; and
WHEREAS, PENCIL Partners match the specific needs of each school with an
appropriate business, community group, or individual, supporting more than 600 local
organizations as active partners in MNPS schools; and
WHEREAS, PENCIL works closely with MNPS leadership to develop programs and
services essential for student success, filling resource gaps and addressing immediate and
ongoing needs; and
WHEREAS, PENCIL offers programs, including the DG PENCIL Box, a free educator
resource center where educators shop each month for in-demand supplies, such as backpacks,
pencils, crayons, filler paper, and more; PENCIL's STEAM Lending Library, which provides
hands-on, essential resources that empower students to meet state computer science

WHEREAS, from 2024 to 2025, PENCIL achieved more than $5.93 million in total

33,000 volunteer hours, and more than $1 million in grants to and for MNPS; and
WHEREAS, PENCIL continues to support MNPS by recruiting business partners and
serving as a teacher resource center, ensuring that each student has equitable access to
resources and opportunities; and
WHEREAS, Public Education Needs Community Involvement and Leadership is wholly
dedicated to serving the needs of our youth and our educators through community partnerships
and is deserving of our praise and approbation; now, therefore,

GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that, on March 30, 2026, National Pencil Day, we honor
