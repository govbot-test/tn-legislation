SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1167
By Crowe
A RESOLUTION to congratulate the Milligan University Buffaloes
disc golf teams, 2026 College Disc Golf National
Champions.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should honor and
commend those extraordinary athletic teams that, through their magnificent exploits, bring honor
to the State of Tennessee and serve as exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, the Milligan University Buffaloes disc golf teams recently made history,
winning both the men's and women's Division I College Disc Golf national championships for the
first time in program history; and
WHEREAS, in the men's competition, the Buffaloes foursome of Ben Wagner, Ethan
Bishop, James Blocker, and Zac Nelson took top honors from among the forty-eight teams
competing in the national championship at Rock Hill, South Carolina; and
WHEREAS, in the women's competition, Milligan Buffaloes Ari Timm and Grace
Sanderson won a thriller after four rounds of play, as Ms. Timm made a birdie putt from distance
on the final hole to clinch the title; and
WHEREAS, a powerhouse collegiate disc golf program, Milligan University won its first
national title at the Division III level in 2025, launching the Buffaloes on the trajectory that saw
them claim two more national championships this season; and
WHEREAS, the Milligan University Buffaloes enjoy the expert guidance and leadership
of Head Coach Tim Barr and Volunteer Assistant Coach Zach Melton; and
WHEREAS, in addition to the teammates listed above, the Milligan University disc golf
team includes Aaron Walker, Owen Gibson, Harrison Greene, Jett Carter, Chris Berney, Sean
Pagenkopf, John Bernard, Stuart Kitchings, Ethan Reab, Isaiah Crawford, Spencer Brown,
Michael Nickens, Hailey Crouch, and Karyn Makwerere; and
SJR1167
018233
- 1 -
WHEREAS, the members of the Milligan University disc golf teams epitomize all that is
good in today's student athletes, as they have achieved a premier level of success in
competition while exhibiting the highest degree of character in all aspects of life; and
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly take great pride in honoring this
extraordinary group of young men and women who have indelibly written their names for all time
into the annals of collegiate disc golf; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate the members and
coaches of the Milligan University disc golf teams upon winning the 2026 Division I College Disc
Golf men's and women's national championships and extend to them our best wishes for every
future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
