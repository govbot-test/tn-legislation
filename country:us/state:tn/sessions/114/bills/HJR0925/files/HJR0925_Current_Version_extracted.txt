HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 925
By McKenzie
A RESOLUTION to congratulate Austin-East High School on
reaching TVAAS Level 5 status.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should pay tribute to those public schools in Tennessee
that, through the natural talents and unflagging commitment to academic excellence evidenced
by their administration, faculty, staff, students, and parents, have achieved significant
milestones; and
WHEREAS, Austin-East High School in Knoxville is one such school that achieved a
TVAAS Level 5 ranking for the first time in 2024-2025, recording its strongest TNReady
achievement results ever across all subjects; and
WHEREAS, TVAAS (Tennessee Value-Added Assessment System) measures year-to-
year academic growth, with Level 5 being the highest possible score for student growth across
all tested subjects; and
WHEREAS, Principal Dr. Rob Speas and the administration, faculty, and staff of Austin-
East High School have built an environment that ensures students have the very best
atmosphere for learning; and
WHEREAS, Austin-East High School's attainment of TVAAS Level 5 status is a
tremendous source of pride for the administration, teachers, students, and parents of the
community; and
WHEREAS, the dedication to academic excellence demonstrated by all the fine
individuals affiliated with Austin-East High School is clearly deserving of our respect, admiration,
and commendation; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
HJR0925
013005
- 1 -
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Austin-East High School on attaining TVAAS
Level 5 status, salute the school's administration, faculty, staff, students, and parents on this
exemplary achievement, and extend to them our best wishes for continued success in the
future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
- 2 - 013005
