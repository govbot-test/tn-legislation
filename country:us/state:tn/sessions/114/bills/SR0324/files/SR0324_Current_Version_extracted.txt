SENATE RESOLUTION 324
By Walley
A RESOLUTION to honor the Adamsville Junior High School girls'
basketball team upon earning a bid to the
TMSAA Class A state championship
tournament.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should salute those
student athletes who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as true
champions of whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such exemplary group of young ladies is the Lady Cardinals girls'
basketball team of Adamsville Junior High School in McNairy County, whose members have
proven themselves to be the fiercest of competitors and truly worthy of admiration by earning a
bid to the 2025-2026 TMSAA Class A state championship tournament; and
WHEREAS, Adamsville made history as the first McNairy County team to advance to the
final four of the state tournament in its new format; and
WHEREAS, the Lady Cardinals claimed county, district, and regional championships this
season and then won their sectional game to earn a state tournament berth; and
WHEREAS, Coach Katie Newsom displayed strategic leadership, and her ability to
adapt to every challenge was crucial in ultimately leading the Lady Cardinals to the state
tournament; and
WHEREAS, the Lady Cardinals were fortunate to enjoy the unfailing support of their
families, fans, fellow students, and the entire community; and
WHEREAS, the members of the Adamsville Junior High School girls' basketball team
epitomize all that is good in today's student athletes, as they have achieved a premier level of
success in competition while exhibiting the highest degree of character in all aspects of life; and

WHEREAS, Adamsville's success was built on a foundation of unity and a shared goal,
and the team chemistry among the players was evident in every game, as they treated the Lady
Cardinals program as a sisterhood; and
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to acknowledge and applaud the Adamsville Junior
High School lady hoopsters for their outstanding season on the hardwood; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and congratulate the
coaches and players of the Adamsville Junior High School girls' basketball team upon earning a
bid to the 2025-2026 TMSAA Class A state championship tournament and extend to them our
best wishes for every continued success in all their future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
