HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1091
By Howell
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Revolution show choir at
Lake Forest Middle School on the occasion of its
twenty-fifth anniversary.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly are pleased to recognize those
successful middle school artists who, through their natural talents and extraordinary efforts,
bring the joys of creative expression and culture to the citizens of this State while promoting the
importance of the performing arts; and
WHEREAS, Revolution show choir at Lake Forest Middle School in Bradley County is
celebrating its twenty-fifth anniversary during the 2025-2026 school year, marking a quarter
century of artistic excellence in choral music and performance; and
WHEREAS, the award-winning music program at Lake Forest Middle School, known as
Revolution, is nationally renowned and offers students a dynamic and enriching experience in
both vocal and instrumental performance; and
WHEREAS, Revolution show choir consists of seventh- and eighth-grade students who
have a love for music and dance and allows students to explore their talents through music; and
WHEREAS, Revolution has received multiple accolades throughout its twenty-five-year
history; the group is currently under the leadership of two award-winning directors, who
endeavor to uplift and encourage students by fostering self-expression through music and
performance; and
WHEREAS, the 2025-2026 edition of Revolution has reached the very pinnacle of
success, partaking in competitions throughout the southeast and claiming five grand
championship titles, five grand vocals championships, five Best Choreography wins, four Best
Female Soloist wins, one Best Male Soloist win, one Best Performer win, one Spirit of Diamond
Classic Award, and one Best Band Award; and

WHEREAS, the members of Revolution show choir continue to demonstrate the utmost
talent, skill, and dedication, proving themselves to be exceptional vocalists and performers of
the highest degree; and
WHEREAS, it is most fitting that we recognize the continuing legacy of this phenomenal
group on this special occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Revolution show choir of Lake Forest Middle
School on the occasion of its twenty-fifth anniversary, extending our best wishes for every
continued success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
