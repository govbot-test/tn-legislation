AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 374* House Bill No. 1358
by deleting subsection (c) in SECTION 1 and substituting instead:
(c) The members of the study committee shall receive no compensation for their
service on the committee, nor are the members entitled to per diem or travel expenses
for the purposes of carrying out their duties under this section. The members of the
study committee who are members of the general assembly shall participate
electronically. Each member participating electronically must be able to simultaneously
hear each other and speak to each other during the meeting.
