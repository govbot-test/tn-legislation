HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1519
By Russell
A RESOLUTION to honor Doug Gunnels of the Tennessee
Department of Transportation on the occasion of
his retirement.
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that we should honor those citizens who have performed
with uncommon devotion and enthusiasm throughout their years of public service; and
WHEREAS, Doug Gunnels is one such estimable person, who is taking his much-
deserved retirement after a distinguished fourteen-year career with the Tennessee Department
of Transportation (TDOT); and
WHEREAS, Mr. Gunnels has ably supported the department in serving the needs of the
General Assembly, providing assistance and insights to legislators on matters related to TDOT;
and
WHEREAS, Doug Gunnels began working with TDOT in 2012 during Governor Haslam's
Administration, and he has continued to serve the department with purpose and commitment
throughout Governor Lee's Administration; and
WHEREAS, prior to his tenure with TDOT, Doug Gunnels represented the 21st District in
the Tennessee House of Representatives as a member of the 96th through 101st General
Assemblies (1989-2001); and
WHEREAS, Doug Gunnels was an effective public servant and served as the Minority
Floor Leader during the 100th General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, Representative Gunnels served the good people of Loudon and Monroe
counties with the utmost integrity and probity for twelve years before retiring from this body; and
WHEREAS, his many accomplishments and accolades aside, Doug Gunnels is most
appreciative of the love and companionship he shares with his wife, Vicki; they have been
blessed with two outstanding children, Brett and the late Cory Gunnels; and

WHEREAS, Doug Gunnels is retiring from TDOT this summer, thus concluding a
remarkable career that included exemplary service in two branches of State government; and
WHEREAS, throughout his estimable career, Doug Gunnels has demonstrated the
utmost professionalism and ability, winning the unbridled respect and admiration of the
members of the General Assembly, his colleagues at TDOT, and the citizens of the State of
Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, it is fitting that he be recognized for his outstanding service and dedication
to the citizens of Tennessee on this special occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor Doug Gunnels on the occasion of his retirement, thank him for
his fourteen years of service with the Tennessee Department of Transportation as their liaison
to the General Assembly, and extend to him our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
