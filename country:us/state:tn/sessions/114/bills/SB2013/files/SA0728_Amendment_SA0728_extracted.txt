Senate Health and Welfare 2
Amendment No. 2 to SB2013
Crowe
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 2013* House Bill No. 1905
by deleting § 63-7-503(3) in SECTION 1 and substituting:
(3) "Program based in this state" means a nursing education program that
maintains a physical campus or facility located in this state that has a minimum of one
(1) full-time faculty member on-site who is assigned to the nursing degree program who
is licensed, registered, or certified or eligible for licensure, registration, or certification
pursuant to this chapter.
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting § 63-7-504(a) in SECTION 1 and substituting:
(a) There is established, and the board shall implement, a moratorium on the
acceptance and approval of applications from nursing education programs not based in
this state that seek approval from the board to operate, enroll students, or place students
for clinical experiences within this state.
