( ) A commercially operated facility that:
(i) Was established in 1959 as a private golf club having an eighteen-
hole golf course, a pool, tennis and pickleball courts, basketball courts, and a
clubhouse offering dining and year-round social activities, including holiday
celebrations, family gatherings, corporate events, and weddings; and
(ii) Is located within five hundred feet (500') of a bend in the Duck River,
within four thousand feet (4,000') of a memorial to a horse that died in 1912, in a
county having a population of not less than fifty thousand two hundred (50,200)
and not more than fifty thousand three hundred (50,300), according to the 2020
or a subsequent federal census;
