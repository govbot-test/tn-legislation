HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1215
By McCalmon
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend the Club America
chapter at Independence High School.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly find it appropriate to honor and
commend those high school clubs and organizations that promote American values; and
WHEREAS, the Club America chapter at Independence High School is one such club
that is deserving of our acknowledgement; and
WHEREAS, the Independence High School Club America was founded by Coleman
Wooten in 2026; and
WHEREAS, the Club America chapter at Independence High School held its first
meeting in February with more than twenty students in attendance; the chapter holds bi-weekly
meetings as the group continues to grow; and
WHEREAS, Club America empowers bold student leaders to promote free thinking,
engage in grassroots activism, and bring their beliefs to life—both on and off campus; and
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly honor this Club
America chapter and its many student members; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend the Independence High School Club America
chapter and its members for their dedication to patriotism and free thinking.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
