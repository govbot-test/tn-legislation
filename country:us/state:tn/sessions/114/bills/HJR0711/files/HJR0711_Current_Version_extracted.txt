HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 711
By Faison
A RESOLUTION to honor the memory of Charlie Kirk.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly were greatly saddened to learn of
the assassination of Charlie Kirk, a distinguished American whose legacy of unwavering
dedication to faith, freedom, and family will endure for generations; and
WHEREAS, Charlie Kirk devoted his life to the service of his country, to advancing the
cause of freedom, and to inspiring young Americans to embrace civic duty, responsibility, and
opportunity; and
WHEREAS, born with a passion for leadership, Charlie Kirk rose to national recognition
at age eighteen as a founder and leader of Turning Point USA, which would go on to connect a
new generation with the values of liberty, faith, and the principles of the United States
Constitution; and
WHEREAS, throughout his career, Mr. Kirk faced adversity with steadfast courage,
demonstrating resilience and resolve in the face of challenges, and remained unwavering in his
belief that faith, perseverance, and moral clarity can guide individuals and nations toward a
brighter future; and
WHEREAS, he was guided by his deep Christian faith, which shaped his vision,
informed his leadership, and sustained him in his work of mentoring, educating, and
encouraging young men and women to pursue truth and live lives of purpose; and
WHEREAS, he believed in and encouraged the process of peaceful debate, openly
stating that "When people stop talking, that's when you get violence"; and

WHEREAS, Charlie Kirk was not only a national figure but also a devoted husband and
father, cherishing his wife and children and exemplifying the virtues of family life in both private
and public spheres; and
WHEREAS, a deranged assassin's bullet took his life on September 10, 2025, while he
was peacefully engaging in political and faith conversations, as he had so many times before in
the spirit of peaceful debate; and
WHEREAS, his legacy endures through the millions of lives he touched and the new
generation of people he empowered; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that the members of this General Assembly do hereby celebrate and honor the
life of Charlie Kirk and recognize his contributions to American youth and the conservative
movement.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that we reflect fondly upon his impeccable character and
his stalwart commitment to living the examined life with courage and conviction and extend our
heartfelt condolences to his wife, children, family, and many friends.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
