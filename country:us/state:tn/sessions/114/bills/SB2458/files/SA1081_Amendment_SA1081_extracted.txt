Section 1:
Amendment No. 4 to SB2458
Bailey
Signature of Sponsor
by adding to § 55-12-143 in Section 1 the following as a new subsection:
(j) The insurance requirements of this section for the delivery availability period
do not apply to a person or entity whose primary business is the retail sale of goods or
services directly to consumers and that owns and operates a digital network solely to
dispatch delivery operators for goods or services sold by that person or entity directly to
consumers.
