Section 1:
By Doggett
SENATE BILL 2373
By Rose
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 5;
Title 6; Title 7; Title 8, Chapter 8; Title 29; Title 40
and Title 41, relative to arrestees.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 41-4-103, is amended by deleting
subsection (b) and substituting:
(b) The jailer may perform evaluations of the persons listed in subdivisions
(a)(1)-(7) for purposes of classification; management; care, including the need for
medical attention; control; and cell assignment.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
