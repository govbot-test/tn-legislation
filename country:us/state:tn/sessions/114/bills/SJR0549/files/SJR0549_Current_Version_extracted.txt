SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 549
By Pody
A RESOLUTION to honor the longstanding partnership between
the Province of Québec and the State of
Tennessee.
WHEREAS, the State of Tennessee and the Province of Québec share a deep and
enduring friendship rooted in mutual values and strong economic ties; and
WHEREAS, the State of Tennessee and the Province of Québec were among the co-
founding members of the Southeastern United States-Canadian Provinces alliance; and
WHEREAS, the State of Tennessee, up to 2022, was the largest trading partner of the
Province of Québec in the Southeast, with almost $3 billion Canadian in trade between the two
regions; and
WHEREAS, in Québec, the city of Montréal is an international hub for aerospace and
renewable energy, which has complemented Tennessee's strengths in health care, automotive
manufacturing, and technology; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee and Québec have demonstrated a steadfast commitment to
promoting innovation and prosperity, and they have benefited immeasurably from their vibrant
partnership; and
WHEREAS, it is most appropriate that this remarkable partnership be specially
recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor the longstanding relationship between the
Province of Québec and the State of Tennessee and extend our best wishes for many more
years of mutual prosperity and growth.
