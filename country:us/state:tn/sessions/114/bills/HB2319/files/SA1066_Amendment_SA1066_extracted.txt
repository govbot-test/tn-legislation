Amendment No. 2 to SB2591
Briggs
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 2591* House Bill No. 2319
by adding the following at the end of the amendatory language of Section 1:
Employees of a local education agency are not county employees for purposes
of this subdivision (c)(1).
AND FURTHER AMEND by adding the following new section immediately preceding the
effective date section and renumbering the effective date section accordingly:
SECTION __. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-51-1501, is amended by adding
the following new sentence to the end of the section:
For purposes of qualifying to run for elected office for the legislative body of a
municipal government or metropolitan government, an employee of a local education
agency for the municipality or metropolitan government is not a municipal government or
metropolitan government employee, as applicable.
