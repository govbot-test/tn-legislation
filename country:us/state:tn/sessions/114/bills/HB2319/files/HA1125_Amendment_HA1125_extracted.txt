Amendment No. 3 to HB2319
Hemmer
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 2591* House Bill No. 2319
by inserting the following new section immediately preceding the effective date section and
redesignating the effective date section accordingly:
SECTION __. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 2-16-101, is amended by
designating subsection (a) as subdivision (a)(1) and adding the following new subdivision (a)(2):
(2) Notwithstanding another law, a person appointed by the governor to fill a
vacancy in the office of United States senator pursuant to subdivision (a)(1) is not
qualified to run for the office of United States senator after completion of the term for
which the person was appointed.
