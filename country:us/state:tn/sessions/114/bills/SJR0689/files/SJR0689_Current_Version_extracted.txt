SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 689
By Hatcher
A RESOLUTION to recognize Kathy Stark on her twenty-fifth
anniversary with Blount County Habitat for
Humanity.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should specially recognize those estimable individuals
who have dedicated themselves to improving the quality of life for their fellow citizens; and
WHEREAS, one such admirable person is Kathy Stark, Executive Director of Blount
County Habitat for Humanity, who has ably served as an employee of that esteemed
organization since April 2001; and
WHEREAS, named to her current position in June 2016, Ms. Stark has played a pivotal
role in shaping Blount County Habitat for Humanity's growth and impact during her career,
including in the residential neighborhoods of Jubilee Ridge, Norwood Village, and Halls Place,
with the latter under her direct supervision; and
WHEREAS, during Ms. Stark's tenure, Blount County Habitat for Humanity established
Foothills Community Development Corporation in 2002, which enabled an additional 100
families in a higher income category to become homeowners; and
WHEREAS, seeking to greatly enhance the nonprofit organization's operational
efficiency, Ms. Stark facilitated the acquisition of the current Habitat administration building and
adjacent property, moving the organization out of rental spaces and into a permanent home in
2006; and
WHEREAS, she played a key role in securing the 2017 purchase of the current location
for the Habitat ReStore at Foothills Mall in Maryville, providing a much-needed upgrade from
smaller, inadequate rental spaces; and

WHEREAS, Ms. Stark's contributions also led to the establishment of the Critical Home
Repair program, which has assisted numerous homeowners in repairing their homes despite
financial constraints; and
WHEREAS, through her tireless efforts, Kathy Stark has made a profound impact on
Blount County, providing affordable housing solutions and improving the lives of countless
individuals and families. Her dedication to Habitat for Humanity's mission is a testament to her
commitment to serving others, and she should be specially recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Kathy Stark on the
occasion of her twenty-fifth anniversary with Blount County Habitat for Humanity, extending our
gratitude for a job well done and our best wishes for every future success and happiness.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
