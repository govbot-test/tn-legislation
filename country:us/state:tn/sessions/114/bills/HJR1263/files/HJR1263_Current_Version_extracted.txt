HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1263
By Hemmer
A RESOLUTION to congratulate James "Jim" Martin Bradfield and
Karen Keene Bradfield of Brentwood upon the
occasion of their fiftieth wedding anniversary.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly take great pleasure in paying tribute
to those successful marriages that have stood the test of time and serve as exemplars for
present and future generations; and
WHEREAS, on June 12, 1976, Jim and Karen Bradfield were joined in holy matrimony in
Bristol, and since that fateful day, this outstanding couple has faced the world as one, basking in
the glow of their love for each other during both life's joys and sorrows; and
WHEREAS, Jim and Karen Bradfield have served the Nashville entertainment industry
through their company Bradfield Stage Lighting; and
WHEREAS, Mr. and Mrs. Bradfield built their life together in the Belmont Neighborhood
for over forty years; and
WHEREAS, Jim and Karen Bradfield have been blessed with their children, Allison
Lewis, Michael Bradfield, and Daniel Bradfield, and twelve grandchildren; and
WHEREAS, for fifty years, this blessed union has grown and prospered as an
expression of time-honored family values and selfless love; and
WHEREAS, marriages such as the Bradfields' truly represent the best and brightest of
our society, as they remind us of the intrinsic value of continuity and commitment in this
everchanging world; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate James "Jim" Martin Bradfield and Karen Keene
