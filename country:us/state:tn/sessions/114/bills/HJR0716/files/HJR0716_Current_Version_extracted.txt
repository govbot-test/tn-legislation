HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 716
By Atchley
A RESOLUTION to recognize Captain Dennis Adams of the
Pigeon Forge Police Department on the
occasion of his retirement.
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that the members of this General Assembly should honor
those citizens who have performed with uncommon devotion and enthusiasm throughout their
careers in law enforcement; and
WHEREAS, Captain Dennis Adams of the Pigeon Forge Police Department is one such
estimable individual, who has devoted his life to the preservation of law and order and the
protection of his fellow citizens; and
WHEREAS, after forty-seven years of distinguished service to the City of Pigeon Forge,
Captain Dennis Adams took a much-deserved retirement on January 31, 2025, having
dedicated himself to the highest tenets of public service and law enforcement throughout his
career; and
WHEREAS, a proud native of Sevier County, Captain Adams is a 1972 graduate of
Sevier County High School; he began his exemplary law enforcement career in August 1978
and completed his foundational training at the Tennessee Law Enforcement Training Academy
in October of that same year; and
WHEREAS, Captain Dennis Adams's dedication and professionalism were recognized
early in his career, when he was named the Employee of the Year for the City of Pigeon Forge
in 1984; and
WHEREAS, committed to continual learning and professional development, Captain
Adams graduated from the prestigious FBI National Academy (Session 1969) and was a
member of the Leadership Sevier Class of 2000, in addition to completing numerous specialized
training courses in firearms, officer tactics, and leadership; and

WHEREAS, Captain Dennis Adams's passion for fostering safety, excellence, and
integrity in law enforcement has made a lasting impact on the Pigeon Forge Police Department
and the broader public safety community; and
WHEREAS, in his retirement, Captain Adams continues to serve his beloved community
in a leadership capacity, now holding office as a Commissioner for the City of Pigeon Forge;
and
WHEREAS, Captain Dennis Adams exemplifies the spirit and allegiance to community
that are characteristic of a true Tennessean; and
WHEREAS, throughout his career in law enforcement, Captain Adams performed his
duties with professionalism and integrity, helping his fellow citizens in any way that he could and
winning the sincere respect and admiration of his colleagues and the citizens of Pigeon Forge;
now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Captain Dennis Adams on his exemplary career
with the Pigeon Forge Police Department and extend to him our best wishes for much continued
success and happiness in his retirement.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
