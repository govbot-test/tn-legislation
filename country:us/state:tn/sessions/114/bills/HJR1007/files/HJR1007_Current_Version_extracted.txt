HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1007
By Keisling
A RESOLUTION to honor Darrell Collins upon his induction into
the Clay County Sports Hall of Fame.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that this General Assembly pay tribute to those gifted athletes,
coaches, administrators, and contributors who attained the very pinnacle of sporting
achievement; and
WHEREAS, Darrell Collins is one such legendary contributor, who was recently inducted
into the Clay County Sports Hall of Fame; and
WHEREAS, an iconic figure within the annals of Clay County sports, Mr. Collins has
lived his entire life as a diehard Bulldog player, fan, announcer, and coach, having a profound
impact on his beloved community; and
WHEREAS, after playing baseball and football for two years, Darrell Collins suffered an
injury that left him physically unable to participate as a traditional athlete. Unwilling to allow his
circumstances to hinder his contributions, he turned to public address announcing and
coaching; and
WHEREAS, accepting the role of full-time announcer for Celina High School, Mr. Collins
became known as the "Voice of the Bulldogs" at Celina football and basketball games, creating
a unique homefield and home-court advantage for the Dawgs with his special brand of
charisma, music, and enthusiasm; and
WHEREAS, Darrell Collins also sang the national anthem at events and won acclaim as
a master of his craft, often hired to serve as the announcer for district and region basketball
tournaments; and
WHEREAS, he also spent many years as a coach for youth teams in multiple sports in
Clay County, including Little League baseball (State Farm Reds), Babe Ruth baseball (Celina
