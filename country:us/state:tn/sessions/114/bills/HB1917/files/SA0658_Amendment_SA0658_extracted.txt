SECTION 33. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 63-22-122, is amended by deleting

(3)
(A) "Limited practice professional counselor" means a person licensed
under this part who:

(i) Is professionally trained in counseling and guidance services
designed to facilitate normal human growth and development through
individual, family, or group counseling;
(ii) Is professionally trained in educational procedures,
assessment, consultation, and research; and
(iii) Assists individuals by the practice of counseling with their
personal, social, career, or educational development as they pass
through life stages.
(B) As used in subdivision (3)(A), "practice of counseling" means
rendering or offering to render to individuals, groups, organizations, or the

methods, or procedures of the counseling profession, including appraisal
activities, counseling, consulting, and referral activities.
(C) This subdivision (3) does not permit a limited practice professional

circumstance problems, occupational problems, and uncomplicated
bereavement; and
(4)
(A) "Licensed professional counselor" means a person licensed under
this part who:
(i) Is professionally trained in the practice of counseling and
guidance services designed to facilitate normal human growth and

(ii) Is professionally trained in educational procedures,
assessment, consultation, and research;
(iii) Assists individuals by the practice of counseling with their
personal, social, career, or educational development as they pass
through life stages; and
(iv) Applies mental health and human development principles in
order to:
(a) Facilitate human development and adjustment
throughout the life span;
(b) Prevent, diagnose, and treat mental, emotional, or

mental health;
(c) Conduct assessments and diagnoses for the purpose

(d) Plan, implement, and evaluate treatment plans using

(ii) The designation of testing reports as psychological.

(i) "Counseling treatment interventions" means the application of
