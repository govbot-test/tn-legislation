SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1272
By Roberts
A RESOLUTION to commend Mitchell McCook for exemplary
service as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this body is pleased to recognize certain persons
who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment of the legislative
process; and
WHEREAS, Mitchell McCook is one such individual who has distinguished himself in his
role as a legislative intern for Senator Kerry Roberts; Mr. McCook's work has resulted in
innumerable benefits for the legislators he assisted and the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, a student at Trevecca Nazarene University, where he is highly regarded by
his peers, Mitchell McCook has achieved academic success while participating in numerous
extracurricular activities; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize himself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further his studies, Mr. McCook brought his many abilities
and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2026 session of the One Hundred Fourteenth
General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, throughout the 2026 legislative session, Mitchell McCook has surpassed all
criteria that define a devoted and conscientious legislative intern; and
WHEREAS, Mr. McCook has performed his many duties with extraordinary insight and
rare decorum; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby commend Mitchell McCook for
