HOUSE RESOLUTION 228
By Keisling
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Brenton West Powell,
2026 Salutatorian of Red Boiling Springs High
School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should pause to pay tribute to those students who,
through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to academic excellence, have
distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Brenton West Powell is one such exceptional student, who was recently
named Salutatorian of the 2026 graduating class at Red Boiling Springs High School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Mr. Powell has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in his remarkable high school career, Brenton Powell has earned an
exemplary grade point average while participating in a number of extracurricular activities; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Powell's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well
for his future success; and
WHEREAS, his noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for his family,
his school, and his community; and
WHEREAS, Brenton Powell's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the
respect, admiration, and commendation of the members of this legislative body; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby
honor and recognize the 2026 Red Boiling Springs High School Salutatorian, Brenton West
