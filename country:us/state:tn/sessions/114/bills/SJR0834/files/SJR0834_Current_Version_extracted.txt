SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 834
By McNally
A RESOLUTION to confirm the appointment of Dr. Clay R. Good
to the Tennessee public utility commission.
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 65-1-101, creates the Tennessee
public utility commission; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 65-1-101(a), provides that two
commissioners of the Tennessee public utility commission shall be appointed by the Speaker of
the Senate; and
WHEREAS, Speaker of the Senate Randy McNally has appointed Dr. Clay R. Good to
serve as a member of the Tennessee public utility commission for a term that will expire on
June 30, 2032; and
WHEREAS, a 1978 honors graduate of Clinton High School, Commissioner Good was
inducted into the National Football Foundation Hall of Fame during his senior year for his
leadership on the field and in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, after completing his undergraduate studies at Middle Tennessee State
University, he was accepted into the University of Tennessee College of Pharmacy at the
University of Tennessee Center for Health Sciences in Memphis; and
WHEREAS, upon graduating from pharmacy school, Dr. Good returned to the Clinton
community, where he began his pharmacy practice at Methodist Medical Center of Oak Ridge;
and
WHEREAS, deeply committed to his profession, Dr. Good served as co-chairman of
Methodist Medical Center's Institutional Review Board, a member of its Ethics Committee,
Executive Cancer Committee, and Palliative Care Team, and chairman of the board of directors
for the Methodist Medical Center Foundation; and

WHEREAS, he also practiced pharmacy in long-term care as the director of
pharmaceutical services at NHC Healthcare of Oak Ridge; and
WHEREAS, for more than a decade, Commissioner Good was the service chairman for
the American Cancer Society in Anderson County, helping cancer patients and their families; he
was honored with the State of Tennessee's Outstanding Achievement Award in recognition of
his work with Methodist's Oncology Department and the American Cancer Society; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Good retired from NHC Oak Ridge in 2018 after thirty-two years and
retired from full-time practice at Methodist Medical Center in 2021, but he still remains on the
hospital's clinical staff; and
WHEREAS, understanding the responsibilities of good citizenship, Dr. Good has served
as a commissioner on the Mayor's 21st Century Commission for the City of Clinton,
subcommittee chair for the revitalization of Clinton's Historic Downtown, and a member of the
City of Clinton's Retail Task Force and the board of directors for the Anderson County Chamber
of Commerce; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 65-1-101(d), provides that each
appointment to the commission shall be confirmed by joint resolution of the General Assembly;
now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we confirm the appointment of Dr. Clay R. Good to
serve as a member of the Tennessee public utility commission for a term to expire on June 30,
2032.
