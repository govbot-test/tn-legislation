AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 815 House Bill No. 725*
by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-40-103(5)(A), is amended by
deleting the language "thirty-five-year" and substituting instead "time".
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-40-103(5)(A), is amended by
adding the following language to the end of the subdivision:
and all costs incurred by a municipality or industrial development corporation
relating to the development of a project on public property adjacent to the district;
SECTION 3. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-40-103(6)(B)(iv), is amended by
deleting the language "thirty-five-year" and substituting instead "time".
SECTION 4. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-40-103(10), is amended by
deleting "fifteen (15) years" and substituting instead "twenty (20) years".
SECTION 5. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-40-104(d), is amended by deleting
the language "expiration of thirty-five (35) years" and substituting instead the language "close of
the state's fiscal year ending June 30, 2052" and deleting the language "thirty-five-year".
SECTION 6. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-40-104, is amended by adding the
following new subsection (h):
(h) For purposes of providing financial assistance relative to the cost of a project
pursuant to a development agreement executed between a private developer and a
municipality or industrial development board on or after July 1, 2026, the minimum
aggregate cost of the private project pursuant to such agreement requires an amount not

is held invalid, then the invalidity does not affect other provisions or applications of the act that
can be given effect without the invalid provision or application, and to that end, the provisions of
this act are severable.
SECTION 11. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
