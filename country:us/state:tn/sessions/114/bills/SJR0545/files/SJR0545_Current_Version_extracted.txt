SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 545
By Pody
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Ray Daniel on the
occasion of his retirement.
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that we should honor those citizens who have performed
with uncommon devotion and enthusiasm throughout their years of industry; and
WHEREAS, Ray Daniel is one such estimable person, who served with distinction for
nearly two decades as owner/operator of Chick-fil-A Providence Marketplace in Mt. Juliet, taking
his much-deserved retirement on May 31, 2025; and
WHEREAS, the Providence Marketplace Chick-fil-A opened on October 12, 2006, and
from that time until his retirement, Mr. Daniel exhibited motivational leadership, dedication to
excellence, and a strong moral compass; and
WHEREAS, highly regarded throughout the Mt. Juliet community, Ray Daniel was an
avid supporter of various community groups, including the Mt. Juliet Fire Department, Special
Olympics, Fellowship of Christian Athletes of Wilson County, the Mt. Juliet Cruise In, and Rehab
23, providing food for active fire/police incidents throughout the county; and
WHEREAS, he also supported the Mt. Juliet Police Department, sponsoring its annual
golf tournament breakfast, and the Wilson County School System, providing teacher breakfasts,
sponsoring fundraiser nights, providing coupons, and backing high school football teams; and
WHEREAS, within the restaurant itself, Ray Daniel promoted family and community by
hosting weekly Kids Nights and holiday events, including "Santa and the Cows," Valentine Date
Nights, Father/Daughter Date Nights, and theme events throughout the year; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Daniel is an active member of Connect Church and a loving husband,
father, and grandfather; he devoted his career to providing quality food, excellent customer
service, and a clean, pleasant environment for his staff and guests; and

WHEREAS, Ray Daniel exemplifies the spirit and allegiance to family and community
that are characteristic of a true Tennessean; and
WHEREAS, throughout his estimable career, Mr. Daniel demonstrated the utmost
professionalism, ability, and integrity, winning the respect and admiration of his colleagues and
a grateful public; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we extend to Ray Daniel our heartfelt wishes for a
happy and fulfilling retirement and every continued success in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
