HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1258
By Towns
A RESOLUTION to recognize and congratulate the Nashville
Alumni Chapter of Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity,
Inc., on the occasion of its 100th anniversary.
WHEREAS, Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity, Inc., was founded on January 5, 1911, and has
maintained a historic commitment to achievement in every field of human endeavor; and
WHEREAS, on April 24, 1926, the Nashville Alumni Chapter of Kappa Alpha Psi
Fraternity, Inc., was duly chartered, making it one of the fraternity's earliest alumni chapters; and
WHEREAS, the establishment of the Nashville Alumni Chapter was initiated under the
leadership of J. Ernest Wilkins, then Grand Keeper of Records and Exchequer, and has since
remained an enduring presence in the Middle Tennessee region; and
WHEREAS, since its chartering, the Nashville Alumni Chapter has provided 100 years of
continuous service through leadership development, educational support, youth mentorship,
civic engagement, and community uplift; and
WHEREAS, the governance and stewardship of the chapter have been carried forward
through the faithful service of its elected Polemarchs: Dr. Henry H. Walker, 1st Polemarch
(1926–1936); Merl R. (Rawls) Eppse, 2nd Polemarch (1936–1937); Swazzie L. Hall, 3rd
Polemarch (1937–1940); William J. Gupton, Sr., 4th Polemarch (1940–1950); William F. Butler,
5th Polemarch (1950–1951); Swazzie L. Hall, 6th Polemarch (1951–1953); Cass F.L. Teague,
Sr., 7th Polemarch (1953–1955); Clyde Leathers, 8th Polemarch (1955–1956); Samuel P.
Massie, 9th Polemarch (1956–1959); O.W. Crump, 10th Polemarch (1959–1961); William J.
Gupton, Sr., 11th Polemarch (1961–1965); A.A. Birch, 12th Polemarch (1965–1967); Shannon D.
Little, 13th Polemarch (1967–1973); Sterlin N. Adams, 14th Polemarch (1973–1976); Joe E.
Seibert, 15th Polemarch (1976–1979); Leonard H. Morton, Sr., 16th Polemarch (1979–1982);
James A. Ellzy, 17th Polemarch (1982–1993); Henry A. Atwater II, 18th Polemarch (1993–1995);
