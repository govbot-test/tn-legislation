SENATE RESOLUTION 299
By Walley
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Lieutenant Shawn
Phinnessee upon being named Haywood
County Deputy of the Year.
WHEREAS, the Volunteer State is especially proud to be the home of so many valiant
men and women who continue the time-honored tradition of public service in law enforcement,
dedicating their time and efforts to aiding those in emergency situations and apprehending
those who have broken the law; and
WHEREAS, Lieutenant Shawn Phinnessee of the Haywood County Sheriff's Office is
one such courageous public servant, who was recently named Haywood County Deputy of the
Year by Sheriff Billy Garrett, Jr.; and
WHEREAS, Lieutenant Phinnessee has contributed greatly to the safety of the citizens
of Haywood County, serving with tremendous courage, fortitude, and professionalism and
making a significant impact for the betterment of his community; and
WHEREAS, Lieutenant Phinnessee epitomizes the spirit and commitment that are
characteristic of a true Tennessean, and his professionalism and dedication to Haywood County
are evident to all who know him; and
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to acknowledge and applaud Lieutenant Shawn
Phinnessee for his dedication to the State of Tennessee and its citizens; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and congratulate
Lieutenant Shawn Phinnessee upon being named Haywood County Deputy of the Year, thank
him for his meritorious service to the citizens of Haywood County and the State of Tennessee,
and extend to him our best wishes for every future success.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
