SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 623
By Gardenhire
A RESOLUTION to commemorate Safari Club International Day in
Tennessee.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly recognize those
exemplary organizations that promote and protect the time-honored tradition of hunting; and
WHEREAS, one such commendable entity is Safari Club International (SCI), a nonprofit
organization founded in 1971, dedicated to protecting the freedom to hunt and promoting wildlife
conservation worldwide; and
WHEREAS, Safari Club International's annual convention in Nashville, recognized as the
largest convention held in the city and a premier gathering of hunting enthusiasts,
conservationists, industry leaders, and government officials from across the nation and around
the world, stands as one of the most significant events supporting the hunting community in the
State; and
WHEREAS, SCI members and affiliates are committed to science-driven wildlife
management principles, conservation funding, outdoor heritage, and responsible hunting
practices; and
WHEREAS, SCI and its members play a vital role in conservation efforts through
advocacy, education, humanitarian initiatives, and direct financial support for wildlife
management programs at the local, state, national, and international levels; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee is home to a strong community of hunters, conservationists,
outdoor enthusiasts, and SCI members whose efforts contribute to conservation funding, rural
economies, and the preservation of Tennessee's outdoor heritage; and

WHEREAS, regulated hunting and conservation programs supported by SCI generate
critical funding for wildlife conservation, habitat restoration, and wildlife research through license
fees, excise taxes, and charitable contributions; and
WHEREAS, SCI supports Tennessee's long-standing commitment and authority to
manage wildlife for the benefit of current and future generations of hunters; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we join with Safari Club International as it
commemorates February 18, 2026, as Safari Club International Day in Tennessee, recognizing
its staff, chapters, and members for their unparalleled commitment to protecting the freedom to
hunt and promoting wildlife conservation worldwide.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
