Section 1:
Senate Finance, Ways, and Means 1
Amendment No. 2 to SB2317
Watson
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting the language "subsection (h)" in subsection (h) of SECTION 1 and substituting
instead the language "subsection (i)".
AND FURTHER AMEND by adding the following new subsection (h) in SECTION 1 and
redesignating existing subsection (h) as subsection (i):
(h) A participating LEA that does not meet the eligibility criteria in subsection (b)
for two (2) consecutive years is not eligible to continue participation in the pilot program
in this section and must not implement the assessment limitations in this section.
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting the language "To be eligible to participate in the pilot
program," in subsection (b) of SECTION 1 and substituting instead the language "To be eligible
to participate in the pilot program, subject to subsection (h),".
