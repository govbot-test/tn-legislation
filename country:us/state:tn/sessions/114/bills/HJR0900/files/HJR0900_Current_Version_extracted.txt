HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 900
By Lamberth
A RESOLUTION to confirm the appointment of Camron Rudd to
the state university board of Tennessee
Technological University.
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-8-101, provides for the governance
of Tennessee Technological University by a state university board; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-8-201, provides that the governor
shall appoint six members to the state university board of Tennessee Technological University;
and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-8-201, further provides that at least
three of the gubernatorial appointees shall be alumni of the institution for which they are serving;
and
WHEREAS, Governor Bill Lee has appointed Camron Rudd to the state university board
of Tennessee Technological University for a term that will expire on June 30, 2031; and
WHEREAS, Camron Rudd is the chief operating officer of the North American
headquarters of Hörmann, a German, family-owned, entry systems manufacturer currently in its
fourth generation of operation with over forty factories worldwide; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Rudd played an instrumental role in consolidating the four independent
Hörmann companies in North America into one company, including five manufacturing locations
and twenty-two sales centers located in the United States, Canada, and Mexico, and locating
the new headquarters in Sparta; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Rudd graduated from Tennessee Technological University with a
Bachelor of Science degree in Mechanical Engineering and a Bachelor of Arts degree in
German; he serves on the Cookeville-Putnam County Chamber of Commerce Board of
Directors; and

WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-8-201, provides that each member
appointed by the governor to the state university board of Tennessee Technological University
shall be subject to confirmation by the senate and the house of representatives; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby confirm the appointment of Camron Rudd to the state university
board of Tennessee Technological University for a term that will expire on June 30, 2031.
