HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1088
By Hale
A RESOLUTION to honor Miles Alexander upon scoring more
than 1,000 points in his high school basketball
career.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should recognize those incredibly successful student
athletes who have distinguished themselves as inspirational leaders among their peers; and
WHEREAS, one such outstanding young athlete is Miles Alexander, a sophomore at
Cannon County High School, who recently joined the 1,000-Point Club for Tennessee high
school basketball by scoring his 1,000th career point; and
WHEREAS, no matter his path in life, Miles Alexander is an outstanding young man who
will continue to make a difference in his community, both on and off the court; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Alexander's outstanding accomplishments are a great source of pride
for his family, his school, and the good people of Cannon County; and
WHEREAS, Miles Alexander exemplifies the spirit, determination, and perseverance for
which Tennesseans are notable, and he is richly deserving of our approbation on this special
occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Miles Alexander upon joining the 1,000-Point
Club by scoring more than 1,000 points in his high school basketball career and wish him much
continued success in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
