SENATE BILL 2518
By Yarbro
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 4,
Chapter 23; Title 9, Chapter 11; Title 9, Chapter
21; Title 9, Chapter 4; Title 9, Chapter 9; Title 54
and Title 67, relative to transportation
infrastructure funding.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 54-2-202(a), is amended by deleting
the language "each January 1 thereafter" and substituting instead the language "each February
15 thereafter".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
