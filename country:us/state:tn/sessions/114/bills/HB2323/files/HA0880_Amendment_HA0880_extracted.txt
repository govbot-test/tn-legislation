Section 1:
House State & Local Government 1
Amendment No. 1 to HB2323
Crawford
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting the amendatory language of SECTION 1 and substituting instead:
( ) "Municipality" means an incorporated city or a town that:
(A) Does not have a school system or LEA within its jurisdiction;
(B) Is the county seat;
(C) Has the first historic district in this state listed on the national register of
historic places; and
(D) Was established by an act of the general assembly of a bordering state;
