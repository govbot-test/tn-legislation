HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1284
By Bulso
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Marina Mason on
being named the 2025 WILLCO Awards Softball
Player of the Year.
WHEREAS, we are pleased to recognize and commend those successful high school
athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring honor to Tennessee and serve as
exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable individual is Marina Mason of Brentwood High School,
an athlete of extraordinary talent, skill, and dedication, who has proven herself a formidable
competitor in softball; and
WHEREAS, in recognition of her success on the softball diamond throughout the school
year, Marina Mason was named Softball Player of the Year at the 2025 WILLCO Awards
celebration; and
WHEREAS, a powerhouse in the pitching circle, Marina Mason struck out 339 batters to
lead the Bruins in District 10-4A, setting the twelfth best strikeout mark in the nation among high
school softball pitchers last year; and
WHEREAS, Marina Mason epitomizes all that is good in today's student athlete, as she
has achieved a high level of success in sports while evincing great character; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Mason's notable achievements are a source of great pride for her
family, her community, and her school, and it is appropriate that we recognize this accomplished
athlete; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Marina Mason on being named the 2025
