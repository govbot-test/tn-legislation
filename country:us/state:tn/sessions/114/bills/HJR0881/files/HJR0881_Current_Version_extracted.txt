HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 881
By Lamberth
A RESOLUTION to confirm the appointment of Drew Goddard to
the heritage conservation trust fund board of
trustees.
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 11-7-104(a), provides for the creation
of the heritage conservation trust fund board of trustees; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 11-7-104(b), provides that the
governor shall appoint eleven members to the board; and
WHEREAS, Governor Bill Lee has appointed Drew Goddard as a member of the
heritage conservation trust fund board of trustees representing Middle Tennessee to a term that
will expire on October 31, 2029; and
WHEREAS, for more than thirty years, Drew Goddard has focused his legal practice in
the area of environmental law, counseling clients on a broad range of topics related to local and
federal environmental laws; and
WHEREAS, elected to the American College of Environmental Lawyers in 2008, Mr.
Goddard has served on external steering committees of the Tennessee Department of
Environment and Conservation and taught environmental law as an adjunct professor at
Vanderbilt University School of Law for thirteen years; and
WHEREAS, the former editor of the Tennessee Environmental Law Letter, he also
served for several years on the various environmental committees and subcommittees of the
Tennessee Chamber of Commerce and Industry and currently co-chairs its Air Pollution
Subcommittee; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Goddard earned a bachelor's degree in engineering from Vanderbilt
University and received his Juris Doctor from Duke University; and

WHEREAS, Drew Goddard is a person of integrity and an active citizen whose
benevolence, acumen, and strong commitment to public service have helped to enhance the
well-being of this State and its citizens; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 11-7-104(b)(2), provides that each
member appointed by the governor to the heritage conservation trust fund board of trustees
shall be confirmed by joint resolution of the general assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that this body confirms the appointment of Drew Goddard to the heritage
conservation trust fund board of trustees for a term that will expire on October 31, 2029.
