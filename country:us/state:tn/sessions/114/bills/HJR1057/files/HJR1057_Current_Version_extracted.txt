HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1057
By Lamberth
A RESOLUTION to call a joint convention of the Senate and
House of Representatives for the purpose of
voting on the confirmation of Judge Kyle Hixson
to the Tennessee Supreme Court, Judge Steve
Maroney to the Tennessee Court of Appeals,
Western Section, and Judge William E. Phillips
II, to the Tennessee Court of Appeals, Eastern

Section 2:
Section, pursuant to Article VI, Section 3 of the
Constitution of Tennessee and Tennessee Code
Annotated, Title 17, Chapter 4.
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that the House of Representatives and the Senate meet in joint convention on
Thursday, March 5, 2026, at 8:00 a.m. (CST) in the chamber of the House of Representatives to
vote on the confirmation of Judge Kyle Hixson to the Tennessee Supreme Court, to vote on the
confirmation of Judge Steve Maroney to the Tennessee Court of Appeals, Western Section, and
to vote on the confirmation of Judge William E. Phillips II, to the Tennessee Court of Appeals,
Eastern Section, pursuant to Article VI, Section 3 of the Constitution of Tennessee and
Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 17, Chapter 4.
HJR1057
