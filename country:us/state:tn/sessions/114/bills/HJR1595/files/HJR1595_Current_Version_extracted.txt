HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1595
By Capley
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Tessa Leann Allen, 2026
Top Ten Senior at Collinwood High School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Tessa Leann Allen is one such exceptional student, who was recently
named a Top Ten Senior of the 2026 graduating class at Collinwood High School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Ms. Allen has achieved outstanding academic success
and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country; and
WHEREAS, in her remarkable high school career, Tessa Allen has earned an exemplary

WHEREAS, Ms. Allen's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well for

WHEREAS, Tessa Allen's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for

BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
