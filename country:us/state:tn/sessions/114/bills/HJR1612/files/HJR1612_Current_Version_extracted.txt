HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1612
By Bulso
A RESOLUTION to commend Alex Barwise for exemplary service
as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this General Assembly is pleased to recognize
certain persons who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment
of the legislative process; and
WHEREAS, Alex Barwise is one such individual, who has distinguished himself in his
role as a legislative intern for Representative Gino Bulso; Mr. Barwise's work has resulted in
innumerable benefits for the legislators he assisted and the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Barwise is a lifelong resident of Williamson County and a graduate of
Centennial High School; and
WHEREAS, a student at Middle Tennessee State University, where he is highly
regarded by his peers, Alex Barwise has achieved remarkable academic success; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize himself with the myriad aspects and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further his studies, Mr. Barwise brought his many abilities
and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2026 session of the One Hundred Fourteenth
General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Barwise has performed his many duties with extraordinary insight and
rare decorum; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby commend Alex Barwise for outstanding service as a legislative
intern for Representative Gino Bulso and extend to him our best wishes for every future
success.
HJR1612
018543
- 1 -
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
