SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 702
By Bailey
A RESOLUTION to congratulate Hannah Haley on being crowned
the 2026 Miss Tennessee Fairest of the Fair.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should specially
recognize those emerging community leaders who strive to improve the quality of life for their
fellow citizens while bringing beauty, grace, and purpose to all their endeavors; and
WHEREAS, one such superlative individual is Hannah Haley, who was recently crowned
the 2026 Miss Tennessee Fairest of the Fair; and
WHEREAS, Hannah Haley, White County's Fairest of the Fair, was crowned Miss
Tennessee Fairest of the Fair at the state pageant held at the Tennessee Association of Fairs
Convention in Murfreesboro on January 16, 2026; and
WHEREAS, facing contestants from an array of backgrounds at the crowning event,
Miss Haley took the stage full of her usual spirit and grace and began to win over the hearts of
viewers; and
WHEREAS, after advancing to the Top Ten, Hannah Haley continued to shine as she
answered an onstage question drawn at random; when the points were tallied and the ribbons
given out, Miss Haley had received the biggest crown of all; and
WHEREAS, Miss Haley's Fairest of the Fair pageant victory is a special source of pride
for her family, her many friends, and the good people of Tennessee, and it is fitting that we
specially recognize this remarkable young woman and her many accomplishments; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby congratulate Hannah Haley on being
