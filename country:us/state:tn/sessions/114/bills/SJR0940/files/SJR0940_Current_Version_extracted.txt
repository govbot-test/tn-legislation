SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 940
By Pody
A RESOLUTION to honor Novamet Specialty Products
Corporation on the occasion of its fiftieth
anniversary.
WHEREAS, Novamet Specialty Products Corporation was founded in 1976, the same
year the United States commemorated its Bicentennial, marking 200 years of independence and
national progress; and
WHEREAS, Novamet was established for the purpose of developing advanced materials
derived from metal powders, materials that are essential to the performance and reliability of
products used in automotive catalysts, consumer electronics, aerospace, metal and electronic
components, medical equipment, industrial coatings, and protective technologies that support
and safeguard America's warriors during times of conflict; and
WHEREAS, in 2010, Novamet was acquired by Tennessee investors and, in 2014,
relocated its operations to the City of Lebanon, in Wilson County, repurposing the building and
campus originally constructed by Toshiba Corporation in 1979, thereby reinvesting in and
strengthening the local community; and
WHEREAS, on April 29, 2026, Novamet will celebrate its fiftieth anniversary,
commemorating five decades of innovation, production, and global distribution of advanced
materials that serve high-technology markets throughout the world; and
WHEREAS, we are honored to recognize and welcome distinguished guests in the
Senate Chamber, including Jeffrey Peterson, President, CEO, and majority owner of Novamet
since 2010, along with long-serving employees whose dedication exemplifies the company's
success: Tim Hebron, a plant operator with twenty-eight years of service; Jerome Pearson, a
plant operator with thirty years of service; Neil Bizarro, regional sales manager, with thirty-seven

years of service; Kevin Haley, quality manager, with forty-four years of service; and Ralph

WHEREAS, the continued success and longevity of Novamet Specialty Products

WHEREAS, Novamet's mission is to develop, produce, and distribute metal powders

commitment to operational excellence, technological advancement, environmental stewardship,
and the cultivation of strong relationships with its communities, employees, customers, and

environmentally responsible products that enhance lives and benefit people around the world;

BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Novamet Specialty
Products Corporation on the occasion of its fiftieth anniversary and extend our sincere
appreciation to its leadership and the employees for their lasting contributions to innovation,
industry, and the citizens of the State of Tennessee.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
