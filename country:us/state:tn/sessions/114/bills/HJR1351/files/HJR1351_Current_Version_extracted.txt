HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1351
By Cepicky
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Mariela Dominguez upon
being named 2026 Boys & Girls Club of South
Central Tennessee Youth of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly pause in their
deliberations to salute those young citizens who, through their remarkable efforts, have
distinguished themselves as emerging community and state leaders of whom we can all be
proud; and
WHEREAS, one such deserving person is Mariela Dominguez, who was recently named
the 2026 Boys & Girls Club of South Central Tennessee Youth of the Year; and
WHEREAS, Mariela Dominguez, a student at Columbia Central High School, has been a
member of the Boys & Girls Club of South Central Tennessee for one year; and
WHEREAS, the Boys and Girls Clubs exist to serve youth and to be a safe place of
encouragement, character building, security, and love for children; and
WHEREAS, since 1947, the Youth of the Year (YOY) has been the Boys & Girls Clubs
of America’s premier recognition program, celebrating the extraordinary achievements of Club
members; and
WHEREAS, Youth of the Year participants embody the values of leadership, service,
academic excellence, and healthy lifestyles; and
WHEREAS, Youth of the Year participants such as Mariela exemplify the critical impact
of Boys & Girls Clubs on the lives of young people; and
WHEREAS, through the Youth of the Year program, Mariela Dominguez and other
Youth of the Year participants are able to showcase their talents and achievements, share their
hopes and dreams, and work toward a bright and positive future; and

WHEREAS, Mariela Dominguez is a shining example that great futures start here–at
Boys & Girls Clubs; and
WHEREAS, the success of this State, the strength of our communities, and the overall
vitality of American society depend, in great measure, upon the dedication of young people such
as Mariela Dominguez, who use their considerable talents and resources to serve others and
who should be specially recognized for their public and community service; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Mariela Dominguez upon being named Boys &
Girls Club of South Central Tennessee Youth of the Year.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that we extend to her our best wishes for every future
success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
