SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 676
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to recognize and honor Jessica Howard upon
being named the 2025 Silver Angels Champion.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should salute those citizens who, through their
extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as community leaders of whom we can all
be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable person is Jessica Howard, who was recently named the
2025 Silver Angels Champion Award winner at the Silver Angels Fall Conference; and
WHEREAS, Silver Angels provides non-medical, in-home care for senior citizens; and
WHEREAS, the Silver Angels Champion Program recognizes branch leadership
professionals who consistently exceed position expectations and demonstrate the highest
standards of excellence; and
WHEREAS, those selected with the honor of being named Silver Angels Champions are
considered role models and committed servant leaders who represent the Sacred Seven values
of the organization: compassion, integrity, accountability, positivity, respect, teamwork, and "be
heroic"; and
WHEREAS, Jessica Howard joined Silver Angels in 2018 as an area scheduling
coordinator for Silver Angels of Tennessee - Greene, LLC; and
WHEREAS, in 2020, Ms. Howard was promoted to the lead branch scheduler and later
promoted to the senior branch scheduler, the position she currently holds; and
WHEREAS, in addition to the 2025 award, Jessica Howard recently received the Silver
Angels Champion award in 2023; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Howard epitomizes the spirit and commitment that are characteristic of
a true Tennessean; and

WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to acknowledge and applaud Jessica Howard for her
many contributions to the good people of Tennessee, her unwavering commitment to others,
and her astute leadership; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and commend Jessica Howard on
being named the 2025 Silver Angels Champion and extend to her our best wishes for every
future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
