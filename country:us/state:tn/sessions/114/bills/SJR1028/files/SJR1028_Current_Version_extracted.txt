SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1028
By Pody
A RESOLUTION to commend Courtney Powell for exemplary
service as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this General Assembly is pleased to recognize
certain persons who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment
of the legislative process; and
WHEREAS, Courtney Powell is one such individual, who has distinguished herself in her
role as a legislative intern for Senator Mark Pody, demonstrating professionalism, integrity, and
a strong work ethic; Ms. Powell's work has resulted in innumerable benefits for the legislators
she assisted and the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, a junior at the University of Tennessee Southern, Ms. Powell has achieved
academic success while pursuing a degree in criminal justice and homeland security, fields that
demand both intellectual rigor and a deep commitment to the safety and well-being of others;
and
WHEREAS, driven by a passion to make a meaningful difference, Courtney Powell
aspires to build a career investigating and combating human trafficking, one of the most
pressing and complex challenges facing our nation and the world today; and
WHEREAS, to prepare herself for this vital work, she has completed several courses
focused on human trafficking, gaining valuable knowledge and insight into the causes, impacts,
and prevention of this serious issue; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Powell's dedication to academic excellence is evidenced by her
consistent placement on the Provost's List, an honor reserved for students who achieve
outstanding scholastic performance; and

WHEREAS, her commitment to learning, service, and justice reflects great credit upon
herself, her family, her university, and this General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize herself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further her studies, Courtney Powell brought her many
abilities and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2026 session of the One Hundred
Fourteenth General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, throughout the 2026 legislative session, Ms. Powell has surpassed all
criteria that define a devoted and conscientious legislative intern; and
WHEREAS, Courtney Powell exemplifies the qualities of leadership, compassion, and
determination that are essential to the continued progress and prosperity of the State of
Tennessee; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and commend Courtney Powell for
outstanding service as a legislative intern for Senator Mark Pody and extend to her our best
wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
