House Finance, Ways, and Means 1
Amendment No. 3 to HB0972
Hicks G
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 1068 House Bill No. 972*
by deleting the following language from subsection (b) in § 55-31-104 of SECTION 1:
the department of revenue may charge a law enforcement agency or another user of the
portal a fee for entering information or sending notification through the portal, but a fee
must not be charged for conducting a public search on the motor vehicle portal.
and substituting instead the following:
the department of revenue may charge a user of the portal, other than a law
enforcement agency, a fee for entering information or sending notification through the
portal, but a fee must not be charged for conducting a public search on the motor vehicle
portal.
