SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 694
By White
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Dow Smith on
receiving the 2025 Business at Its Best Award.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should salute those
citizens who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as community
leaders of whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable person is Dow Smith, who was recently presented with
the 2025 Business at Its Best Award; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Smith founded Dow Smith Company more than thirty years ago,
beginning his first project in January 1992 with the renovation of Building 661 at the Smyrna
Airport while operating out of a red Chevy S-10 pickup truck that also served as his office and
sole piece of equipment; and
WHEREAS, following the successful completion of that project, Dow Smith upgraded his
office by moving into the very building he had renovated, thereby launching a journey of steady
growth and reinvention; over the decades, the company has operated from a series of locations
in Smyrna, including Building 660, 301 Jefferson Pike, 51 North Lowry, and its current
headquarters at 205 Sam Hager Street; and
WHEREAS, under Mr. Smith's leadership, Dow Smith Company evolved from a low-bid
general contractor into a distinctive design-build firm known for its innovative solutions and
client-centered service; he continues to guide the company today in his role as visionary; and
WHEREAS, Dow Smith has intentionally embraced a Marketplace Ministry since 2004,
integrating his Christian faith into his business life and leadership; he draws inspiration and
grounding from prayer, including the “Welcoming Prayer” by Thomas Keating, the “Serenity
Prayer,” and the “Lord's Prayer”; and

WHEREAS, he is especially moved by the words of Bob Dylan, "If you're not busy being
born, you're busy dying," a sentiment he sees echoed in Scripture and in the teachings of
Jesus, and one he strives to embody in both life and work; and
WHEREAS, at the heart of Mr. Smith's success is his devotion to family; he feels at his
best when with his beloved wife, Cindy, and finds his greatest joy when they are together with
their sons, Harris and Mitchell; and
WHEREAS, the Business at Its Best Award honors not only entrepreneurial
achievement, but also service, leadership, and values in action, all of which Mr. Smith has
demonstrated with integrity and purpose; and
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to acknowledge and applaud Dow Smith for his
dedication to the State of Tennessee and its citizens; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Dow Smith on receiving
the 2025 Business at Its Best Award and extend to him our best wishes for every continued
success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
