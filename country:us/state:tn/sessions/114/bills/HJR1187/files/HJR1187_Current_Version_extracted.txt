HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1187
By Reneau
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Sergeant Robin
Davenport of the Chattanooga Police
Department upon his receipt of the Spirit of
Community Policing Award.
WHEREAS, our nation was conceived by individuals who were willing to sacrifice their
personal concerns to ensure the public safety and welfare, and the Volunteer State is especially
proud to be the home of so many valiant individuals who have performed above and beyond the
call of duty in service of public safety; and
WHEREAS, one such individual is Sergeant Robin Davenport of the Chattanooga Police
Department, who was recently honored with the Chattanooga Police Department Spirit of
Community Policing Award, in recognition of his dedication to forging strong, positive
relationships between the police and the community; and
WHEREAS, Sergeant Davenport's commitment to proactive engagement and youth
development has profoundly impacted Chattanooga citizens, building trust through key
initiatives such as the Police Activities League (PAL) and the Police Explorers Program,
ensuring new officers grasp core community service principles; and
WHEREAS, the efforts of Sergeant Davenport have made a lasting impact on the
Chattanooga Police Department and the community it serves; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Sergeant Robin Davenport of the Chattanooga
Police Department upon his receipt of the Spirt of Community Policing Award.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
