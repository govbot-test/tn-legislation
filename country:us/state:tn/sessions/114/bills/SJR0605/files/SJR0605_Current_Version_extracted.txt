SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 605
By Massey
A RESOLUTION to join with General Federation of Women's
Clubs Ossoli Circle in celebrating March 2026 as
Women's History Month.
WHEREAS, the month of March was officially designated as Women's History Month in
1987, and since that time, Women's History Month has provided an opportunity to recognize
and celebrate the achievements, contributions, and resilience of women throughout history; and
WHEREAS, the accomplishments of women have advanced our society in countless
fields, including education, science, business, government, the arts, and community leadership;
and
WHEREAS, one group of impactful women that calls the Volunteer State home is the
General Federation of Women's Clubs (GFWC) Ossoli Circle of Knoxville, the oldest Federated
Women's Club in the South, founded on November 20, 1885; and
WHEREAS, as GFWC Ossoli Circle celebrates 140 years of service and history-making
events, members are committed to remembering their foremothers and inspiring the next
generation of women to foster a culture of equality, respect, and opportunity for all; and
WHEREAS, throughout the history of Tennessee and our nation, women have had and
continue to have a tremendous impact; Tennessee women, in particular, directly impacted the
passing of the 19th Amendment giving women the right to vote; and
WHEREAS, the vote that gave women the right to vote was cast in Tennessee by a man
who was initially planning to vote against it; he changed his mind after reading a letter from his
mother, an Ossoli Circle member; and
WHEREAS, the Knoxville Suffrage Coalition is at work to build the Suffrage Museum in
Knoxville in order to do the important work of preserving a first-hand view of the role Tennessee
women played in the Suffrage Movement; and

WHEREAS, it is vital that we support the achievements and progress of women, past
and present, throughout the month of March and beyond, honoring their service and their
commitment to a brighter, more equitable future for all citizens of this State and nation; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we join with General Federation of Women's Clubs
Ossoli Circle in celebrating March 2026 as Women's History Month, reflecting on the important
role women have played—and continue to play—in shaping our shared history.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
