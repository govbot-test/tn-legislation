SENATE BILL 2226
By Johnson
HOUSE BILL 2538
By Lamberth
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 64,
Chapter 9, relative to the Megasite Authority of
West Tennessee.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 64-9-109(f)(1), is amended by
deleting the last sentence of the subdivision and substituting:
The authority shall provide each adverse party not less than fourteen (14) days'
written notice prior to assessing a penalty under this subdivision (f)(1).
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
