AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 2649* House Bill No. 2606
by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 38, Chapter 6, Part 1, is amended by
adding the following new section:
(a) The Tennessee bureau of investigation must establish a cold case unit,
staffed by no fewer than five (5) cold case detectives.
(b) A local law enforcement agency may submit an unsolved missing person or
homicide case to the cold case unit following the passage of ten (10) years without
resolution.
(c) A district attorney general may submit an unsolved missing person or
homicide case to the cold case unit at any time.
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
