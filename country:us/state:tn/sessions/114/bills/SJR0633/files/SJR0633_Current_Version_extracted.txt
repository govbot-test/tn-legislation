SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 633
By Johnson
A RESOLUTION to confirm the appointment of Tony Treadway to
the state university board of East Tennessee
State University.
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-8-101, provides for the governance
of East Tennessee State University by a state university board; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-8-201, provides that the governor
shall appoint six members to the state university board of East Tennessee State University; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-8-201, further provides that at least
three of the gubernatorial appointees shall be alumni of the institution for which they are serving;
and
WHEREAS, Governor Bill Lee has appointed Tony Treadway to the state university
board of East Tennessee State University for a term that will expire on June 30, 2031; and
WHEREAS, Tony Treadway is the President, CEO, and founder of The Creative Energy
Group Inc., a full-service advertising agency located in Johnson City; he was previously a
journalist for a local television station; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Treadway served eight years on the Johnson City School Board and
continues to give back to his community as a member of Johnson City's Industrial Development
Board; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Treadway earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Mass
Communications and Political Science from East Tennessee State University (ETSU),
graduating magna cum laude; and
WHEREAS, an ardent supporter of his alma mater, Mr. Treadway has received an
Award of Honor from the ETSU Alumni Association and has been inducted into the ETSU Mass
Communications Department's Hall of Fame; and

WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-8-201, provides that each member
appointed by the governor to the state university board of East Tennessee State University shall
be subject to confirmation by the senate and the house of representatives; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby confirm the appointment of Tony
Treadway to the state university board of East Tennessee State University for a term that will
expire on June 30, 2031.
