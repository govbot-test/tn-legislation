SENATE RESOLUTION 280
By Walley
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Ansley Moore on her
selection as the 2025-2026 Michie Elementary
School Teacher of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should pause to
specially recognize those outstanding teachers who have devoted their careers to providing the
highest-quality education for Tennessee's students; and
WHEREAS, Ansley Moore is one such estimable educator, who has distinguished
herself as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Moore has been selected as the 2025-2026 Michie Elementary School
Teacher of the Year, a most befitting honor that recognizes the positive influence she has on so
many young lives; and
WHEREAS, Ansley Moore is an extremely talented and compassionate educator who
works assiduously to motivate her students to reach their full potential, both as scholars and as
citizens; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Moore serves as an inspiration to her colleagues and students alike,
and her friendship and counsel are highly valued by those who are fortunate to know her; and
WHEREAS, Ansley Moore truly epitomizes the dedicated teacher who is wholly
committed to the highest ideals of public service and quality education for all, and she should be
specially recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby honor and
congratulate Ansley Moore on being chosen as the 2025-2026 Michie Elementary School
Teacher of the Year, commend her exceptional service to the children of Tennessee, salute her
