Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 39-13-519(b), is amended by
designating the current language as subdivision (b)(1) and adding the following subdivision
(b)(2):
(2) Notwithstanding § 63-1-176 or another law to the contrary, when the victim of
a sexually oriented crime is a minor, the consent of a parent or guardian is not required
for the victim to receive a forensic medical examination pursuant to this section.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
