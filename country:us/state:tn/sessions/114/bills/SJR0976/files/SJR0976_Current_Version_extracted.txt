SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 976
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Tyrese Stovall on his
selection as TCCAA Co-Freshman of the Year in
men's basketball.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should recognize those incredibly successful student
athletes who have distinguished themselves as inspirational leaders among their peers; and
WHEREAS, Tyrese Stovall of Walters State Community College was recently named
Tennessee Community College Athletic Association Co-Freshman of the Year in men's
basketball in honor of his outstanding performance as a first-year collegiate player on the
hardwood; and
WHEREAS, Tyrese Stovall was the very epitome of consistency for the Senators during
the 2025-2026 season, averaging 10.4 points and six rebounds per game and shooting 69%
from the field; and
WHEREAS, in his best game of the year, Tyrese Stovall scored 20 points and pulled 12
rebounds versus Volunteer State; and
WHEREAS, Tyrese Stovall exemplifies the spirit, determination, and perseverance for
which Tennesseans are notable, and he is richly deserving of our approbation on this special
occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Tyrese Stovall on his
selection as TCCAA Co-Freshman of the Year in men's basketball and wish him much
continued success both on and off the court.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
