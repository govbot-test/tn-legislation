SENATE RESOLUTION 449
By Akbari
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Paytn Gibson, Carter
Holmes, and Ty Miller of Memphis East T-STEM
Academy on winning third prize in the twenty-
second annual C-SPAN StudentCam
competition.
WHEREAS, we take great pleasure in recognizing those high school students who work
to make a positive difference in our society; and
WHEREAS, Paytn Gibson, Carter Holmes, and Ty Miller of Memphis East T-STEM
Academy recently won third prize in C-SPAN's StudentCam, an annual national video
documentary competition that encourages students to think critically about issues that affect our
communities and our nation; and
WHEREAS, for the twenty-second anniversary of the StudentCam competition, C-SPAN
asked students in grades six through twelve to create a five to six minute video documentary on
either a historical or contemporary topic that relates to the competition theme: America's 250th
Anniversary, Exploring the American Story through the Declaration of Independence; and
WHEREAS, the Memphis East T-STEM Academy team's video, titled "The Pursuit,"
focused on the topic of immigration reform; and
WHEREAS, this prestigious honor is a great source of pride for their families, their
community, and their school, as well as their teacher advisor, Marshonn Calvin; and
WHEREAS, Paytn Gibson, Carter Holmes, and Ty Miller are actively engaged in
identifying and solving the problems we are facing in America, and they should be commended
both for their abilities as documentarians and their civic involvement as concerned citizens; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and congratulate
