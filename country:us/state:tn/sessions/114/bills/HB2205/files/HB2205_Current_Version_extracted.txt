Section 1:
By Yager
HOUSE BILL 2205
By Keisling
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 68,
Chapter 221, relative to water.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 68-221-1205(c), is amended by
adding the following as a new subdivision:
(4) Are located in a rural county. As used in this subsection (c), "rural county"
means a county having a population of less than fifty-seven thousand (57,000),
according to the 2020 or a subsequent federal census.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
