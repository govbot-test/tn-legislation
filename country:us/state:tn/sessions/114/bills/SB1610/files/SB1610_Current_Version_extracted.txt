Section 1:
By Williams
SENATE BILL 1610
By Bailey
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 7,
Chapter 51, Part 26, relative to sober living
homes.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-51-2601(3), is amended by
deleting the subdivision and substituting:
(3) "Local government" means a county, and an incorporated city or town within
such county, with a population of:
(A) Not less than three hundred sixty-six thousand two hundred
(366,200) and not more than three hundred sixty-six thousand three hundred
(366,300), according to the 2020 or a subsequent federal census; and
(B) Not less than seventy-nine thousand eight hundred (79,800) and not
more than seventy-nine thousand nine hundred (79,900), according to the 2020
or a subsequent federal census;

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
