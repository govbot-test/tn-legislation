Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 68, Chapter 1, Part 1, is amended by
adding the following as a new section:
The department of health, in collaboration with the department of human
services, shall make available and require educational materials on blood lead level
testing for a pregnant woman who receives vouchers for the special supplemental food
program for women, infants, and children administered by the department of human
services. The department of health shall also provide the woman educational
information on specialized nutritional services, education on how to mitigate lead in the
body, and other existing wraparound services tailored for people with elevated blood
lead levels or lead poisoning.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
