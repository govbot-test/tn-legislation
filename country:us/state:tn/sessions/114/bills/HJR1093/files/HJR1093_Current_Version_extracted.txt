HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1093
By Keisling
A RESOLUTION to honor Max Moody and Rex Reagan on
winning two consecutive StrikeKing BassMaster
National Championships.
WHEREAS, we are pleased to specially honor and commend those successful young
anglers, who, through their magnificent exploits, bring honor to Tennessee and serve as
exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, Max Moody and Rex Reagan of Pickett County High School recently won
their second consecutive StrikeKing BassMaster National Championship; and
WHEREAS, in the summer of 2024, Max Moody and Rex Reagan competed on Lake
Chickamauga in the StrikeKing BassMaster National Championship, and during that
tournament, they set the BassMaster three-fish limit record with a weight of 21 pounds and 5
ounces; and
WHEREAS, posting a three-day total of 43 pounds and 3 ounces, Mr. Moody and Mr.
Reagan won their first national title as freshmen; and
WHEREAS, competing on Clarks Hill Reservoir, Georgia, as sophomores in 2025, the
dynamic duo from Pickett County won their second consecutive StrikeKing BassMaster National
Championship by posting a three-day total of 36 pounds and 11 ounces; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Moody and Mr. Reagan have brought great honor to their school, as
their consecutive championships have distinguished Pickett County as only the second high
school in the nation with multiple national titles; and
WHEREAS, Max and Rex's championship seasons would not have been possible
without the leadership, guidance, and instruction of their coach, Anthony Scott; and

WHEREAS, these young men's noteworthy achievements, both on and off the water, are
a tremendous source of pride for their families, their classmates at Pickett County High School,
and the entire community; and
WHEREAS, Max Moody and Rex Reagan have displayed uncommon talent,
determination, and perseverance in their quest for excellence, and it is wholly fitting that they
should be recognized for their accomplishments; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Max Moody and Rex Reagan of Pickett County
High School on winning two consecutive StrikeKing BassMaster National Championships and
extend to them our best wishes for continued success in all their future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
