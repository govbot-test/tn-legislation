SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1261
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Hannah Marsh upon
being named to the South Atlantic Conference
Carolinas All-Conference First Team.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly are pleased to recognize and
commend those successful student athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring honor
to Tennessee and serve as exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable individual is Hannah Marsh of Tusculum University, an
athlete of extraordinary talent, skill, and dedication, who has proven herself a formidable
competitor on the beach volleyball court; and
WHEREAS, in recognition of her athletic excellence, Hannah Marsh was recently named
to the South Atlantic Conference Carolinas All-Conference First Team after enjoying an
outstanding season with the Tusculum University Pioneers; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Marsh and her partner, Sofia Vega Barrantes, compiled a 7-3
conference record and 12-11 overall mark and won ten matches as Tusculum's number-one
pair, including victories over Florida Southern and Division I Eastern Kentucky, among others;
and
WHEREAS, Hannah Marsh epitomizes all that is good in today's student athlete, as she
has achieved a high level of success in sports while evincing great character both on and off the
court; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Marsh's notable achievement is a source of great pride for her family,
her school, and the State of Tennessee, and it is appropriate that we recognize this
accomplished athlete; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
