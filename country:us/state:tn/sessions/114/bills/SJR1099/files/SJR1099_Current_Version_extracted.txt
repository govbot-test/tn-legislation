SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1099
By Roberts
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Martie Davis on her
retirement as Administrator of Elections for
Hickman County.
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that the members of this General Assembly should honor
those citizens who have performed with uncommon devotion and enthusiasm throughout their
years of public service; and
WHEREAS, Martie Davis is one such estimable person who, having served with
distinction for nearly fourteen years as the Administrator of Elections for Hickman County, took
her much-deserved retirement from that position in November 2025; and
WHEREAS, during her tenure, Ms. Davis oversaw the administration of local, State, and
federal elections, ensuring that each election was conducted in an orderly, efficient, and lawful
manner; and
WHEREAS, she was responsible for managing voter registration, maintaining the
integrity of the county's voting rolls, and ensuring the proper technical operation of election
equipment, thereby safeguarding the electoral process for the citizens of Hickman County; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Davis's long career in public service was marked by her steadfast
dedication to the administrative processes of the Election Commission and her commitment to
upholding the integrity and reliability of elections; and
WHEREAS, throughout her estimable career, Martie Davis has demonstrated the utmost
professionalism, ability, and integrity, winning the unbridled respect and admiration of her
colleagues; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
