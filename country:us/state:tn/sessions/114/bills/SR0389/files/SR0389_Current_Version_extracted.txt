SENATE RESOLUTION 389
By Walley
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Kady Johnson of
Adamsville High School on receiving All-State
honors in soccer.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should recognize those
incredibly successful student athletes who have distinguished themselves as inspirational
leaders among their peers; and
WHEREAS, gifted senior midfielder Kady Johnson led her Adamsville High School Lady
Cardinals soccer team to 12 wins and the Final Four of the TSSAA Class A State Tournament in
2025; and
WHEREAS, in recognition of her outstanding performance on the pitch, Kady Johnson
was named to the Tennessee Sportswriters Association's Class A All-State Team; and
WHEREAS, being selected for the All-State Team is a supreme accolade, as only the
strongest players in Tennessee are so honored each year; and
WHEREAS, Kady Johnson's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride
for her family, her teammates, and the entire Adamsville community; and
WHEREAS, Kady Johnson epitomizes all that is good in today's student athletes, as she
has achieved a high level of success in soccer while evincing great character both on and off
the pitch; and
WHEREAS, Kady Johnson exemplifies the spirit, determination, and perseverance for
which Tennesseans are notable, and she is richly deserving of our approbation on this special
occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and congratulate

Kady Johnson of Adamsville High School on receiving All-State honors in Class A girls' soccer
and wish her much continued success and happiness in the years to come.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
