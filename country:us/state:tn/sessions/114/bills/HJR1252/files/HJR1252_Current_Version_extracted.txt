HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1252
By Howell
A RESOLUTION to honor Ocoee Church of God on the occasion
of its fiftieth anniversary.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly take great pride in honoring those
faith-based institutions whose steadfast service and spiritual leadership have strengthened their
communities and positively influenced the lives of countless citizens; and
WHEREAS, the churches of our nation have been an integral part of our history as a
people and have had an unparalleled impact in forming and maintaining a stable and just
society; and
WHEREAS, one such dynamic and historic community of faith is Ocoee Church of God,
whose members are celebrating the church's fiftieth anniversary this year; and
WHEREAS, established in 1976, the Ocoee Church of God, located in Ocoee in Polk
County, will commemorate fifty years of ministry and service on May 31, 2026, marking a
significant milestone of faith, dedication, and community impact; and
WHEREAS, the Ocoee Church of God was founded by Pastors Robert "Bob" Lee
Whitmire and Jean Whitmire, whose faithful leadership and vision guided the church for many
years until Pastor Bob's passing in 2001; and
WHEREAS, in February 2020, Pastors Michael "Mike" Lee Whitmire and Sandy
Whitmire answered the call to lead the Ocoee Church of God, continuing the Whitmire family's
legacy of ministry nineteen years after the passing of Pastor Bob Whitmire; and
WHEREAS, throughout its fifty-year history, the Ocoee Church of God has faithfully
discipled and mentored numerous church leaders, singers, musicians, teachers, and pastors
who are now ministering throughout Tennessee, across the United States, and around the world

WHEREAS, the church's welcoming and loving spirit reflects its mission to be a place

WHEREAS, guided by the belief that the Holy Bible is the infallible Word of God, the

WHEREAS, under the leadership of Pastors Mike and Sandy Whitmire, the church

Children's Ministry, Youth Ministry, Men's and Women's Ministries, Benevolence Ministry, Hope

and
WHEREAS, the Ocoee Church of God's legacy of faithful ministry has strengthened
families, uplifted individuals, and enriched the spiritual life of the region for five decades; and
WHEREAS, thanks to the church's faith, leadership, and strength of its members, Ocoee
Church of God has persevered and continues to be a light in the community; it is truly one of the
great religious institutions and catalysts for positive spiritual change in our State and nation, and
the members of this exceptional congregation should be appropriately honored for their
exemplary service to their fellow Christians and to the good people of Tennessee; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
