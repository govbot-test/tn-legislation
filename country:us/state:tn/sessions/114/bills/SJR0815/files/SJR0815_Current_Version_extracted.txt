SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 815
By Watson
A RESOLUTION to recognize and honor the Andrew Jackson
Foundation upon the occasion of its 125th
anniversary.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should honor those
exemplary organizations that preserve and promote Tennessee's rich history and heritage; and
WHEREAS, one such noteworthy institution is the Andrew Jackson Foundation, which
continues the vital work begun by the Ladies' Hermitage Association in 1889 to preserve and
interpret the home and legacy of our nation's seventh president, Andrew Jackson; and
WHEREAS, founded by a group of visionary women dedicated to honoring President
Jackson's legacy, the Ladies' Hermitage Association, now known as the Andrew Jackson
Foundation, laid the groundwork for more than a century of historical stewardship and civic
engagement; and
WHEREAS, the Foundation's beloved Spring Outing traces its origins to the turn of the
twentieth century, when members gathered on the front lawn of The Hermitage Mansion for a
luncheon, traveling by horse, carriage, or tallyho to celebrate the special occasion; and
WHEREAS, today, 137 years later, the Andrew Jackson Foundation continues its
important mission of preserving The Hermitage, a 1,120-acre National Historic Landmark, while
educating the public about the life, legacy, and enduring influence of Andrew Jackson and those
who called the estate home; and
WHEREAS, the 125th Spring Outing, held on May 13, 2025, continued this treasured
tradition and served as a highlight for members and guests who support the Foundation's
enduring mission to preserve, educate, and inspire; and
WHEREAS, the magnolia wreath displayed during the event stood as a solemn and
beautiful tribute to the devoted members of the Ladies' Hermitage Association and the Andrew

Jackson Foundation who have passed away, whose efforts helped preserve this national
treasure for future generations; and
WHEREAS, the General Assembly wishes to recognize and commend the Andrew
Jackson Foundation for its dedicated leadership in carrying forward this legacy of preservation,
education, and civic inspiration; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend the Andrew Jackson
Foundation upon the occasion of its 125th anniversary, extend our gratitude for its 137 years of
devoted stewardship of The Hermitage, and offer best wishes for continued success in its
mission to preserve, educate, and inspire future generations.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
