by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 37-1-186, is amended by adding the
following as a new subsection:
(d) A court that issues an order granting custody or guardianship of a child to a
person who qualifies as a relative caregiver shall not require the relative caregiver to
provide the child with the child's own bedroom, unless there are specific safety or
medical reasons that would make placement of the child with another child unsafe.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 37-2-414, is amended by adding the
following as a new subsection:
(f) In determining whether placement of a child with a relative for kinship foster
care is in the best interest of a child, the department shall not require the relative to
provide the child with the child's own bedroom, unless there are specific safety or
medical reasons that would make placement of the child with another child unsafe.

Section 4:
SECTION 3. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
