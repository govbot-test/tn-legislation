HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 849
By Martin B
A RESOLUTION to honor Eric Swenson upon being named the
2025 TFCA Division 1, Class 2A Coach of the
Year and West Tennessee Coach of the Year.
WHEREAS, we take great pleasure in paying tribute to those outstanding coaches who,
through hard work and commitment, distinguish themselves among the storied coaches and
legendary high school programs of Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, Eric Swenson of Huntingdon High School was recently named the 2025
Coach of the Year by the Tennessee Football Coaches Association (TFCA) and the West
Tennessee Coach of the Year by the Tennessee Sports Writers Association and The Jackson
Sun, honors that truly reflect the commitment to athletic excellence displayed by Coach
Swenson and the Mustangs; and
WHEREAS, during his nearly two decades as coach of the Huntingdon Mustangs,
Coach Swenson has amassed a 175-52 record, surpassing the legendary Paul Ward to become
the all-time winningest football coach in the program's history; and
WHEREAS, when combined with his four-year record as a coach at Hickman County
High School, Coach Swenson's career record is fast approaching over 200 wins; and
WHEREAS, in addition to posting stellar records, Coach Swenson has won nine district
championships at Huntingdon, including two state title appearances as head coach, garnering
runner-up laurels in 2012; he also made three state title appearances as an assistant coach;
and
WHEREAS, his playoff record is 31-17, and his teams have averaged 37.6 points per
game, for a total of 8,498 points scored throughout his tenure; he has a combined record of 21-
18 against Milan High School and McKenzie High School, Huntingdon's two biggest rivals; and

WHEREAS, Coach Eric Swenson epitomizes the spirit and commitment that are
characteristic of a true Tennessean; he has served with great diligence and dedication,
positively influencing the lives of countless young people with his display of character and
integrity; and
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to acknowledge and applaud Coach Swenson for his
outstanding leadership as head coach of the Huntingdon High School football team; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor Eric Swenson upon being named the 2025 TFCA Division 1,
Class 2A Coach of the Year and West Tennessee Coach of the Year, extending to him our best
wishes for much continued success both on and off the gridiron.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
