SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 994
By Bailey
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate George McKeel on the
celebration of his 100th birthday.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should pay tribute to those citizens who are celebrating
special occasions in their estimable lives; and
WHEREAS, George McKeel will commemorate his 100th birthday with a centennial
celebration at the Cumberland County Elks Lodge #2751 in Crossville on February 21, 2026;
and
WHEREAS, Mr. McKeel has lived a remarkable life, having experienced firsthand the
breadth and span of American history; and
WHEREAS, a gallant veteran of the United States Navy, George McKeel proudly served
his nation aboard the USS Chester during World War II operations in Japan, where he was
instrumental in protecting the vessel from Kamikaze attacks; and
WHEREAS, during his service, Mr. McKeel was an Electronics Technician Mate First
Class, in which position he was responsible for maintaining and repairing vital electronic
systems, including radar, sonar, and radio transmitters; and
WHEREAS, George McKeel is a highly respected member of his community; he played
a significant role in the founding of the Cumberland County Elks Lodge #2751 in 1987 and has
demonstrated steadfast dedication as a charter member for a number of years; and
WHEREAS, as Mr. McKeel celebrates his 100th birthday, he remains a beloved member
of his community, and he is a blessing to all who have the good fortune of knowing him; and
WHEREAS, we wish to grasp this golden opportunity to specially recognize one of
Tennessee's most outstanding citizens on this very special occasion; now, therefore,

BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
