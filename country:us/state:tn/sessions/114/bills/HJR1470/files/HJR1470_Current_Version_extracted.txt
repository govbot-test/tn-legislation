HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1470
By Sparks
A RESOLUTION to commend Sarah Elizabeth Vincion Lewis for
perfect attendance throughout thirteen
consecutive years of schooling.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this General Assembly is pleased to recognize
certain students for their outstanding academic performance; and
WHEREAS, Sarah Elizabeth Vincion Lewis is one such individual, who has distinguished
herself by maintaining a perfect record of attendance for thirteen years—from elementary school
through high school—as a student at Thurman Francis Elementary School, Stewart's Creek
Middle School, and Stewart's Creek High School; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Lewis will graduate from high school on Sunday, May 17,2026; this
milestone will only be further accentuated by her remarkable consistency and dedication; and
WHEREAS, throughout her academic career, Ms. Lewis has demonstrated strong
character, responsibility, determination, and a commitment to learning; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Lewis's achievement marks not only a great personal success but
brings pride to her family and community; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby commend Sarah Elizabeth Vincion Lewis for her outstanding
achievement of a perfect attendance record and extend to her our best wishes for every future
success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
