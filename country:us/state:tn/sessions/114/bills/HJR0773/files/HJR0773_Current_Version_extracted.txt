HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 773
By Farmer
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Dr. Hans Ballew on
his selection as the 2025-2026 Sevier County
School System Supervisor of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
specially recognize those outstanding professionals who have devoted their careers to providing
the highest quality education for Tennessee's students; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Hans Ballew is one such estimable administrator, who has distinguished
himself as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Ballew has been selected as the 2025-2026 Sevier County School
System Supervisor of the Year, a most befitting honor that recognizes the positive influence he
has on so many young lives; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Ballew has served with the Sevier County School System for over two
decades; he began his career as a social studies teacher at Boyds Creek Elementary School,
where he served for nine years before transitioning to serve as an assistant principal at Pigeon
Forge Primary and Pi Beta Phi elementary schools; he then served as principal of Northview
Primary STEM School for six years before being named Supervisor of K-6 Science and Social
Studies for Sevier County Schools; and
WHEREAS, a lifelong resident of Sevier County, Dr. Ballew has a deep appreciation for
area schools, families, and traditions that make the county a special place for learning and
growth; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Hans Ballew is an extremely talented and compassionate administrator
who works assiduously to motivate his students, teachers, and staff to reach their full potential
as scholars, professionals, and citizens; and

WHEREAS, Dr. Ballew serves as an inspiration to the Sevier County School System,
and his friendship and counsel are highly valued by those who are fortunate to know him; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Ballew truly epitomizes the dedicated professional who is wholly
committed to the highest ideals of public service and quality education for all, and he should be
specially recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and congratulate Dr. Hans Ballew on being chosen as
the 2025-2026 Sevier County School System Supervisor of the Year, commend his exceptional
service to the children of Tennessee, salute his professional expertise and personal excellence,
and wish him all the best in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
