HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1574
By Sparks
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Joshua Whitt, 2026
Salutatorian of Lancaster Christian Academy.
WHEREAS, it is only fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
pay tribute to those students in our state who, through their own natural abilities and unwavering
pursuit of academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Joshua Whitt is one such individual, who has been named Salutatorian of
the 2026 graduating class at Lancaster Christian Academy; and
WHEREAS, through dedication to his studies, careful recollection of vast amounts of
data, composure through his examinations, and his steadfast commitment of time and talent,
Mr. Whitt has achieved a remarkable academic feat and joined the ranks of the brightest and
most notable students in the State of Tennessee and the nation; and
WHEREAS, in his educational career, Mr. Whitt has maintained an exemplary grade
point average while participating in a number of extracurricular clubs and activities, including
playing on the Lancaster Christian Academy (LCA) Basketball Team, acting as part of the
SERVE class, and working on the LCA Bible Class's community service project; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Whitt has earned the prestigious honor of "Mr. Basketball"; and
WHEREAS, his intellectual aptitude and ambition most certainly bode well for his future
endeavors; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Whitt's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for his
parents, John and Marlena Whitt; his siblings, Mercy, Hope, Faith, and Grace; his school, and
his community; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Whitt's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of this General Assembly; now, therefore,
HJR1574
018515
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor Joshua Whitt of Lancaster Christian
Academy on being named the 2026 Salutatorian, salute his impressive academic achievement,
and extend to him our best wishes for a happy, successful future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
