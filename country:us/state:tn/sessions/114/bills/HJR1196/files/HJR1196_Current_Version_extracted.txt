HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1196
By Reneau
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend FTO Jared Wilcox and
Officer Joel Crawford upon their receipt of the
Lifesaving Medal.
WHEREAS, our nation was conceived by individuals who were willing to sacrifice their
personal concerns to ensure the public safety and welfare, and the Volunteer State is especially
proud to be the home of so many valiant individuals who have performed above and beyond the
call of duty in service of public safety; and
WHEREAS, two such individuals are FTO Jared Wilcox and Officer Joel Crawford of the
Chattanooga Police Department (CPD), who were recently awarded the CPD Lifesaving Medal
in recognition of their extraordinary actions in the line of duty; and
WHEREAS, on January 17, 2025, FTO Wilcox and Officer Crawford responded to a
report of a shooting and, upon arrival, located two gunshot victims in urgent need of medical
attention; and
WHEREAS, the officers immediately began treating the more critically wounded victim,
who had sustained multiple gunshot wounds, conducting a rapid medical assessment and
applying a chest seal and compression bandage to address breathing difficulties and control
severe bleeding; and
WHEREAS, through their decisive actions, composure under pressure, and prompt
lifesaving care, the officers stabilized the victims and enabled emergency medical personnel to
transport both individuals without the need for additional on-scene interventions; and
WHEREAS, as a direct result of their efforts, both victims survived their injuries, and the
actions of FTO Wilcox and Officer Crawford were instrumental in preserving lives; now,
therefore,

BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend FTO Jared Wilcox and Officer Joel Crawford upon
their receipt of the Chattanooga Police Department Lifesaving Medal and extend to them our
sincere appreciation for their heroic service to the people of Tennessee.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
