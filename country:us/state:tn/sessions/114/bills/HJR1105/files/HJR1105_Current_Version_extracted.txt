HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1105
By Pearson
A RESOLUTION to honor the Westwood Longhorns boys' and
girls' basketball teams, District 16A Division
champions.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly take great pride in recognizing the
achievements of young people whose hard work, determination, and dedication bring honor to
their schools and communities; and
WHEREAS, we congratulate both the Westwood High School girls' and boys' basketball
teams for recently winning the district championship against Mitchell High School; and
WHEREAS, the boys were down by 12 points and came back and tied the game at 50
points; the game went into overtime, where the Westwood Longhorns were victorious, with the
final score 57 to 54 over the Mitchell Tigers; and
WHEREAS, the student athletes of the Westwood Longhorns boys' and girls' basketball
teams of Westwood High School have demonstrated exceptional commitment to teamwork,
discipline, and athletic excellence throughout the season; and
WHEREAS, through countless hours of practice, perseverance, and unwavering focus,
these talented young athletes rose to the highest level of competition in their division; and
WHEREAS, their remarkable efforts culminated in both the Westwood Longhorns boys'
and girls' basketball teams capturing the 16A Division Championship, an extraordinary
accomplishment that reflects the strength of their program and the unity of their players and
coaches; and
WHEREAS, this achievement stands as a testament not only to the skill of the players,
but also to the leadership of their coaches, the support of their families, the encouragement of
their teachers, and the pride of the entire Memphis community; and

WHEREAS, the Westwood Longhorns have served as outstanding ambassadors for
their school and have inspired younger students throughout their community to pursue
excellence both on and off the court; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend the Westwood Longhorns boys' and girls'
basketball teams for winning the District 16A Division Championship, celebrating their
dedication, sportsmanship, and perseverance, and extending our best wishes for their continued
success in all future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
