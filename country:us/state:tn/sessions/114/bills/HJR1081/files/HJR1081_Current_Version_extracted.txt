HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1081
By Lynn
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend the Mt. Juliet Fire
Department upon the grand opening of Fire
Station 3.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly take great pleasure in recognizing
those firefighters and first responders who, through their courage and unwavering commitment
to duty, safeguard the lives and property of the citizens of Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, the Mt. Juliet Fire Department has long served its community with courage
and distinction, providing fire suppression, emergency medical response, rescue services, and
public safety education while protecting lives and property throughout the city; and
WHEREAS, on March 6, 2026, the Mt. Juliet Fire Department will celebrate the grand
opening of Fire Station 3 with a ceremonial uncoupling of the hose, marking the beginning of a
new chapter of service to the community; and
WHEREAS, located on Old Lebanon Dirt Road, the new station replaces the former East
Hill Street station and represents an important investment in the safety and future growth of the
community by improving response times and enhancing the department's ability to serve the
citizens of Mt. Juliet; and
WHEREAS, the Mt. Juliet Fire Department operates under the capable leadership of Fire
Chief Mark A. Foulks, EFO, CFO, CEMSO, whose dedication and professionalism exemplify the
highest ideals of the fire service and whose leadership continues to strengthen the department's
proud tradition of service; and
WHEREAS, the opening of the Mt. Juliet Fire Department's new Fire Station 3 marks an
important milestone in the continued growth and service of the department, strengthening its
ability to protect life and property while upholding the finest traditions of the fire service; and

WHEREAS, it is wholly fitting that we recognize this occasion and the dedicated
firefighters who serve the citizens of Mt. Juliet, Wilson County, and this great State; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend the Mount Juliet Fire Department upon the grand
opening of Fire Station 3 and extend our best wishes for continued success as the department
carries forward its proud mission of service to the citizens of this State.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
