SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1163
By Lowe
A RESOLUTION to honor Bill Winters on his exemplary tenure on
the Bradley County Commission.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should specially
recognize those Tennesseans who have performed with uncommon devotion and ability
throughout their careers in public service; and
WHEREAS, one such outstanding person is Bill Winters, who is taking a well-earned
retirement from public office in 2026 after a distinguished four-term tenure as a member of the
Bradley County Commission, representing District 7; and
WHEREAS, in addition to representing his constituents with the greatest purpose and
commitment for sixteen years, Mr. Winters ably served as the vice chair of the Commission from
2022-2024; and
WHEREAS, a strong advocate for education, Commissioner Winters supported the
establishment of the PIE Center and the expansion of school facilities in Bradley County; and
WHEREAS, he previously made valuable contributions to public education in his
community as a member of the Bradley County Board of Education; and
WHEREAS, Commissioner Winters has served as an educational titan and mentor to
many in the community, including Senator J. Adam Lowe, offering professional and spiritual
guidance and counsel for more than fifteen years; and
WHEREAS, Commissioner Winters has worked with elected officials, community
leaders, and citizens to shape Bradley County's future while protecting its character and sense
of community; and
WHEREAS, he has been instrumental in preserving and advancing the collective
interests of the citizens of Bradley County; and

WHEREAS, by not seeking reelection in 2026, Bill Winters is concluding a remarkable
career spent in dedicated service to the good people of Bradley County; and
WHEREAS, Commissioner Winters is most deserving of our approbation on this special
occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor Bill Winters on his exemplary tenure as a
member of the Bradley County Commission, commend his ongoing commitment to his
community and its progress, and wish him all the best in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
