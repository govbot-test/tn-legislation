AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 2013* House Bill No. 1905
by inserting the following at the end of SECTION 1:
63-7-507. Application to out-of-state nurse anesthesia educational programs.
(a) Notwithstanding § 63-7-504 or this part to the contrary, this part does not
apply to a nurse anesthesia educational program that is physically located outside this
state and that places students in this state for purposes of clinical education or training,
as long as the program:
(1) Is accredited by the Council on Accreditation of Nurse Anesthesia
Educational Programs (COA), or is undergoing a COA capability review and
establishing eligibility for accreditation;
(2) Is offered through a regionally accredited institution of higher
education authorized to operate in its home state;
(3) Prepares graduates for national certification as certified registered
nurse anesthetists (CRNAs) in accordance with COA standards;
(4) Ensures that all students participating in clinical education within this
state comply with applicable Tennessee licensure requirements, supervision
requirements, and all other state and federal laws governing clinical practice and
patient safety; and
(5) Receives approval for clinical placements from the board.
(b) This part does not require an out-of-state nurse anesthesia educational
program meeting the requirements of subsection (a) to obtain additional authorization
solely for the placement of students in clinical training experiences within this state.
