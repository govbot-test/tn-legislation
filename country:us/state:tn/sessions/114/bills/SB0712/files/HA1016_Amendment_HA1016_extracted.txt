AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 712 House Bill No. 664*
by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 49, Chapter 3, Part 3, is amended by
adding the following as a new section:
An LEA or public charter school shall not use public funds to initiate or maintain
any civil action or proceeding against this state or an agency or officer of this state to
challenge a school or district accountability measure established pursuant to this title.
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
