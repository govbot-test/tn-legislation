HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 971
By Reeves
A RESOLUTION to honor the Fairview Titans on an undefeated
season.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that this General Assembly should salute those young athletes
who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as true champions of
whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such exemplary group of boys is the Fairview Titans of the Tri-County
Football League, whose members have proven themselves to be the fiercest of competitors and
truly worthy of admiration by means of a perfect 11-0 season record without conceding a single
point to their opponents; and
WHEREAS, the Titans' defensive dominance is unheard of at any level of football, and
their zero points allowed is truly astonishing; and
WHEREAS, the Fairview Titans' perfect season demonstrates the high level of
performance that results when talent meets purpose; and
WHEREAS, the team's coaches began the season with a clear purpose of "building
better boys, not just better players"; under their leadership, these young men stayed "humble
and hungry" and "refused to be ordinary"; and
WHEREAS, using discipline, accountability, and hard work as their touchstones, the
Titans' coaches and players fought off the siren song of complacency to win eleven consecutive
games without allowing a single score on defense; and
WHEREAS, although their accomplishments on the gridiron are impressive, football was
merely a vehicle for the athletes and coaches to build something larger than themselves; and

WHEREAS, together, the Titans established a real brotherhood that inspired unity,
conviction, and confidence, while molding good citizens and strong leaders through trust and
sacrifice; and
WHEREAS, the members of the Fairview Titans football team epitomize all that is good
in today's young athletes, as they have achieved a premier level of success in competition while
exhibiting the highest degree of character in all aspects of life; and
WHEREAS, the perseverance, determination, work ethic, and talent of this group of
young men not only made possible an undefeated season but also surely bode well for their
future success in all their endeavors; and
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly find it appropriate to acknowledge
and applaud the Fairview Titans for their outstanding season on the gridiron; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend the members and coaches of the Fairview Titans
upon redefining excellence with an eleven-victory undefeated season while allowing zero points
by their opponents and extend to them our best wishes for every continued success in football
and in life.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
