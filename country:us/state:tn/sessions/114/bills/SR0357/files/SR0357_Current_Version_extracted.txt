SENATE RESOLUTION 357
By Haile
A RESOLUTION to commend Cicely Tharp for exemplary service
as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this body is pleased to recognize certain persons
who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment of the legislative
process; and
WHEREAS, Cicely Tharp is one such individual, who has distinguished herself in her
role as a legislative intern for Senator Ferrell Haile; her work has resulted in innumerable
benefits for the legislators she assisted and the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, born to Sondra and Phillip Tharp, Ms. Tharp is originally from Cookeville but
grew up in the greater Nashville area; she graduated from Davidson Academy in 2022; and
WHEREAS, a student at Tennessee Technological University, where she is highly
regarded by her peers, Cicely Tharp has achieved academic success while pursuing a
bachelor's degree in political science with an emphasis in legal studies; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Tharp has achieved many accomplishments during her time at
Tennessee Tech, including receiving recognition on the Dean's List from 2022 to 2025,
participating in the Tennessee Intercollegiate State Legislature Moot Court team in 2024 and
2025, being a spirit representative for Omega Chi, and holding a State of Tennessee Seal of
Biliteracy in Spanish; and
WHEREAS, upon graduating, Cicely Tharp plans to attend law school in the State and
seek a career in public service, specifically in the legal field, in order to serve and help the
citizens of Tennessee; and

WHEREAS, in addition to her love of public service, Ms. Tharp has a passion for
reading, writing, spending time outside, and crafting; her faith and her family are two of the most
important things in Ms. Tharp's life, as she credits them for where she is today; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize herself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further her studies, Cicely Tharp brought her many abilities
and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2026 session of the One Hundred Fourteenth
General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, throughout the 2026 legislative session, Ms. Tharp has surpassed all
criteria that define a devoted and conscientious legislative intern; and
WHEREAS, Cicely Tharp has performed her many duties with extraordinary insight and
rare decorum; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby commend Cicely
Tharp for outstanding service as a legislative intern for Senator Ferrell Haile and extend to her
our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
