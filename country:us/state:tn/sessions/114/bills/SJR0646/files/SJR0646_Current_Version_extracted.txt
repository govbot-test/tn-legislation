SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 646
By Johnson
A RESOLUTION to confirm the appointment of Dr. Elaine
Swafford to the advisory board for the University
of Tennessee at Chattanooga.
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-501, established an advisory
board for the University of Tennessee at Chattanooga; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-501, provides that the governor
shall appoint five (5) members to such advisory board; and
WHEREAS, Governor Bill Lee has appointed Dr. Elaine Swafford to the advisory board
for the University of Tennessee at Chattanooga for a term that shall expire on June 30, 2029;
and
WHEREAS, a lifelong educator with over forty years of service, Dr. Swafford is the CEO
of the Chattanooga Girls Leadership Academy (CGLA), the first all-girls, public charter STEM
school in Tennessee. Previously, she served as executive principal of the Howard School of
Academics and Technology and Area II Superintendent for the Hamilton County Department of
Education; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Swafford was the founding CEO of the Chattanooga Preparatory School
in 2016. She and her team received approval for the first Chattanooga public charter
Montessori elementary school, which opened at Highland Park in August 2021; and
WHEREAS, prior to accepting her calling to lead CGLA, she served as Vice President of
Student Affairs at Chattanooga State Community College, where she had previously served as
Vice President of Economic and Community Development; and
WHEREAS, an active part of her community, Dr. Swafford was a member of Class V of
Leadership Tennessee and served as president of the Chattanooga Downtown Rotary in 2020-
