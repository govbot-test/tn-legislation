HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 957
By Salinas
A RESOLUTION to honor Antonio Gibson of the New England
Patriots.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly are pleased to recognize a
University of Memphis alumnus who reached the acme of professional football as a member of
the New England Patriots; and
WHEREAS, a multi-skilled football star, Antonio Gibson played for the University of
Memphis for two seasons, thrilling Tiger fans with his success on the gridiron, both at wide
receiver and at running back; and
WHEREAS, in his two years at Memphis, Mr. Gibson had 44 receptions for 834 yards
with 10 touchdowns, 369 rushing yards and four touchdowns, and 647 return yards with a
touchdown; and
WHEREAS, in 2019, he was named the American Athletic Conference (AAC) Special
Teams Player of the Year and made first-team All-AAC as a return specialist and second-team
as a wide receiver; and
WHEREAS, in the 2020 NFL Draft, Antonio Gibson was drafted as a running back by the
Washington Commanders in the third round as the 66th overall pick; he recorded more than
1,000 yards from scrimmage and led the NFL in rushing touchdowns by a rookie that season;
and
WHEREAS, in 2024, Mr. Gibson signed a three-year contract with the New England
Patriots and finished the season with over 700 total yards from scrimmage, becoming a key
component at running back and wide receiver, as well as on special teams; and

WHEREAS, in 2025, he was named AFC Special Teams Player of the Week after
scoring a 90-yard touchdown on a kickoff return against the Miami Dolphins in Week 2 of the
regular season; and
WHEREAS, the 2025 New England Patriots posted an excellent 14-3 regular season
record, winning the AFC East for the first time since 2019, and then won three playoff games to
reach Super Bowl LX; and
WHEREAS, Antonio Gibson has reached the very pinnacle of his profession, and he is
most worthy of our approbation; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate University of Memphis standout Antonio
Gibson, now a member of the New England Patriots, upon his team reaching Super Bowl LX
and extend to him our best wishes for much continued success on the gridiron and in life.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
