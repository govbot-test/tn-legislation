SENATE BILL 1752
By Hensley
HOUSE BILL 1732
By Stinnett
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Section
37-1-408; Title 49 and Section 68-6-103, relative
to training.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 49, Chapter 5, Part 3, is amended by
adding the following as a new section:
49-5-304.
(a) Due to the considerable amount of training requirements in state law for
educators, school personnel, and volunteers at LEAs and public charter schools, the
following is a non-exhaustive, non-binding, informative list of the training requirements
and opportunities established in state law:
(1) The following statutes reference annual training:
(A) Section 49-5-1007 for the teacher code of ethics;
(B) Section 49-6-321(a) for a full-time employee to receive
seizure safety and first aid training;
(C) Section 49-6-805(7) for teachers and other school personnel
to receive school safety training;
(D) Section 49-6-815(b)(1)(E) for faculty or staff members
carrying a handgun on school grounds to receive training in school
policing;
(E) Section 49-6-816(d)(1)(D) for employees carrying a concealed
handgun on school property;

(b) This section does not exempt any individual from having to comply with a
training requirement established in or pursuant to the laws of this state, nor does it
repeal or amend any training opportunity, that are not referenced in this section.
