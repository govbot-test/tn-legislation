SENATE RESOLUTION 433
By Watson
A RESOLUTION to commend Lily Gale for exemplary service as a
legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this body is pleased to recognize certain persons
who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment of the legislative
process; and
WHEREAS, Lily Gale is one such individual who has distinguished herself in her role as
a legislative intern for Senator Bo Watson; Ms. Gale's work has resulted in innumerable benefits
for the legislators she assisted and the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, a senior at Belmont University, where she is highly regarded by her peers,
Lily Gale has achieved academic success while pursuing a degree in political science and
working as a sales associate, serving as a campaign intern, and being active in Greek life; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize herself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further her studies, Ms. Gale brought her many abilities
and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2026 session of the One Hundred Fourteenth
General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, throughout the 2026 legislative session, Lily Gale has surpassed all criteria
that define a devoted and conscientious legislative intern; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Gale has performed her many duties with extraordinary insight and rare
decorum; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby commend Lily Gale
for outstanding service as a legislative intern for Senator Bo Watson and extend to her our best
wishes for every future success.
