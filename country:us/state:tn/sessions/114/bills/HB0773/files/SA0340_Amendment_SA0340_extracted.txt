Amendment No. 1 to SB0760

AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 760* House Bill No. 773
by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 1-3-120(f), is amended by adding the
following as new subdivisions:
(8) Does not require a fee or a subscription for a person to view the website; and
(9) Is a separate and distinct website from the newspaper of general circulation
that is owned by an entity other than the newspaper of general circulation.
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2025, the public welfare requiring it.
