HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1087
By Hale
A RESOLUTION to honor Abby Alexander upon scoring more
than 1,000 points in her high school basketball
career.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should recognize those incredibly successful student
athletes who have distinguished themselves as inspirational leaders among their peers; and
WHEREAS, one such outstanding young athlete is Abby Alexander, a senior at Cannon
County High School, who recently joined the 1,000-Point Club for Tennessee high school
basketball by scoring her 1,000th career point; and
WHEREAS, no matter her path in life, Abby Alexander is an outstanding young woman
who will continue to make a difference in her community, both on and off the court; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Alexander's outstanding accomplishments are a great source of pride
for her family, her school, and the good people of Cannon County; and
WHEREAS, Abby Alexander exemplifies the spirit, determination, and perseverance for
which Tennesseans are notable, and she is richly deserving of our approbation on this special
occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Abby Alexander upon joining the 1,000-Point
Club by scoring more than 1,000 points in her high school basketball career and wish her much
continued success in her future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
