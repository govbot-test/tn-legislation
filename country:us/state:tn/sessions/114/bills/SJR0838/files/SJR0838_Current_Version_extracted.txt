SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 838
By Hatcher
A RESOLUTION to honor Derrick Davis on his many
accomplishments as a football coach.
WHEREAS, we take great pleasure in paying tribute to one of the best high school
football coaches of all time, a gentleman who earned the respect and admiration of his peers
and football fans across Tennessee during the course of his exemplary career; and
WHEREAS, Derrick Davis, head coach of the Polk County High School Wildcats football
team, is stepping down from his gridiron duties after twenty-three years at the helm, although he
will continue his teaching career; and
WHEREAS, during his twenty-three seasons at Polk County (2000-2018 and then 2022-
2025), Coach Davis led the Wildcats to an outstanding 143-113 overall record and five
consecutive region titles from 2009 through 2013; and
WHEREAS, during the thirteen-year period from 2001 through 2013, Coach Davis and
the Wildcats won an amazing 108 games for an average of 8.3 victories per season; and
WHEREAS, Derrick Davis piloted Polk County to the TSSAA state playoffs in nineteen of
his twenty-three seasons as head coach, an incredible feat; and
WHEREAS, he and his team also twice defeated the powerful Class 6-A Bradley Central
(2009 and 2013) despite the fact that their regional rival is a much larger school; and
WHEREAS, during his unparalleled career as a football coach, Derrick Davis produced
twenty-five players who achieved All-State honors; and
WHEREAS, Derrick Davis has distinguished himself as a great football coach, role
model, and leader, and he should be recognized on this special occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
SJR0838
013715
- 1 -
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Coach Derrick Davis for his
incomparable career at Polk County High School, praise his many contributions to Tennessee
high school football and the growth and maturation of hundreds of young men, and extend to
him our best wishes for every continued success and happiness.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
