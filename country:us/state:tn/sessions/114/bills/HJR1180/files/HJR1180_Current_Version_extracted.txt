HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1180
By Reneau
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Shea Jefferson upon her
receipt of the Professional Staff Leadership
Award.
WHEREAS, our nation was conceived by individuals who were willing to sacrifice their
personal concerns to ensure the public safety and welfare, and the Volunteer State is especially
proud to be the home of so many valiant individuals who have performed above and beyond the
call of duty in service of public safety; and
WHEREAS, Shea Jefferson was recently honored with the Chattanooga Police
Department's (CPD's) Professional Staff Leadership Award in recognition of her strategic
oversight of CPD's facilities, fleet, and finance as Director of Organizational Development and
Training; and
WHEREAS, renowned for her versatility and trustworthiness, Shea Jefferson has been
vital in enhancing CPD's internal operations and readiness by developing training programs to
ensure high-level performance; and
WHEREAS, her sound judgment and foresight maintain operational efficiency and fiscal
responsibility; and
WHEREAS, respected for her collaborative approach, Shea Jefferson empowers staff,
fosters innovation, and strengthens departmental cohesion, improving service delivery; and
WHEREAS, Shea Jefferson's sustained leadership has made a lasting impact on the
Chattanooga Police Department and improved its effectiveness; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Shea Jefferson upon her receipt of the
Professional Staff Leadership Award from the Chattanooga Police Department.
HJR1180
016027
- 1 -
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
