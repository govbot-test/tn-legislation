SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 898
By Rose
A RESOLUTION to honor Dr. John Combs on the occasion of his
retirement as Director of Schools for Tipton
County.
WHEREAS, it is the honor of this General Assembly to recognize those citizens of this
great State who have gone above and beyond the call of duty in serving Tennessee's most
valuable resource, our children; and
WHEREAS, Dr. John Combs has served dutifully and faithfully as Director of Schools for
Tipton County for the past eight years; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Combs has announced his intention to retire from this vital position in
the summer of 2026; and
WHEREAS, during an outstanding career with Tipton County Schools that has spanned
thirty-three years, Dr. Combs has served as a high school English teacher and coach, testing
coordinator, principal, TIF grant administrator, Director of Instruction, and finally, as Director of
Schools; and
WHEREAS, Dr. John Combs has touched the lives of tens of thousands of students,
faculty, staff, and families during his estimable tenure with Tipton County Schools; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Combs's leadership has been instrumental during several crucial
episodes affecting the children and families of Tipton County, most notably, the reconstruction
of Crestview elementary and middle schools after a natural disaster struck the City of Covington
in the spring of 2023; and
WHEREAS, in addition to his professional success, Dr. John Combs is a devoted father,
grandfather, husband, friend, community leader, and man of faith; and

WHEREAS, Dr. Combs served for many years as a deacon at Covington Church of
Christ, where he and his wife, Karla Combs, have attended since moving to Covington in 1993;
and
WHEREAS, the citizens of Tipton County and many other Tennesseans have benefited
from Dr. Combs's longstanding commitment to leadership and service, both in his professional
and personal endeavors; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor Dr. John Combs on the occasion of his
retirement as Director of Schools for Tipton County and applaud his esteemed career spent in
service to the students, faculty, staff, and families of Tipton County.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that we extend to Dr. John Combs our best wishes for
much continued success and happiness, as we look forward to the many years of public service
he will undoubtedly pursue during his hard-earned and well-deserved retirement.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
