HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1113
By Capley
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Greer Addison Herring,
2026 Top Ten Senior of Spring Hill High School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Greer Addison Herring is one such exceptional student, who was recently
named a Top Ten Senior of the 2026 graduating class at Spring Hill High School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Ms. Herring has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in her remarkable high school career, Greer Herring has earned an
exemplary grade point average while participating in numerous extracurricular activities,
including girls' soccer; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Herring's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well
for her future success as a student at the Aveda Institute and in her career as a hairstylist; and
WHEREAS, Greer Herring's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for
her parents, Anna Herring and Jason Herring, her school, and her community; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Herring's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of the members of this General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor Greer Addison Herring on being named a
