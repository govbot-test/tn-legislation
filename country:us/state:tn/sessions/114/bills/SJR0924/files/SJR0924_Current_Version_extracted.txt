SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 924
By Stevens
A RESOLUTION to honor Drake Lehmkuhl as the all-time leading
scorer in Gleason High School basketball
history.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should recognize those incredibly successful student
athletes who have distinguished themselves as inspirational leaders among their peers; and
WHEREAS, Drake Lehmkuhl of Gleason High School recently broke a record that had
stood for nineteen years to become the all-time leading scorer in Gleason High School
basketball history; and
WHEREAS, Drake Lehmkuhl topped the previous record of 2,292 points in a Senior
Night victory over South Fulton on January 30, 2026, and finished the 2025-2026 season with
2,376 points and 964 rebounds for his career; and
WHEREAS, this incredible achievement is the greatest among many during Drake
Lehmkuhl's senior season, in which he led the Gleason Bulldogs to the regional semifinals and
an 18-8 record; and
WHEREAS, Drake Lehmkuhl was also named to the All-District team for the fourth
consecutive season and the All-District Tournament team for the third consecutive season as a
senior; and
WHEREAS, he was also honored as Weakley County Basketball Player of the Year for
both the 2023-2024 and 2024-2025 campaigns; and
WHEREAS, Drake Lehmkuhl is indeed an outstanding basketball player, and his
prowess on the hardwood is appreciated by roundball experts, as evidenced by his invitation to
play in the Basketball Coaches of Tennessee All-Star Game in March 2026; and

015380
- 1 -
WHEREAS, in addition to being a gifted multi-sport athlete, Drake Lehmkuhl is an
excellent scholar and a leader among his peers; he serves as class president and student
council president while maintaining a 4.0 grade point average; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Lehmkuhl's outstanding accomplishments are a great source of pride for
his family, his school, and the entire Gleason community; and
WHEREAS, Drake Lehmkuhl exemplifies the spirit, determination, and perseverance for
which Tennesseans are notable, and he is richly deserving of our approbation on this special
occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor Drake Lehmkuhl as the all-time leading
scorer in Gleason High School basketball history, recognize him as a model student athlete, and
wish him much continued success both on and off the court.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
