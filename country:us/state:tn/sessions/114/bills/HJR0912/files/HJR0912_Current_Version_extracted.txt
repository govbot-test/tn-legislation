HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 912
By Marsh
A RESOLUTION to recognize Justified Honors, four-time
Tennessee Walking Horse World Grand
Champion.
WHEREAS, the Tennessee Walking Horse National Celebration, held annually in
Shelbyville, is the world's premier showcase for the Tennessee Walking Horse breed and
represents the highest level of excellence in training, horsemanship, and athletic achievement;
and
WHEREAS, Justified Honors has distinguished himself as one of the most accomplished
Tennessee Walking Horses in the history of the Celebration by winning the World Grand
Championship on four occasions, a feat achieved by no other walking horse; and
WHEREAS, Justified Honors is also the first walking horse to earn four World Grand
Championship titles by unanimous decision of the panel of judges, reflecting his consistent
excellence, soundness, presence, and performance at the highest level of competition; and
WHEREAS, in addition to his four World Grand Championship titles, Justified Honors
earned a sixth World Champion title with his victory in the Stallion Preliminary class in 2025,
underscoring his sustained dominance and versatility across multiple years of competition; and
WHEREAS, owned by Lisa and Dr. Jim Baum, Justified Honors was acquired in 2020
and quickly demonstrated exceptional talent, presence, and athletic ability, earning top honors
as a young stallion and progressing to historic success at the Celebration; and
WHEREAS, Justified Honors achieved the distinction of becoming the first four-year-old
to win the World Grand Championship since 2013, placing him among a select group of horses
whose early excellence has left a lasting mark on the breed; and

WHEREAS, following a temporary setback in 2024 and successful colic surgery in the
spring of 2025, Justified Honors returned to competition with resilience and determination,
culminating in his historic fourth World Grand Championship title; and
WHEREAS, the success of Justified Honors reflects the dedication and collaboration of
the Baum family, trainer and rider John Allan Callaway, the Callaway Stables team, farrier
James Elliott, and numerous professionals whose collective expertise and commitment
exemplify the highest standards of the Tennessee Walking Horse industry; and
WHEREAS, the Callaway family has established a remarkable legacy within the
Celebration, with multiple World Grand Championship titles spanning generations, and John
Allan Callaway has distinguished himself as one of the most accomplished riders in Celebration
history; and
WHEREAS, beyond his accomplishments in the show ring, Justified Honors is
contributing to the future of the breed as a sire, producing world-champion offspring across
multiple divisions, thereby ensuring his influence on generations to come; and
WHEREAS, the achievements of Justified Honors honor the traditions, integrity, and
excellence of the Tennessee Walking Horse and serve as a source of pride for the Shelbyville
community and the State of Tennessee; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we recognize and commend Justified Honors for his unprecedented
accomplishments at the Tennessee Walking Horse National Celebration, honor his owners,
trainer, and team for their dedication and stewardship, and celebrate his lasting contribution to
the history and future of the Tennessee Walking Horse industry.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation to the owners, trainer, and team of Justified Honors as a lasting expression of
