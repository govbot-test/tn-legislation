HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1227
By Keisling
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Coach Marv West upon
his retirement as head coach of the Oneida High
School girls' basketball program.
WHEREAS, we take great pleasure in recognizing those high school coaches and
educators whose devotion and leadership have positively impacted the lives of countless
student athletes during their careers; and
WHEREAS, one such distinguished educator is Coach Marv West, who has announced
his retirement after twenty-three seasons as head coach of the Oneida High School girls'
basketball program; and
WHEREAS, during his remarkable tenure, Coach West compiled an outstanding record
of 478-252, leading the Lady Indians to seven district tournament championships, seven region
tournament championships, eleven substate appearances, and five state tournament
appearances; and
WHEREAS, under his leadership, the Lady Indians achieved winning records in twenty-
one of his twenty-three seasons, including fifteen seasons with twenty or more wins, a
testament to the consistency and excellence of his program; and
WHEREAS, throughout his career, Coach West mentored numerous student athletes
who earned All-District, All-Region, and All-State honors, and he was himself the recipient of
multiple Coach of the Year awards; and
WHEREAS, Coach West has built a legacy defined by character development,
discipline, and a commitment to excellence, instilling in his players values that will serve them
well beyond their years on the court; and
WHEREAS, his leadership has been a source of pride for the Oneida community, and
his dedication to his students, school, and community has left an indelible mark on the Home of
the Indians; and
HJR1227
016274
- 1 -
WHEREAS, throughout his distinguished career, Coach Marv West has excelled both as
a coach and as a mentor, and he is worthy of our approbation on this special occasion; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Coach Marv West upon his retirement as head
coach of the Oneida High School girls' basketball program and extend to him our best wishes
for much continued success and happiness in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
