SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 614
By Briggs
A RESOLUTION to propose an amendment to Article XI, Section
5 of the Constitution of Tennessee to authorize
state lottery funding for veterans' services.
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that a majority of all the members of each house
concurring, as shown by the yeas and nays entered on their journals, that it is proposed that the
Constitution of Tennessee be amended by adding the following new paragraph after the second
paragraph in Article XI, Section 5:
Notwithstanding this section to the contrary, the legislature may authorize the entity
operating the state lottery to develop and operate lottery games to be used exclusively as a
revenue stream to fund veterans' services in this state as specified by the legislature.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the foregoing be referred to the One Hundred
Fifteenth General Assembly and that this resolution proposing such amendment be published in
accordance with Article XI, Section 3 of the Constitution of Tennessee by posting such
amendment on the official website of the Secretary of State and on the official website of the
General Assembly.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the Clerk of the Senate is directed to deliver copies
of this resolution to the Secretary of State, with this final resolving clause being deleted from
such copies.
