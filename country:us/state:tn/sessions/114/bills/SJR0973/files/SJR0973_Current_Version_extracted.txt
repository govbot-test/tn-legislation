SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 973
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor the Gatlinburg-Pittman Junior High
School Lady Highlanders basketball team upon
winning the TMSAA Class AA state
championship.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should salute those student athletes who, through their
extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as true champions of whom we can all be
proud; and
WHEREAS, one such exemplary group of young ladies is the Lady Highlanders
basketball team of Gatlinburg-Pittman Junior High School, whose members have proven
themselves to be the fiercest of competitors and truly worthy of admiration by winning the 2025-
2026 TMSAA Class AA state championship; and
WHEREAS, before claiming the title of state champion, the Lady Highlanders defeated
Alcoa for the district championship, Meigs County for the regional championship, and West
Greene Middle in the sectional title game to earn a state tournament berth; and
WHEREAS, in the semifinals, the young women from Gatlinburg-Pittman flew past
Prescott South Middle by a score of 42-22 to set up a battle for the state crown with E.O.
Coffman, from which they emerged triumphant after a hard fought 25-18 victory; and
WHEREAS, the Lady Highlanders finished the season with a record of 30-3, winning the
state championship on a 15-game winning streak; and
WHEREAS, Gatlinburg-Pittman's Coach Alex Bowers displayed strategic leadership and
a crucial ability to adapt to every challenge, ultimately leading the Lady Highlanders to the state
championship; and
WHEREAS, the Lady Highlanders were fortunate to enjoy the unfailing support of their
families, fans, fellow students, and the entire community; and

- 1 -
WHEREAS, the members of the Gatlinburg-Pittman Junior High School girls' basketball
team epitomize all that is good in today's student athletes, as they have achieved a premier
level of success in competition while exhibiting the highest degree of character in all aspects of
life; and
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to acknowledge and applaud the Gatlinburg-Pittman
Junior High School Lady Highlanders for their outstanding season on the hardwood; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate the coaches and players
of the Gatlinburg-Pittman Junior High School Lady Highlanders basketball team upon winning
the 2025-2026 TMSAA Class AA state championship and extend to them our best wishes for
every continued success in all their future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
