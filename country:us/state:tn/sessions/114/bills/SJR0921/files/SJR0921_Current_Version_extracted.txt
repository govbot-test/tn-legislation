SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 921
By Walley
A RESOLUTION to honor the memory of Douglas Leon "KingFish"
Fish of Hickory Valley.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly were greatly saddened to learn of
the passing of Douglas Leon "KingFish" Fish of Hickory Valley; and
WHEREAS, Leon Fish was an exemplary public servant and consummate professional
who worked assiduously to improve the quality of life for his fellow citizens in numerous
capacities; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Fish was born on April 20, 1942, in Bolivar; in adulthood, he was a well-
regarded member of the Moose Lodge and dedicated his time to the Hickory Valley Volunteer
Fire Department; and
WHEREAS, Leon Fish was extremely involved in local affairs; he was frequently
approached by friends and neighbors regarding political and public office matters; and
WHEREAS, his favorite pastimes were doing yardwork, cooking for friends and families,
and hosting cookouts, where laughter and camaraderie were always present; and
WHEREAS, deeply devoted to his family, Mr. Fish always endeavored to remain true to
family values of the highest order; he cherished his role as a father, and his grandchildren and
great-grandchildren were the light of his life; and
WHEREAS, he will be sadly mourned but fondly remembered by his sisters, Crystal
Sterling, Phyllis Fish Phillips, and Polly Fish Morris; his daughters, Debbie Fish (Michael) Pierce
and Lisa (Johnny) Salinas; his two grandchildren; and his three great-grandchildren; and
WHEREAS, Leon Fish was preceded in death by his parents, Leo Tate Fish and
Chessie Fish, and his sister, Alice Faye Smith Fish; and

WHEREAS, Mr. Fish leaves behind an indelible legacy of integrity and probity in public
life, compassion and loyalty in private life, and diligence and dedication in all his chosen
endeavors; and
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should remember the
bountiful life of this exceptional public servant and human being; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor the memory of Douglas Leon "KingFish"
Fish, reflecting fondly upon his impeccable character and his stalwart commitment to living the
examined life with courage and conviction.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that we extend our sympathy and offer our condolences
to the family of Mr. Fish.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
