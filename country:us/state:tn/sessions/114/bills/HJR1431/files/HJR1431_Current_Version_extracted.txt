HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1431
By Helton-Haynes
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Bailey Renee Keene,
2026 Salutatorian of East Hamilton High School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Bailey Renee Keene is one such exceptional student, who was recently
named Salutatorian of the 2026 graduating class at East Hamilton High School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Ms. Keene has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in her remarkable high school career, Bailey Keene has earned an
exemplary grade point average while participating in numerous extracurricular activities; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Keene's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well
for her future success; and
WHEREAS, Bailey Keene's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for
her family, her school, and her community; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Keene's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of this General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor the 2026 East Hamilton High School
Salutatorian, Bailey Renee Keene, on her impressive academic achievement and extend to her
our best wishes for a happy, successful future.
