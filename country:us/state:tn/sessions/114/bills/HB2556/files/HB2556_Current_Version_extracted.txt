SENATE BILL 2244
By Johnson
HOUSE BILL 2556
By Lamberth
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 4;
Title 56; Title 63, Chapter 5; Title 68 and Title 71,
relative to dental hygienists.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 63-5-103(a)(2), is amended by
deleting "period of five (5) years or more" and substituting "period of six (6) years or more".
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it, and
applies to dental hygienist members appointed to the board of dentistry on or after that date.
