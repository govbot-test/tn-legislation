SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 682
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Isaac Nuckols of
Unicoi County High School on being named to
the All-Region 1-3A Academic Team.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
excellence, have distinguished themselves in both their athletic and scholastic endeavors; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable individual is Isaac Nuckols of Unicoi County High
School, an athlete and scholar of extraordinary talent, skill, and dedication; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Nuckols was recently named to the All-Region 1-3A Academic Team in
recognition of his superlative academic record; and
WHEREAS, Isaac Nuckols epitomizes all that is good in today's student athlete, as he
has achieved a high level of success in sports and academics while evincing great character in
his every endeavor; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Nuckols's notable achievements are a source of great pride for his
family, his school, and his community; and
WHEREAS, Isaac Nuckols's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of the members of this General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and congratulate Isaac Nuckols of
Unicoi County High School on being named to the All-Region 1-3A Academic Team, wishing
him much success and happiness in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
