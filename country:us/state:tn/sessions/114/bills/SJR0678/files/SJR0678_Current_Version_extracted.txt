SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 678
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to recognize and honor Sheila Webber upon
being named the Sevierville Chamber of
Commerce Friendship Award winner.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should salute those citizens who, through their
extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as community leaders of whom we can all
be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable person is Sheila Webber, who was named the
Sevierville Chamber of Commerce Friendship Award winner in December 2025; and
WHEREAS, Sevierville Chamber of Commerce Friendship Awards are presented
monthly, and recipients are all considered for the Chamber Friend of the Year Award; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Webber currently serves as the branch manager and assistant vice
president of Citizens National Bank; and
WHEREAS, throughout her fruitful career, Sheila Webber has remained deeply
committed to supporting Chamber events, Chamber activities, and her community; and
WHEREAS, heavily involved in her community, Ms. Webber continually raises money for
nonprofit organizations and donates items for those in need; and
WHEREAS, Sheila Webber epitomizes the spirit and commitment that are
characteristics of a true Tennessean; and
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to acknowledge and applaud Ms. Webber for her
many contributions to the good people of Tennessee, her unwavering commitment to others,
and her astute leadership in Sevierville; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and commend Sheila Webber on
