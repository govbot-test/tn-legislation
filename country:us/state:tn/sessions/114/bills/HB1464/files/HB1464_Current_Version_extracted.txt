Section 1:
By Stevens
HOUSE BILL 1464
By Powers
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 5;
Title 6; Title 7, Chapter 51; Title 8 and Title 50,
relative to electrical linemen.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-51-210(a), is amended by adding
the following new subdivision:
( ) "Electrical lineman" means an individual who:
(A) Installs, maintains, or repairs electrical transmission and power
distribution systems; and
(B) Is employed by a local government or an entity that contracts with a
local government for the installation, maintenance, or repair of such systems;

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-51-210(b), is amended by adding
the language "or electrical lineman" after "emergency responder" wherever it may appear.

Section 4:
SECTION 3. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-51-210(d), is amended by deleting
the period at the end of the subsection and substituting ", or being both an emergency
responder and an electrical lineman.".

Section 5:
SECTION 4. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.
