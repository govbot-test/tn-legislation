Amendment No. 1 to SB1907
Watson
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 1907 House Bill No. 1464*
by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:
SECTION 1. This act is known and may be cited as "The Chance Carlton Act."
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-51-210(a), is amended by adding
the following new subdivision:
( ) "Electrical lineman" means an individual who:
(A) Installs, maintains, or repairs electrical transmission and power
distribution systems; and
(B) Is employed by a municipality, county, utility district, or other local
governmental entity;
SECTION 3. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-51-210(b), is amended by adding
the language "or electrical lineman" after "emergency responder" wherever it may appear.
SECTION 4. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-51-210(d), is amended by deleting
the period at the end of the subsection and substituting ", or being both an emergency
responder and an electrical lineman.".
SECTION 5. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-51-210, is amended by adding the
following as a new subsection:
(f) A denial of a claim made under this section by the estate of an electrical
lineman is subject to review by the department of finance and administration within
ninety (90) days of the denial. The department of finance and administration has the
authority to review the claim and issue a final order, which is binding upon this state.
