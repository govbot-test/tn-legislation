HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1246
By Capley
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Jeana Marks, 2026
Valedictorian of Hampshire Unit School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Jeana Marks is one such exceptional student, who was recently named
Valedictorian of the 2026 graduating class at Hampshire Unit School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Ms. Marks has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in her remarkable high school career, Jeana Marks has earned an
exemplary grade point average while participating in numerous extracurricular activities,
including volleyball, BETA club, Fellowship of Christian Athletes, Future Business Leaders of
America, and HOSA; and
WHEREAS, Jeana Marks's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes
well for her future success as a student at Trevecca Nazarene University, where she will study
to become a registered dietitian; and
WHEREAS, a dedicated member of her community, Jeana Marks has participated in the
Thanksgiving turkey handout, helped serve food, and helped set up school events; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Marks's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for
her parents, Tracy Marks and Tonya Marks, her school, and her community; and
HJR1246
016561
- 1 -
WHEREAS, Jeana Marks's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of the members of this General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor Jeana Marks on being named the 2026
Hampshire Unit School Valedictorian, salute her impressive academic achievement, and extend
to her our best wishes for a happy, successful future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
