SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1159
By Lowe
A RESOLUTION to honor the Cleveland High School Blue
Raiders on winning their ninth consecutive
TSSAA state dual wrestling championship in

Section 2:
2026.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that this General Assembly should salute those student athletes
who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as true champions of
whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such exemplary group of young athletes is the Cleveland High School
Blue Raiders, whose members have all proven themselves to be the fiercest of competitors and
truly worthy of admiration by winning the 2026 TSSAA Division I, Class AA state boys' dual
wrestling championship; and
WHEREAS, the 2026 title marks an incredible ninth consecutive state duals
championship for Cleveland, a feat that extends the record the Blue Raiders had previously set;
and
WHEREAS, Cleveland also recorded the team's sixth consecutive undefeated duals
season in 2025-2026; and
WHEREAS, in the final match versus Dobyns-Bennett, Cleveland scored 32 consecutive
points to transform a three-point lead into a 53-18 victory and a state championship; and
WHEREAS, in addition to the state duals title, Cleveland produced several individual
state boys' wrestling champions this season, including Jose Cordero, Dominic Cordero, Kellen
Sanders, Braxton Pulley, and Gabe Moore; and
WHEREAS, the following young men are proud to compete on the wrestling mat as a
Cleveland Blue Raider: Gabriel Ayala, Ralston Blake, Aiden Boyd, Hudson Chittum, Hayden
Clark, Preston Congdon, Dominic Cordero, Jose Cordero, Taylor Crawley, Max Denetolis,

Sepenuk, Zechariah Shipman, Cason Stewart, Xavier Terry, Caleb Valentin, Bryce Webb, and
Elijah York; and
WHEREAS, Cleveland was piloted to the promised land by Head Coach Josh Bosken
and his assistant coaches, Matt High, Jacob Murphy, Shawn Cordell, Brooks Sacharczyk, and
Evan Vermillion; and
WHEREAS, the Cleveland High School Blue Raiders epitomize all that is good in today's
student athletes, as they have achieved a premier level of success in competition while
exhibiting the highest degree of character in all aspects of life; and
WHEREAS, the perseverance, determination, work ethic, and talent of this group of
young men not only made possible a record-extending championship season but also surely
bode well for their future success; and
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly find it appropriate to acknowledge
and applaud the Cleveland High School boys' wrestling team; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate the members and
coaches of the Cleveland High School Blue Raiders boys' wrestling team on winning the 2026
TSSAA Division I, Class AA state dual wrestling championship, thus claiming their ninth
consecutive state duals title, and extend to them our best wishes for every continued success in
all their future endeavors.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
