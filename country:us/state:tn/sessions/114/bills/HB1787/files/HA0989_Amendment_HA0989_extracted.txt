subsection (b) and substituting:
(b)

(a)(2)(A) is a Class A misdemeanor.
(2) Patronizing prostitution under subdivision (a)(2)(B) or (a)(2)(C) is a
Class B felony.
(3) Patronizing prostitution under subdivision (a)(2)(D) is a Class A
felony.
