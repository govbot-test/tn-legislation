SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 548
By Johnson
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate the Fairview Titans
upon winning the 2025 TriCounty Jr Pro Football
and Cheer Super Bowl Championship.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly are pleased to specially recognize
and commend those exemplary sports teams whose dedication, ability, and achievements have
brought pride and distinction to their communities and to this State; and
WHEREAS, the Fairview Titans youth football team completed an extraordinary 2025
season, culminating in the team's victory in the TriCounty Jr Pro Football and Cheer Super Bowl
Championship; and
WHEREAS, demonstrating exceptional discipline, teamwork, and skill, the Titans
completed the season with a perfect 11–0 record, scoring 330 points while never letting a single
point hit the scoreboard against them throughout the entirety of their season, a defensive
accomplishment unprecedented in recorded peewee football history; and
WHEREAS, championships are achieved only through hard work, dedication, and the
ability to work together as a team and excel with a cooperative spirit; the Fairview Titans
exhibited each of these rare qualities during their season; and
WHEREAS, the Titans' remarkable success is a testament to the steadfast leadership
and coaching of Calvin Billingsley, Josh Greifenkamp, Adam Wynne, Jason Vanatta, and Alex
Ward, who emphasized character development, growth, accountability, and resilience both on
and off the field; and
WHEREAS, throughout their championship-winning football season, the Fairview Titans
have distinguished themselves through their dedication, sportsmanship, perseverance, and
unity; and

WHEREAS, the members of the championship-winning Fairview Titans include Evan
Barry, Knox Richie, Owen Smigielski, Arbor Wynne, Cooper Ward, Wylder Deering, Hudson
Lanham, Bryar Miller, Benjamin Flexer, Edward Dial, Easton Pflieger, Maximus Copeland,
Hawkins Hillin, Kareem Welker, Franco Garnica, Cruz Greenberg, Leland Vanatta, Reed
Greifenkamp, Jack Gasparo, and Odell Billingsley; and
WHEREAS, the achievements of the exceptional Fairview Titans reflect the support and
commitment of their families, community, and local supporters, all of whom contributed to this
historic season; and
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to acknowledge and applaud the Fairview Titans for
serving as exemplars of the high quality of the youth of Tennessee; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate the Fairview Titans
youth football team upon winning the 2025 TriCounty Jr Pro Football and Cheer Super Bowl
Championship, extending to the players, coaches, and supporters our best wishes for continued
success in all future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
