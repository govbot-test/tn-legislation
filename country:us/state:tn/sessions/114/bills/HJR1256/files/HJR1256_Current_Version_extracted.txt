HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1256
By Keisling
A RESOLUTION to commend Ruthie Brawner upon her retirement
from Macon Bank & Trust Company.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should salute those
citizens who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as community
leaders of whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such individual is Ruthie Brawner of Macon Bank & Trust Company,
who, after serving with that fine institution for twenty-nine years, is taking her much-deserved
retirement; and
WHEREAS, throughout her career, Ms. Brawner has consistently demonstrated
acumen, an excellent work ethic, and leadership ability; and
WHEREAS, during her twenty-nine-year tenure, Ruthie Brawner has made untold
contributions to this State and has helped grow and fortify Macon Bank & Trust Company, such
that this historic institution will continue to serve its community for generations to come; and
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly find it appropriate to pause in their
deliberations to acknowledge and applaud Ms. Brawner for her dedication to the State of
Tennessee and its citizens; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and commend Ruthie Brawner upon the occasion of her
retirement from Macon Bank & Trust Company, applaud her dedication to excellence, and
extend to her our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
