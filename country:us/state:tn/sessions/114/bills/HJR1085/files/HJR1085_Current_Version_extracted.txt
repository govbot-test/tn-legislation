HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1085
By Hale
A RESOLUTION to recognize and honor Jackie Wilkerson on the
occasion of his retirement.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
honor those individuals who have answered a divine call to the ministry and have served with
dedication, vigor, and enthusiasm to perform God's work for their congregations and
communities; and
WHEREAS, one such individual is Jackie Wilkerson, who will be taking his much-
deserved retirement as International Secretary of United Pentecostal Church, following astute
leadership and devout service in that role; and
WHEREAS, Brother Wilkerson began his journey in the ministry in Tennessee in 1979
as an evangelist from the Missouri District serving churches in Tennessee; in 1981, he was
elected pastor of Humboldt's First United Pentecostal Church, now Harvest Church; and
WHEREAS, he retired as pastor of the church in Humboldt in 2010 after twenty-nine
years of devoted service, during which time he concurrently served as the Section 8 Sunday
School Representative and Tennessee District Youth Secretary; and
WHEREAS, a trusted leader of the United Pentecostal Church, Brother Wilkerson
served as Section 8 (later Section 9) District Presbyter for nineteen years, from 1991 to 2010,
and has served as Tennessee District Secretary for the past sixteen years, bringing his total
service as an official of the Tennessee District to forty-one years; and
WHEREAS, throughout his many worthy endeavors, Jackie Wilkerson has enjoyed the
love, support, and co-ministry of his wife, Sharron Wilkerson; together, they have been blessed
with two sons, Jared (Erica) Wilkerson and Seth (Kelsey) Wilkerson, as well as six
grandchildren, Canaan, Judah, Eden, Delay, Matthew, and Elyse; and

WHEREAS, Brother Wilkerson has continued to demonstrate excellence in his calling as
a man of God, and he has served the United Pentecostal Church well during his tenure as
International Secretary; and
WHEREAS, his abiding love for the ministry and his many contributions to his church,
his community, and this State are deeply appreciated, and it is wholly fitting that he be
recognized on this special occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we recognize and honor Jackie Wilkerson on the occasion of his
retirement as International Secretary of United Pentecostal Church, extending our best wishes
for his every future success and happiness.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
