Amendment No. 2 to HB1973
Johnson
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 2106* House Bill No. 1973
by adding the following new sections immediately preceding the penultimate section and
renumbering the subsequent sections accordingly:
SECTION __. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 49, Chapter 6, Part 26, is amended by
adding the following as a new section:
A participating school shall report to the state board of education if the
participating school knows that an educator who holds a license issued by the state
board of education violates § 49-5-1008.
SECTION __. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 49, Chapter 6, Part 35, is amended by
adding the following as a new section:
A private school that enrolls recipients shall report to the state board of education
if the private school knows that an educator who holds a license issued by the state
board of education violates § 49-5-1008.
SECTION __. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 49, Chapter 10, Part 14, is amended
by adding the following as a new section:
A participating school shall report to the state board of education if the
participating school knows that an educator who holds a license issued by the state
board of education violates § 49-5-1008.
