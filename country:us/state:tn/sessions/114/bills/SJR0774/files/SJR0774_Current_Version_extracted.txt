SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 774
By Seal
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Bryson Headrick of
Sevier County High School on being named the
Region 1-5A Offensive Player of the Year.
WHEREAS, we are pleased to specially honor and commend those successful high
school athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring honor to the State of Tennessee
and serve as exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable athlete is Bryson Headrick of Sevier County High
School, a football player of extraordinary talent, skill, and athleticism, who has been named the
Region 1-5A Offensive Player of the Year; and
WHEREAS, a gifted receiver, Bryson Headrick posted 1,200 receiving yards and caught
17 touchdown passes this past season while setting the school record for career receiving yards
with 2,193; and
WHEREAS, a powerhouse with the football, Bryson Headrick also rushed for 67 yards
and four touchdowns for the Bears during the 2025 campaign; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Headrick epitomizes all that is good in today's student athlete, as he has
achieved a high level of success in sports while evincing great character both on and off the
field; and
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that we recognize and commend this elite athlete and
outstanding young gentleman; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Bryson Headrick of
Sevier County High School on being named the Region 1-5A Offensive Player of the Year and
extend to him our best wishes for much continued success in his future endeavors.
SJR0774
011754
- 1 -
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
