Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 40-35-501(x), is amended by adding
the following as a new subdivision:
(3)
(A) By July 1, 2027, and each July 1 thereafter, the commissioner of
correction shall deliver to the chair of judiciary committee of the senate and the
chair of the committee of the house of representatives with jurisdiction over the
department of correction a report containing the following data in regard to parole
under this subsection (x):
(i) The number of individuals certified as eligible for parole;
(ii) The number of individuals granted parole after being certified
as eligible;
(iii) The number of individuals denied parole after being certified
as eligible;
(iv) The number of individuals certified as eligible for parole and
awaiting a hearing at the end of the reporting period;
(v) The number of individuals who were released under this
subsection (x); and
(vi) The number of individuals who were granted parole after
being certified as eligible but were not released.
(B) The data in subdivision (x)(3)(A) must include:

(i) The individual's race;
(ii) The individual's ethnicity;
(iii) The individual's sex;
(iv) The individual's age;
(v) The individual's reason for certification as parole eligible;
(vi) The role of the person who initiated the certification of the
individual as parole eligible;
(vii) The highest class of offense for which the individual is
incarcerated;
(viii) The facility where the individual is or was incarcerated;
(ix) The length of time the individual has been incarcerated;
(x) Whether a hearing was held for the individual's request for
certification as parole eligible;
(xi) The outcome of the individual's request for certification as
parole eligible;
(xii) The reason for the outcome of the individual's request for
certification as parole eligible;
(xiii) The length of time between submission of the individual's
request for certification as parole eligible and the commissioner's
decision;
(xiv) The length of time between the commissioner's certification
and the individual's parole hearing;
(xv) The length of time between the board's recommendation for
release and release from custody;
(xvi) Whether the individual was released from custody;
(xvii) Whether the individual died prior to the commissioner's

the medical parole process.
