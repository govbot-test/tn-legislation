SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1161
By Lowe
A RESOLUTION to honor Thomas Crye on the occasion of his
retirement from the Bradley County Commission.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
specially recognize those estimable public servants whose lives exemplify dedicated service,
integrity, and commitment to the people of Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, Thomas Crye has rendered exemplary service to the citizens of Bradley
County, serving as a county commissioner for twelve years, from 2014 to 2026, during which
time he has demonstrated steadfast leadership, sound judgment, and an unwavering
commitment to the public good; and
WHEREAS, sworn in as a member of the Bradley County Commission on September 1,
2014, he has further distinguished himself through his service as vice chairman from 2019 to
2022 and as chairman since 2022, providing capable leadership and guidance and exhibiting a
keen ability to address the needs of his community; and
WHEREAS, after years of devoted public service, Thomas Crye will retire at the
conclusion of his present term, bringing to a close a tenure marked by integrity, diligence, and
commitment to District 2 and the entire Bradley County community; and
WHEREAS, a passionate advocate for education, Thomas Crye was instrumental in
establishing the PIE Center and has worked tirelessly to expand local school facilities, ensuring
that students throughout District 2 have the resources they need to thrive; and
WHEREAS, throughout his public service career, Mr. Crye has embodied the highest
ideals of public service, earning the respect, gratitude, and admiration of his colleagues and
fellow citizens; and
SJR1161
018141
- 1 -
WHEREAS, Thomas Crye is wholly committed to the noble precepts of public service
that have earned Tennessee recognition as the Volunteer State, and he should be specially
recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and commend Thomas Crye for
his years of distinguished service as a member of the Bradley County Commission, extend our
sincere appreciation for his lasting contributions to education, public service, and community
life, and wish him continued success in all his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
