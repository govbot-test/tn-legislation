HOUSE RESOLUTION 145
By Cepicky
A RESOLUTION to recognize Lafayette Chapter #4 Royal Arch
Masons of Tennessee on the occasion of its
200th anniversary.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should recognize those exemplary organizations that,
through an abiding commitment to excellence, have contributed greatly to this State for many
years; and
WHEREAS, one such notable organization is Lafayette Chapter #4 Royal Arch Masons
of Tennessee, which is celebrating its 200th anniversary this year; and
WHEREAS, Lafayette Chapter #4 Royal Arch Masons of Tennessee is a Masonic
fraternal organization that also contributes to charitable causes, such as Royal Arch Medical
Research Assistance, Autism Awareness, and Aide of Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, for 200 years, men from all walks of life in Maury County and its environs
have joined this esteemed organization to improve their character and contribute to society
through practicing Royal Arch moral teachings; a number of members throughout the Chapter's
existence have been leaders of distinction, including President James K. Polk; and
WHEREAS, an anniversary of this magnitude gives members a chance to celebrate their
long and distinguished history of charity and community service, reflect back with respect and
reverence on the work, struggles, and accomplishments of those who have gone before them,
and look toward the future with strength, confidence, and pride; and
WHEREAS, the members of Lafayette Chapter #4 Royal Arch Masons of Tennessee
have dedicated themselves to laudable acts that have assisted countless individuals and have
made a positive impact on their community and on this State as a whole, and they should be
formally recognized on this special occasion; now, therefore,
HR0145
011850
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and
commend the members, both past and present, of Lafayette Chapter #4 Royal Arch Masons of
Tennessee for their dedication to public service, congratulate them on the organization's 200th
anniversary, and extend to them our best wishes for every continued success in all their future
endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
