Section 2:
Section 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 37, Chapter 5, Part 5, is amended by
adding the following as a new section:
(a) The department shall submit to the speaker of the senate, the speaker of the
house of representatives, and the governor by October 1 of each year, a report
regarding the status of child care agencies licensed or approved by the department.
(b) The report must include:
(1) The number of child care agencies licensed or approved by the
department;
(2) The number of licenses issued per category for family boarding
home, group care home, child placing agency, maternity home, child abuse
agency, detention center, temporary holding resource, and any other category for
which the department issues a license or approval;
(3) The number of corrective action plans instituted by the department
during the year in regard to a licensed or approved child care agency; and
(4) A description of each child care agency that has failed to comply with
a corrective action plan, including the name of the licensed or approved child
care agency and the portion of the child care agency's corrective action plan for
which the child care agency was noncompliant.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
