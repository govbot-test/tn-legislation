HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 759
By Lamberth
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Robert Earl Dudgeon
on the celebration of his ninetieth birthday.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pay tribute to
those citizens who are celebrating special occasions in their estimable lives; and
WHEREAS, Robert Earl Dudgeon celebrates his ninetieth birthday on March 8, 2026, a
milestone that will be commemorated as yet another precious souvenir of life's rich pageant;
and
WHEREAS, Mr. Dudgeon has lived an amazing life and has experienced firsthand the
breadth and span of American history; and
WHEREAS, born in Campbellsville, Kentucky, on March 8, 1936, Robert Dudgeon is a
gallant veteran of the United States Army, which he valiantly served as a Specialist in the
Armored Infantry; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Dudgeon is a joy to be around and a blessing to all who have the good
fortune of knowing him; and
WHEREAS, as Robert Dudgeon marks his ninetieth birthday, he remains a beloved
resident of Gallatin, who is admired for his military service and his commitment to his family,
faith, community, and country; and
WHEREAS, we wish to grasp this golden opportunity to specially recognize one of
Tennessee's most outstanding citizens on this very special occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Robert Earl Dudgeon on the celebration of his
ninetieth birthday and extend to him our best wishes for many happy returns of the day.
