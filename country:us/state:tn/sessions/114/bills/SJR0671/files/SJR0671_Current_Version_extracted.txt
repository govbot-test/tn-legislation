SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 671
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Dr. Lana Luttrell on
her selection as the Greeneville City Schools
Principal of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
specially recognize those outstanding professionals who have devoted their careers to providing
the highest quality education for Tennessee's students; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Lana Luttrell is one such estimable administrator, who has distinguished
herself as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Luttrell has been selected as the Greeneville City Schools Principal of
the Year, a most befitting honor that recognizes the positive influence she has on so many
young lives; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Lana Luttrell is passionate about creating meaningful learning
experiences that inspire curiosity, confidence, and success in all students; she believes in the
power of collaboration and works closely with teachers, parents, and community members to
ensure that every child has the resources and support needed to succeed; and
WHEREAS, under Dr. Luttrell's leadership, Tusculum View Elementary School has
continued to thrive, emphasizing academic excellence, student well-being, and a strong sense
of community; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Luttrell is an extremely talented and compassionate administrator who
works assiduously to motivate her students, teachers, and staff to reach their full potential as
scholars, professionals, and citizens; and
WHEREAS, she serves as an inspiration to the faculty and staff of Tusculum View
Elementary School, and her friendship and counsel are highly valued by those who are
fortunate to know her; and
SJR0671
011536
- 1 -
WHEREAS, Dr. Luttrell truly epitomizes the dedicated principal who is wholly committed
to the highest ideals of public service and quality education for all, and she should be specially
recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and congratulate Dr. Lana Luttrell
on being chosen as the Greeneville City Schools Principal of the Year, commend her
exceptional service to the children of Tennessee, salute her professional expertise and personal
excellence, and wish her all the best in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
