HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 959
By Salinas
A RESOLUTION to honor Eric Gregory of the New England
Patriots.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly are pleased to recognize a Memphis
native who has reached the acme of professional football as a member of the New England
Patriots; and
WHEREAS, a multi-skilled athlete, Eric Gregory became a star on the football field
during his high school days at Memphis Central High School, where he totaled 56 tackles and
12 sacks during his three-year career; and
WHEREAS, after high school, Mr. Gregory played for the University of Arkansas over six
seasons, making forty-four starts and playing in sixty-one games between 2019-2024; and
WHEREAS, on May 9, 2025, Eric Gregory was signed as a free agent by the Cincinnati
Bengals of the National Football League; he was claimed by the New England Patriots on
August 29, 2025, and made his NFL debut on November 13, 2025, against the New York Jets;
and
WHEREAS, the 2025 New England Patriots posted an excellent 14-3 regular season
record, winning the AFC East for the first time since 2019, and then won three playoff games to
reach Super Bowl LX; and
WHEREAS, Eric Gregory has reached the very pinnacle of his profession, and he is
most worthy of our approbation; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Eric Gregory of Memphis, now a member of the
