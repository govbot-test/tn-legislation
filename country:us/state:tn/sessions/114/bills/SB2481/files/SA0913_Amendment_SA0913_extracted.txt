Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-51-1104, is amended by adding
the following as new subsections:
(g)
(1) The board shall not issue a license unless the applicant certifies that
a sign is posted at each entrance of the establishment that states: "Attention: By
engaging in this type of entertainment, you may be contributing to an increase in
domestic assault, rape or sexual assault, and human trafficking.".
(2) A sign posted pursuant to subdivision (g)(1) must be no smaller than
eight and one-half inches by eleven inches (8 1/2" x 11"). The required language
must be printed in at least forty-eight-point type in boldface, block letters,
centered on the sign. The letters must be black on a white background.
(h)
(1) If an adult-oriented establishment lawfully offers for sale, loan, or use
sexually oriented materials, including print, video, and other media, then the
board shall not issue a license to the establishment unless the applicant certifies
that a sign is posted at each display of sexually oriented material that states:
"Attention: By purchasing, borrowing, or using this pornographic material, you
may be contributing to an increase in domestic assault, rape or sexual assault,
and human trafficking.".
