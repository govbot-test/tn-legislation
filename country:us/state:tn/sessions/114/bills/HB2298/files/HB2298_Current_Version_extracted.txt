SENATE BILL 2664
By Akbari
HOUSE BILL 2298
By Hardaway
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 39,
relative to criminal offenses.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 39-13-102(a), is amended by
deleting the language "Involved the use or display of a deadly weapon" and substituting
"Involved the use, display, or brandishing of a deadly weapon".
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 39-11-106(a), is amended by adding
the following new subdivision:
( ) "Brandish" means to wave or exhibit a weapon in a manner that would be
found by a reasonable person to indicate a threat to use the weapon;
SECTION 3. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.
