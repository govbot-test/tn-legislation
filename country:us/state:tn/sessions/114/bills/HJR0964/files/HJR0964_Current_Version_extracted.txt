HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 964
By Slater
A RESOLUTION to recognize Dr. Jon Forlines of Welch College
on the occasion of his retirement.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should specially
recognize, particularly on the occasion of their retirement, those outstanding higher education
administrators who have devoted their careers to enriching the lives of Tennessee's students;
and
WHEREAS, one such exceptional individual is Dr. Jon Forlines, Vice President for
Student Services and Dean of Students at Welch College in Gallatin; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Forlines recently announced that he will be taking his well-deserved
retirement after more than twenty-four years of distinguished service; he will continue to play a
role in the success of Welch College as Special Advisor to President Matt Pinson; and
WHEREAS, the son of former Welch College Dean of Students and theology professor
Leroy Forlines and Fay Forlines, Dr. Forlines graduated from Welch College in 1980, after which
time he served as an associate and youth pastor in North Carolina for many years; and
WHEREAS, he began his doctoral studies in psychology while living in Polston Hall, the
women's residence hall on the West End of Welch's campus, where his wife, Welch College
Dean of Women, served as women's resident director; he completed his Ph.D. at Tennessee
State University in 1998; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Jon Forlines served as a psychological examiner in the Tennessee
prison system until beginning his current role at Welch College in 2001; throughout his tenure,
he has earned a reputation for being kind, compassionate, and committed, always centering
students with a desire to see them grow in wisdom and in faith; and

WHEREAS, throughout his many worthy endeavors, he has enjoyed the love and
support of his wife, Susan, and their children—Stephen, who died in childhood, and Joel, Jared,
and Anna, all three Welch graduates; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Forlines has demonstrated the utmost professionalism, ability, and
integrity throughout his career, winning the respect and admiration of everyone associated with
Welch College; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we extend to Welch College's Vice President for Student Services and
Dean of Students Dr. Jon Forlines our heartfelt wishes for a happy and fulfilling retirement.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
