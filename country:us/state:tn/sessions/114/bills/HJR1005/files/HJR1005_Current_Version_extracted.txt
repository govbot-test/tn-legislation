HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1005
By Keisling
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Jon Godwin upon his
induction into the Clay County Sports Hall of
Fame.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that this General Assembly pay tribute to those gifted athletes,
coaches, and administrators who have attained the very pinnacle of sporting achievement; and
WHEREAS, Jon Godwin is one such legendary athlete, who was recently inducted into
the Clay County Sports Hall of Fame; and
WHEREAS, an iconic figure within the annals of the Clay County High School boys'
sports, Mr. Godwin excelled in basketball, football, and baseball during his time at Clay County
High, from 2004 to 2006; and
WHEREAS, after transferring from Jackson County for his junior and senior seasons,
Mr. Godwin was a starting tailback on the Clay County football team and a starting outfielder in
baseball, but he was best known for his exploits on the basketball court; and
WHEREAS, despite playing only two seasons as a Bulldog, he compiled statistics that
would rival four full years, including scoring 929 points, grabbing 257 rebounds, and handing out
248 assists; and
WHEREAS, as a junior, he was named to the Nera White Holiday All-Tournament team,
earned the District 7A tournament MVP honor, and made the Region 4-A All-Tournament team;
and
WHEREAS, Jon Godwin's banner season as a senior in 2005-2006 saw him lead Clay
County High School to its first-ever appearance in the state tournament, which was also the first
appearance by a new school in twenty years; and

WHEREAS, that year, he also made the Tennessee Sports Writers Association's All-
State team, was named Tri-Lakes Conference MVP, District 7A and Region 4A Tournament
MVP, and made the Herald-Citizen All-Upper Cumberland 1st team; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Godwin positively influenced the lives of countless individuals with his
display of character and integrity throughout his athletic career, and it is fitting that we should
honor him on this special occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Jon Godwin upon his induction into the Clay
County Sports Hall of Fame and extend to him our best wishes for much continued success and
happiness.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
