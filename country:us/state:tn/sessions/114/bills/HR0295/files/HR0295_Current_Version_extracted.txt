HOUSE RESOLUTION 295
By Pearson
A RESOLUTION to honor Sarah Houston for her exceptional
leadership and to commend Protect Our Aquifer
for its efforts to safeguard Tennessee's water
resources.
WHEREAS, the members of this legislative body are pleased to recognize those
dedicated individuals and organizations whose tireless efforts protect the natural resources of
our State and promote the health and well-being of our citizens; and
WHEREAS, Sarah Houston serves with distinction as the Executive Director of Protect
Our Aquifer, a Memphis-based nonprofit organization committed to preserving and protecting
the region's drinking water supply; and
WHEREAS, under Ms. Houston's visionary leadership, Protect Our Aquifer has become
a leading voice in advocating for the protection of the Memphis Sand Aquifer, one of the purest
and most vital sources of drinking water in the nation; and
WHEREAS, through education, community engagement, and policy advocacy, Ms.
Houston has worked diligently to raise awareness about the importance of safeguarding
groundwater resources from contamination and overuse; and
WHEREAS, Protect Our Aquifer has played a critical role in advancing responsible
environmental policies, strengthening public understanding of water sustainability, and
mobilizing citizens to take action in defense of clean and safe drinking water; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Houston's steadfast commitment to environmental stewardship has
helped ensure that current and future generations of Tennesseans have access to high-quality,
affordable drinking water; and

WHEREAS, her leadership exemplifies the highest standards of public service and civic
responsibility, inspiring communities across Memphis and the State of Tennessee to value and
protect their natural resources; and
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we honor Sarah Houston and Protect Our Aquifer for their
invaluable contributions to environmental protection and public health; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby
honor and commend Sarah Houston for her outstanding service as Executive Director of Protect
Our Aquifer and extend our sincere appreciation for her leadership in protecting Tennessee's
precious water resources.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that we commend Protect Our Aquifer for its ongoing
efforts to preserve the integrity of the Memphis Sand Aquifer and to promote environmental
awareness and sustainability throughout the State.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
