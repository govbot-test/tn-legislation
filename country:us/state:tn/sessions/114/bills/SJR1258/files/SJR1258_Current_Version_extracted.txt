SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1258
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor Qitarah Castlin, South Atlantic
Conference Women's Track Athlete of the Week.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly are pleased to recognize and
commend those successful athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring honor to
Tennessee and serve as exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable individual is Qitarah Castlin of Tusculum University, an
athlete of extraordinary talent, skill, and dedication, who has proven herself a formidable
competitor; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Castlin was recently named the South Atlantic Conference WePlayed
Sports Women's Track Athlete of the week for the week ending April 12, 2026; and
WHEREAS, while competing at the Tennessee Invite, Qitarah Castlin won the women's
100-meter relay with a time of 11.48 seconds and ran the leadoff leg to win the 4X100-meter
relay that was timed in 44.50 seconds; and
WHEREAS, she also ran a career-best in the 200-meter relay at 23.61 seconds, earning
a third-place finish in the event; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Castlin's 11.48 finish in the 100-meter relay is sixth-best in Division II
competition this season, while the Tusculum University Pioneers currently sit atop the 4X100
performance list with their time of 44.47 seconds during the Electric City Invitational on April 3,
2026; and
WHEREAS, a native of Griffin, Georgia, Qitarah Castlin holds the Tusculum University
record in 400-meter competition with 55.72 seconds and is second on the program performance
list in both the 100-meter and 200-meter events; and

WHEREAS, Ms. Castlin epitomizes all that is good in today's student athlete, as she has
achieved a high level of success in competition while evincing great character in all aspects of
life; and
WHEREAS, her notable achievements are a source of great pride for her family, her
community, and her university, and it is appropriate that we recognize this exceptional athlete;
now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Qitarah Castlin, South
Atlantic Conference Women's Track Athlete of the Week, as we extend to her our best wishes
for much continued success in her future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
