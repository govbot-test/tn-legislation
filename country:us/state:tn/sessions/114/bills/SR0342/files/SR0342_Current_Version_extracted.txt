SENATE RESOLUTION 342
By McNally
A RESOLUTION to confirm the appointment of Kelly G Rollins of
Murfreesboro to the State Board of Education.
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-1-301, provides for the creation of
the State Board of Education; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-1-301, further provides that the
Speaker of the Senate shall appoint members representing the third, fourth, and seventh
congressional districts to the State Board of Education; and
WHEREAS, Speaker Randy McNally has appointed Kelly G Rollins of Murfreesboro to
the State Board of Education as a member representing the Fourth Congressional District for a
term that will expire on March 31, 2031; and
WHEREAS, a highly respected member of his community, Kelly Rollins retired in 2020
after a distinguished thirty-seven-year career in management with United Parcel Service, having
worked his way up the ranks from part-time employee to serve in a number of management
roles, including package operations, marketing, business development, supply chain solutions,
and sales; and
WHEREAS, a first-generation graduate, Mr. Rollins holds a Bachelor of Business
Administration in Marketing and Management degree from Middle Tennessee State University;
and
WHEREAS, an active part of his community, he has lent his time and expertise to a
number of charitable, civic, and nonprofit endeavors, including the Boys & Girls Club, the
Community Foundation of Rutherford County, the Blue Raider Athletic Association, Leadership
Rutherford, and the Murfreesboro City Planning Commission; and
