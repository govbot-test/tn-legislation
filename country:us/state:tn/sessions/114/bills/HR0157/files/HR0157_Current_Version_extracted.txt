HOUSE RESOLUTION 157
By Sherrell
A RESOLUTION to recognize James Bennett on the occasion of
his retirement.
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that we should honor those citizens who have performed
with uncommon devotion and enthusiasm throughout their careers; and
WHEREAS, after fifty years of distinguished service, James Bennett of Warren County
will soon take his much-deserved retirement, having shaped generations of Warren County
students through his devotion to public education; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Bennett's career stands as a testament to service, integrity, and an
unwavering belief in the potential of every child; and
WHEREAS, for thirty-eight years, James Bennett's service in public education saw him
enjoy tenures as a teacher, coach, and principal, guiding students not only through academics
but through the lessons of character, perseverance, and responsibility; and
WHEREAS, in the classroom, on the ball field, and in the halls of the schools of Warren
County, he modeled the values that strengthen communities and change lives; and
WHEREAS, following his retirement from school administration, he continued his
commitment to public service as the District 1 School Commissioner, elected by the people of
Warren County for three consecutive terms for a total of twelve years; and
WHEREAS, as a member of the Board of Education for Warren County, Mr. Bennett
brought the same steady leadership, thoughtful judgment, and student-centered focus that
defined his decades as an educator; and
WHEREAS, throughout his career, James Bennett dedicated his life to teachers,
students, families, and to Warren County, always striving to ensure that every child had the

opportunity to learn, grow, and succeed; his half-century of service reflects a rare and profound
devotion to the community he loves; and
WHEREAS, thousands of students carry his influence into their families, workplaces,
and communities. Warren County is stronger because of his leadership, and Tennessee is
better for his service; and
WHEREAS, for the past fifty years, James Bennett has demonstrated the utmost
professionalism, ability, and integrity, winning the sincere respect and admiration of his
colleagues and the citizens of the State of Tennessee; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we extend to
James Bennett our deepest appreciation for his fifty years of faithful service to the children of
Warren County and offer our heartfelt wishes for a happy and fulfilling retirement and every
continued success in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
