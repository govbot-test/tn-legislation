SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1263
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Sylvia Liszewski upon
being named to the South Atlantic Conference
Carolinas All-Conference Second Team.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should recognize and
commend those successful student athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring honor
to Tennessee and serve as exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, Sylvia Liszewski of Tusculum University is one such estimable individual, an
athlete of extraordinary talent, skill, and dedication, who has proven herself a formidable
competitor on the beach volleyball court; and
WHEREAS, in recognition of her athletic excellence, Sylvia Liszewski was recently
named to the South Atlantic Conference Carolinas All-Conference Second Team after enjoying
an outstanding season with the Tusculum University Pioneers; and
WHEREAS, competing alongside her teammate Brynn Smith, Ms. Liszewski helped lead
her team through a demanding schedule, contributing to multiple conference victories and
demonstrating consistency, determination, and teamwork throughout the season; and
WHEREAS, Sylvia Liszewski epitomizes all that is good in today's student athletes, as
she has achieved a high level of success in sports while evincing great character both on and
off the court; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Liszewski's notable achievement is a source of great pride for her
family, her school, and the State of Tennessee, and it is appropriate that we recognize this
accomplished athlete; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and congratulate Sylvia Liszewski

upon being named to the South Atlantic Conference Carolinas All-Conference Second Team,
extending to her our best wishes for continued success in all her future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
