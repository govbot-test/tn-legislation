HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1221
By Hale
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend the Jackson County High
School boys' basketball team upon winning the
2026 TSSAA Division I, Class A state
championship.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that this General Assembly should salute those student athletes
who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as true champions of
whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such exemplary group of lads is the Blue Devils boys' basketball team
of Jackson County High School, whose members have proven themselves to be the fiercest of
competitors and truly worthy of admiration by winning the 2026 TSSAA Division I, Class A state
championship, the first state championship in school history; and
WHEREAS, in the championship game at the "Glass House" in Murfreesboro, the
Jackson County Blue Devils defeated Humboldt by a score of 52-39 to claim the Gold Ball
trophy presented to the state champion; and
WHEREAS, Class A Mr. Basketball winner, Will McCrary, was named tournament MVP
and was joined on the All-Tournament team by fellow Blue Devils Aden Netherton, Parker
Patterson, and Alex Meadows; and
WHEREAS, the Jackson County Blue Devils finished their season with an overall record
of 32-5, having claimed the District 7-A tournament championship, the Region 4-A tournament
championships, and the Class A tournament championship; and
WHEREAS, the state championship is the crowning achievement for seniors Isaiah
Thomas, Parker Patterson, Skyler Stacy, Alex Meadows, Aden Netherton, and Will McCrary,
who tallied 100 total wins for the Blue Devils on the hardwood during their careers; and

WHEREAS, Jackson County's superb performance would not have been possible
without the expert guidance of Head Coach Kevin Thomas and his coaching staff and the
unfailing support of the players' families, their fans, and their community; and
WHEREAS, the members of the Jackson County boys' basketball team epitomize all that
is good in today's student athletes, as they have achieved a premier level of success in
competition while exhibiting the highest degree of character in all aspects of life; and
WHEREAS, the perseverance, determination, work ethic, and talent of this group of
young men not only made possible a championship but also surely bode well for their future
success; and
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly find it appropriate to acknowledge
and applaud the Jackson County Blue Devils for their historic season on the hardwood; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend the Jackson County High School boys' basketball
team upon winning the 2026 TSSAA Division I, Class A state championship, and extend to them
our best wishes for every continued success in all their future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
