SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 978
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Jasmin Coleman of
Walters State Community College on being
named the TCCAA Coach of the Year.
WHEREAS, we take great pleasure in paying tribute to those outstanding individuals
who, through hard work and commitment, distinguish themselves among the legendary
basketball coaches of Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, one such notable individual is Jasmin Coleman, head coach of the Walters
State Community College women's basketball team, who was recently named the 2025-2026
TCCAA Coach of the Year, an honor that truly reflects her commitment to athletic excellence;
and
WHEREAS, impressively, this marks the fifth time in her six-year career that Coach
Jasmin Coleman has received this prestigious honor as she led the Walters State Lady
Senators to a 24-2 record; and
WHEREAS, the Walters State Lady Senators were undefeated, 20-0, in TCCAA regular
season play, which marks the first time the program has finished with an unblemished record
against their fellow Tennessee community colleges since the 2018-2019 season; and
WHEREAS, Coach Jasmin Coleman epitomizes the spirit and commitment that are
characteristic of a true Tennessean; she has served with great diligence and dedication,
positively influencing the lives of countless young people with her display of character and
integrity; and
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to acknowledge and applaud Jasmin Coleman for her
outstanding leadership as head coach of the Walters State Community College women's
basketball team; now, therefore,
SJR0978
015891
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Jasmin Coleman of
Walters State Community College upon being named the 2025-2026 TCCAA Coach of the Year
and extend to her our best wishes for much continued success both on and off the basketball
court.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
- 2 - 015891
