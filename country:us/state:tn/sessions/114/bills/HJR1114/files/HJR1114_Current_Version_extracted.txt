HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1114
By Capley
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Isabella Meadow Aaron,
2026 Top Ten Senior of Spring Hill High School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Isabella Meadow Aaron is one such exceptional student, who was recently
named a Top Ten Senior of the 2026 graduating class at Spring Hill High School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Izzy Aaron has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in her remarkable high school career, Ms. Aaron has earned an exemplary
grade point average while participating in numerous extracurricular activities, including Color
Guard, Winter Guard, Fellowship of Christian Athletes, National Honor Society, and Prom
Committee; and
WHEREAS, Izzy Aaron is a Raider Leader and a volunteer with Showers on Wheels;
and
WHEREAS, Ms. Aaron's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well
for her future success as a student at Columbia State Community College, where she plans to
study for her future career in the field of project management or interior design; and
WHEREAS, Izzy Aaron's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for
her parents, Recena Aaron and Shawn Aaron, her school, and her community; and

WHEREAS, Ms. Aaron's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of the members of this General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor Isabella Meadow Aaron on being named a
2026 Spring Hill High School Top Ten Senior, salute her impressive academic achievement,
and extend to her our best wishes for a happy, successful future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
