HOUSE RESOLUTION 189
By Johnson
A RESOLUTION to commend Erin Craven for exemplary service
as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, the members of this legislative body are pleased to
recognize certain persons who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the
betterment of the legislative process; and
WHEREAS, Erin Craven is one such individual, who has distinguished herself in her role
as a legislative intern for Representative Gloria Johnson and Representative Sam McKenzie;
Ms. Craven's work has resulted in innumerable benefits for the legislators she assisted and the
General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, a native of Nashville, Erin Craven is a graduate of Antioch High School and
has been instilled with the passion for leadership, excellence, and public service; and
WHEREAS, a senior at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville, where she is highly
regarded by her peers, Ms. Craven has achieved academic success while pursuing a double
major degree in political science and philosophy with a minor in public health and a
concentration in law and politics; she has a strong interest in public health policy and plans to
obtain a master's degree in public health before attending law school and becoming a medical
malpractice attorney; and
WHEREAS, during her collegiate career, Erin Craven completed an undergraduate
research thesis on the effects of reconstructive nature and language in eyewitness testimonies;
and
WHEREAS, Ms. Craven's leadership extends beyond the college campus, as she has
volunteered in the Nashville and Knoxville communities; and

WHEREAS, eager to familiarize herself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further her studies, Erin Craven brought her many abilities
and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2026 session of the One Hundred Fourteenth
General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, throughout the 2026 legislative session, Ms. Craven has surpassed all
criteria that define a devoted and conscientious legislative intern; and
WHEREAS, Erin Craven has performed her many duties with extraordinary insight and
rare decorum; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we commend
Erin Craven for outstanding service as a legislative intern for Representative Gloria Johnson
and Representative Sam McKenzie and extend to her our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
