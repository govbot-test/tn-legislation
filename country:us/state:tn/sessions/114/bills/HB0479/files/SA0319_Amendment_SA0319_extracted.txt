Senate State and Local Government 1
Amendment No. 1 to SB0530
Briggs
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 530 House Bill No. 479*
by deleting subsection (c) in the amendatory language of Section 1 and substituting instead the
following:
(c) As used in this section, unless the context otherwise requires, "eligible
recipient" means an individual who is:
(1) A member of the Tennessee national guard;
(2) Eligible for TRICARE reserve select or TRICARE dental program
coverage; and
(3) Not receiving health or dental insurance coverage through a civilian
employer.
