SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1277
By Bailey
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Samuel Ryan Ferrell,
2026 Salutatorian of Stone Memorial High
School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Samuel Ryan Ferrell is one such exceptional student, who was recently
named Salutatorian of the 2026 graduating class at Stone Memorial High School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Mr. Ferrell has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in his remarkable high school career, Samuel Ferrell has earned an
exemplary grade point average while participating in numerous extracurricular activities; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Ferrell's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well
for his future success; and
WHEREAS, his noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for his family,
his school, and his community; and
WHEREAS, Samuel Ferrell's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of the members of this General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor the 2026 Stone
Memorial School Salutatorian, Samuel Ryan Ferrell, on his impressive academic achievement
and extend to him our best wishes for a happy, successful future.
