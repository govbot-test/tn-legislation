AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 778* House Bill No. 816
by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 55-50-504, is amended by adding
the following as a new subsection:
(k) Points must not be assessed to a person's driver record for the offense of
operating a motor vehicle while the person's driving privilege is cancelled, suspended, or
revoked.
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2025, the public welfare requiring it.
