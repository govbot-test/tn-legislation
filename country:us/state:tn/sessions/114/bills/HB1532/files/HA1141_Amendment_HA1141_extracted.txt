House State & Local Government 1
Amendment No. 1 to HB1532
Crawford
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 2530 House Bill No. 1532*
by deleting SECTION 1(b) and substituting:
(b) A deceased member or former member of the general assembly is not permitted to
lie in state at the state capitol if:
(1) The member or former member was convicted of a felony, unless the
member or former member was pardoned for the felony; or
(2) The former member was expelled from office and was not reelected for a
subsequent term.
(c) As used in this section, "immediate family member" means a spouse, parent, sibling,
or child.
