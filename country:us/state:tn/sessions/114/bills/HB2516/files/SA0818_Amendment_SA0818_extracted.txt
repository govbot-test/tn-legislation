Amendment No. 2 to SB2547
Reeves
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 2547* House Bill No. 2516
by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 68-211-803, is amended by adding
the following as a new subsection:
(d) It is further declared to be the policy of this state to plan for sufficient landfill
capacity to dispose of solid waste that is not recovered or recycled.
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 68-211-813, is amended by deleting
subsection (c) and substituting:
(c) Each region shall develop a plan in accordance with §§ 68-211-814 and 68-
211-815, including a narrative identifying where waste will be disposed and managed for
the next ten (10) years, and for achieving compliance with the waste reduction and
recycling goal required by § 68-211-861. The plan must be updated and resubmitted to
the department at least once every ten (10) years.
SECTION 3. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 68-211-814, is amended by deleting
subdivision (a)(1) and substituting:
(a)
(1) Each region shall submit its new or revised plan to the department of
environment and conservation by July 1, 2028. The plan shall be formulated in
strict compliance with § 68-211-815. After receiving a plan, the department shall
notify the region within thirty (30) days whether the plan includes all of the
information required by § 68-211-815, and if not, identify all additional required
information. If the department determines the plan is incomplete, the region shall

submit all required information within sixty (60) days of receipt of notification.
The department shall approve or disapprove the plan based on compliance with
§ 68-211-815 and the criteria established pursuant to subdivision (a)(4) within
one hundred twenty (120) days after receipt of a complete plan. If a plan is

Alternatively, the region may file a petition for appeal in accordance with § 68-
211-113(a)(1) within thirty (30) days of receiving the letter of disapproval.
SECTION 4. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 68-211-814(a), is amended by
adding the following as a new subdivision:
(4) The underground storage tanks and solid waste disposal control board shall
promulgate rules establishing criteria for review of regional plans by December 31, 2027.
SECTION 5. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
