HOUSE BILL 2151
By Baum
SENATE BILL 1902
By Bailey
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated,
Title 4, Chapter 31; Title 7, Chapter 51; Title 42,
Chapter 4 and Title 42, Chapter 3, relative to
airport authorities.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 42-4-104(b)(2), is amended by
adding the following sentence at the end of the subdivision:
Notice must also be published on the governing body's website for at least two (2) weeks
prior to the date set for the hearing.
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
