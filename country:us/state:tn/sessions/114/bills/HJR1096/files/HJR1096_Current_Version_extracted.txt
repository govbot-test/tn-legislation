HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1096
By Haston
A RESOLUTION to honor the memory of Gretchen Qualls Sadler
of Linden.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly were greatly saddened to learn of
the passing of Gretchen Qualls Sadler of Linden; and
WHEREAS, Gretchen Sadler, affectionately known as "Ma," was a beloved wife, mother,
grandmother, aunt, sister, and friend to many; and
WHEREAS, a native of Perry County, Ms. Sadler was born on December 20, 1931, to
Lonzo and Genevieve Qualls; and
WHEREAS, Gretchen Sadler was a kind, compassionate, and loving woman who loved
the Lord, her family, and everyone she met; and
WHEREAS, Gretchen Sadler loved to sew and quilt, stitching warmth and love into every
piece she made; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Sadler was deeply devoted to her faith, and her favorite sayings were
"Lord, help us" and "Lord, watch over us," words she spoke often and truly lived by; and
WHEREAS, Gretchen Sadler was deeply devoted to her family, and she always
endeavored to remain true to family values of the highest order; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Sadler was happiest when cooking for family, friends, or anyone who
walked through her door; her grandchildren and great-grandchildren were her pride and joy, and
she spent her life protecting her family fiercely and giving her whole heart to those she loved;
and
WHEREAS, Ms. Sadler is lovingly remembered by her children, George (Sheryl) Sadler
and Patsy (Jack) Sanford; bonus son, Rob Bills; grandchildren, Vawneda (Jason) Bates, Ashley
(Jared) Gardner, Boomer (Jade) Sadler, Becky (Jimbo) McFall, Jesse (Brian) Keeton, William
