HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1153
By Terry
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Morgan Freeman for his
extraordinary contributions to film, culture, and
the State of Tennessee.
WHEREAS, Morgan Freeman is one of the most recognizable and respected actors in
the history of American cinema; and
WHEREAS, it has long been on the bucket list of the State of Tennessee to properly
honor Morgan Freeman, whose remarkable career and enduring legacy have brought distinction
to his home state and inspiration to audiences around the world; and
WHEREAS, as this resolution is read, it is only fitting to imagine its words delivered in
the unmistakable voice of Morgan Freeman himself, as his narration has guided generations
through stories of hope, perseverance, and humanity; and
WHEREAS, born in Memphis, Morgan Freeman represents the rich cultural and artistic
heritage of our great State, serving as a source of pride for Tennesseans everywhere; and
WHEREAS, throughout a celebrated career that has spanned decades, Mr. Freeman
has delivered unforgettable performances in many acclaimed motion pictures, including The
Bucket List, Driving Miss Daisy, Bruce Almighty, Along Came a Spider, Se7en, and The
Shawshank Redemption, each performance demonstrating his exceptional talent, commanding
presence, and profound ability to connect with audiences; and
WHEREAS, his work has earned numerous awards and honors, including an Academy
Award and multiple lifetime achievement recognitions, solidifying his place among the greatest
performers of all time; and
WHEREAS, beyond his achievements on screen, Morgan Freeman has elevated
storytelling itself through narration, education, and cultural contributions that have deepened
public appreciation for history, science, and the arts; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting that this General Assembly pause to recognize a son of
Tennessee whose accomplishments have reflected great credit upon this State; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and commend Morgan Freeman for his extraordinary
career, his lasting cultural impact, and the pride he brings to Memphis and to the entire State of
Tennessee.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that we extend our sincere appreciation and best wishes
to Morgan Freeman for continued success and happiness.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
