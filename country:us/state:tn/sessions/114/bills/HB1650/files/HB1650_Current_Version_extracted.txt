SENATE BILL 1661
By Gardenhire
HOUSE BILL 1650
By Helton-Haynes
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 33;
Title 63; Title 68 and Title 71, relative to health
care.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 63-5-106, is amended by deleting the
section and substituting:
The board shall make an annual report of its proceedings and activities by
February 1 each year, to the governor and the general assembly, with copies to be
made available to any interested parties.
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
