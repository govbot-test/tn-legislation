Section 2:
SECTION 12. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-4-933(d)(2), is amended by
deleting the subdivision and substituting instead the following:
(2)
(A) Except as provided in subdivision (d)(2)(B), for a period of six (6)
years after the student is admitted to an eligible postsecondary institution if
satisfactory progress is achieved and maintained.
(B) The student is not eligible to receive the Tennessee HOPE foster
child tuition grant pursuant to this subdivision (d)(2) after the student has earned:
(i) A bachelor's degree unless the student maintains continuous
enrollment toward an advanced degree; or
(ii) An advanced degree.
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting subdivision (d)(4) in Section 17 and substituting:
(4) Notwithstanding another law to the contrary, the commission may conduct
private meetings to discuss or deliberate on matters related to personnel or contracts;
provided, that the commission shall not take action on matters related to personnel or
contracts in a private meeting.
