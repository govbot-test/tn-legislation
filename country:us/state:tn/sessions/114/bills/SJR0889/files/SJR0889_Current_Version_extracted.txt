SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 889
By Pody
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Ken Nelson, Sr., on
the celebration of his ninetieth birthday.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those citizens who are celebrating special occasions in their estimable lives; and
WHEREAS, Ken Nelson, Sr., will celebrate his ninetieth birthday on February 24, 2026,
a milestone to be commemorated as yet another precious souvenir of life's rich pageant; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Nelson has lived an amazing life and has experienced firsthand the
breadth and span of American history; and
WHEREAS, Ken Nelson has proudly served the Cedar Senior Center in Lebanon as
president for the past nine years, and under his guidance, the center has witnessed significant
growth in fundraising, grants, donations, and volunteerism; additionally, building expansions and
facility and grounds improvements, among other projects, were completed with grants from
WEHF and the City of Lebanon; and
WHEREAS, following the COVID-19 pandemic, the Cedar Senior Center has continued
to move forward and surpass goals; today, fundraisers, including quilt raffles and pancake
breakfasts, operate with normal expectations; and
WHEREAS, Ken Nelson's astute leadership and devoted work as president have
resulted in innumerable benefits for the Cedar Senior Center and its members; he continues to
arrive early for his duties, ensuring the kitchen staff is present, deliveries are received and
stored, and the center is operative and ready for lunches, fellowship, and activities; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Nelson is a joy to be around and a blessing to all who have the good
fortune of knowing him; and
SJR0889

WHEREAS, as Ken Nelson marks his ninetieth birthday, he remains a beloved member
of his community who is admired for his faith, integrity, and enthusiasm for life; and
WHEREAS, we wish to grasp this golden opportunity to specially recognize one of
Tennessee's most outstanding citizens on this very special occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and congratulate Ken Nelson, Sr.,
on the celebration of his ninetieth birthday and extend to him our best wishes for many happy
returns of the day.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
