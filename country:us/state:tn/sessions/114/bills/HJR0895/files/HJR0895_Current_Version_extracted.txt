Section 1:
HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 895
By Lamberth
A RESOLUTION to confirm the appointment of Jennifer Hampton
to the advisory board for the University of
Tennessee at Martin.
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-501, established an advisory
board for the University of Tennessee at Martin; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-501, provides that the governor
shall appoint five (5) members to such advisory board; and
WHEREAS, Governor Bill Lee has appointed Jennifer Hampton to the advisory board for
the University of Tennessee at Martin (UTM) for a term that shall expire on June 30, 2029; and
WHEREAS, Jennifer Hampton is a seasoned financial advisor and dedicated advocate
for higher education in West Tennessee; she earned her undergraduate degree in economics
and finance from UTM, graduating summa cum laude, and later completed a master's degree in
business; and
WHEREAS, a financial advisor with Edward Jones in Martin, Jennifer Hampton has
served on the University of Tennessee Board of Governors, the UTM College of Business
Advisory Board, and the UTM-City of Martin Town and Gown Board; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-501, provides that each member
appointed by the governor to the advisory board for the University of Tennessee at Martin shall
be confirmed by the General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby confirm the appointment of Jennifer Hampton to the advisory
board for the University of Tennessee at Martin for a term that expires on June 30, 2029.
HJR0895
