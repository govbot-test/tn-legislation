by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 50-1-702(13), is amended by
deleting "at least six (6) employees" and substituting "at least one (1) employee".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 50-1-703(f), is amended by deleting
subdivision (3) and substituting:
(3) The private employer shall submit to the commissioner evidence of
compliance with subdivision (a)(1) and subsection (b) within forty-five (45) days of the
final order. If the private employer fails to submit such documentation, then the
commissioner shall request an order consistent with § 4-5-320, requiring the appropriate
local government with respect to business licensure pursuant to title 67, chapter 4, to
suspend the private employer's license:
(A) For a first or second violation, until the employer remedies the
violation; provided, however, if the private employer's license has also been
suspended pursuant to § 50-1-103(e)(1)(B) or (e)(1)(C), then the license shall
remain suspended until the expiration of the period provided for in § 50-1-
103(e)(1)(B) or (e)(1)(C); or
(B) For a third or subsequent violation, permanently.

Section 4:
SECTION 3. This act takes effect January 1, 2027, the public welfare requiring it, and
applies to conduct occurring and employees hired on or after that date.
