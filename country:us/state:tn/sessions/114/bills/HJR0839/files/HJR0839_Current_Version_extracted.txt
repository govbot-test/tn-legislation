HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 839
By Travis
A RESOLUTION to honor Allan Dale Hannah for his fifty years of
service to the City of Pikeville Volunteer Fire
Department.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should specially recognize those honorable Tennesseans
who volunteer their time to serve and protect the good people of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable person is Allan Dale Hannah, who has served his
community with distinction as a firefighter for the City of Pikeville Volunteer Fire Department
since October 20, 1975; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Hannah has served in many leadership capacities throughout his fifty
years of service, including as Lieutenant, Captain, and Assistant Chief; his wealth of experience
and extensive training has made him an unparalleled asset to the department's team; and
WHEREAS, enjoying a distinguished career, Mr. Hannah was instrumental in expanding
the department's apparatus fleet and increasing services provided by the department; and
WHEREAS, Dale Hannah was born in Bethesda, Maryland, on May 28, 1950, to Sallie
B. and Florian Hannah; and
WHEREAS, his many professional accomplishments aside, Dale Hannah enjoys the
love and support of his wife, Elizabeth Hannah, and cherishes his role as a father to Jodie
Young, Allan Hannah, and Michael Hannah and Jessica Hannah; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Hannah's tireless dedication behind the scenes does not go unnoticed;
his commitment to his community exemplifies selflessness and unwavering support; and
WHEREAS, he has served not only as a responder and leader but as a source of
inspiration to his fellow firefighters and community members alike, embodying the spirit of
volunteerism, mentorship, and resilience; and

WHEREAS, Mr. Hannah is wholly committed to the noble precepts of public service that
have earned Tennessee recognition as the Volunteer State, and he should be specially
recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Allan Dale Hannah for his dedicated service as a
member of the City of Pikeville Volunteer Fire Department and extend our best wishes for his
every future success and happiness.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
