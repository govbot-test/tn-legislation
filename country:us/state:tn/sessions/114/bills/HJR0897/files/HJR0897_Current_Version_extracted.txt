HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 897
By Lamberth
A RESOLUTION to confirm the appointment of Gary A. Passons,
MD, to the advisory board for the University of
Tennessee Health Science Center.
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-501, established an advisory
board for the University of Tennessee Health Science Center; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-501, provides that the governor
shall appoint five (5) members to such advisory board; and
WHEREAS, Governor Bill Lee has appointed Gary A. Passons, MD, to the advisory
board for the University of Tennessee Health Science Center for a term that shall expire on
June 30, 2029; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Gary Passons is a board-certified ophthalmologist with decades of
experience in comprehensive and surgical eye care and clinical education; he earned his
undergraduate degree from Vanderbilt University in 1976 and his medical degree from the
University of Tennessee Health Science Center in 1980; and
WHEREAS, the founding member of Passons Eye Center, PLLC, in Memphis, Dr.
Passons is a member of the American Academy of Ophthalmology and the Memphis Society of
Ophthalmology, which he has served as president since 1994; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-501, provides that each member
appointed by the governor to the advisory board for the University of Tennessee Health Science
Center shall be confirmed by the general assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby confirm the appointment of Gary A. Passons, MD, to the
