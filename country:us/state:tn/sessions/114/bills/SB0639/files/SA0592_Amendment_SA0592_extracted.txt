by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-35-401(b), is amended by adding
the following as a new subdivision:
( ) "Galvanized requiring replacement" means a galvanized service line that is
or was at any time downstream of a lead service line, or is currently or was previously
connected to a lead service line, and is required to be replaced pursuant to applicable
federal drinking water regulations, including the federal Lead and Copper Rule, 40
C.F.R. Part 141, Subpart I, as amended.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-35-401(c)(1)(B)(iii), is amended by
deleting the subdivision in its entirety and substituting instead the following:
(iii) "Project for a private purpose" does not include the renewal or replacement
or assessment of:
(a) Private water lines behind a utility-owned meter when such
rehabilitative maintenance or construction is deemed necessary by a
municipality, utility district, metropolitan government, or other public entity to
address lead service lines or galvanized requiring replacement lines located on
private property, but only for the portion of the service line extending from the
utility-owned meter to the exterior building envelope of the structure served, or
other water lines for the purpose of addressing serious health issues; or
(b) Sewage lines on private property when such rehabilitative
maintenance or construction is deemed necessary by a municipality, utility

Section 4:
SECTION 3. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-35-401(c), is amended by adding
the following as a new subdivision:

described in subdivisions (c)(1)(B)(iii)(a) and (b) if the property owner consents and
