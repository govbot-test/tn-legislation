House Education 1
Amendment No. 1 to HB0640
White
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 400* House Bill No. 640
by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-6-1507(a), is amended by adding
the following as a new subdivision:
(4) Guidance for TALLC members on how to align tutoring services for students
with the remediation the student needs and with the academic standards adopted by the
state board of education for the student's grade level.
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-6-1509(b)(1), is amended by
adding "and the Tennessee accelerating literacy and learning corps (TALLC)" after "the learning
loss remediation and student acceleration program".
SECTION 3. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-6-1509(b)(2), is amended by
adding the following as a new subdivision:
(D) A comparison of the effectiveness of after-school learning mini-camps,
learning loss bridge camps, summer learning camps, and tutoring services provided by
TALLC members in remediating learning loss and improving student academic
performance.
SECTION 4. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 49, Chapter 6, Part 15, is amended by
adding the following as a new section:
The department shall provide LEAs with a list of secure data management
systems that utilize platforms capable of tracking the effectiveness of intervention
methods, resource allocation, and student progress in real time across all schools in the
LEA. The systems listed must be capable of complying with the Family Educational
