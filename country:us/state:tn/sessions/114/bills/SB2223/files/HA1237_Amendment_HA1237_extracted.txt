Section 1:
Amendment No. 5 to HB2219
Salinas
Signature of Sponsor
by adding the following as a new subdivision in Section 1:
(5) On or before January 31, 2027, and each January 31 thereafter, the sheriff of
each county shall submit a report to the mayor and legislative body of the sheriff's
county, the governor, the speaker of the house of representatives, and the speaker of
the senate. The report under this subdivision (b)(5) must provide:
(A) The costs incurred by the county in relation to the participation in the
287(g) program, such as equipment, pay, overtime pay, and hiring, and for
counties receiving state and federal funding, the allocation of such funding;
(B) The division of labor as it relates to immigration enforcement;
(C) Traffic stop data broken out by demographic and infraction; and
(D) Jail demographic data broken out by crime category, including holds
of detainees for the United States bureau of immigration and customs
enforcement (ICE).
