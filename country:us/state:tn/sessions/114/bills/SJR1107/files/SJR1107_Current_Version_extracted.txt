SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1107
By Haile
A RESOLUTION to honor the Station Camp High School JROTC
Drill Team on taking top honors at the Mt. Juliet
All-Services Drill Championship.
WHEREAS, we are proud to honor those high school students and their instructors
whose commitment to this nation and dedication to excellence can only be categorized as
outstanding; and
WHEREAS, the members and instructors of the Station Camp High School Junior
Reserve Officer Training Corps (JROTC) Drill Team comprise one such admirable and
deserving group, which recently won top honors at the Mt. Juliet All-Services Drill Championship
on March 21, 2026; and
WHEREAS, in an exemplary display of exacting precision, the Station Camp High
School JROTC Drill Team brought home first-, second-, and third-place honors in various
competitions, along with a second-place overall finish; and
WHEREAS, under the outstanding leadership of Station Camp High School JROTC
instructors Master Sergeant Archie Harville and Colonel Todd Overby, the Station Camp cadets
were inspired to reach their full potential and strive for excellence; and
WHEREAS, known for its discipline, leadership opportunities, and preparation for college
and careers, the JROTC program at Station Camp High School not only prepares students for
future academic and career endeavors but also helps mold them into capable, principled
leaders who will serve their communities and our nation with honor; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate the cadets and
instructors of the Station Camp High School Junior Reserve Officer Training Corps Drill Team
