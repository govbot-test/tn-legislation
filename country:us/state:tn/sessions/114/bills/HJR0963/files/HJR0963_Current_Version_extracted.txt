HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 963
By Cochran
A RESOLUTION to honor the memory of Dr. Julie Aderhold
Goodin of Englewood.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly were greatly saddened to learn of
the untimely passing of Dr. Julie Aderhold Goodin; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Julie Goodin was a beloved member of her community who worked
assiduously to improve the lives of her fellow citizens in numerous capacities; and
WHEREAS, a native of Etowah and a lifelong resident of McMinn County, Dr. Goodin
embodied a deep and abiding commitment to her community, choosing throughout her life to
invest her talents, leadership, and heart in the place she proudly called home; and
WHEREAS, an accomplished student and gifted athlete, Dr. Julie Goodin attended
Mountain View Elementary School and McMinn Central High School, where she distinguished
herself as one of the most celebrated basketball players in the school's history and earned
numerous honors, including All-State, All-American, and Tennessee Miss Basketball; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Goodin continued her athletic and academic pursuits at Clemson
University, where she earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in Secondary Education in 2004 and
enjoyed an illustrious collegiate basketball career; and
WHEREAS, answering a calling to education, she returned to Tennessee and earned a
Master of Arts degree in Instructional Leadership in 2007 and an Education Specialist degree in
Educational Leadership in 2008 from Tennessee Technological University; she later completed
her Doctorate of Educational Leadership in 2024 from Arkansas State University; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Goodin served in many capacities throughout her distinguished career
with McMinn County Schools, including as teacher, coach, mentor teacher, club sponsor, bus
driver, assistant principal, Pre K-2 Curriculum Supervisor and Secondary Supervisor; her

WHEREAS, in June 2025, Dr. Julie Goodin was selected to be the first female Director

WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
remember the bountiful life of this exceptional public servant and human being; now, therefore,
