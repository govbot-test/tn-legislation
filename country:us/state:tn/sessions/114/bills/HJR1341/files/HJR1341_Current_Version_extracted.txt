HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1341
By Grills
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend the Dyersburg High
School Lady Trojans basketball team upon
winning the 2026 TSSAA Division I, Class AAA
state championship.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that this General Assembly should salute those student athletes
who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as true champions of
whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such exemplary group is the Lady Trojans basketball team of
Dyersburg High School, whose members have proven themselves to be the fiercest of
competitors and truly worthy of admiration by winning the 2026 TSSAA Division I, Class AAA
state championship, their second state championship in the past three years; and
WHEREAS, poised for ultimate victory in the Class AAA state tournament at MTSU's
Murphy Center, Dyersburg bested Daniel Boone 46-34 in the quarterfinals and defeated Alcoa
61-53 in the semifinal round to advance to the final; and
WHEREAS, in the title game, the Lady Trojans prevailed over White County by a score
of 54-45 to claim the Gold Ball trophy that signifies a state champion and finish the 2025-2026
campaign with an outstanding 28-6 record; and
WHEREAS, Lady Trojans Kaliyah Lewis, KayKay Strayhorn, and Aerie Quinn were
named to the All-Tournament team and joined by Lady Trojan Chelby Jordan, who was named
Most Valuable Player and made her mark on the state tournament record book, recording 8
steals in the championship game, tying for second all-time, totaling 74 points in the tournament
for tenth all-time, and finishing with 15 steals for the tournament, tying for first all-time; and
WHEREAS, the members of this championship team include seniors D'Zya Currie,
Kathleen Harrell, Lily-Kate Jones, Chelby Jordan, Elizabeth Lemons, and Aerie Quinn; juniors

K'Laya Currie, Makayla Johnson, Kaliyah Lewis, and KayKay Strayhorn; sophomores Bri Parr

WHEREAS, during their four-year career with the Lady Trojans basketball program, the

WHEREAS, Dyersburg's superb performance would not have been possible without the
expert guidance of Head Coach Tim Strayhorn and his assistant coaches, A.J. Adams and
Sherelle Warren, the commitment of manager I'Aunna McElrath, and the unfailing support of the
players' families, their fans, and their community; and
WHEREAS, the members of the Dyersburg girls' basketball team epitomize all that is
good in today's student athletes, as they have achieved a premier level of success in
competition while exhibiting the highest degree of character in all aspects of life; and
WHEREAS, the perseverance, determination, work ethic, and talent of this group of
young women not only made possible a championship but also surely bode well for their future
success; and
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly find it appropriate to acknowledge
and applaud the Dyersburg Lady Trojans for their championship season on the hardwood; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend the Dyersburg High School Lady Trojans
