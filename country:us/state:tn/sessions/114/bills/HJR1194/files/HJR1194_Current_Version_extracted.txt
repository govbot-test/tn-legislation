HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1194
By Reneau
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend FTO Kristen Thomas
upon her receipt of the Chattanooga Police
Department Lifesaving Medal.
WHEREAS, our nation was conceived by individuals who were willing to sacrifice their
personal concerns to ensure the public safety and welfare, and the Volunteer State is especially
proud to be the home of so many valiant individuals who have performed above and beyond the
call of duty in service of public safety; and
WHEREAS, one such individual is FTO Kristen Thomas of the Chattanooga Police
Department (CPD), who was recently awarded the CPD Lifesaving Medal in recognition of her
extraordinary actions in the line of duty; and
WHEREAS, on October 5, 2025, FTO Thomas responded to a pedestrian-struck call on
Gunbarrel Road, where she encountered a chaotic two-vehicle crash scene involving multiple
victims, including one who was mortally injured and another who suffered a catastrophic leg
injury with severe blood loss; and
WHEREAS, with emergency medical personnel attending to the most critical patient and
no additional officers immediately available, FTO Kristen Thomas managed the scene alone,
demonstrating remarkable composure as she assessed victims, coordinated resources, and
maintained control under extremely difficult circumstances; and
WHEREAS, upon observing that one victim was experiencing life-threatening blood loss
due to a near-amputation of her leg, FTO Thomas swiftly applied a tourniquet to stop the
hemorrhaging while providing calm reassurance; and
WHEREAS, medical professionals later confirmed that FTO Thomas's prompt and
decisive actions were instrumental in saving the victim's life, exemplifying the highest standards
of professionalism, courage, and dedication to duty; now, therefore,
HJR1194
016039
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend FTO Kristen Thomas upon her receipt of the
Chattanooga Police Department's Lifesaving Medal and extend to her our sincere appreciation
for her heroic service to the people of Tennessee.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
