Section 1:
Amendment No. 2 to HB1710
Jones J
Signature of Sponsor
by inserting the following new section in SECTION 15:
4-58-114.
Notwithstanding this chapter to the contrary, a public or private homeless shelter,
emergency relief center, or another type of facility that provides shelter, care, food, or
similar services during a declared state of emergency due to a natural disaster is exempt
from the requirements of this chapter to verify the lawful status of an individual for
purposes of receiving public benefits or to report the lawful status of an individual to a
governmental entity or official during such emergency.
