AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 2327* House Bill No. 2264
by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 39, Chapter 17, Part 3, is amended by
adding the following as a new section:
(a) A person commits the offense of interfering with a religious assembly if the
person acts to intentionally obstruct, disturb, or interfere with the activities of the religious
assembly by making an utterance, gesture, or display in a manner that is offensive to the
sensibilities of an ordinary person, either within the place where the meeting is held, or
so near it as to disturb the order and solemnity of the activities.
(b) As used in this section, "religious assembly" means a church, temple,
synagogue, mosque, or other place of worship.
(c) A violation of this section is a Class A misdemeanor.
(d) If conduct that is a violation of this section is also a violation of § 39-14-406,
then the offense may be prosecuted under either, but not both, statutes as provided in §
39-11-109.
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.
