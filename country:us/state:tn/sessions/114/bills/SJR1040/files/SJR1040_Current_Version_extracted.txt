SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1040
By Pody
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Parker Belle Hayes of
Mt. Juliet upon her graduation from the
University of Tennessee, Knoxville.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Parker Belle Hayes is one such exceptional student, who will soon graduate
from the University of Tennessee, Knoxville, with a Bachelor of Arts degree; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Ms. Hayes has maintained an exemplary grade point
average and completed a four-year degree in just two years and nine months; and
WHEREAS, while earning her degree, Parker Hayes was heavily involved in the Phi Mu
sorority and served as Composite Chairman, Work Week Captain, Go Greek Video Committee
member, and Recruitment Day Head; additionally, she served her community by volunteering
numerous hours at Dolly Parton Children's Hospital, where she spent most of her time with
oncology patients and their parents; and
WHEREAS, during her final year of college, she expanded upon her interest in
embroidery by opening her own successful boutique sewing business, Stitched by PB; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Hayes's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well
for her future success; and
WHEREAS, Parker Hayes's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for
her family, her school, and her community; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Hayes's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of the members of this General Assembly; now, therefore,

BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
