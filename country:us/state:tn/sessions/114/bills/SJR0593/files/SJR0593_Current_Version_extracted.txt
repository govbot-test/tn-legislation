SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 593
By Bowling
A RESOLUTION to designate Marlene Edwards Tidwell as "Poet
Laureate Emerita of Tennessee."
WHEREAS, Marlene Edwards Tidwell is an inspirational poet whose writings capture
beauty through the expression of vivid images; and
WHEREAS, Mrs. Tidwell grew up on a farm in rural Tennessee, where she dreamed and
dared to pen the language of her heart to paper; and
WHEREAS, her journey of writing began as a child on the front porch as she gazed
upon the beautiful rural vistas that surrounded her; and
WHEREAS, Mrs. Tidwell believes strongly in the power of the written word and has
devoted her life to bringing the rich and wonderful experience of poetry to others; and
WHEREAS, her inspiring poems reverberate with the timeless resonance of family,
nature and the fragrance of life; and
WHEREAS, she has composed articulate and moving poems that vividly capture the
pride and sense of belonging we all feel as Tennesseans, including Christian, nature, and
inspirational written works; and
WHEREAS, in her book, Nature Sings, a beautiful collection of poems, photography, and
vignettes inspired by nature and its seasons, Mrs. Tidwell captures the beauty and essence of
nature and a wide array of subjects through the expression of vivid images in her words; and
WHEREAS, in 2021, her poem, "My Beloved Tennessee," was named by the 112th
General Assembly as an official State poem of Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, that poem reads:
My thoughts wander on your path again
Captivated by the beauty of rolling hills

WHEREAS, Mrs. Tidwell's poems have been described as "an enlightened collection of
one exceptional woman's ardent expressions, revelations, and impressions; deep-layered words
poured from this soul's well, and the artistry within their assembly"; and
WHEREAS, Mrs. Tidwell is a loving wife, mother, and grandmother and is respected and
admired by her friends and community for her many talents and achievements, and is most
deserving of the commendation of this body; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that in recognition and appreciation of her numerous
contributions to poetry and her other laudable literary endeavors, Marlene Edwards Tidwell is
hereby designated as "Poet Laureate Emerita of Tennessee," a title by which she shall
henceforth be known.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
