HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1604
By Sparks
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Jerry Nash for his
outstanding contributions to children around the
world through the advancement of Chess in
Education.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly take great pleasure in recognizing
individuals who have distinguished themselves through service, leadership, and a steadfast
commitment to improving the lives of others; and
WHEREAS, Jerry Nash has demonstrated exceptional dedication to the intellectual and
personal development of young people through his tireless advocacy of Chess in Education, a
discipline proven to strengthen critical thinking, enhance problem-solving abilities, and promote
thoughtful decision making; and
WHEREAS, through the strategic use of chess as an educational tool, Mr. Nash has
helped foster essential life skills in children, including conflict resolution, patience, discipline,
and the positive reinforcement of self-worth; and
WHEREAS, serving as Chairman of the International Chess Federation's Chess in
Education Committee, Mr. Nash has traveled extensively across the globe, inspiring young
minds and working collaboratively with educators, communities, and organizations to expand
access to meaningful educational opportunities; and
WHEREAS, his international efforts have had a profound and lasting impact,
empowering children from diverse backgrounds to reach their full potential both inside and
outside the classroom; and
WHEREAS, as a proud native of the State of Tennessee, Mr. Nash embodies the very
best of our State's values, reflecting a spirit of service, leadership, and compassion that brings
great credit to his home State; and
HJR1604
018592
- 1 -
WHEREAS, his selfless devotion to future generations stands as a shining example of
the power of volunteerism and the enduring influence of those who dedicate their lives to
uplifting others; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and commend Jerry Nash for his remarkable
contributions to improving the lives of children worldwide through Chess in Education and
extend to him our best wishes for continued success in all his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
