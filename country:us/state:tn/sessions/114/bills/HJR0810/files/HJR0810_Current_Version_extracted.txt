HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 810
By Powell
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend the Wright Middle School
Mariachi Ensemble and Director Maikel
Rodriguez.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should recognize and
celebrate those exceptional individuals and organizations whose contributions enrich the
cultural fabric of our State and uplift the lives of our citizens, especially our youth; and
WHEREAS, the Wright Middle School Mariachi Ensemble, under the leadership of
Director Maikel Rodriguez, has achieved statewide and national recognition for its outstanding
musicianship, perseverance, and cultural pride; and
WHEREAS, in October 2025, this talented group of student musicians had the distinct
honor of serving as the opening act for Grammy Award–winning artist Jason Isbell at the Ryman
Auditorium, a revered venue often referred to as the "Mother Church of Country Music"; and
WHEREAS, this remarkable opportunity represented not only a milestone for the
students and the Wright Middle School community but also a testament to the power of music
education and the enduring strength of cultural heritage; and
WHEREAS, the mariachi ensemble reflects the rich diversity of Metro Nashville Public
Schools, welcoming students from across the globe, including Honduras, Morocco, and Cuba,
and offering them a space to express pride in their identities and traditions through the art of
mariachi; and
WHEREAS, Director Maikel Rodriguez, himself a Cuban immigrant whose path to music
education was forged through resilience and dedication, has revived and transformed Wright
Middle School's mariachi program through tireless mentorship, inclusivity, and high standards of
excellence; and

WHEREAS, through Mr. Rodriguez's leadership, the students have performed at notable
venues such as Nissan Stadium, the Country Music Hall of Fame, and Plaza Mariachi and have
brought the spirit of mariachi to local elementary schools across the city, inspiring the next
generation; and
WHEREAS, the group's performance at the Ryman not only provided a prestigious
platform but also earned the ensemble a $5,000 donation, which will help fund official uniforms
and necessary equipment, further strengthening the program's long-term sustainability; and
WHEREAS, the ensemble's success is rooted in values that transcend the stage: pride
in one's roots, camaraderie, and a shared commitment to community and artistic growth; and
WHEREAS, on this noteworthy occasion, it is most fitting that we honor the hard work
and talent of the Wright Middle School Mariachi Ensemble and Director Maikel Rodriguez; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend the Wright Middle School Mariachi Ensemble and
Director Maikel Rodriguez for their inspiring performance as the opening act for Jason Isbell at
the Ryman Auditorium, applaud their outstanding season of performances, and extend to them
our best wishes for much continued success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
