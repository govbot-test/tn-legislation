Senate Finance, Ways, and Means 2
Amendment No. 3 to SB0676
Watson
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 676* House Bill No. 754
by adding the following as a new subsection (i) in SECTION 2:
(i) The department of commerce and insurance shall, within ninety (90) days of
the effective date of this act, apply to the federal centers for medicare and medicaid
services (CMS) for a waiver pursuant to 45 CFR § 156.111 to allow the department to
add detransition procedures to the state's essential health benefits (EHB) for qualified
health plans (QHPs) offered on the marketplace exchange.
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting subsection (b) in SECTION 2 and substituting instead:
(b)
(1) If an insurance policy that is offered outside of the marketplace
exchange and that is issued, renewed, or amended in this state on or after the
effective date of this act, includes coverage for gender transition procedures,
then the policy must also include coverage for detransition procedures.
(2) Upon approval and implementation of the waiver sought in subsection
(i), if an insurance policy that is offered on the marketplace exchange and that is
issued, renewed, or amended in this state on or after the effective date of the
approved change to the state's EHB, includes coverage for gender transition
procedures, then the policy must also include coverage for detransition
procedures.
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting the word "and" at the end of subdivision (a)(1) in
SECTION 2, deleting the period at the end of subdivision (a)(2) in SECTION 2 and substituting
instead "; and", and adding the following as a new subdivision (a)(3) in SECTION 2:
