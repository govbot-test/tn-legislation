Amendment No. 3 to HB0754
Jones J
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting from the heading to § 68-1-2503 in SECTION 1 the language "Right to public
transparency" and substituting "Patient information protections".
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting § 68-1-2503(a) in SECTION 1 and substituting:
(a) A gender clinic operating in this state shall not report to the department, the
department shall not request from a gender clinic operating in this state, and the
department shall not publish, however obtained, any information regarding a patient
seeking a gender transition procedure, including, but not limited to:
(1) Treatment history;
(2) Mental health diagnosis;
(3) Prescription records;
(4) Surgery information; and
(5) The patient's state and county of residence.
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting subsections (b)-(e) of § 68-1-2503 in SECTION 1 and
redesignating any remaining subsections accordingly.
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting subsection (c) in SECTION 2 and substituting:
(c) An entity providing insurance coverage for detransition procedures in this
state shall not report to the department, the department shall not request from such
entity, and the department shall not publish, however obtained, any information
regarding insurance claims for gender transition procedures, including, but not limited to,
claim information related to:
(1) Treatment history;
