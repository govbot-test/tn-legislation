HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 718
By Clemmons
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend the CSX Santa Train.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly take great pleasure in recognizing
those estimable organizations that have a longstanding tradition of uplifting the children of this
State who are most in need; and
WHEREAS, for eighty-three years, the CSX Santa Train has been one of the nation's
most cherished holiday traditions, bringing joy and hope to the heart of Appalachia; and
WHEREAS, since 1943, the Santa Train has made a 110-mile journey through
Kentucky, Virginia, and Tennessee, with Santa and his elves tossing toys, handing out
backpacks filled with supplies, and spreading Christmas cheer to children and their families; and
WHEREAS, in 2025, the CSX Santa Train provided more than 15,000 toys, 6,000 train
safety books, and 5,000 bags with cold-weather gear to the hundreds of individuals who were
gathered along its route, which spanned from Shelby, Kentucky, to Kingsport, Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, the Santa Train is made possible by devoted volunteers, CSX employees
who spend weeks packing all the backpacks with toys, and community partners, including
Appalachian Power, Food City, and the Kingsport Chamber of Commerce; and
WHEREAS, for more than eight decades, the CSX Santa Train has brought joy, hope,
and Christmas magic to children who might not otherwise receive any gifts for the holiday; and
WHEREAS, it is most fitting that we recognize one of this State's most exemplary
charitable events at this time; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we recognize and commend the CSX Santa Train for its compassionate
