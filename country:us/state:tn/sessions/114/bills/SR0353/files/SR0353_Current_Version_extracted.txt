SENATE RESOLUTION 353
By Oliver
A RESOLUTION to commend Iyana Debro for exemplary service
as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this body is pleased to recognize certain persons
who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment of the legislative
process; and
WHEREAS, Iyana Debro is one such individual, who has distinguished herself in her role
as a legislative intern for Senator Charlane Oliver; her work has resulted in innumerable benefits
for the legislators she assisted and the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, a student at Tennessee State University (TSU), where she is highly
regarded by her peers, Ms. Debro has achieved academic success while pursuing a Bachelor of
Arts degree in Political Science; and
WHEREAS, Iyana Debro has achieved many accomplishments during her time at TSU,
including receiving the Freshman Scholarship and recognition on the Dean's List from 2023 to
2025; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize herself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further her studies, Ms. Debro brought her many abilities
and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2026 session of the One Hundred Fourteenth
General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, throughout the 2026 legislative session, Iyana Debro has surpassed all
criteria that define a devoted and conscientious legislative intern; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Debro has performed her many duties with extraordinary insight and
rare decorum; now, therefore,
SR0353
016099
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby commend Iyana
Debro for outstanding service as a legislative intern for Senator Charlane Oliver and extend to
her our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
- 2 - 016099
