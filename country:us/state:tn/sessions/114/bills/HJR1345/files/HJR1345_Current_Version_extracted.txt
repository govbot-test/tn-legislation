HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1345
By Williams
A RESOLUTION to commend Eleanor Anne Schiller for her
service as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this body is pleased to recognize certain persons
who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment of the legislative
process; and
WHEREAS, Eleanor Anne Schiller is one such individual who has distinguished herself
in her role as a legislative intern for Representative Ryan Williams and the House Finance
Subcommittee; Ms. Schiller's work has resulted in innumerable benefits for the legislators she
assisted and the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, a student at Tennessee Technological University, where she is highly
regarded by her peers, Ms. Schiller is majoring in history and has compiled an impressive
academic record as a Presidential Scholar and a consistent member of the Dean's List; she is
also the president of the Alpha Mu Gamma Honor Society and a member of the Phi Alpha Theta
Honor Society and the Phi Kappa Phi Honor Society; and
WHEREAS, further demonstrating her commitment to scholarship and campus
involvement, Eleanor Schiller has served as treasurer of the History Club and now holds the
position of vice president; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize herself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further her studies, Ms. Schiller brought her many abilities
and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2026 session of the One Hundred Fourteenth
General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, during her tenure as a legislative intern, she has risen to every challenge
presented to her, going above and beyond in the performance of her duties by coordinating

legislative intern for Representative Ryan Williams and the House Finance Subcommittee and
