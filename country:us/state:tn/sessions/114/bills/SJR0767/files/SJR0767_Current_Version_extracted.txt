SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 767
By Seal
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Ayden Hood of Pigeon
Forge High School on being named the Region
1-3A Co-offensive Player of the Year.
WHEREAS, we are pleased to specially honor and commend those successful high
school athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring honor to the State of Tennessee
and serve as exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable athlete is Ayden Hood of Pigeon Forge High School, a
football player of extraordinary talent, skill, and athleticism, who has been named the Region 1-
3A Co-offensive Player of the Year; and
WHEREAS, a powerhouse with the football, Ayden Hood had 1,502 rushing yards this
past season, breaking the state record for most yards rushed by a quarterback in a single game
with 461 in the Tigers' win against Cocke County; and
WHEREAS, he is also now ranked fourteenth in TSSAA history for most points scored
by a single player in a game with forty-four points against Cocke County; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Hood epitomizes all that is good in today's student athlete, as he has
achieved a high level of success in sports while evincing great character both on and off the
field; and
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that we recognize and commend this elite athlete and
outstanding young gentleman; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Ayden Hood of Pigeon
Forge High School on being named the Region 1-3A Co-offensive Player of the Year and
extend to him our best wishes for much continued success in his future endeavors.
