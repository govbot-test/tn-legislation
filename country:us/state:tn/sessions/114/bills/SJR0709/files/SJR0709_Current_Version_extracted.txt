SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 709
By Johnson
A RESOLUTION to congratulate Lauren Banovac of Brentwood
High School on winning the 2025 TSSAA Class
AAA Girls' Cross Country Individual
Championship.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly are pleased to specially honor and
commend those successful high school athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring
honor to Tennessee and serve as exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable athlete is Lauren Banovac of Brentwood High School,
who won the 2025 TSSAA Class AAA Girls' Individual State Cross Country Championship; and
WHEREAS, a senior at Brentwood High School, Ms. Banovac secured her first State title
with a personal best time of 17:17.15, nearly ten seconds ahead of the runner-up; and
WHEREAS, Lauren Banovac led the Lady Bruins to their sixth consecutive team State
title at the 2025 meet; in past State meets, she finished fifth in 2024, eighth in 2023, and tenth in
2022; and
WHEREAS, her display of athleticism and ambition surely bodes well for her future
success as a student athlete at Georgetown College; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Banovac's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for
her family, her school, and her community; and
WHEREAS, Lauren Banovac's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of our
respect, admiration, and commendation; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Lauren Banovac of
Brentwood High School on winning the 2025 TSSAA Class AAA Girls' Cross Country Individual
