HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1213
By Doggett
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend the Richland High
School Lady Raiders basketball team upon
winning the 2026 TSSAA Division I, Class A
state championship.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that this General Assembly should salute those student athletes
who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as true champions of
whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such exemplary group is the Lady Raiders girls' basketball team of
Richland High School, whose members have proven themselves to be the fiercest of
competitors and truly worthy of admiration by winning the 2026 TSSAA Division I, Class A state
championship; and
WHEREAS, in the championship game, the Lady Raiders prevailed over Middleton in a
heroic struggle that will be remembered forever in the Richland community, winning by a score
of 34-33 to claim the Gold Ball trophy that signifies a state champion and finish their 2025-2026
campaign with an outstanding 28-3 record; and
WHEREAS, this year marks the first state championship in program history for the
Richland Lady Raiders, with their victory in the "Glass House" in Murfreesboro following on the
heels of their District 9A and Region 5A championships and their Sectional victory that punched
their ticket to the state tournament; and
WHEREAS, Lady Raiders Baylee Hollis, Bailey Blair, Alizabeth Gordon, and Dannie
Lynn Galloway were named to the Class A All-State Tournament team, with Ms. Blair being
named the Most Valuable Player; and
WHEREAS, no strangers to awards and accolades, all five seniors on the Richland Lady
Raiders girls' basketball team were named to the Basketball Coaches Association of Tennessee

WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly find it appropriate to acknowledge
and applaud the Richland Lady Raiders for their historic season on the hardwood; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate the players, coaching staff, team support staff,
administrators, cheerleaders, and fans of the Richland High School Lady Raiders girls'
basketball team upon winning the 2026 TSSAA Division I, Class A state championship, and
extend to them our best wishes for every continued success in all their future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
