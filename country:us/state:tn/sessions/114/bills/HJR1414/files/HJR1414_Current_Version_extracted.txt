HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1414
By Capley
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Riley Baker, 2026 Top
Ten Senior at Frank Hughes School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pay tribute to
those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to academic
excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Riley Baker is one such exceptional student, who was recently named a
Top Ten Senior of the 2026 graduating class at Frank Hughes School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Mr. Baker has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in his remarkable high school career, Riley Baker has earned an exemplary
grade point average and participated in numerous extracurricular activities; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Baker's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well
for his future success; and
WHEREAS, Riley Baker's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for
his family, his school, and his community; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Baker's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of our respect,
admiration, and commendation; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor Riley Baker of Frank Hughes School on
