SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1110
By Yager
A RESOLUTION to honor Trent Heath upon his exemplary service
to Senator Ken Yager and the Tennessee
General Assembly.
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that we should honor those valuable legislative employees
who have performed with unwavering devotion and enthusiasm throughout their tenure with the
General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, one such esteemed individual is Trent Heath, the former Executive
Legislative Assistant to Senator Ken Yager, who rendered exemplary service in the office of the
Senate Republican Caucus from 2024 to 2025; and
WHEREAS, a resident of Dickson, Trent Heath earned his Bachelor of Criminal Justice
and Associate of Science in Criminal Justice degrees from Austin Peay State University and
completed an Associate of Science University Parallel Pre-Law degree from Nashville State
Community College; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Heath began his legislative employment as an intern for Senator Mike
Bell in January 2022 during the 112th General Assembly, prior to being hired as a Bill Clerk by
the Office of the Senate Clerk in September 2022; and
WHEREAS, in December 2023, Trent Heath was named the Assistant Research Analyst
for the Senate Judiciary Committee, in which position he was responsible for redlining bills for
the committee, creating talking points, and researching topics as requested by the chair,
Senator Todd Gardenhire; and
WHEREAS, having taken his leave of the General Assembly as an employee, Mr. Heath
now serves as the Government Relations Coordinator for Tennesseans for Student Success;
and
SJR1110
013549
- 1 -
WHEREAS, Trent Heath has demonstrated the utmost professionalism, ability, and
integrity, earning the respect and admiration of his colleagues and the members of the General
Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Trent Heath for his
exemplary service to Senator Ken Yager and the Tennessee General Assembly and extend to
him our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
