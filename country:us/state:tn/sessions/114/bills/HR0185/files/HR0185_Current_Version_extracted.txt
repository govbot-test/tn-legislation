HOUSE RESOLUTION 185
By Love
A RESOLUTION to honor Reverend Dr. William Seth Martin,
senior pastor-elect of Lake Providence Baptist
Church in Nashville.
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that we should honor those individuals who have answered
a divine call to the ministry and who have served with dedication, vigor, and enthusiasm to
perform God's work for their congregations and their communities; and
WHEREAS, Reverend Dr. William Seth Martin is one such committed minister of the
Gospel, who is the senior pastor-elect at Lake Providence Baptist Church in Nashville; and
WHEREAS, prior to his installation at Lake Providence Baptist Church, Dr. William Seth
Martin served as a pastor at Sanctuary Covenant Church and was the founding pastor of The
Brook Community Church; and
WHEREAS, a native of Little Rock, Arkansas, Dr. Martin is the son of Pastor Kenneth
and Laura Martin and was licensed to preach at Saint Mark Baptist Church under the leadership
of Dr. Phillip Pointer; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Martin holds a Bachelor of Arts degree in Journalism from Hampton
University, a Master of Ministry degree from Southwestern Baptist Theological Seminary, and a
Doctor of Ministry degree from United Theological Seminary; and
WHEREAS, he has served in a variety of ministry roles, including Director of Ministries
at Victory Cathedral Worship Center in Chicago, Illinois; Youth Pastor at New Hope Baptist
Church in Arkansas; and Chapel Assistant Lead at Hampton University Memorial Church in
Virginia; and
WHEREAS, a sought-after preacher and speaker, Dr. Martin is the author of Faith
Moves: Leaving Comfort for Your Calling, and his ministry and insights have been featured on
CNN, MSNBC, and The Washington Post; and

WHEREAS, throughout his many worthy endeavors, Dr. Martin has been blessed with
the love and support of his wife, Hannah Monique Martin, and their three children, Zoe
Elizabeth, Elena Simone, and William Seth, Jr.; and
WHEREAS, a true shepherd of the flock, Dr. Martin has an abiding love for the ministry
and continues to demonstrate excellence in his calling as a man of God; and
WHEREAS, Reverend Dr. William Seth Martin's many contributions to his community
and this State are deeply appreciated, and it is wholly fitting that he be recognized on this
special occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we commend
and congratulate Reverend Dr. William Seth Martin upon his installation as senior pastor-elect
at Lake Providence Baptist Church in Nashville, extending our best wishes for his success and
happiness in all future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
