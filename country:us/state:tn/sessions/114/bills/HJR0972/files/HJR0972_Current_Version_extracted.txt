HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 972
By Reneau
A RESOLUTION to recognize and congratulate William A. and
Vickie S. Thomas upon the occasion of their
fiftieth wedding anniversary.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly take great pleasure in paying tribute
to those successful marriages that have stood the test of time to serve as exemplars for present
and future generations; and
WHEREAS, William A. and Vickie S. Thomas were joined in holy matrimony in 1976,
and since then, this outstanding couple has faced the world as one, basking in the glow of their
love for each other during both life's joys and sorrows; and
WHEREAS, Willie and Vickie Thomas are highly respected citizens of the Ooltewah
community, as they always endeavor to treat others as they would like to be treated; and
WHEREAS, a leader in his community, Willie Thomas has served the local Republican
Party through the Pachyderm Club for the past four years, meeting and building relationships
with senators, representatives, judges, and clerks; and
WHEREAS, Vickie Thomas remains active with odd jobs, including home health care;
she also contracted with a service for home photographs, an endeavor that has taken her
throughout the county and to Georgia; and
WHEREAS, deeply devoted to their family and family values, Willie and Vickie Thomas
have been blessed with a loving family, including their children and a host of cherished and
talented grandchildren; and
WHEREAS, Willie and Vickie Thomas enjoy traveling in their leisure time and enjoyed a
trip to Italy in 2024; more recently, the Thomases held an annual family cookout, with family
attending from East Tennessee, Memphis, and Georgia; and

WHEREAS, for five decades, this blessed union has grown and prospered as an
expression of time-honored family values and selfless love; and
WHEREAS, Willie and Vickie Thomas's family members and friends honor this fiftieth
wedding anniversary, celebrating the abundant blessings given the Thomases through their
marriage and cherishing these two true partners in life who have served as inspirations to all
who know them as they continue to enjoy the fruits of the seeds they have sown; and
WHEREAS, marriages such as the Thomas's truly represent the best and brightest of
our society, as they remind us of the intrinsic value of continuity and commitment in this ever-
changing world; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and congratulate William A. and Vickie S. Thomas on the
celebration of their fiftieth wedding anniversary and extend to them our warmest wishes for
continued happiness in the years to come.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
