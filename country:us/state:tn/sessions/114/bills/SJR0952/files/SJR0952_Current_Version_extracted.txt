SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 952
By Gardenhire
A RESOLUTION to commend Alitrise Renate Soderstrom
Theodoriches for exemplary service as a
legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this General Assembly is pleased to recognize
certain persons who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment
of the legislative process; and
WHEREAS, Alitrise Renate Soderstrom Theodoriches is one such individual, who has
distinguished herself in her role as a legislative intern for Senator Todd Gardenhire; Ms.
Theodoriches's work has resulted in innumerable benefits for the legislators she assisted and
the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Theodoriches is originally from Oregon, but she has built deep roots in
her now beloved home state of Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, a student at Lipscomb University, where she is highly regarded by her
peers, Ms. Theodoriches has achieved academic success studying political science and pre-law
while participating in numerous extracurricular activities and maintaining honor-roll distinction
every semester; and
WHEREAS, Alitrise Theodoriches previously supported Lipscomb University as the
Government, Community Relations and Strategic Partnerships Intern, where she monitored
legislative developments and tracked bill progression during the first year with the One Hundred
Fourteenth General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, she also participated in the Lipscomb Initiative for Education at the Debra K.
Johnson Rehabilitation Center, engaging in coursework centered on justice, community, and
transformation alongside incarcerated women; and

WHEREAS, Ms. Theodoriches takes pride in her research, having previously completed
an independent study examining disparities in church participation in the Paycheck Protection
Program during COVID-19 through interviews and quantitative data analysis; and
WHEREAS, outside of her internship, Alitrise Theodoriches enjoys hiking, roller skating,
a good fun fact, a cup of coffee, traveling home to the Pacific Northwest, and serving the Lord
with all her heart; and
WHEREAS, she is genuinely committed to supporting and uplifting children and youth,
shaped by the resilience she developed through early life challenges; she hopes to continue
serving within state and local government while furthering her education through graduate
studies and, ultimately, law school; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize herself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further her studies, Alitrise Theodoriches brought her many
abilities and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2026 session of the One Hundred
Fourteenth General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, throughout the 2026 legislative session, Ms. Theodoriches has surpassed
all criteria that define a devoted and conscientious legislative intern; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Theodoriches has performed her many duties with extraordinary insight
and rare decorum; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby commend Alitrise Renate Soderstrom
Theodoriches for outstanding service as a legislative intern for Senator Todd Gardenhire and
extend to her our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
