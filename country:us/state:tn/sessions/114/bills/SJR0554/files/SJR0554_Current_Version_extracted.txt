SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 554
By Roberts
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Dr. Cathy Beck, director
of Cheatham County Schools, on the occasion
of her retirement.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we specially recognize, particularly on the occasion of their
retirement, those outstanding school administrators who have devoted their careers to providing
the highest-quality education for Tennessee's students; and
WHEREAS, one such exceptional individual is Dr. Cathy Beck, who, after a
distinguished career as an educator and administrator, took her much-deserved retirement from
the Cheatham County school system on June 30, 2025; and
WHEREAS, after serving as an assistant director of schools in Colorado, Dr. Beck
relocated to Tennessee in 2017 to come on board at Cheatham County Schools, which had
seen a twenty-year stretch of instability, with multiple directors serving in the position an
average of two years; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Cathy Beck accomplished much and navigated several challenges in
her tenure; the district achieved its accreditation and the Unified Champion District designation
from the Special Olympics, as well as being named a Best for All district by the Tennessee
Department of Education; and
WHEREAS, she also helped the district navigate through several trying times, including
the COVID-19 pandemic, all while making sure the voices of parents, students, and teachers
were heard and that everyone had the tools necessary to succeed; and
WHEREAS, during Dr. Beck's tenure as director of schools, she and Pickett County's
teachers and staff have worked hard to ensure that every student entering a classroom across
