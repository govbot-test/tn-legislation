House State & Local Government 1
Amendment No. 1 to HB1537
Crawford
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 1811 House Bill No. 1537*
by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting instead:
SECTION 1.
(a) The Tennessee advisory commission on intergovernmental relations (TACIR)
shall conduct a study and prepare a report on recommendations regarding the
qualifications for assessors of property and whether a board could be created to review
and certify the qualifications of candidates for assessors of property.
(b) The study and report must examine the current qualifications for assessors of
property, the qualifications of assessors of property in surrounding states, the minimum
experience and education required to ensure the successful completion of the duties of
an assessor of property, the legality and efficacy of creating an assessor of property
certification board, and any other topics related to assessors of property TACIR finds
relevant.
(c) All appropriate state departments and agencies shall provide assistance to
TACIR in connection with the study required by subsection (a).
(d) On or before January 15, 2027, TACIR shall report its findings and
recommendations, including any proposed legislation, to the chair of the committee of
the house of representatives having jurisdiction over local government matters, the chair
of the state and local government committee of the senate, and the legislative librarian.
(e) It is the legislative intent that this study must be conducted from TACIR's
existing resources.
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
