HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1191
By Reneau
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Clifford and Carol
Ackerson for their receipt of the Chattanooga
Police Department Citizens Police Academy
Alumni Association Member of the Year award.
WHEREAS, our nation was conceived by individuals who were willing to sacrifice their
personal concerns to ensure the public safety and welfare, and the Volunteer State is especially
proud to be the home of so many valiant individuals who have performed above and beyond the
call of duty in service of public safety; and
WHEREAS, Clifford and Carol Ackerson are two such individuals, who were recently
honored with the Chattanooga Police Department (CPD) Citizens Police Academy Alumni
Association (CPAAA) Member of the Year award, in recognition of their outstanding involvement
with the Chattanooga Police Department; and
WHEREAS, Mr. and Mrs. Ackerson graduated from the Citizens Police Academy in the
spring of 2017; immediately following graduation, they joined the CPAAA and have remained
active, engaged members ever since; and
WHEREAS, the Ackersons consistently attend monthly meetings, volunteer at numerous
Chattanooga Police Department events, and represent the organization with enthusiasm,
professionalism, and a positive attitude; and
WHEREAS, whenever the CPD issues a call for volunteers, Mr. and Mrs. Ackerson
reliably step forward, their welcoming demeanor and great sense of humor making them a
pleasure to work alongside; and
WHEREAS, Clifford and Carol Ackerson have contributed greatly to the Chattanooga
Police Department through their continued commitment, dedication, and support; now,
therefore,
HJR1191
016016
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Clifford and Carol Ackerson upon their receipt of
the CPAAA Member of the Year award from the Chattanooga Police Department.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
- 2 - 016016
