SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 862
By Crowe
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Gina Harjani upon being
named the winner of the National Oratorical
Contest.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly take great pleasure in recognizing
those outstanding young people who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment
to excellence, have distinguished themselves in national competition; and
WHEREAS, Gina Harjani is one such exemplary individual, who was recently selected
as the winner of the American Legion's National Oratorical Contest, a program designed to
develop a deeper knowledge and appreciation of the United States Constitution among high
school students; and
WHEREAS, a graduate of Gatlinburg-Pittman High School, Ms. Harjani was selected as
Valedictorian of her class, achieving a perfect 4.0 grade point average and demonstrating a
steadfast commitment to academic excellence; and
WHEREAS, she is currently enrolled as a freshman at Vanderbilt University on a full
scholarship, where she continues to pursue her educational goals; and
WHEREAS, Gina Harjani has demonstrated a strong commitment to civic engagement
through her A.C.C.E.P.T. initiative (Advocating for constitutional, civic, and ethical protection in
technology), which she has introduced to the director and chairman of the National Americanism
Commission, Tennessee's U.S. Senators and the 1st District Representative, and the Tennessee
General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, as a past attendee and junior counselor of the American Legion Auxiliary
Girls State program, she continues to encourage student participation by visiting high schools
across Tennessee to promote both the Girls State and Boys State programs; and

WHEREAS, Ms. Harjani's many accomplishments and her dedication to service are a
source of pride to her family, her community, and this State; and
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to pause in our deliberations to recognize Gina
Harjani for her dedication to excellence in public speaking, her outstanding academic
accomplishments, and her commitment to civic engagement and leadership; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Gina Harjani upon being
named the winner of the National Oratorical Contest, applaud her many achievements, and
extend to her our best wishes for every continued success in her future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
