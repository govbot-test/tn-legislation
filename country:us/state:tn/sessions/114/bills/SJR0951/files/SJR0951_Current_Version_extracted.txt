SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 951
By Yarbro
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Molly Foster on her
selection as an Extraordinary Educator.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should pause to specially recognize those outstanding
teachers who have devoted their careers to providing the highest-quality education for
Tennessee's students; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable educator is Molly Foster, who has distinguished herself
as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, Mrs. Foster was recently named an Extraordinary Educator by Curriculum
Associates, a most befitting honor that recognizes the positive influence she has on so many
young lives; and
WHEREAS, the Curriculum Associates Extraordinary Educators program establishes a
tradition of annually celebrating best-in-class teachers who meet or exceed established criteria
and who receive recognition locally and nationally for their accomplishments; and
WHEREAS, the annual program celebrates and connects exemplary teachers from
around the country, and those selected for this honor represent the very best of the teaching
profession; and
WHEREAS, Mrs. Foster earned a bachelor's degree in elementary education from
Mississippi State University and obtained a master's degree in literacy from Lipscomb
University; and
WHEREAS, Molly Foster has taught first through fourth grades across all subject areas
and currently serves as a third-grade teacher at Franklin Elementary School in the Franklin
Special School District in Franklin; and

WHEREAS, in her seventh year of teaching, Mrs. Foster also serves as a Model
Classroom Teacher and as a member of the Writing Revolution team, helping lead professional
learning focused on strengthening writing instruction; and
WHEREAS, during the 2024–2025 school year, the students in Mrs. Foster's classroom
demonstrated exceptional academic growth on the i-Ready Diagnostic and the TCAP
assessments, surpassing local and State averages in both reading and mathematics; and
WHEREAS, in addition to her professional accomplishments, Molly Foster enjoys the
love and support of her husband, Garrett, and their son, Sonny, and in her leisure time, she
enjoys crafting, sewing, and organizing; and
WHEREAS, Mrs. Foster truly epitomizes the dedicated teacher who is wholly committed
to the highest ideals of public service and quality education for all, and she should be specially
recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Molly Foster on her
selection as a Curriculum Associates Extraordinary Educator, commend her exceptional service
to the children of Tennessee, salute her professional expertise and personal excellence, and
wish her all the best in her future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
