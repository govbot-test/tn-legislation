SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 563
By Campbell
A RESOLUTION to recognize and commend the parent coalition
of Metro Nashville Public Schools for its $5
million fundraising project to enhance school
facilities at Percy Priest, Lakeview, and Paragon
Mills elementary schools.
WHEREAS, parents and guardians are vital to the academic success of young students
in their charge, and Tennessee is proud to be the home of so many dedicated, resourceful
individuals who greatly improve student outcomes and well-being; and
WHEREAS, a coalition of Nashville parents recently partnered with Metro Nashville
Public Schools (MNPS) to raise $5 million toward upgrading existing facilities and building new
ones, a feat that speaks to the power of community engagement and philanthropy in enhancing
educational spaces; and
WHEREAS, the initiative was spearheaded by Shannon Martin and Katie Agnew, both
parents at Percy Priest Elementary School, which was in need of a multipurpose area suited for
the modern demands of schooling; and
WHEREAS, this need sparked a conversation about not just one school but about
expanding this vision to benefit the entire community, and sights were set on a project to
enhance gymnasiums at three elementary schools—Percy Priest, Lakeview, and Paragon
Mills—that MNPS was already extensively renovating or rebuilding; and
WHEREAS, recognizing the importance of these spaces where children not only engage
in physical activity but also come together for community events, the parents set a goal that
seemed as immense as it was crucial: to raise over $5 million to bring these visions to life; and
WHEREAS, through the PENCIL Foundation's support and the unwavering dedication of
many local citizens, MNPS parents rallied together to meet the fundraising target in short order,
within only sixty-seven days; and

WHEREAS, these new gymnasiums and multipurpose spaces are not just investments
in infrastructure; they are investments in the future of MNPS children. They will serve as hubs
for learning, sports, and community events, providing environments where every child can
thrive; and
WHEREAS, Shannon Martin, Katie Agnew, and their fellow MNPS parents proved
themselves resourceful, centering the well-being of not only their own children but that of
students throughout the district; and
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we honor these enterprising MNPS parents for their
inspirational actions; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we recognize and commend Shannon Martin, Katie
Agnew, and their fellow Metro Nashville Public Schools parents for their $5 million fundraising
efforts toward enhancing and expanding school facilities at Percy Priest, Lakeview, and
Paragon Mills elementary schools, expressing our gratitude for their commitment to the students
of this State.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
