SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 828
By Seal
A RESOLUTION to honor the Rush Strong girls' basketball team
upon finishing third in the 2025 TSSAA Middle
School Class A state championship tournament.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that this General Assembly should salute those student athletes
who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as true champions of
whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such exemplary group of young ladies is the Lady Plainsmen girls'
basketball team of Rush Strong Middle School in Jefferson County, whose members have
proven themselves to be the fiercest of competitors and truly worthy of admiration by finishing
third in the 2025 TSSAA Middle School Class A state championship; and
WHEREAS, Rush Strong scored a basket with only thirteen seconds remaining in the
game to defeat Rogersville City 38-37 and claim the Region 1-A championship; and
WHEREAS, in the sectional round, Rush Strong bested Midway 46-43 to earn a berth in
the state tournament; and
WHEREAS, the Lady Plainsmen finished a strong third in the state tournament by
thrashing Adamsville 55-33 in the consolation game; and
WHEREAS, the Rush Strong coaches displayed strategic leadership, and their ability to
adapt to every challenge was crucial in ultimately leading the Lady Plainsmen to the state
tournament; and
WHEREAS, the Lady Plainsmen were fortunate to enjoy the unfailing support of their
families, fans, fellow students, and the entire community; and
WHEREAS, the members of the Rush Strong Middle School girls' basketball team
epitomize all that is good in today's student athletes, as they have achieved a premier level of
success in competition while exhibiting the highest degree of character in all aspects of life; and

WHEREAS, Rush Strong's success was built on a foundation of unity and a shared goal,
and the team chemistry among the players was evident in every game, as they treated the Lady
Plainsmen program as a sisterhood; and
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to acknowledge and applaud the Rush Strong Middle
School lady hoopsters for their outstanding season on the hardwood; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate the coaches and players
of the Rush Strong girls' basketball team upon finishing third in the 2025 TSSAA Middle School
Class A state championship tournament and extend to them our best wishes for every continued
success in all their future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
