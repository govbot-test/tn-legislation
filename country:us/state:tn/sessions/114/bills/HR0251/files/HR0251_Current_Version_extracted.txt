HOUSE RESOLUTION 251
By McCalmon
A RESOLUTION to honor Robert Malloy for his promotion to 1st
Degree Black Belt in Kajukenbo.
WHEREAS, the members of this legislative body are pleased to specially honor and
commend those citizens who, through their unwavering dedication and unflagging commitment
to excellence, have distinguished themselves as extraordinary athletes of whom we can all be
proud; and
WHEREAS, Robert Malloy earned promotion to 1st Degree Black Belt in Kajukenbo and
the accompanying title of "Sibak" on May 31, 2025, after relentless training and supreme
devotion to this discipline; and
WHEREAS, an avid practitioner of the martial arts, Mr. Malloy also holds a Black Belt in
Taekwondo; and
WHEREAS, a retired U.S. Army combat medic, Mr. Malloy is treasured as a dear friend,
a proud father and husband, and an adept practitioner of the martial arts; and
WHEREAS, the Williamson County martial arts community is better because of Robert
Malloy, who epitomizes mastery and loyalty in all his endeavors; and
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we recognize this outstanding veteran for attaining the
pinnacle of Kajukenbo; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and
congratulate Robert Malloy for his promotion to 1st Degree Black Belt in Kajukenbo and the
accompanying title of "Sibak" and wish him much continued success in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
