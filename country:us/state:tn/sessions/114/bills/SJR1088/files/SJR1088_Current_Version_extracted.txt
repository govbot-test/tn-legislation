SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1088
By Lowe
A RESOLUTION to honor Howard Thompson on the occasion of
his retirement.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
specially recognize those estimable public servants whose lives exemplify dedicated service,
integrity, and commitment to the people of Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, Howard Thompson has rendered exemplary service to the citizens of
Bradley County, serving as a county commissioner for an extraordinary thirty years, during
which time he demonstrated steadfast leadership, sound judgment, and an unwavering
commitment to the public good; and
WHEREAS, after three decades and eight election wins, Mr. Thompson recently
announced that he will not seek reelection to serve as county commissioner for Bradley
County's 4th District, bringing to a close a record characterized by capable leadership and
guidance and a keen ability to solve problems at hand; and
WHEREAS, he will continue to serve the constituents of District 4 until the end of his
elected term, on September 1, 2026; and
WHEREAS, throughout his time as county commissioner, Howard Thompson has
embodied the highest ideals of public service, earning the respect, gratitude, and admiration of
colleagues and the citizens he has served; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Thompson is wholly committed to the noble precepts of public service
that have earned Tennessee recognition as the Volunteer State, and he should be specially
recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
