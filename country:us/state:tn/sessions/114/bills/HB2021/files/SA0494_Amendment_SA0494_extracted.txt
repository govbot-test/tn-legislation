AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 2358 House Bill No. 2021*
by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 63-3-101(a), is amended by deleting
"soft tissue structures extending no higher than the distal tibial metaphyseal flair" and
substituting "only the soft tissues of the lower leg distal to the tibial tuberosity".
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
