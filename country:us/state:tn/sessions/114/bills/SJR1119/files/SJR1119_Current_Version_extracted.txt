SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1119
By Lowe
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend the Walker Valley High
School Mustangs boys' basketball team upon its
historic 2025-2026 season.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that this General Assembly should salute those student athletes
who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as true champions of
whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such exemplary group of lads is the Walker Valley High School
Mustangs boys' basketball team, whose members have proven themselves to be the fiercest of
competitors and truly worthy of admiration by posting the most successful season in program
history; and
WHEREAS, in the 2025-2026 season, the Walker Valley Mustangs compiled a record of
31-4, making it all the way to the championship game of the TSSAA Division I, Class 4A state
tournament at the Murphy Center in Murfreesboro for the first time; and
WHEREAS, a true display of grit, effort, and brotherhood, the Mustangs' superb
performance would not have been possible without the stellar leadership of Head Coach Will
Campbell and the unfailing support of their families, their fans, and their community; and
WHEREAS, the members of the Walker Valley boys' basketball team epitomize all that is
good in today's student athletes, as they have achieved a premier level of success in
competition while exhibiting the highest degree of character in all aspects of life; and
WHEREAS, the perseverance, determination, work ethic, and talent of this group of
young men not only made possible an outstanding season but also surely bode well for their
future success; and

WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly find it appropriate to acknowledge
and applaud the Walker Valley Mustangs for their outstanding season on the hardwood; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend the members and coaches
of the Walker Valley High School Mustangs boys' basketball team upon their historic 2025-2026
season and extend to them our best wishes for every continued success in all their future
endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
