HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1011
By Howell
A RESOLUTION to recognize Donna Alley Simpson for her
honorable and astute service to the people of
Tennessee as Bradley County Clerk.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
specially recognize those estimable public servants who have dedicated themselves to
improving the quality of life for their fellow citizens; and
WHEREAS, longtime Bradley County Clerk Donna Alley Simpson announced that she
will be retiring at the end of her current term on August 31, 2026, after rendering nearly thirty-
nine years of devoted service to the citizens of Bradley County; and
WHEREAS, throughout her exemplary twenty-eight-year tenure as Bradley County
Clerk, Ms. Simpson has carried out her responsibilities with diligence and integrity; her
remarkable success as a public servant and civic leader is directly attributable to her reliance on
the time-honored values of hard work and common sense and her uncommon ability to work
well with people from all walks of life; and
WHEREAS, a native of Bradley County, Ms. Simpson graduated from Bradley High
School and Cleveland State Community College; she received the designation of Certified
Public Administrator from the University of Tennessee in 2000; and
WHEREAS, she served with acumen and alacrity as president of the Tennessee County
Clerks Association, which named her County Clerk of the Year in 2006; she also served as the
president of the County Officials Association of Tennessee, which named her Outstanding
County Official in 2011; and

WHEREAS, highly involved in her community, Ms. Simpson also served as a volunteer
for United Way and the Chamber of Commerce, and she is on the board of the Art is Here
Partnership; and
WHEREAS, a strong advocate for the citizens of Bradley County, Donna Alley Simpson
has compiled a singular record of success during her exemplary public service career, and she
should be specially recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby commend Donna Alley Simpson for her honorable and astute
service to the people of Tennessee as Bradley County Clerk, salute her unswerving
commitment to living the examined life with courage and conviction, and extend to her our
heartfelt wishes for a happy and fulfilling retirement and every continued success in her future
endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
