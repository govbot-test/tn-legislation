HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1334
By Terry
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Anthony Griffin for his
service to the Tennessee House of
Representatives and his exemplary leadership
and academic achievements.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly are pleased to recognize those
outstanding young individuals who have distinguished themselves through their commitment to
public service, leadership, and academic excellence; and
WHEREAS, Anthony Griffin has demonstrated exceptional dedication to public service
through his work as an intern in the Tennessee House of Representatives for Chairman Bryan
Terry; and
WHEREAS, during his tenure, Mr. Griffin staffed the House Health Committee, attended
leadership meetings, and monitored legislation and public policy developments, gaining
valuable experience in the legislative process; and
WHEREAS, he conducted policy research and assisted with drafting, proofreading, and
tracking legislation and committee materials, providing meaningful contributions to the work of
the committee and the office of Chairman Terry; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Griffin supported legislative operations through constituent services,
scheduling, correspondence management, and daily office coordination, demonstrating
professionalism and strong organizational skills; and
WHEREAS, in addition to his legislative service, Mr. Griffin served as Lead Canvasser
for Representative John Gillespie's reelection campaign, where he organized voter outreach
efforts, trained volunteers, and contributed to increased voter engagement; and
WHEREAS, a proven leader among his peers, Mr. Griffin served as Head Delegate in
the Tennessee Intercollegiate State Legislature, where he led a delegation of more than twenty
students and mentored them in legislative drafting and debate; and

WHEREAS, he was elected Speaker of the Senate within the Tennessee Intercollegiate
State Legislature, presiding over sessions, enforcing parliamentary procedure, and coordinating
legislative operations with skill and integrity; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Griffin has also demonstrated exceptional leadership within Sigma Phi
Epsilon at the University of Memphis, serving as Vice President of Finance and Vice President
of Recruitment, where he strengthened financial operations and significantly increased chapter
membership; and
WHEREAS, his strong work ethic is further reflected in his experience in the service
industry, where he consistently delivered high-quality performance in a fast-paced environment;
and
WHEREAS, Mr. Griffin is pursuing a Bachelor of Arts degree in Political Science and
History at the University of Memphis with a minor in Legal Thought and Liberal Arts, preparing
for a future in public service and leadership; and
WHEREAS, Anthony Griffin exemplifies the highest standards of integrity, leadership,
and dedication, bringing great credit to himself, his family, his university, and the State of
Tennessee; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Anthony Griffin for his outstanding service as an
intern in the Tennessee House of Representatives and extend to him our best wishes for
continued success in all his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
