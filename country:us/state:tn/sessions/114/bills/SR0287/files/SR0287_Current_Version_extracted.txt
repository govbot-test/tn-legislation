Section 1:
SENATE RESOLUTION 287
By Johnson
A RESOLUTION to confirm the appointment of Matt Herriman of
Murfreesboro to the Tennessee board of
regents.
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-8-101, provides for the creation of
the Tennessee board of regents; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-8-201, provides that the governor
shall appoint ten members to the Tennessee board of regents; and
WHEREAS, Governor Bill Lee has appointed Matt Herriman of Murfreesboro to the
Tennessee board of regents as the member representing the 4th Congressional District to a
term that will expire on June 30, 2028; and
WHEREAS, an accomplished individual, Matt Herriman is a real estate broker with
Compass RE and a member of The Recurve Team, where he focuses on land, development,
and commercial real estate across Middle Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Herriman has previously served as a state director for the National Rifle
Association's Institute for Legislative Action, a legislative liaison for Governor Bill Lee, a political
director for the Tennessee Senate Republican Caucus, and as a director of external affairs and
development for Noble Education Initiative; and
WHEREAS, a native of West Tennessee, Mr. Herriman earned his bachelor's degree
from the University of Tennessee, Knoxville; and
WHEREAS, Matt Herriman is a person of integrity and an active citizen whose
benevolence, acumen, and strong commitment to public service have helped to enhance the
well-being of this State and its citizens; and
SR0287
011595
- 1 -
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-8-201, provides that each member
appointed by the governor to the Tennessee board of regents shall be confirmed by the senate;
now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that this body does hereby confirm
the appointment of Matt Herriman to the Tennessee board of regents for a term that will expire
on June 30, 2028.
