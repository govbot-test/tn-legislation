SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 863
By Crowe
A RESOLUTION to recognize and honor Cooper Castle for his
election as the American Legion Tennessee
Boys State governor.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should recognize and
commend those emerging young leaders who have demonstrated a keen interest in citizenship
and government; and
WHEREAS, in May 2025, Cooper Castle of Washington County participated in
Tennessee Boys State, a weeklong program promoting civic engagement and government, held
in Nashville; and
WHEREAS, throughout this immersive educational experience, more than 300 delegates
from across Tennessee convened to explore the workings of state and local government,
enhance their leadership skills, and engage in meaningful public service; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Castle participated in a mock election during Boys State, successfully
running for governor; upon election to this position, he went through a rigorous interview
process to earn the opportunity to participate in American Legion Boys Nation; and
WHEREAS, Cooper Castle and only one other student from Tennessee traveled to our
nation's capital for Boys Nation in July 2025 as "senators," drafting a bill and resolution as part
of the experience; and
WHEREAS, a devout Christian and senior homeschool student, Mr. Castle maintains a
straight-A academic record while participating in the Marine Corps Junior Reserve Officer
Training Course program at Daniel Boone High School, which he has done for nearly four years;
and

WHEREAS, Cooper Castle has distinguished himself through his dedication, teamwork,
and passion for learning, embodying the values of responsible citizenship and civic
responsibility; and
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to commend this young man for his outstanding
representation of Washington County and the State of Tennessee and to encourage him to
continue serving as a role model in his community; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Cooper Castle for his
election as governor of American Legion Tennessee Boys State and for his participation at Boys
Nation, extending our best wishes for his every continued success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
