Section 1:
Senate Health and Welfare 1
Amendment No. 1 to SB2579
Crowe
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting subdivision (a)(8) from § 63-25-107 in SECTION 2 and substituting:
(8) Require all Tennessee-licensed dietitians and nutritionists who participate in
the Dietitian Licensure Compact and all applicants for licensure as a dietitian or
nutritionist to submit to a fingerprint-based criminal history record check to be conducted
by the Tennessee bureau of investigation, and a fingerprint-based national criminal
history record check to be completed by the federal bureau of investigation, with:
(A) The results being submitted to the commissioner of health, or the
commissioner's designee, as long as the designee is an employee of the
department of health; and
(B) The applicant being responsible for any fees associated with the
criminal history record check.
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting subsection (b) from § 63-25-107 in SECTION 2, and by
deleting the designator "(a)" from current subsection (a).
AND FURTHER AMEND by inserting the following immediately preceding the existing
subsection (d) of § 63-25-108 in SECTION 2 as a new subsection (d), and redesignating the
existing subsection (d) as subsection (e):
(d) An applicant for licensure as a dietitian or nutritionist must submit to a
fingerprint-based criminal history record check to be conducted by the Tennessee
bureau of investigation, and a fingerprint-based national criminal history record check to
be completed by the federal bureau of investigation, with:
