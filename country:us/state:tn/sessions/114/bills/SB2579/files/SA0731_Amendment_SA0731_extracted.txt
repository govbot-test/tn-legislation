Senate Health and Welfare 2
Amendment No. 2 to SB2579
Crowe
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 2579 House Bill No. 2029*
by deleting "or" at the end of § 63-25-104(d)(9)(C) in SECTION 2.
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting the period at the end of § 63-25-104(d)(10)(C) in
SECTION 2 and substituting "; or".
AND FURTHER AMEND by adding the following as a new subdivision under § 63-25-104(d) in
SECTION 2:
(11) The dissemination of general non-medical nutrition information, dietary
education, or public health information provided orally, in writing, digitally, or through
electronic media when such information does not constitute individualized medical
nutrition therapy.
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting "June 30, 2026" wherever it appears in § 63-25-106(b)(1)
in SECTION 2 and substituting ”December 31, 2026".
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting "July 1, 2026" wherever it appears in § 63-25-108(d) in
SECTION 2 and substituting "January 1, 2027".
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting § 63-25-115 in SECTION 2 and substituting:
63-25-115. Rules.
The board is authorized to promulgate rules, including emergency rules if
necessary, to effectuate this chapter. The rules must be promulgated in accordance
with the Uniform Administrative Procedures Act, compiled in title 4, chapter 5.
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting "July 1, 2026" in SECTION 4 and substituting "January 1,
2027".
