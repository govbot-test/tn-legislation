HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1036
By Howell
A RESOLUTION to honor the memory of Blake Gary Davis of
Cleveland.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly were greatly saddened to learn of
the untimely passing of Blake Gary Davis; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Davis was an exemplary public servant and consummate professional
who worked assiduously to improve the quality of life for his fellow citizens in numerous
capacities; and
WHEREAS, Blake Davis, a native of Cleveland, was a devoted member of North
Cleveland Church of God, which he served faithfully for many years; and
WHEREAS, for almost twenty-eight years, Mr. Davis worked at Ocoee Utilities, where he
built lasting friendships and was known for his strong work ethic and dedication; and
WHEREAS, an avid outdoorsman, Blake Davis felt most at home in nature, and he
enjoyed camping and rafting; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Davis was known for his quick wit, great sense of humor, and
unmistakable sarcasm, and those who knew him appreciated his sharp humor and playful spirit;
and
WHEREAS, Blake Davis was also deeply devoted to his family, and he always
endeavored to remain true to family values of the highest order; he was a fiercely protective
brother, a devoted son, a loving husband, and a dedicated, doting father and soccer dad; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Davis is survived by his wife of almost twenty-five years, Mollie Davis;
his children, Hannah, Aiden, and Noah; his father, D. Gary Davis; his stepmother, Sonya Davis;
his mother, Susan Allmon Ramsey; his siblings, April Davis (Allen) May, Cameron (Kassie)
Davis, Callie (Dale) Kent, and Tammy Ramsey (Kenny) Myers; his nieces and nephews,

Greyson May, Davis Kent, Hope Kent, Paige White, James White, Brooklyn Allen, Carly Allen,

conviction.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that we extend our sympathy and condolences to the
family and friends of Mr. Davis.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
