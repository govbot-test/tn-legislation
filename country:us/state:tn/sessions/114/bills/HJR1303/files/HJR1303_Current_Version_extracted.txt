HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1303
By Faison
A RESOLUTION to commend Ayanna Jackson for exemplary
service as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this General Assembly is pleased to recognize
certain persons who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment
of the legislative process; and
WHEREAS, Ayanna Jackson is one such individual, who has distinguished herself in her
role as a legislative intern for Representative Jeremy Faison; her work has resulted in
innumerable benefits for the legislators she assisted and the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, a student of the Honors College at Tennessee State University, where she
is highly regarded by her peers, Ms. Jackson has achieved academic success while pursuing a
bachelor's degree in criminal justice; and
WHEREAS, during her collegiate career, Ayanna Jackson has consistently made the
President's List since 2022; she is a member of the Alpha Lambda Delta Honor Society, the
National Association of Colored Women's Clubs, Inc., and the Frankie J. Pierce Federated
Chapter of Women of Empowerment, which she served as Campus Outreach Chair from 2024
to 2025; and
WHEREAS, a native of Atlanta, Georgia, Ms. Jackson currently serves Metro Nashville
Public Schools with acumen and alacrity as a mentor and teacher assistant, in which roles she
directly supports and monitors students in grades pre-K through four and assists with lesson
planning and instruction; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize herself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further her studies, Ayanna Jackson brought her many
