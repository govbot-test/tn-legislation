SENATE RESOLUTION 360
By Walley
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Adamsville High
School band students for their selection to the
Mid-South Honor Band at the University of
Mississippi.
WHEREAS, the members of this legislative body take great pleasure in recognizing
those students who, through dedication and talent, achieve excellence in academic and
extracurricular pursuits; and
WHEREAS, a group of accomplished student musicians from Adamsville High School
were selected to participate in the Forty-seventh Annual Mid-South Honor Band Festival at the
University of Mississippi, held from February 26 through February 28, 2026; and
WHEREAS, participation in the Mid-South Honor Band Festival is a distinction earned by
students who demonstrate superior musical ability, discipline, and commitment to their craft; and
WHEREAS, Adamsville High School Band Directors Tammye Hight and Dusty Hurley
recommended these outstanding students for participation in the program, recognizing their
exceptional skill and dedication; and
WHEREAS, among the participating students, Riley Driskell placed fifth in the Concert
Band, Benjamin Elder placed first in the Symphonic Band, Eli Glidewell placed first in the
Concert Band, Noah Hight placed thirteenth in the Symphonic Band, Jacob Leitschuh placed
seventh in the Symphonic Band, and Lorelai Wood placed fourth in the Symphonic Band; and
WHEREAS, after two days of intensive rehearsals, these students performed in concert
at the Gertrude C. Ford Center on February 28, 2026, showcasing their musical talents at a high
level of achievement; and
WHEREAS, the accomplishments of these students reflect great credit upon
themselves, their school, their instructors, and their community; now, therefore,

BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and congratulate the

Honor Band Festival at the University of Mississippi and commend them for their outstanding
musical achievements.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
