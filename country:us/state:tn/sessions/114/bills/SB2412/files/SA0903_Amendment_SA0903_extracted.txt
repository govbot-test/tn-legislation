by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 63-6-1108, is amended by deleting
the section and substituting:
(a) The attorney general and reporter may bring a civil action to:
(1) Enforce compliance with this part; or
(2) Impose a civil penalty, as described in subsection (b), for a knowing
violation of § 63-6-1103(b).
(b)
(1) Except as provided in subdivision (b)(2), the attorney general and
reporter may impose a civil penalty of ten thousand dollars ($10,000) per
violation of § 63-6-1103(b).
(2) If a court determines that an individual suffered serious bodily injury
or death due to a violation of § 63-6-1103(b), then the attorney general and
reporter may impose a civil penalty of one million dollars ($1,000,000).
(3) Each individual to whom an abortion-inducing drug is provided in
violation of § 63-6-1103(b) constitutes a separate violation.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it, and
applies to actions occurring on or after that date.
