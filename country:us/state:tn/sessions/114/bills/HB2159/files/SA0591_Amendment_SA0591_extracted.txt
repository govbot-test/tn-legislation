Senate Transportation and Safety 1
Amendment No. 1 to SB2360
Massey
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 2360 House Bill No. 2159*
by deleting "by certified mail, return receipt requested," in the amendatory language of
subsection (c) in SECTION 1 and substituting "using three-day delivery".
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting subdivision (f)(2) in SECTION 2 and substituting:
(2) A garagekeeper may enforce a lien under this section after the expiration of
thirty (30) days from the date of receipt of the notice provided under § 55-31-205(c).
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting subdivision (f)(3)(B) in SECTION 2 and substituting:
(B) A copy of the confirmation of receipt, which may include an electronic
signature, photograph of delivery, or other verifiable proof that the notice was delivered
to the address of the last known registered owner of the motor vehicle.
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting the effective date section and substituting:
SECTION 4. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.
