SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 632
By Johnson
A RESOLUTION to confirm the appointment of Tom Boyd to the
state university board of Middle Tennessee
State University.
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-8-101, provides for the governance
of Middle Tennessee State University by a state university board; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-8-201, provides that the governor
shall appoint six members to the state university board of Middle Tennessee State University;
and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-8-201, further provides that at least
three of the gubernatorial appointees shall be alumni of the institution for which they are serving;
and
WHEREAS, Governor Bill Lee has appointed Tom Boyd to the state university board of
Middle Tennessee State University for a term that will expire on June 30, 2031; and
WHEREAS, Tom Boyd is an investment advisor representative with Decker Wealth
Management; he has more than four decades of experience in financial management and
banking; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Boyd earned a Bachelor of Science degree from Middle Tennessee
State University and holds an M.B.A.; and
WHEREAS, he previously served with the Tennessee Army National Guard; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-8-201, provides that each member
appointed by the governor to the state university board of Middle Tennessee State University
shall be subject to confirmation by the senate and the house of representatives; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
