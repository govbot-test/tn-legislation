SENATE RESOLUTION 313
By Walley
A RESOLUTION to honor the Collinwood Middle School softball
team, Tennessee River Valley Conference
champions.
WHEREAS, we are pleased to specially honor and commend those successful young
athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring honor to Tennessee and serve as
exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, such estimable young Tennesseans are the members of the Collinwood
Middle School softball team, who won the Tennessee River Valley Conference Championship
on April 24, 2025, in Lobelville; and
WHEREAS, playing with confidence, skill, and tenacity, the Trojanettes were undefeated
in their conference this past season and ended the year with an exemplary 15-1 record; and
WHEREAS, displaying great sportsmanship and outstanding character, both individually
and collectively, the players and coaches of the Collinwood Middle School softball team
conducted themselves in a manner befitting the integrity of the State of Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, the young women's noteworthy achievements, both on and off the field, are
a tremendous source of pride for their families, their classmates at Collinwood Middle School,
and the entire community; and
WHEREAS, the Collinwood Middle School softball team displayed uncommon talent,
determination, and perseverance in its quest for athletic excellence, and it is wholly fitting that
the members of the team should be commended; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we congratulate the members
and coaches of the Collinwood Middle School softball team on winning the 2025 Tennessee

success in all their future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
