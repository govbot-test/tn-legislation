Section 1:
HOUSE BILL 2489
By Reedy
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 49,
Chapter 2, relative to directors of schools.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-2-203(a)(13), is amended by
adding the following as a new subdivision:
(D)
(i) If a school board terminates its director of schools without cause, then
the school board may offer or provide severance pay to the terminated director of
schools; provided, that the amount of severance pay offered or provided must not
exceed the annual salary amount the director of schools was receiving at the
time of termination;
(ii) If a school board terminates its director of schools with cause, then
the school board shall not offer or provide severance pay to the terminated
director of schools;

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2025, the public welfare requiring it, and
applies to contracts entered into or renewed on or after such date.
