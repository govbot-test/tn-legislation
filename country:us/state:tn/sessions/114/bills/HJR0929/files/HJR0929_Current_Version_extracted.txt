HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 929
By Jones R
A RESOLUTION to recognize and congratulate Zoe Scheiderich,
Miss Tennessee 2025.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should specially recognize those emerging community
leaders who strive to improve the quality of life for their fellow citizens while bringing beauty,
grace, and purpose to all their endeavors; and
WHEREAS, one such superlative individual is Zoe Scheiderich of Lebanon, the 2025
title holder for the Miss Tennessee pageant, a most befitting honor, as it recognizes her
dedication to excellence and her devotion to serving her community; and
WHEREAS, a student at Middle Tennessee State University, Ms. Scheiderich is highly
regarded by her peers and is pursuing a major in entertainment journalism; and
WHEREAS, Zoe Scheiderich is a classically trained dancer, and her passion for dance
has taken her to elite programs, including the School of American Ballet, Miami City Ballet,
Boston Ballet, and Pacific Northwest Ballet, and led her to choreograph an original piece and
direct a full student production at Project Ballet; and
WHEREAS, she has earned over $25,000 in scholarships through competing in the Miss
America Organization; at the 2026 Miss America competition, she was a Top Eleven finalist and
a Preliminary Fitness Winner; and
WHEREAS, Zoe Scheiderich has shown exceptional dedication to her community
service initiative, Elevate: Empowering Girls, Equipping Women; she has presented her platform
to State leaders at the Tennessee State Capitol, partnered with Girls, Inc., of Tennessee, and
worked closely with TenThirtyFive and Life Options Network, supporting women and children in
need; and

WHEREAS, she recently expanded her efforts internationally through Wings of Hope,
traveling to Kenya to present confidence workshops to young women rescued from tribal
marriages; and
WHEREAS, with aspirations of becoming a sideline commentator and ESPN anchor,
Zoe Scheiderich brings charisma, compassion, and confidence to her role as the seventy-
second Miss Tennessee, determined to inspire women and dance her way across the State;
and
WHEREAS, Ms. Scheiderich is an exceptional role model and community leader, and it
is fitting that we recognize her worthy reign as Miss Tennessee 2025; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we commend and congratulate Zoe Scheiderich, Miss Tennessee 2025,
extending our best wishes for her success during her ambassadorial year and in all her future
endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
