Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 10-7-503, is amended by adding the
following as a new subsection:
(i)
(1) An individual who is not a citizen of this state is entitled to inspect or receive
a copy of any law enforcement record or report pertaining solely to that individual,
including, but not limited to, an arrest report, incident report, crash report, or citation, if
the individual submits:
(A) A written request identifying the specific record sought;
(B) A copy of a valid government-issued photo identification; and
(C) A signed affidavit affirming that the record requested pertains solely
to the individual requester.
(2) This subsection (i) does not authorize the release of a record that is:
(A) Otherwise confidential by law;
(B) Privileged under Rule 16 of the Tennessee Rules of Criminal
Procedure; or
(C) Privileged under the investigative privilege.
(3) This subsection (i) does not prohibit a records custodian from requiring a
requestor to pay the reasonable costs incurred in producing the requested material
pursuant to subdivision (a)(7)(B).

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.
