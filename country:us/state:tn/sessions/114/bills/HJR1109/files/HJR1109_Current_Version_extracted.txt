HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1109
By Hawk
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Sapana Das, 2026
Valedictorian of Chuckey-Doak High School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Sapana Das is one such exceptional student, who was recently named
Valedictorian of the 2026 graduating class at Chuckey-Doak High School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Ms. Das has achieved outstanding academic success
and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country; and
WHEREAS, in her remarkable high school career, Sapana Das has concurrently earned
an associate degree from Walters State Community College, all while maintaining an exemplary

4.54 grade point average and participating in numerous extracurricular activities; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Das is an active member of the Chuckey-Doak FFA Chapter and has
