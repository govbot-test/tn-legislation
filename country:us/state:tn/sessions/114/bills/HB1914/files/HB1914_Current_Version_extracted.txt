SENATE BILL 1613
By Jackson
HOUSE BILL 1914
By Boyd
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 58,
Chapter 7, relative to state veterans' homes.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 58-7-101, is amended by deleting
subsection (d) and substituting instead:
(d) Each state veterans' home established pursuant to this chapter must be
eligible for and receive the veterans' administration per diem payments available to
qualified residents in accordance with applicable federal regulations.
(e) In addition to providing skilled nursing services, each state veterans' home
may provide and receive payment for other services for the support and care of
honorably discharged veterans who served in the United States armed forces as
authorized by state or federal law.
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 58-7-103(c)(1), is amended by
deleting the subdivision and substituting instead:
(1) The authority to determine the location and type of the Tennessee state
veterans' homes, facilities, and services. In selecting the sites, preference must be
given to publicly owned land. Land for the sites may be purchased only if suitable
publicly owned land is not available;
SECTION 3. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 58-7-103(c)(8), is amended by
deleting the subdivision.
SECTION 4. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 58-7-104(b)(3), is amended by
deleting the subdivision and substituting instead:
