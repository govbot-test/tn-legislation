SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 560
By Campbell
A RESOLUTION to commemorate February 2026 as
"Spay/Neuter Awareness Month" in Tennessee.
WHEREAS, Spay/Neuter Awareness Month is an important observance dedicated to
promoting responsible pet ownership by raising awareness on the importance of spaying and
neutering companion and stray animals to reduce the number of homeless animals in the State
of Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, each year, shelters euthanize hundreds of thousands of healthy, adoptable
cats and dogs because of the lack of resources to care for them, while more are uncared for
and unreported as they struggle to survive on the streets; and
WHEREAS, cats and dogs are domesticated animals who rely on humans to care for
them, and the most effective way to address the companion animal homelessness crisis is to
spay and neuter pets; and
WHEREAS, spaying and neutering are routine, safe, and beneficial surgeries; and
WHEREAS, spayed and neutered animals live longer, healthier lives with reduced risk
for certain cancers and other life-threatening diseases; and
WHEREAS, spaying and neutering reduce the burden placed on public and private
animal shelters and other animal protection and welfare services; and
WHEREAS, Tennesseans can learn more about their options for spaying and neutering
their pets at those local animal shelters, rescue groups, and spay-and-neuter organizations that
work tirelessly to promote and protect animal welfare; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
SJR0560
010613
- 1 -
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we join with citizens from across this State in
commemorating the month of February 2026 as "Spay/Neuter Awareness Month" in Tennessee.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
