Section 1:
Amendment No. 4 to SB2658
Johnson
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting the language "resulting from an act or omission committed in good faith" in § 29-34-
216(a) in Section 4 and substituting instead "resulting from an act or omission of the emergency
utility worker committed in good faith".
AND FURTHER AMEND by adding the following subsection to § 29-34-216 in Section 4:
(d) Notwithstanding subsection (a), this section does not provide immunity from
civil liability to an employer of an emergency utility worker for the negligent acts or
omissions of the employer or of the emergency utility worker.
