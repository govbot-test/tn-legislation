HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1646
By Camper
A RESOLUTION to honor the South Side Class of 1976 on the
occasion of its fiftieth class reunion.
WHEREAS, high school reunions are an important part of the fabric of our society, as
they provide an excellent opportunity to remember the past, renew old acquaintances, and
embrace and look forward to the future; and
WHEREAS, it is the great pleasure of the members of this General Assembly to honor
the graduates of South Side High School's Class of 1976, who are joining together this year to
celebrate their fiftieth class reunion on May 21–24, 2026, at The Guest House at Graceland in
Memphis, with the theme "A Golden Legacy—50 Years of Strength, Spirit, and Scrapper Pride";
and
WHEREAS, reunion activities will include time for reconnecting at The Guest House at
Graceland, a class picnic on the historic school grounds of South Side High School, and a
seventies-themed game night to be held at the Nyshari Bre & Desire Event Venue; the weekend
will conclude with a worship service at River of Life M B Church and the reunion gala at
Graceland; and
WHEREAS, a great many of the members of the South Side High School Class of 1976,
the distinguished Bicentennial Class, have gone on to achieve great things in life, truly making
proud their classmates, their peers, and the great State of Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, the South Side High School Class of 1976 enjoyed the leadership of
President Reginald Wiseman, Vice President Henry Snowden, Secretary and Assistant
Treasurer Sharon Reese, Assistant Secretary Sandra Hollister, Treasurer Michael Stovall,
Chaplain Paula Stepter, Assistant Chaplain Michael Wise, Parliamentarian Bruce Jefferies, and
Assistant Parliamentarian Doris Martin; and

WHEREAS, the Class of 1976 chose Reginald Wiseman and Priscilla Snowden as Mr.
and Miss South Side, with Darryl Elion and Paula Stepter being selected as first alternates and
Michael Stovall and Sharon Webster as second alternates; and
WHEREAS, honorifics were also extended to Waymon McDonald and Earline Walker,
Mr. and Miss Senior, and Darrell Clark and Anita Johnson, Mr. and Miss Yearbook; and
WHEREAS, the valedictorian of the South Side Class of 1976 was Gloria Hobbs, and
the salutatorian was Mark McCormick; and
WHEREAS, as the celebration of the South Side High School Class of 1976's fiftieth
class reunion takes place, the members of this General Assembly find it appropriate to
recognize this special group of people; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend the members of the South Side High Class of
1976 as they celebrate their fiftieth class reunion on May 21–24, 2026, and extend to them our
best wishes for many more years of success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
