HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1551
By Camper
A RESOLUTION to commend Suzy Bengin for her service as a
legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, the members of this General Assembly are
pleased to recognize certain persons who have committed themselves, through their adroit
service, to the betterment of the legislative process; and
WHEREAS, Suzy Bengin is one such individual, who has distinguished herself in her
role as a legislative intern for Representative Karen Camper; Ms. Bengin's work has resulted in
innumerable benefits for the legislators she assisted and the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, a student at Belmont University, where she is highly regarded by her peers,
Suzy Bengin is majoring in political science and minoring in business administration; she is a
student in the Global Honors Program and a member of the Alexander Hamilton Society; and
WHEREAS, during her collegiate career, Ms. Bengin has participated in many
extracurricular activities, including the 2025 Tennessee Campus Civic Engagement Summit; the
Civic Engagement and Advocacy Club, which she served as director of public outreach; and the
Student Government Association, which she served as senator and vice chair of diversity,
equity, and inclusion; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize herself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further her studies, Suzy Bengin brought her many abilities
and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2026 session of the One Hundred Fourteenth
General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, through her service, Ms. Bengin has proven herself to be an integral part of
the success of the office and committee she served and a valuable contributor to the work of the
General Assembly on behalf of the citizens of House District 87; and

WHEREAS, Suzy Bengin has performed her many duties with extraordinary insight and
