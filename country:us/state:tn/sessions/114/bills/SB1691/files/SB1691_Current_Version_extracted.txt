HOUSE BILL 1506
By Hicks T
SENATE BILL 1691
By Briggs
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 2,
Chapter 10, Part 2 and Title 3, Chapter 6, Part 1,
relative to training programs for members of the
bureau of ethics and campaign finance.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 2-10-214(a), is amended by inserting
the language "the executive director of the bureau of ethics and campaign finance, an attorney
employed by the bureau of ethics and campaign finance, or" immediately after the language
"provided by".
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 3-6-116(a), is amended by inserting
the language "the executive director of the bureau of ethics and campaign finance, an attorney
employed by the bureau of ethics and campaign finance, or" immediately after the language
"provided by".
SECTION 3. This act takes effect upon becoming law, the public welfare requiring it.
