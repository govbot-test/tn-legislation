Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 63-6-233, is amended by adding the
following as a new subsection:
(d)
(1) In establishing continuing medical education requirements pursuant
to subsection (b), the board shall require each licensee to complete a minimum
of one (1) of the required hours on a topic related to nutrition. A licensee is only
required to complete the one (1) hour on a topic related to nutrition once.
(2) Any continuing medical education requirement related to nutrition,
pursuant to subdivision (d)(1), must be available free of charge to a physician.
(3) This subsection (d) terminates January 1, 2033.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 63-9-107(a), is amended by adding
the following as a new subdivision:
(3)
(A) In establishing continuing osteopathic medical education
requirements for licensees, the board shall require each licensee to complete a
minimum of one (1) of the required hours on a topic related to nutrition. A
licensee is only required to complete the one (1) hour on a topic related to
nutrition once.

(B) Any continuing osteopathic medical education requirement related to
nutrition, pursuant to subdivision (a)(3)(A), must be available free of charge to a

(C) This subdivision (a)(3) terminates January 1, 2033.

Section 4:
SECTION 3. The board of medical examiners and board of osteopathic examination are
each authorized to promulgate rules, including emergency rules if necessary, to effectuate this
act. The rules must be promulgated in accordance with the Uniform Administrative Procedures
Act, compiled in Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 4, Chapter 5.

SECTION 4. For the purpose of promulgating rules, including emergency rules, this act
