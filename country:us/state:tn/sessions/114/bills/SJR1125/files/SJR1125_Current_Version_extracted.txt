SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1125
By Hensley
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Addison Osborne, 2026
Top Ten Senior of Lewis County High School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Addison Osborne is one such exceptional student, who was recently named
a Top Ten Senior of the 2026 graduating class at Lewis County High School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Ms. Osborne has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in her remarkable high school career, Addison Osborne has earned an
exemplary grade point average while participating in numerous extracurricular activities,
including Beta Club and HOSA, and obtaining Patient Care Tech, EKG Tech, and Phlebotomy
licenses; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Osborne has received many awards and accolades, including awards
for the highest grade in multiple subjects, including Tennessee history, Spanish, and
government and civics; and
WHEREAS, Addison Osborne's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely
bodes well for her future success as a student at the University of Tennessee at Martin Parsons
Center, where she will study nursing; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Osborne's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for
her parents, Justin Osborne and Tabitha Osborne, her school, and her community; and
SJR1125
017660
- 1 -
WHEREAS, Addison Osborne's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the
respect, admiration, and commendation of the members of this General Assembly; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor Addison Osborne
on being named a 2026 Lewis County High School Top Ten Senior, salute her impressive
academic achievement, and extend to her our best wishes for a happy, successful future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
