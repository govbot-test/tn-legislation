HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1018
By Capley
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Noah Scott Parker, 2026
Top Ten Senior of Zion Christian Academy.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Noah Scott Parker is one such exceptional student, who was recently
named a Top Ten Senior of the 2026 graduating class at Zion Christian Academy; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Mr. Parker has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in his remarkable high school career, Noah Parker has earned an
exemplary grade point average while participating in numerous extracurricular activities; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Parker's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well
for his future success; and
WHEREAS, Noah Parker's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for
his family, his school, and his community; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Parker's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of the members of this General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor Noah Scott Parker on being named a
2026 Zion Christian Academy Top Ten Senior, salute his impressive academic achievement,
and extend to him our best wishes for a happy, successful future.
