SENATE RESOLUTION 283
By Harshbarger
A RESOLUTION to honor the Dobyns-Bennett High School Band
on the occasion of its 100th anniversary.
WHEREAS, it is the privilege of the members of this legislative body to recognize those
Tennessee ensembles that epitomize hard work and dedication and serve as exceptional
ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such renowned group of young musicians is the Dobyns-Bennett High
School Band, which, since 1926, has been a source of pride for Kingsport, Sullivan County, and
the State of Tennessee as one of the nation's most decorated high school music programs; and
WHEREAS, the Dobyns-Bennett Band program has a long and rich history of musical
excellence and annually enrolls between 350 and 375 students, who participate in three concert
bands and two marching bands; and
WHEREAS, Dobyns-Bennett Band classes meet in yearlong singletons or "skinnies" and
are scheduled by instruments: woodwinds, brass, and percussion, plus color guard and music
theory and technology offerings; program alumni have continued playing music at many of the
finest university and conservatory band programs in America; and
WHEREAS, the Dobyns-Bennett High School Band's performance highlights include the
Pasadena Tournament of Roses Parade (2002, 2007, 2014, and 2020), the Presidential
Inaugural Parade of 2013, the Lord Mayor of London's Millennium Parade in 2000, the Macy's
Thanksgiving Day Parade (1984, 1988, 1993, 2004, and 2011), and a featured performance at a
University of Tennessee football game in 2011; and
WHEREAS, an eighteen-time winner of the Tennessee Bandmasters Association
Sweepstakes Award, Dobyns-Bennett has performed on the most prestigious stages—including
the American Bandmasters Association Convention, the Tennessee Music Education

Convention, the New York Wind Band Festival in Carnegie Hall, the Midwest International Band
and Orchestra Clinic, the Percussive Arts International Convention, the Music for All National

WHEREAS, the Dobyns-Bennett High School Band's many highlights and trophies

Tennessee Governor's Cup (2006, 2007, and 2014); and
WHEREAS, the success of the Dobyns-Bennett High School Band over the past century
has been aided in no small part by the support of band members, the community, and the
administrators, faculty, and staff of Dobyns-Bennett High School; and
WHEREAS, as a result of its continued dedication to the highest expertise of musical
and marching performance, the Dobyns-Bennett High School Band serves as the gold standard
in the State of Tennessee; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor the members and
directors of the Dobyns-Bennett High School Band, both past and present, on the occasion of
the band's 100th anniversary, applaud their dedication to excellence in music and technique, and
extend to them our best wishes for every continued success in the future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
