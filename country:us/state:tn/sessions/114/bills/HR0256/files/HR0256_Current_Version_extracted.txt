HOUSE RESOLUTION 256
By Vital
A RESOLUTION to honor Coach Scott Shephard for his
leadership, service, and lasting contributions to
girls' lacrosse at Ooltewah High School and in
Hamilton County.
WHEREAS, over the past eleven years, the girls' lacrosse program at Ooltewah High
School has grown from an initial concept into a respected, competitive, and school-sanctioned
athletic program; and
WHEREAS, Ooltewah High School was the first high school in Hamilton County to offer
a girls' lacrosse team, establishing a foundation for the sport in the region; and
WHEREAS, in the absence of an established blueprint, feeder programs, or institutional
support, Coach Scott Shepherd, alongside his wife, Tara Shepherd, demonstrated initiative and
leadership by creating the Ooltewah Lady Owls Lacrosse Club, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit
organization, with Coach Shepherd leading coaching efforts and Mrs. Shepherd managing
operations with the support of a governing board; and
WHEREAS, Coach Shepherd began by building the program at the club level, recruiting
players, organizing facilities, and teaching the fundamentals of lacrosse to student athletes who
often had no prior exposure to the sport, successfully forming a competitive varsity-level team
within six months each year; and
WHEREAS, through persistence and advocacy, the program achieved official
recognition and sanctioning, representing a significant milestone for Ooltewah High School and
advancing opportunities for girls' athletics throughout the region; and
WHEREAS, as the Southeast representative for the Tennessee Girls' Lacrosse
Association (TGLA), Coach Shepherd played a vital role in promoting and advancing the sport
until it achieved sanctioned status; and

WHEREAS, under Coach Shepherd's leadership, approximately 200 student athletes
have participated in the program, with approximately 160 games coached, achieving a record of
64-95-1 and qualifying for four regional playoff appearances; and
WHEREAS, eight student athletes from the program have advanced to compete at the
collegiate level across NAIA, Division III, and Division II institutions, gaining both athletic and
academic opportunities; and
WHEREAS, beyond athletic accomplishments, Coach Shepherd has instilled values of
resilience, discipline, teamwork, and perseverance, contributing to the personal development
and confidence of his student athletes; and
WHEREAS, Coach Shepherd has made significant personal sacrifices, dedicating
countless hours to practices, competitions, travel, and mentorship, while consistently advocating
for resources, recognition, and expanded opportunities for his players; and
WHEREAS, through challenges, growth, and the demands of building a program from
the ground up, Coach Shepherd has remained a steady and respected leader, positively
impacting the lives of young women both on and off the field; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we recognize
and commend Coach Scott Shepherd for his outstanding leadership, dedication, and service to
Ooltewah High School, Hamilton County, and the advancement of girls' lacrosse. His
contributions have left a lasting legacy in the development of student athletes and the growth of
the sport, and his efforts serve as an example of commitment, perseverance, and community
impact.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
