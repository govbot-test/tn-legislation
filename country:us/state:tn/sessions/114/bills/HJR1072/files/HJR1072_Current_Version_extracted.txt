HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1072
By Keisling
A RESOLUTION to commend and congratulate Racquel Hubbs
Hawley of the U.S. Air Force upon her promotion
to Lieutenant Colonel.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly take great pleasure in honoring
those members of the United States military who, throughout their distinguished careers, have
served with honor, bravery, and an unswerving commitment to duty; and
WHEREAS, one such individual is Racquel Hubbs Hawley, who was promoted to the
rank of Lieutenant Colonel in the United States Air Force during a formal ceremony at McGhee
Tyson Air National Guard Base in Knoxville on February 8, 2026; and
WHEREAS, during the ceremony, Lieutenant Colonel Hawley, who is currently serving
on active duty in the Air Force JAG Corps, was also awarded the Meritorious Service Medal for
outstanding achievement during her tenure as Staff Judge Advocate of the 134th Air Refueling
Wing, a post she has held since January 2023; and
WHEREAS, as Staff Judge Advocate, she serves as the senior legal advisor for the
installation and leads an office of five legal professionals who provide legal services to more
than 1,350 military members; and
WHEREAS, Lieutenant Colonel Racquel Hawley enjoys the love and support of her
husband, Billy, and their daughters, Lochlyn and River; and
WHEREAS, throughout her distinguished career, Lieutenant Colonel Hawley has served
these United States with honor and distinction, and it is wholly fitting that we recognize her on
this noteworthy occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we commend and congratulate Racquel Hubbs Hawley of the U.S. Air
