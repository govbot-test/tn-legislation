SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 607
By Massey
A RESOLUTION to recognize Steve Borden of the Tennessee
Department of Transportation on the occasion of
his retirement.
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that we should honor those exemplary employees of the
State of Tennessee who have performed with uncommon devotion and enthusiasm throughout
their years of public service; and
WHEREAS, Steve Borden is one such estimable person, who is taking his much-
deserved retirement after a distinguished thirty-four-year career with the Tennessee Department
of Transportation (TDOT); and
WHEREAS, currently serving as Region 1 Director, Mr. Borden began his illustrious
career with TDOT as an intern, following in the footsteps of his father and great-grandfather,
who were both longtime TDOT employees; and
WHEREAS, an incredible leader with a passion for transportation in East Tennessee,
Mr. Borden worked his way through the ranks, serving as Region 1 Assistant Director for five
years before rising to his current post of Region 1 Director, which he has held for the past
sixteen years; and
WHEREAS, Steve Borden worked tirelessly to develop an amazing team in Region 1,
leading by example with his professionalism, dedication, and work ethic; his steady, proactive,
and people-focused leadership on the ground in the immediate aftermath and recovery of
Hurricane Helene may have been the most important work of his career; and
WHEREAS, TDOT and the State of Tennessee are better off thanks to Mr. Borden's
many years of service, and he will certainly be missed by his colleagues and elected officials
throughout East Tennessee; his lasting legacy will be felt by his fellow citizens through the
dedication and skill of the phenomenal team he built at Region 1 who will continue its important
work; and
SJR0607
011650
- 1 -
WHEREAS, throughout his estimable career, Steve Borden has demonstrated the
utmost professionalism, ability, and integrity, winning the unbridled respect and admiration of his
colleagues and the citizens of the State of Tennessee; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Steve Borden on the
occasion of his retirement for his meritorious service to the State of Tennessee. We extend to
him our best wishes for a happy and fulfilling retirement and every continued success in his
future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
