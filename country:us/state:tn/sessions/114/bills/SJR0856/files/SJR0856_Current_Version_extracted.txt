SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 856
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Isaac Drinnon on
winning the 2025-2026 Hamblen County
Spelling Bee.
WHEREAS, our super spellers and their impressive accomplishments are rightfully
included in the pantheon of Tennessee champions; and
WHEREAS, Isaac Drinnon, an eighth-grade student at East Ridge Middle School, has
earned inclusion among this select group by winning the 2025-2026 Hamblen County Spelling
Bee; and
WHEREAS, renowned throughout Hamblen County as a spelling phenomenon, Isaac
Drinnon clinched the title with the winning word "maladaptive"; and
WHEREAS, spellers of Isaac's quality are not born but made, as they combine academic
excellence, intensive practice, and composure under pressure to produce a unique competitor;
and
WHEREAS, Isaac Drinnon has truly earned his laurels as a champion speller, and it is
most appropriate that he "bee" recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Isaac Drinnon on
winning the 2025-2026 Hamblen County Spelling Bee and extend to him our best wishes for
much continued success and happiness in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
SJR0856
