Amendment No. 8 to HB6004
Dixie
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 6001* House Bill No. 6004
by adding the following as a new subsection (j) in Section 3:
(j) If an LEA has experienced disenrollment, then the department shall increase
the LEA's TISA allocation for the respective school year by the rate of inflation. The
state shall fund one hundred percent (100%) of the increase to an LEA's TISA allocation
pursuant to this subsection (j). If an LEA is eligible to receive additional state funds
under subsection (i), then the LEA is also eligible to receive additional state funds under
this subsection (j). For purposes of this subsection (j), "disenrollment" has the same
meaning as defined in subdivision (i)(4).
