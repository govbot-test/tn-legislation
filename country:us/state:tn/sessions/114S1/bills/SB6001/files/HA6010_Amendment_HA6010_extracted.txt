Amendment No. 5 to HB6004
Powell
Signature of Sponsor
AMEND S e n ate Bill No. 6001* House Bill No. 6004
by inserting the following new section immediately preceding the antepenultimate section and
renumbering the subsequent sections accordingly:
SECTION __. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 4-49-104, is amended by adding
the following as a new subsection:
( ) Notwithstanding 4-49-401(e)(1) or this section to the contrary, an amount as
determined annually by the general assembly in the appropriations act must be allocated
to and used by local education agencies as capital funds for schools that have had a
shooting on their campuses. It is the legislative intent that the initial amount
appropriated for such schools in fiscal year 2025-2026 is fifty million dollars
($50,000,000).
